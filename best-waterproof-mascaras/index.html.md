# 11 Best Waterproof Mascaras ...

Waterproof mascaras are a must-have for any woman. Using waterproof cosmetics comes in so handy whether you live in a humid environment, are going to work out or play sports or if you like using a long-lasting formula that you won’t need to reapply or worry about running in the middle of the day. Some ladies find that waterproof mascara holds the curl in their lashes better, too! There are lots of occasions where waterproof mascara can save the day, so now you just have to pick which one you want to use. Peruse our picks for the best waterproof mascaras to help you decide.

## 1. Lancôme Hypnôse Drama Waterproof Mascara

![Lancôme Hypnôse Drama Waterproof Mascara](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/11/19045645_7696_521x462.jpg)

Lancôme’s Hypnôse Drama Waterproof Mascara boasts a unique curved brush that coats each lash with Texturizing Complex formula with highly saturated waxes, Triple Coating System with vitamin B and tons of volume-boosters for full, lush lashes that won’t flake off.

**Price:** $27.00 at [lancome-usa.com](http://www.lancome-usa.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)

This luxe, award-winning mascara volumizes, lengthens, defines and conditions your lashes in just one stroke so your lashes are thick and dramatic all day long. This innovative mascara coats each lash with water-resistant tubes, so once you apply it you’re set until you decide to take it off.
