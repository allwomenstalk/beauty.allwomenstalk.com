# Target Exercises for Girls Who Want a Beautiful Toned and Tight Face ...

You probably include plenty of exercises in your routine that target your abs, arms, legs, back and shoulders. Did you know that you should also be doing moves that target your face? That’s right – there are exercises that will tone and tighten your face so that you can look younger and lovelier anytime. They are super easy to do and you aren’t going to have to spend a ton of time doing them because they are pretty fast and simple. Here are the best.

## 1. Firm Your Forehead with Eyebrow Raises

![hair,person,hairstyle,beauty,brown hair,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/oy/tj/s2sm1_hair_person_hairstyle_beauty_brown-hair_600x600.jpg)

Do you want your forehead to look younger? Don’t we all? It’s as easy as working those forehead muscles so that they stay firm and fresh. To do the move, press down just above your eyes, while also lifting your forehead. Repeat the move 10 times during a couple of weekly sessions and you’ll start to notice results in just a couple of weeks.

## 2. Tone Your Cheeks by Raising Them

![hair,human hair color,face,person,blond,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fv/dt/hk5bv_hair_human-hair-color_face_person_blond_600x600.jpg)

This move is very similar to one above, but works the muscles in your cheeks. High, firm cheeks are associated with youth so this move should have you feeling and looking younger in no time. Press down gently on your cheeks with a couple of fingers, then raise the muscles in your cheeks at the same time. Repeat 10 times.

## 3. Keep Your Neck Looking Young with This Easy Lip Move

![human action,face,facial expression,hair,person,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ul/o6/moadu_human-action_face_facial-expression_hair_person_600x600.jpg)

The neck is one of the first places to show your age. You don’t want a saggy, old neck so be sure to include a few sets of this move several times each week to fight it off. Sit down and tip your head back, close your eyes and relax. Pull your lower lip up over your upper lip, hold for a second, and then relax. Do this move at least 10 times during each set.

## 4. Use This Move to Get Rid of a Double Chin

![hair,eyebrow,face,black hair,nose,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ei/ii/x89nk_hair_eyebrow_face_black-hair_nose_600x600.jpg)

Want to create tone and definition in your chin? This move is easy and effective and will likely become one of your new favorites. Sit with your head tilted back and your lips relaxed. Round your lips and hold the move for 20 seconds. Repeat several times to give your chin a really good workout.

## 5. Keep Your Lips Looking Young and Fresh

![hair,face,eyebrow,person,nose,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/vn/pc/wux38_hair_face_eyebrow_person_nose_600x600.jpg)

Bet you didn’t know you need to work your lip muscles. This move should help keep them from getting saggy and wrinkly. Purse your lips as if you’re getting ready to kiss someone. Then lift the pucker up toward your nose and hold for 10 seconds. Repeat 5 times, several times each week.

## 6. Here’s Another One for Your Cheeks

![hair,human hair color,face,eyebrow,hairstyle,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/jj/ag/gg01m_hair_human-hair-color_face_eyebrow_hairstyle_600x600.jpg)

This move is super easy to do and helps get your cheeks into tip top shape in mere minutes. Start by smiling with your mouth closed. Then suck your cheeks in while holding the smile. This move isn’t the easiest one, but that’s how you know it’s working. You’ll get better with practice.

## 7. Get Your Eyes in Shape by Opening and Closing Them

![hair,human action,face,person,nose,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/hd/ee/skqvk_hair_human-action_face_person_nose_600x600.jpg)

Keep the area around your eyes toned and firm without doing anything other than blinking. To do the move, press gently on your temples with a couple of fingers. Then rapidly open and close your eyes five times. Easy, right?
