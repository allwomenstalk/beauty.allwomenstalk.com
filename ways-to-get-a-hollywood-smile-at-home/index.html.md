# Genius Ways to Get a Hollywood Smile at Home ...

Looking for **ways to get a Hollywood smile at home**? When you think about all of your favourite Hollywood actors and actresses, there is definitely one thing that they all have in common, and that is amazing teeth! It seems like if you want to make a living in on the big screen, you need to have a winning smile filled with sparkling pearly white teeth, and whilst the likes of Jennifer Aniston and Tom Cruise most definitely go to an expensive dental expert, there are some ways that you can replicate their pain staking oral routines at home. If you want [to introduce the Hollywood smile into your life and your place of work](https://lemaclinic.com/hollywood-smile/), then here are the best **ways to get a Hollywood smile at home**.

## 1. Enough Brushing

Proper brushing is one of the best **ways to get a Hollywood smile at home**. Many people make the mistake of not brushing their teeth for long enough each day. It’s not just about giving them a quick once over and then rinsing, you should be aiming to be brushing for a sustained period of two to three minutes at least twice a day. If you are bad at timekeeping, then just throw on your favourite song and brush away until it stops.

## 2. Right Toothbrush

You might not think that the type of toothbrush you use is that important, but it really is. Most dentists agree that electric toothbrushes are the way to go because they deliver a more efficient and effective clean, removing many of the stains that manual toothbrushes can’t.

## 3. Right Toothpaste

There are dozens of different kinds of toothpaste on the market that are tailored to different dental issues, from sensitivity to the kind of flavour you prefer! Obviously, if the aim of the game is to achieve a beautiful Hollywood smile, then you want to be on the lookout for [toothpaste](https://reviews.allwomenstalk.com/fluoride-free-toothpaste/) that professes to be especially good for the process of whitening.

## 4. Baking Soda

One particular DIY method that has been touted through the years is that of brushing your teeth using baking soda. It is something that is included in smaller doses in many different toothpaste brands, but if you are looking for a concentrated dose of the good whitening stuff, then an extra dash of it on your toothbrush every day for about a fortnight to bring positive results.

## 5. Diet

The whiteness of your teeth can be heavily influenced by your diet. Stay away from things like coffee, dark sodas, and [red wine](https://allwomenstalk.com/must-know-facts-about-red-wine/), because they are famous for leaving stubborn stains on your teeth over time.
