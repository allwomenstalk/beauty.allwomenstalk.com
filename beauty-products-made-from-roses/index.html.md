# 10 Beauty Products Made from Roses ...

Beauty products made from roses are so much more than just fragrant and nice to look at; they’re really great for our skin! If you’ve never experienced the benefits of using rose beauty products, you’re missing out on a ton of benefits, like diminishing redness, moisturizing, relaxing and toning the skin. Here are 10 lovely beauty products made from roses so you too can experience the therapeutic effects of the rose!

## 1. Fresh Rose Face Mask

![Fresh Rose Face Mask](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/beauty/2013/01/1_fresh-rose-face-mask_216x200.jpg)

Beauty products made from roses come in all different formulas and this particular rose beauty product comes in the form of a soothing mask. This mask is made with 50% pure rosewater and infused with real rose petals so it’s the real deal! This rose face mask is made to hydrate, calm and tone your skin, and will restore radiance to all skin types!

## 2. Jurlique Rose Body Oil

![Jurlique Rose Body Oil](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/beauty/2013/01/2_jurlique-rose-body-oil_169x456.jpg)

This next rose beauty product contains a blend of different floral extracts and oils to make a nourishing and moisturizing oil to use all over the body. If you like floral scents, you’ll love the light, rose fragrance you experience from massaging this oil on parched and dull skin. Use this body oil alone or mix it with your body lotion for the ultimate pampering experience.

## 3. The Body Shop 100% Natural Lip Roll-on Rose

![The Body Shop 100% Natural Lip Roll-on Rose](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/beauty/2013/01/3_the-body-shop-100-natural-lip-roll-on-rose_96x376.jpg)

Let’s show our lips some love with a roll-on lip balm. If you’re not a fan of the typical sweet or medicated fragrances of traditional lip balms, this will be a nice change of pace for you. This unique lip balm is all-natural and rose flavored! This lip balm is ideal for spring or summer with its slight cooling effect and natural sheen!

## 4. Korres Wild Rose + Vitamin C Advanced Brightening Sleeping Facial

![Korres Wild Rose + Vitamin C Advanced Brightening Sleeping Facial](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/beauty/2013/01/4_korres-wild-rose-vitamin-c-advanced-brightening-sleeping-facial_240x200.jpg)

When we think of roses, I think the petal-soft texture is one of the most memorable aspects of the flower. And Korres’ sleeping facial combines the benefits of a night cream and facial with wild rose oil and rose water for a super soft and hydrating experience. This sleeping facial works wonders while you sleep so you can wake up feeling fresh and soft as a rose!

## 5. Dior Crème De Rose Smoothing Plumping Lip Balm

![Dior Crème De Rose Smoothing Plumping Lip Balm](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/beauty/2013/01/5_dior-cr-me-de-rose-smoothing-plumping-lip-balm_250x250.jpg)

One of the prettiest rose beauty products is Dior’s smoothing plumping lip balm. This luxe lip balm contains Rose of Damas essential oil for its anti-aging properties, as well as smoothing and soothing shea butter for a truly pampering experience for your pucker.

## 6. Shea Terra Organics Pure Moroccan Rose Water

![Shea Terra Organics Pure Moroccan Rose Water](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/beauty/2013/01/6_shea-terra-organics-pure-moroccan-rose-water_173x194.jpg)

Rose water has been a skin care essential for women throughout the ages for its cooling, calming and cleansing properties. This fragrant rose water can be used to make your own face mask, used as a toner or as a cooling and hydrating face mist!

## 7. Omorovicza Rose Lifting Serum

![Omorovicza Rose Lifting Serum](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/beauty/2013/01/7_omorovicza-rose-lifting-serum_250x250.jpg)

Serums are a great addition to any skin care routine and this rose lifting serum is perfect for all skin types. This serum contains a mixture of Rosa Damascena Flower Water and various fruit and plant extracts to renew elasticity, firm skin and minimize the appearance of any imperfections.

## 8. Badger Damascus Rose Beauty Balm

![Badger Damascus Rose Beauty Balm](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/beauty/2013/01/8_badger-damascus-rose-beauty-balm_281x160.jpg)

Not to be confused with BB creams, this beauty balm can be used on your face, and body to moisturize and restore. The brand created this rose beauty product to keep skin healthy as it evolves over time, so it works with your skin as you age! This cream contains rose oil to cleanse pores and decrease wrinkles, as well as other certified organic and cruelty-free ingredients.

## 9. Ren Moroccan Rose Oil Otto Body Wash

![Ren Moroccan Rose Oil Otto Body Wash](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/beauty/2013/01/9_ren-moroccan-rose-oil-otto-body-wash_390x461.jpg)

If you’re looking for an all-natural body wash with a comforting fragrance, Ren’s Moroccan Oil body wash gives you all that and more. This body wash is made without sulfates, parabens or animal products and moisturizes and soothes dry or sensitive skin. Follow up with an emollient lotion and enjoy soft, supple skin.

## 10. The Body Shop Wild Rose Targeted Hand Oil

![The Body Shop Wild Rose Targeted Hand Oil](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/beauty/2013/01/10_the-body-shop-wild-rose-targeted-hand-oil_48x280.jpg)

Sometimes our hands need extra attention with all the harsh weather conditions and environmental stressors. The Body Shop’s rose hand oil was specially made to hydrate and tone the skin on our hands. Use this to moisturize and even out skin tone on hands as well as hydrate your cuticles!

We can use rose beauty products in just about every step of our beauty routine! The great thing about these fragrant and gentle beauty products is that they can do wonders for all skin types. Do you use any beauty products with rosewater or rose oil?
