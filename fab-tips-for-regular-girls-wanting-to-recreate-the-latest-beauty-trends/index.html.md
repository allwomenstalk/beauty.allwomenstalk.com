# Fab Tips 🤗 for Regular Girls Wanting to Recreate the Latest Beauty Trends ...

Keeping up with summer beauty trends is hard enough, but to recreate them is a whole other ball game. If you'd like to know how to recreate the latest and greatest in beauty trends, follow the tutorials theeverygirl.com put together below and learn to rock these looks yourself!

## 3. Ombre Lips

![hair, eyebrow, face, red, hairstyle,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/nr/fk/slii9bd159355b74a2d98876962293_500x750.jpg)

![hair, face, hairstyle, eyebrow, nose,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/lh/k5/cxtylgia59355b74cfb20980525819_540x810.jpg)

To create the illusion of a fuller lip, try using two different lipsticks. First, pick out a lipstick in any shade that strikes your fancy, then pick out a lighter version of the same shade. Need guidance? Try NARS Semi-Matte Lipsticks in both [Heat Wave](https://rstyle.me/%7E9XDkg) and [Shanghai Express](https://rstyle.me/%7E9XJCG). Apply Shanghai Express all over lips, and then apply Heat Wave in the center of lips on both top and bottom. The lighter color in the center will give an illusion of a fuller lip.

![lipstick, cosmetics, lip,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/h3/u5/hzucgal659355ceea82c3897858456_500x501.jpg)

Nars
[Heat Wave Lipstick, $28](https://rstyle.me/%7E9XJCG)

## 8. Clean Dewy Skin

![hair, eyebrow, face, cheek, forehead,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/q2/kn/bpevtwaw59355b769c969305453636_564x846.jpg)

![hair, eyebrow, face, cheek, nose,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ky/ua/nd7mmlvh59355b76e2d8f738773889_564x845.jpg)

Nothing looks prettier during hot summer months then clean dewy skin. Amp up the dew by using [Smashbox’s Primer Oil](https://rstyle.me/%7E9XDkt) on the highest points of your face; cheekbones, temples, bridge of nose, brow bones, and above the lip. This creates a “highlighted” look without the shimmer and gives a super clean, dewy finish to skin.

![skin, product, lotion, cosmetics, nail polish,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/r7/hv/g0n9vsju59355ceee1af5773201190_562x627.jpg)

[Photo Finish Primer Oil, $42](https://rstyle.me/%7E9XJCO)

## 12. Flushed Eighties Cheeks

## 14. Grey Lipstick

![eyebrow, color, face, hair, cheek,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wz/t2/y7fyyftv59355b79cd9a4907196379_564x846.jpg)

Dark lipstick colors in the summer, you ask? Yes! Grey lipstick is making its way into the summer season. To keep it fresh, make your lips the focus by using minimal eye makeup and starting with a clean dewy face. Then, try [NYX’s Liquid Suede Cream Lipstick](https://rstyle.me/%7E9XDkK) in Stone Fox to give your summer look an edge.

## 16. Monochromatic

![eyebrow, face, hair, cheek, forehead,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/qn/zr/opvqso1b59355b7a15b1c950240908_564x660.jpg)

![eyebrow, hair, face, cheek, nose,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ms/xd/ls5p2l8j59355b7a6ae29763142191_500x750.jpg)

To keep your summer beauty routine easy, try using the same color for eyes, cheeks, and lips! NARS has a stick called [The Multiple](https://rstyle.me/%7E9XDkP) that is made in 20+ shades and can be used by easily sweeping the cream stick on eyes cheeks and lips. Just use the warmth of your fingers to blend the product in effortlessly for a monochromatic look.
