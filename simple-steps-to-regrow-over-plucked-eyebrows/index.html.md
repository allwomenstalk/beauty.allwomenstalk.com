# 8 Simple Steps to Regrow over\-plucked Eyebrows ...

If you ever went through a skinny eyebrow phase like I once did, you probably searched high and low for instructions on how to regrow over-plucked eyebrows, right? Now that fuller eyebrows are big again, I tracked down some easy ways for us tweezer-loving gals to regrow our eyebrows so we too can flaunt a set of full and lush brows! Eyebrows really frame the face and having a good set of eyebrows can really make a difference in your everyday look so keep reading for some easy tips for a full fringe!

## 1. Toss the Tweezers

The very first lesson in how to regrow over-plucked eyebrows is to halt all tweezing and waxing for at least a month or two to let your hair follicles head and recover. I know this is asking a lot of you, but it’s best to give the hair removal a break for awhile and start over again. If you can’t stand seeing stray hairs, it’s okay to pluck above your brow line and between your brows but nothing else!

## 2. Supplement with Supplements

Another one of the easy ways to regrow eyebrows is to take vitamins. Vitamin A, B6, C, E and Biotin all help the body to grow hair and nails. You can easily get your dose of these vitamins with foods like carrots, green leafy vegetables, fruits, nuts and brown rice or you can always pop a vitamin in the morning to help ensure you’re getting your fill of these vitamins and nutrients to promote new hair growth.

## 3. Water It down

Another important step in how to regrow over-plucked eyebrows is to be sure you are properly hydrated by drinking lots of water! Drinking plenty of water will help ensure your hair is hydrated and healthy to help it to grow back faster and stronger. Drinking water and staying hydrated is great for your skin as well as a host of other things like healthy skin so drink up!

## 4. Engage in Massage Therapy

One of the little known ways to regrow eyebrows is to massage the eyebrow area with a small amount of olive, coconut or castor oil. Grab a fine eyebrow brush and dip it into the oil of your choice and massage the oil into the direction your hairs are growing and make this part of your daily routine. This technique helps to enhance the growth of your brows while strengthening the hair.

## 5. Fake the Fringe

Perhaps the most commonly utilized and effortless method in how to regrow over-plucked eyebrows is to fill in gaps in your eyebrows using makeup. Whether you choose an eyebrow pencil or brow powder, be sure to use light strokes to fake hair growth and avoid drawing one long line unless you’re fond of the Sharpie eyebrow look! If you’re missing a lot of hair and need guidance on how to shape your brows, try using an eyebrow stencil or consult a professional.

## 6. Prime with Protein

Another home remedy to regrow eyebrows is to include lots of protein in your diet. Your hair is made up of mostly protein so it’s important to pump up your protein intake while you’re trying to regrow your eyebrows. Excessive caffeine, sugar and fat can hinder hair growth so try to limit your intake of those items while you’re in the process of growing out your eyebrows.

## 7. Just Beat It

One of the more interesting ways to regrow eyebrows involves using egg yolk. Yes girls, you read that right! Grab an egg and separate the white from the yolk and beat the yolk. Grab a cotton ball or pad and soak it in the egg yolk and apply to your eyebrow are for 10-15 minutes. You can also use milk and raw onion juice the same way to help improve your hair follicles and regrow brows!

## 8. Get Clinical

Rogaine is used by many people to regrow the hair on their head but did you know it also plays a big role in how to regrow over-plucked eyebrows too?! Grab a small spare toothbrush, a child’s size works best and dip it into the bottle and lightly brush the product on to one or two areas on the eyebrows. It’s totally possible to overdo it and end up with Bert & Ernie eyebrows so use the Rogaine sparingly when you try this trick!

There are lots of techniques in how to regrow over-plucked eyebrows so if you or someone you know ever gets a little crazy with the tweezers, not to worry! One or all of these ways to regrow eyebrows will help enhance and strengthen the hair you have while nourishing your hair follicles to promote new hair growth! Do you have any eyebrow horror stories? Do you have any special tips and tricks to regrow eyebrows?
