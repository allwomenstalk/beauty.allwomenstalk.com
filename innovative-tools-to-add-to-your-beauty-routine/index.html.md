# 7 Innovative Tools to Add to Your Beauty Routine ...

There are so many innovative beauty tools introduced on a regular basis, but you might not know about them all. Adding them to your routine can make your entire get-up easier and faster to get through while also letting you look fabulous each and every day, no matter what life throws at you. To get a clear picture of all of these innovative beauty tools, you’d have to follow the beauty news all the time. I know you don’t have time for that, so I collected some of the best so you don’t have to do the work. Here they are!

## 1. Home Microdermabrasion Kits

Have you ever wished you could cram in just a bit more time for a trip to the spa for a microdermabrasion treatment? Me too! However, you can still get the job done with a home kit. This is definitely one of my favorite innovative beauty tools because I can do a spa worthy job right in the comfort of my own home. Look for a kit at your favorite drugstore or make-up retailer.

## 2. Instant Manicures

Are your nails discolored from polish or acrylic nails? You are not alone. There are women everywhere who wish they could instantly make their nails look fabulous. Well, now you can. Several beauty product manufacturers have created a product that will whiten, brighten and generally improve the appearance of your fingernails without having to book a manicure appointment. The powder works by using bubbles to get rid of dead skin cells and the top layer of the fingernail, leaving your nail area looking fresh and healthy.

## 3. Nail Art Polish

It’s no secret that nail art is trending in a huge way right now. That means you can find loads of products to help you achieve the look you want. Nail polish pens that allow you to draw your design are one that is pretty popular. Now some nail polish companies are selling polish with huge polka dots mixed in so that you can create nail art with the swipe of a brush. Genius!

## 4. Steaming Flat Iron

Heat styling can really do a number on your hair. That’s why this new product has been a long time coming. Redken is the first to introduce it, but other hair tool companies have similar products. This flat iron works by delivering steam to your hair while you straighten it, which helps hydrate your strands and keep them lush and healthy.

## 5. Make up Eraser

We’ve all had the mascara brush slip or gotten too crazy with the eyeliner and had to start over. Not anymore! Look for a make-up eraser and you can fix issues like that just like you erase a mistake on a paper. Use the tip of it to get rid of mistakes or excess product, then fill it in like you want it without having to wash your face and start from scratch. This has got to be one of the best inventions ever!

## 6. Fake Lashes

There are lots of ladies who wish their lashes were fuller or longer. Fake lashes have come a really long way! They are available in most make-up and drugstores and go on easily without the danger of glue in your eyes. They allow you to fill out your lash line and get the look you crave. Make sure you follow the directions really closely for the best effect.

## 7. Electronic Facial Cleansers

When you need a deep clean, you might think you need to see a professional, but that simply isn’t true. Adding an electronic facial cleanser to your collection allows you to get deep down in your skin, making it clean with very little effort. You don’t need to use it every day, but adding it to your routine a couple of times each month will clean out your pores and keep your skin looking fresh.

What other innovative beauty tools do you know about?
