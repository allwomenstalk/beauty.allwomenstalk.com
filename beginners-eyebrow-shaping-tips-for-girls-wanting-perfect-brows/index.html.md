# Beginner's Eyebrow Shaping Tips for Girls Wanting Perfect Brows ...

Here is the ultimate beginners guide to eyebrow shaping for girls wanting perfect brows! Eyebrows have recently become a beauty craze--everyone has taken notice of how important it is to have a strong brow game. It's true--having shapely eyebrows can make a huge difference for your face. Eyebrow pencils, powders, and gels will be your new bff. According to Mera Kilane, you should go about two shades darker if you have light eyebrows, and two shades lighter if you have dark brows. Use these eyebrow shaping tutorials for beginners and get the brows of your dreams!

## 1. How to Shape Perfect Natural Eyebrows by [mirellabellebeauty](https://www.youtube.com/channel/UCshhzFcbpuwLZrMI7CUq5hw)

If you’re looking for a tutorial that focuses fully on eye brow shaping, rather than filling, this is the one for you. You’ll learn how to achieve a natural brow shape that fits your face and makes your eyes pop! As she explains in the video, filling your brows in is more so when you have the hair already there, but this tutorial is geared at those of you who have over-plucked or may not have a lot of hair to fill in. She uses the _Anastasia Beverly Hills Brow Whiz in Dark Brown_, as well as the _Brow Powder_.

![cosmetics, cue stick, mascara,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/y8/fn/dsn2zwum5909e4e655db9381950303_600x600.jpg)

## 2. How to Get Perfect Brows: How to Tweeze, Trim & Shape Your Eyebrows: 2 Methods by [Kandee Johnson](https://www.youtube.com/channel/UC9TreTE-iXwfwQl72DzDurA)

In this tutorial, you’ll learn two methods for shaping and maintaining your eyebrows. You’ll need an all-white eyeliner, such as the _Jane Cosmetics Eyeliner in Pure White_, _Tweezerman Tweezers_ and _Cuticle Scissors_, _Tinkle Razors_, and a makeup brush. Once you’ve shaped them to your desired look, use the _Anastasia of Beverly Hills Dip Brow_ to fill them in.

![cosmetics, organ, eye, eyelash, ball pen,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/z4/kb/uud0v4g15909e4e620fb3459316546_600x600.jpg)

## 3. How To: Change The Shape of Your Eyebrows by [MakeupandArtFreak](https://www.youtube.com/channel/UCi0r7qJnZuXqS9-bffgOs9g)

Your natural eyebrow shape might be gorgeous how it is, but sometimes it’s fun to switch it up a little. In this tutorial, you’ll learn how to change the shape of your eye brows, whether you want them straighter, or with more of an arch. When creating your desired brow shape with a pencil, she cautions not to use a color that is too close to your natural brow color. If you have black brows like she does, use a dark brown shade like the _NYX Auto Eyebrow Pencil in Dark Brown_. This will make it look less artificial.

![pen, product, cosmetics, ball pen,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ba/hl/wlwtwww95909e4e7896bf933496001_600x600.jpg)

## 4. Basics: Perfect Brows Tutorial by [From Head to Toe](https://www.youtube.com/channel/UCecyOxHZpL3yfNFaUz2W_Ow)

For this brow shaping tutorial, you’re going to need a few things. She recommends products such as the _Sephora Angled Liner Brush_ to get your desired brow shape, plus the _Lioele Auto Eyebrow Pencil in Grey Black_ and an _Anastasia Beverly Hills Brow Palette_. She shows you how to shape them after they’ve grown out to get the brows you’ve always wanted!

![product, cosmetics, cue stick,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/dd/fa/mqbm5xli5909e4e69367f050556200_600x600.jpg)

## 5. Easy Eyebrow Tutorial by [mayratouchofglam](https://www.youtube.com/channel/UCdYIfA_OTih2--tbcKO0b5g)

If you have barely-there eyebrows, this tutorial demonstrates how to fill and shape them. Unlike most eyebrow tutorials, she recommends using eye shadow to fill in your brows, rather than an eyebrow pencil or palette. She uses _Makeup Geek in Mocha_. If you have really thin eyebrows, she shows you how to make them look thicker, and how to define and blend them for a natural look.

![brown, face powder, hairstyle, eye, powder,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/is/aw/dktn8y0r5909e4e7372b3744756916_494x494.jpg)

## 6. Eyebrow Shaping by [TheMakeupChair](https://www.youtube.com/channel/UC-1-zPmT368J8JRbsK_1keA)

You’ll learn to get a gorgeous, arched eyebrow shape with this tutorial. Using a business card and a white eye pencil, you’ll learn exactly where to draw lines for the right brow shape. She provides you with tips for plucking your brows, and suggests using _Witch Hazel_ afterwards if you want to sooth the skin. From there, she shows you how to lengthen and fill in your brows in a way that creates the illusion of hairs.

![distilled beverage, drink, liqueur,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/w5/eg/h2e8r4hd5909e4e74dd4f640236291_600x600.jpg)

## 7. Eyebrow Shaping Tutorial for Beginners by [WISHTrendTV](https://www.youtube.com/channel/UC6sK-8-j1jmQFuxmuk9ainw)

This video covers four basic eyebrow tutorials for beginners, plus four different looks depending on the brow shape. There are also tons of tips thrown in throughout the video, so it’s definitely worth a watch! She explains why different brow shapes work for different face shapes, so you can figure out which one will work best for you. You’ll need an eyebrow pencil such as the [Etude House Drawing Eye Brow](https://web.archive.org/web/https://www.amazon.com/gp/product/B00819JPMG?ie=UTF8&linkCode=as2&camp=1634&creative=6738&tag=clwiachofwi09-20&creativeASIN=B00819JPMG) to create your desired shape and fill in your brows.

![product, cosmetics, cue stick, eye,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/al/q2/jxzvbhh75909e4ec32dfc690450709_500x500.jpg)

## 8. Brow Shaping for Beginners by [OliveSkinBeauty](https://www.youtube.com/channel/UCXWAHRae2ab2zkJG2jbiH1A)

With this tutorial, you’ll learn how to get the perfect eyebrow shape using an eyebrow stencil such as the _Absolute New York Stencil Kit_. She demonstrates a unique way to use the stencil, which is great if the stencil doesn’t fit perfectly to your eyebrow. You’ll also need an eyebrow angle brush and comb, as blending is a big part of this technique. _The Anastasia Duo_ has both!

![pink, product, eyelash, magenta, eye,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/vh/qf/ii696owo5909e4ec7655f649747720_500x500.jpg)

## 9. Perfect Eyebrows in 3 Minutes by [Tina Yong](https://www.youtube.com/channel/UC0ng0jJflTuJBBH5DGvr1Pw)

In just three minutes, you can get perfectly shaped eyebrows using the techniques in this tutorial. Before shaping them, it’s important to brush your brows into place. Use a tool such as the _Maybelline New York Brush ‘n Comb_ in order to see any gaps you need to fill in. She also uses the _Benefit Hoola Bronzing Powder_ along with her brow pencil to give her brows a more natural shade.

![brush, tool,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/el/nl/opa7tp0n5909e50a1c621461238995_500x600.jpg)
