# Beauty Routines According to Your Star Sign ...

It doesn't matter if you swear by astrology 💯 percent or only read your horoscope when you're feeling a little curious; there are a lot of interesting things that you can learn from your star sign. If I'm being totally honest, I usually read my horoscope to see if there's any sound career or love advice worth following - OR if I should do the complete opposite. But recently, I've been *really* getting into beauty tips 💋 based on my zodiac sign (any other Cancers ♋ in the house 👏👋?) and incorporating them into my daily routines. Here's how these star-studded 😉 beauty routines will compliment your personality and upgrade your makeup game 💄 at the same time 🙌.

## 1. Capricorn ♑

Capricorns 🐐 aren't afraid of splurging on tried-and-true beauty products.Your skin is your largest organ, which makes taking care of it your number one priority. For extra brownie points, treat yourself to a facial or rock the latest perfume.

## 2. Aquarius ♒

Whether it's their personal style or makeup palette, Aquarians swear by the mentality "Go bold or go home". Naturally, this translates into your makeup routine. You have an adventurous streak, which means there's no better feeling than trying out new brands and falling in love with them 😍. It doesn't hurt that your favorite brands are all cruelty-free.

## 3. Pisces ♓

Pisces 🐟 are big on highlighting their best features. To do this, try different facial creams that will keep your skin looking smooth or rock glam [metallic eyeshadow](https://makeup.allwomenstalk.com/amazing-metallic-eye-shadows/) to get that Hollywood look.

## 4. Aries ♈

Give an Aries 🐏 a bold red lip 💋 and she will be ready to conquer the world. You are just as eager to put your favorite features on display. You can also trust that your eyebrows will always be on fleek 👌.

## 5. Taurus ♉

Your down to earth nature definitely shows in your personal style. You also tend to gravitate towards affordable products that are just as effective as their high-end brand twins. Incorporate a simple natural look into your everyday routine using browns, pinks, and beiges.

## 6. Gemini ♊

You go through so many eyeshadow combos that your Instagram feed always keeps your followers guessing what you'll do next. Being short on time has never stopped you from perfecting your look. Because you don't swear by one brand, it's easy for you to switch things up from time to time.

## 7. Cancer ♋

A little shimmer never hurt anyone. As a water sign ruled by the moon, it's only natural that Cancers can't resist palettes that mimic the healthy glow of moonlight. This means that highlighter and eyeshadow in shades of blue, seafoam and lavender are your best friends.

## 8. Leo ♌

Everything about a Leo's haircare routine 💇 screams #hairgoals. Whether you love rocking a sexy bob or prefer your hair longer, using quality products at home or hitting up the top hair salon is a must.

## 9. Virgo ♍

Virgos know that beauty starts from the inside out 💚. Like the Capricorn, taking care of your skin is super important to you. Keep your face looking healthy and rejuvenated by using organic makeup.

## 10. Libra ♎

You have an extensive collection of lipsticks and eyeshadows. You also may have organized your makeup based on how you feel that day and what look you're going for. Get ready for your night out with your bae or besties by rocking bold, bright, and metallic colors 👊.

## 11. Scorpio ♏

Scorpios aren't afraid of rocking a darker, more [sultry look](https://makeup.allwomenstalk.com/amazing-sultry-makeup-tips/). Embrace fall head on by rocking a burgundy or dark brown lip. You can also show off your fierce personality with bold lashes 😉.

## 12. Sagittarius ♐

You travel everywhere which means that your makeup look for the day is pretty practical but chic. All you really need is a moisturizing gloss, face cleansing towelettes and travel sized facial creams.
