Easy Breezy Tips to Help You Feel Beautiful Everyday ...

Look on the Bright Side • Take Time to Apply Makeup and Style Your Hair • Exercise • Drink Enough Water • Use a Whitening Toothpaste • More ...

By Alicia

Every woman wants to feel beautiful. That’s a universal desire women across the world have. But not everyday is naturally a feel beautiful kind of day. But it can be with these 7 tips!

1. Look on the Bright Side

Before you discount this tip as a silly suggestion, give it some serious thought. Wouldn’t you agree that you feel happier when you look on the bright side of things? Having an optimistic outlook makes you feel happier and therefore, better all the way around. This includes how you feel about yourself. I know that I’ve never felt beautiful when I was looking for the negative in a situation and I’d say you haven’t either!

2. Take Time to Apply Makeup and Style Your Hair

No woman has to wear makeup to be beautiful. But if you normally wear makeup and style your hair then doing so is going to make you feel much prettier because this’s the way you’re accustomed to seeing yourself. I don’t feel fully awake until my makeup is on and my hair is styled and I’ve heard many other women attest to that, as well. While we love the results of makeup application and hair styling, we also feel better about ourselves when we’ve put effort in to look our best. It’s also a ritual that helps you feel ready to face the day.

3. Exercise

Exercise not only makes you feel beautiful but it makes you look that way, too. Okay, maybe not immediately after exercising when you’re all hot and sweaty. But exercise does pay off in giving you a gorgeous glow to your skin and a toned body. I feel better about myself and the condition of my body when I exercise regularly. Those benefits are a part of what makes you feel beautiful, too.

4. Drink Enough Water

It’s difficult to explain every way that water is good for your body so let’s just say it’s more beneficial than you can imagine. Drinking water is one of the healthiest decisions you could make and it also makes a difference in your appearance. Your skin will have a beautiful healthy luster to it that nothing else can give you. And as much as I love makeup, no cosmetic product can replace that. So drink up, buttercup!

5. Use a Whitening Toothpaste

Whitening your teeth will make you feel beautiful. There’s just something about knowing your teeth look lovely that boosts your confidence. You want to smile more to show off your pretty teeth. You feel better about yourself and that’s great! There’re many ways you can whiten your teeth from professional whitening to at home kits to whitening toothpastes.

6. Choose Clothes That’re Flattering for You

This one may not come easily to you but that’s okay. There’s lots of helpful information online about figuring out what clothing is flattering for your figure. There’s also great information on what colors work well for your skin tone, hair and eye color. This’s a fascinating subject to learn about. It’s empowering to learn these things so you know exactly what to look for when you go shopping.

7. Don’t Neglect Your Inner Beauty

This can make you feel more beautiful than any other item on this list. When you work on becoming a beautiful person on the inside then it’ll always radiate to the outside. People can always see inner beauty in another. If you’re wondering how you can grow in inner beauty, these’re a few words of advice on the subject. Practice kindness, have a grateful heart and be compassionate toward others.

8. Smile

It sounds so simple and natural but maybe you aren't smiling enough. A smile can go a long way when it comes to not only how others perceive you, but also how you perceive you. Flashing a smile even if you don't feel like it will instantly make you feel more beautiful!

9. Eat Right

Eating right will give you energy and power! You'll feel so much better when you eat a salad or some grilled chicken as opposed to how you feel when you scarf down a whole pizza. Fuel your body with the good stuff and you'll be amazed at how good eating healthy actually makes you feel physically and mentally! No more food guilt if you're eating the right stuff.

10. Think It to Feel It

If you want to feel beautiful then you need to believe you're beautiful. This sounds like something out of a self help book I know, but it's great advice. Stop the, "I'm not enough" attitude in your head and replace it with, "I am enough." Giving yourself a positive boost regularly will help others see you that way because you'll start believing you're great.

These’er 10 tips to help you feel beautiful every day. Which ones are you going to work on? I’d love to hear from you!
