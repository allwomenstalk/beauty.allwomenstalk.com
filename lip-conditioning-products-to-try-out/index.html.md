# 7 Lip Conditioning Products to Try out ...

A** Lip Conditioner** is a must-try if you’re after the perfect pout. A good lip conditioner can have many benefits. These include moisturising, restorative, and protective properties. A **lip conditioner** with SPF properties is ideal for the summer months for protecting lips, while a conditioner with an intensive formula can help restore lips in the winter months. **Lip conditioners** also help create the perfect base for lipsticks. If you’re in the market for a **lip conditioner**, then take a look at the following products.

This lip conditioner contains a blend of antioxidants and nutrients. It functions to hydrate, protect, and repair dry lips. When it comes to beauty products, my favourites are the ones that multitask. This lip conditioner also doubles as a lipstick base.

## 2. M•a•C Lip Conditioner

![M•a•C Lip Conditioner](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/o7/xc/grk2c32254c936a5e91ab814185080_163x245.jpg)

**Price:** $15.00 at [shop.nordstrom.com](http://shop.nordstrom.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
The big drawcard for this lip conditioner is its hydrating properties. This product has an emollient design, meaning that it softens the skin. It contains almond oil, shea butter, and vitamins A and E to name a few. You can use this conditioner by itself or under lipstick. This conditioner is also available in stick form.

## 3. Ole Henriksen Fresh Lips SPF 15

**Price:** $15.00 at [sephora.com](http://www.sephora.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
This lip conditioner also comes with SPF 15 protection. It aims to nourish dry, chapped, and peeling lips. The active ingredients in this product include vitamin E, camphor, cocoa butter, and menthol. This product also claims to have lip plumping properties.

## 4. Joe Malone Vitamin E Lip Conditioner SPF 15

![Joe Malone Vitamin E Lip Conditioner SPF 15](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/dr/oy/lxas7kxc54c936a76a208258569968_163x243.jpg)

At $25, this lip conditioner is a bit of a splurge. The product is enriched with vitamin E and enhanced with UVA/UVB protection. It also contains honey which acts as a natural emollient as well as adding a hint of flavour to the formula. If you’re out to treat yourself, then this lip conditioner is a must.

## 5. Estee Lauder Lip Conditioner SPF 15

**Price:** $16.50 at [shop.nordstrom.com](http://shop.nordstrom.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
This lip conditioner comes in stylish gold packaging. It’s one of those beauty items that are instantly covetable. Not only does this product look good, but it also has a rich hydrating formula. It contains vitamins C and E, shea butter, and also offers SPF protection.

## 6. Jack Black Intense Therapy Lip Balms

![Jack Black Intense Therapy Lip Balms](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/beauty/2011/12/P12573_hero_250x250.jpg)

At only $10 for two products, this set of lip balms is great value for money. Each product contains conditioning ingredients and antioxidants. They also offer broad spectrum UVA/UVB sun protection. This set is perfect for keeping one balm in your handbag and the other at home.

## 7. Shiseido the Skincare Protective Lip Conditioner

_ **Price:** $22.50 at barneys.com_
This lip conditioner is intended to be used as a daily treatment. It has protective properties and provides smoothing and moisturising benefits. It can be used alone or as a lipstick base.

Keep in mind that if you’re going to be spending a lot of time outdoors, a lip conditioner that offers UVA/UVB protection is worthwhile considering. Like the rest of our skin, lips can also be damaged by the sun’s rays, and a lip conditioner is a great way to both help restore and protect lips in the future.
