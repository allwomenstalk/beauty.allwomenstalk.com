# How to Pose like You're a Celeb on the Red Carpet ...

You might not get the chance to walk down the red carpet, but you should still learn to pose like you are. It'll help you up your selfie game!

Here's the lowdown: If you're taking a picture while sitting, make sure you cross your legs, rest an arm over them, and push yourself forward a bit. Meanwhile, if you're taking a headshot, you should take a deep breath and smile as you exhale.

If you take a full body shot instead, then you should bend one knee and pop your hip out. You can even take a candid photo by crossing your arms and pretending you have no idea a camera is in the area.
