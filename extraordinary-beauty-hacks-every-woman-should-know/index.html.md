# 5 Extraordinary Beauty Hacks Every Woman Should Know ...

Woman love to find ways to create a shortcut to a beautiful skin. You are always looking for some tricks that will make your hair look glossier and your skin look smoother. Well, your wait is over. Here you will find everything that you need to know. These proven hacks will leave you amazed! Scroll to check them out!

## 1. Green Tea is Our Best Friend

You may have heard how the entire world is switching to green tea. It is a magical [detox drink](https://food.allwomenstalk.com/detox-drinks-thatll-make-you-feel-better-than-ever/) and is great for your body. It helps your body to function well, and aids in digestion. Dietitians recommend drinking green tea 15 minutes after eating a meal. But did you know that you can use green tea as an ingredient for a face pack instead of drinking? Doing so will reduce swelling around your eyes. Frozen green tea bags will do wonders for getting rid of stubborn dark circles and patches.

## 2. Coconut Oil Works Miracles

When you were a teenager, your mother always told you about the great benefits of oiling your hair and keeping your hair healthier. We often cause damage to our hair by straightening it, applying chemicals, and a lot more. Here is a quick solution to get healthy hair back again! Gently massage your hair with some coconut oil ten minutes prior to washing. Get some lustrous locks by choosing this hack!

## 3. Forget Threading Your Eyebrows, Use Tweezers

We all know how much we work on our eyebrows. When holidays are on, we tend to grow our eyebrows to their full growth in order to get a perfect shape. Eyebrows are one the important elements in a woman’s beauty. You can shape and style your eyebrows by using tweezers. They performs the same job and help you attain a curvy brow.

## 4. Conduct a Patch Test before Applying Harmful Chemicals

Let be your skin or your hair, they have all been gone through rough chemicals that can result in reactions. Before using a hair removal cream, make sure to try a patch first to prevent some severe reaction. Hair color contains paraphenylenediamine that causes allergy to some people. Apply a patch of color behind your ear and wait for at least 6 hours to check if your skin is allergic to the product or not.

## 5. Use Natural Oils on Your Skin

Stop using creams and moisturizers, it is time to go natural. Natural oils like jojoba, almond, coconut, etc. do not contain chemicals. They are safe to use. If you have dry skin, going natural is the right decision to take. Winters demand a lot of oiling to your skin to protect it from external damage.
