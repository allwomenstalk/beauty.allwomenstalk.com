# A Beginners Guide to Tweezing Your Brows ...

If you're new to tweezing, you might be feeling a little bit overwhelmed. That's totally understandable, since it can be daunting to figure out which brow hairs to pluck and which should stay where they are. I've gone to the experts and put together this step by step guide to help you go from overgrown to perfectly polished with ease.

## 1. Choose the Perfect Tweezers

![Choose the Perfect Tweezers](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wy/fm/uhohbzuc564dffafba09c354524472_600x900.jpg)

Choose very sharp, slanted tweezers because they will do the best job for you.

## 2. Take a Shower

![Take a Shower](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/sq/cc/ldh720to5669e8d1d5a3a594774527_600x658.jpg)

According to experts, warm water will soften your hairs, making them much easier to pluck.

## 3. Use a Numbing Gel

![Use a Numbing Gel](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/s9/rc/fa2ks01z564e02c454cb0753967749_600x811.jpg)

Does tweezing hurt so much that you dread it? Dab on a bit of numbing gel to keep things from being too painful.

## 4. Use a Regular Mirror, Not a Magnifying One

![Use a Regular Mirror, Not a Magnifying One](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ve/h6/vl95kw635669e94fcb8c4180466091_600x721.jpg)

A magnifying mirror can make you think you need to pluck more hairs than you do. Use a regular mirror to get perfect brows.

## 5. Figure out Your Starting Points

![Figure out Your Starting Points](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/oy/mm/w4b3mxtu564dfbe7ebea2052533438_600x880.jpg)

Use the bottom of your tweezers and line them up from the side of your nose. This is where your brows should start. Check out line #1 in the image.

## 6. Mark Your Ending Points

![Mark Your Ending Points](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wf/zd/kx8iws61564dfd26cb4a5810795473_600x800.jpg)

Now, leave your tweezers next to your nostril and extend it diagonally to the outer corner of your eye. This is where your brow should end.

## 7. Determine Where Your Arch Should Be

![Determine Where Your Arch Should Be](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/l6/ww/hx3e0cjx5669e99aa6943716309811_600x856.jpg)

Your arch should be just at the outer edge of your iris, right at your brow bone.

## 8. Decide on How Thick to Keep Your Brows

![Decide on How Thick to Keep Your Brows](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/xt/pm/jo5qh91p564dfd9c6eb09975717116_600x800.jpg)

To do this, draw a line along the bottom edge of your brow, letting you see where strays below it are lingering.

## 9. Shape Your Arch

![Shape Your Arch](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/i8/i0/xq6fc4285669e9ca0b824642540523_580x738.jpg)

Use your tweezers to remove a row of hair along the bottom of where your arch should be.

## 10. Continue Row by Row in Your Arch

![Continue Row by Row in Your Arch](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/pi/wm/mt8xio2q5669e9df122e1523743360_600x900.jpg)

Continue row by row, removing hairs until you reach the lien you drew on your brow in step #4. Your arch is done!

## 11. Remove the Other Hairs below Your Line

![Remove the Other Hairs below Your Line](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ev/so/j7cj60mu564e027f62a4d455222655_600x700.jpg)

Now that you've shaped your arch, carefully pluck the rest of your hairs that fall outside the line you drew.

## 12. Use the Three Hairs Rule

![Use the Three Hairs Rule](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/zq/gj/opyatcw95669ea689f8c5177445620_576x1024.jpg)

Experts suggest plucking three hairs, then stepping back to see the results. If you need to, keep going, three hairs at a time. If not, you're done.

## 13. Use Hydrocortisone Cream

![Use Hydrocortisone Cream](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/c2/zr/fbdfsuhm5669eb9704963876714786_600x900.jpg)

Prevent redness and irritation after tweezing by dabbing on some hydrocortisone cream.

## 14. Style Your Brows

![Style Your Brows](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/u5/sh/wr2wezx35669ec122eb54820648348_600x805.jpg)

Use a comb and style your brows to make sure things look like they should. Tweeze any stray hairs you come across.

## 15. Fill Them in

![Fill Them in](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/e0/mw/bu5zerkp564e046a3d3df801325351_600x900.jpg)

Plucked too many hairs? Have a thin spot? Use brow filler to event things out.

## 16. Wait Three Weeks to Tweeze Again

![Wait Three Weeks to Tweeze Again](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wa/zi/nln7vjjc564e04a9e45d3914057967_600x900.jpg)

It might sound like a long time, but experts say this gives you a chance to see your brows natural growth patterns, which will make it easier to determine the shape of your brows next time you pluck.

Are you ready to have the best brows ever now?
