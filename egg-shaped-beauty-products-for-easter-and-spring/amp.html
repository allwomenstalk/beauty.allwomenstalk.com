9 Egg-Shaped Beauty Products for Easter and Spring ...

The Ped Egg • Eos Lip Balm • Tony Moly Egg Pore Silky Smooth Balm • Beauty Blender Makeup Sponge • Holika Holika Lazy and Easy Smooth Egg Peeling Gel • More ...

By Treva

It's surprising how many great egg-shaped beauty products there are. There's no better time than springtime to try some of them out, since you might need to do a little spring cleaning by replacing your winter beauty products. It's just a shame that the Easter bunny only carries chocolate eggs -- it would be pretty awesome if he filled baskets with great, calorie-free egg-shaped beauty products like these:

1. The Ped Egg

This is one of the best egg-shaped beauty products for spring because it will help you get your feet ready for sexy sandal season. It's also the perfect product to make you put down that chocolate egg -- you might lose your appetite while grating your foot like a block of cheese. The Ped Egg might be a little gross, but it's one of the most effective "As Seen on TV" products you'll ever buy. You also don't have to order it from TV since it's now sold at drugstores like Walgreens.
Buy at amazon.com

2. Eos Lip Balm

Yes, these balms aren't perfect little eggs, but they are pretty amazing. They're inexpensive, and you shouldn't have any problem finding them at your local drugstore. The spherical shape of this lip balm makes it more fun to apply, and it comes in yummy flavors like Summer Fruit, Strawberry Sorbet, and Sweet Mint. The balms make perfect gifts for teen or tween daughters who are too old for Easter egg hunting. Just put a few different flavors in a small Easter egg basket.
Buy at walmart.com

3. Tony Moly Egg Pore Silky Smooth Balm

Here's another great Asian beauty product. Korean company Tony Moly makes a few different products that come in incredibly cute packaging like that pictured above. This golden egg contains a makeup primer that soothes skin with plant extracts like camellia flower. The primer has a weird, putty-like texture, but it sinks into the skin as you massage it in. It reduces the appearance of pores and makes makeup application easier by making it glide on. Other Egg Pore products include the Tightening Pack and the Blackhead Out Oil Gel.
Buy at yesstyle.com

4. Beauty Blender Makeup Sponge

This popular makeup sponge is another product that makes makeup application easier. The pretty pink sponge has a pointy end that's perfect for applying makeup on tough spots like the corners of the nose and around the eyes. It creates a flawless finish, and you can change its texture by getting it wet. You can purchase a variety of Beauty Blender products at Sephora.
Buy at sephora.com

5. Holika Holika Lazy and Easy Smooth Egg Peeling Gel

Korean beauty products can be cute as well as highly effective. This is so easy to use. Just apply a small amount to dry, clean skin and massage until dead skin appears to roll off. Rinse gently away. This peeling gel contains albumen and it creates a soft, smooth complexion by removing dead skin cells, evening out the pores and improving elasticity.
Buy at amazon.com

6. Lady Gaga Fame Perfume

How cool is it that this perfume is black? It makes me think of a dragon egg. The fragrance is fittingly a dark, strong scent that's not too sweet (it's like the anti-Britney Spears scent). It features a blend of incense, apricot, belladonna, jasmine, orchidea, and honey.

Looking like a prototype droid from Star Wars, this ionic steamer cleans out your pores, opens your sinuses and clears congestion, all in the comfort of your own home - great for acne-prone skin. After 10 minutes your complexion has a warm, healthy glow and feels well-hydrated.

Frequently Asked Questions

What are some popular egg-shaped beauty products for Easter?
Oh, there are so many fun options! Brands often release special edition skincare and makeup items in egg shapes around Easter. You can find anything from lip balms to face masks all nicely tucked away in adorable egg-shaped packaging.
Why are egg-shaped beauty products popular during Easter and Spring?
Well, Easter and Spring are all about fresh starts and new beginnings, and the egg symbolizes rebirth and renewal. Plus, who can resist those cute egg designs? They just add a cheerful vibe to your beauty routine!
Are egg-shaped beauty products just about the packaging?
Not at all! While the packaging is super cute, the products inside are usually just as amazing. Many brands put a lot of effort into creating high-quality skincare and makeup products specifically for these special editions.
Where can I buy egg-shaped beauty products?
You can find them in a variety of places! Many beauty brands release these special products on their own websites, and you can also find them at major retailers like Sephora, Ulta, or even department stores. Don’t forget to check out Amazon too!
What kinds of beauty products are commonly sold in egg-shaped packaging?
You’ll find a mix of everything! Lip balms, face masks, bath bombs, and even small makeup palettes can come in egg-shaped packaging. It’s a fun way to celebrate the season while also adding some great new products to your beauty collection.
