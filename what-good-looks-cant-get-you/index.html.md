# Proof Positive That Looks Arent Everything ...

There are many **reasons why looks don't matter** but a lot of us assume that beautiful people have easy lives, because they can flirt their way to the top. While there are definitely perks to being pretty, it can't actually get you everything you want. There are plenty of things that you have to work hard for in life in order to achieve. The world isn't that unfair, because there are **reasons why looks don't matter** and here are a few things that good looks could never get you:

## 1. A Job You'll Keep

![person, costume, I'm, gonna, get,](https://img.allw.mn/content/u9/pd/zf5l15tu579cebf0e207f020337059_490x252.gif)

If an interviewer is biased, a model might have more luck getting a job than an average looking girl. However, she won't be able to keep the job for long if she doesn't know what she's doing. Even if her good looks somehow got her the job, her hard work is the only thing that will help her keep it. No matter how pretty a person is, they aren't going to continue to get paid if they don't do a job well done.

## 2. Respect

![person, respect, you, respecting,](https://img.allw.mn/content/ce/fq/axa07y54579cebf557efc766105361_580x327.gif)

Respect is something you earn. Even though men might be more polite to pretty women, it doesn't mean that they respect them. In order to earn respect, you need to treat others with respect. A gorgeous gal who walks all over her friends and treats men like garbage won't be respected by her peers.

## 3. A Real Relationship

![IT'S, NOT, GONNA, EASY, IT'S,](https://img.allw.mn/content/fj/tn/us2xg5q0579cebf18ab8c384985009_500x200.gif)

Pretty women have to be careful when it comes to relationships, because a man might only be interested in them for their looks. The only way for her to enter a real, meaningful relationship is to have a good personality to go along with her looks. If she's just a pretty face, then she won't be considered marriage material by most men.

## 4. Genuine Friends

![person, human action, contact sport, arm, combat sport,](https://img.allw.mn/content/tp/o0/xq6jgnmf579cebf131984273513958_500x280.gif)

Real friends don't care if you're pretty or not. All they care about is your personality. They want to have a good time with you, and in order to do that, you have to be fun. You also have to be someone that they can trust. Otherwise, you'll be stuck as an acquaintance and never reach BFF status.

## 5. A Long Life

![person, cartoon, singing, anime,](https://img.allw.mn/content/fc/yg/zmzjbtun579cebf15d62b423307116_500x301.gif)

It's possible for a woman to be skinny without being healthy. She might be naturally thin, even though she never exercises, always smokes, and drinks often. If that's the case, then she's not guaranteed to live a long, happy life. If she wants to remain healthy, she has to workout and take care of her body, just like any other woman does.

## 6. Peace of Mind

![hair, person, hairstyle, Being, mean,](https://img.allw.mn/content/w1/o3/r18qbl26579cebf17dfe8337233245_500x278.gif)

There are plenty of pretty people who are philanthropists. However, there are also pretty people who are bad seeds. Anyone who's part of the latter category won't have peace of mind. They won't be able to look in the mirror and like what they see, even if their exterior is flawless.

## 7. True Happiness

![person, facial expression, blond, profession, singing,](https://img.allw.mn/content/u0/rb/w98yvv3r579cecdde6155172235803_640x314.gif)

Your good looks might be able to get you out of a speeding ticket or get you backstage to meet your favorite band members. While those little things would certainly brighten your day, your looks can't give you genuine happiness in the long run. That's something that you have to search and fight for.

Your looks aren't a one way ticket to paradise. Of course, there's nothing wrong with being beautiful! You just need to have a good personality to go along with those good looks. Have you ever gotten special treatment, because of the way you looked?
