# The Best Toothpaste Types to Use for a Great Smile ...

Every year, there are many, many new toothpastes on the market. How are you supposed to choose the best toothpaste? Most are effective but there are a few general guidelines to follow.

What is the first thing a person notices about you? You guessed it. Your smile.

The following YouTube video shows just how mamy toothpastes there are to choose from!

CBS
Published on Feb 24, 2011

For review:

## 1. Flouride

Flouride is the main ingredient in the best toothpastes.

## 2. ADA

Look for the American Dental Association seal. The seal shows that the toothpaste passed all of the toothpaste trials.

## 3. Frequency

You should brush your teeth at least twice each day.

## 4. Baking Soda

If using a baking soda based toothpaste, do not use as often as is too abrasive.

Again, you only need flouride and the ADA seal for twice a day usage for that gorgeous smile!
