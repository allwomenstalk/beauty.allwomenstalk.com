The result is a smoother, more luminous appearance that reflects light more effectively, helping the eye area look healthier and more energized.

Firmness and elasticity are also key pillars of the formula. This advanced pairing works to improve the appearance of elasticity, smooth the look of fine lines, and support healthier-looking skin. What distinguishes the First Care Activating Eye Serum is its **comprehensive approach to dark circles**, one of the most common and notoriously difficult eye-area concerns. Rather than treating dark circles as a single issue, Sulwhasoo targets the three primary visible contributors: **shadow, inflammation, and pigmentation.**

For **shadow-related dark circles**, often associated with volume loss and structural changes that occur with age, the formula focuses on improving firmness and elasticity. By supporting a more lifted appearance, the serum helps minimize the shadows that can create a tired or hollow look beneath the eyes.

For **inflammation-related discoloration**, which can contribute to puffiness and a fatigued appearance, the serum works to support circulation and reduce visible swelling. The inclusion of the Ginseng Caffeine™ Complex helps address these concerns, creating a smoother, more refreshed eye contour.

The texture is designed with **daily use in mind**, offering a lightweight yet nourishing experience that absorbs comfortably into the skin.
