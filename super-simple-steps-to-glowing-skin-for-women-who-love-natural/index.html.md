# 3 Super Simple  Steps to Glowing Skin for Women Who Love Natural ...

Everyone has a different skin type therefore finding a skincare routine that will work for you is difficult. This skincare routine is only 3 steps and is all natural, which means it will work on the most sensitive skin.

## 1. Castile Soap

![eyebrow, face, cheek, lip, red,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/by/j7/fxanyj8v5946748952f48200033398_600x600.jpg)

Castile soap is amazing because it gets rid of all dirt in the skin and it is also natural and free of toxins, doesn’t encourage super bacteria, lathers easily, and environmentally friendly. Castile soap doesn’t dry out the skin and is also extremely affordable, also did I mention, it’s all natural.

## 2. Witch Hazel

![eyebrow, face, hair, cheek, nose,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/u3/cg/gktzhqem594674a3d8a94533418730_600x750.jpg)

Witch hazel is a natural astringent that removes excess oil from the skin. It can also be used as a toner and fades bruises and blemishes from the face and also other areas of the body. To make sure that the witch hazel doesn’t dry out the skin, make sure you purchase the one, which is alcohol free.

## 3. Coconut Oil

![hair, face, person, black hair, eyebrow,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/w4/s5/nb4mbktn59467501bcdb7866722663_600x600.jpg)

Coconut oil is naturally antibacterial, antifungal and moisturizing. By applying a thin layer at night before bed, your skin will be hydrated and glowing by morning. Coconut oil is also thin enough that it won’t clog your pores like some other oils may do. When looking for coconut oil make sure it is described as “virgin,” “cold pressed,” or “raw.”
