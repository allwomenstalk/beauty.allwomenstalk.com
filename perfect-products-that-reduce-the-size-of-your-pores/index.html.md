# 7 Perfect Products That Reduce the Size of Your Pores ...

There are lots of potential products that reduce the size of your pores, but which ones actually work? Pores don’t have muscles so they don’t really open or close, but it is possible to minimize the appearance of large pores. Since there are a dizzying amount of products that promise to shrink your pores, in both drugstores and high end beauty stores; I decided to hunt down the best of the best. Check out some of these highly rated products that reduce the size of your pores and minimize those pores now!

## 1. Bare Minerals Pure Transformation Night Treatment

![Bare Minerals Pure Transformation Night Treatment](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/kx/z3/tr3s6gs3_600x465.jpg)

One of the newer products that reduce the size of your pores that I discovered is Bare Minerals’ Pure Transformation Night Treatment. It might feel weird applying a powder to your face at night, but it’s a replenishing treatment that helps reduce the size of pores and the look of fine lines, encourage faster cell turnover and leave your skin glowing. It comes in four shades and they won’t come off on your pillow! Avaialbe at bareescentuals.com.

## 2. Dr. Brandt Skincare Pores No More Pore Refiner

![Dr. Brandt Skincare Pores No More Pore Refiner](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/0l/h8/a8g3xoce_450x440.jpg)

This cult favorite is a beauty essential for anyone looking to minimize pores. Apply this primer all over your face to create a barrier to prevent your makeup from clogging pores. It’s also a key beauty product for anyone looking to blur the appearance of fine lines, excess shine and other imperfections. You can even use this on top of makeup to keep skin shine-free and even. Buy at Sephora.com.

## 3. Benefit the Porefessional

![Benefit the Porefessional](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/rg/ly/pdwdxk3f_600x522.jpg)

As you can probably tell, primers are great for minimizing the appearance of pores. The Porefessional is one of the more highly rated and adored primers that can be worn under makeup or alone, to decrease the appearance of fine lines and wrinkles, smooth and even out skin and blur large pores. Oil-free and translucent so it works for all skin tones. Buy from Ulta.com.

## 4. Maybelline Baby Skin Instant Pore Eraser

![Maybelline Baby Skin Instant Pore Eraser](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/nl/yv/nvjy36qq_400x400.jpg)

It seems like people either love or hate this next product. Some feel that it’s a drugstore dupe for The Porefessional while others think it’s worthless. Either way, the price is nice and I like that drugstores are carrying products that are similar to higher end brands. This product can be worn alone or under makeup for visibly smoother skin. Available at Ulta.com.

## 5. Clinique Pore Refining Solutions Instant Perfector

![Clinique Pore Refining Solutions Instant Perfector](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/u2/ar/0bhh9606_450x434.jpg)

Clinique’s Instant Perfector is a really convenient product that can be used alone, and under or over makeup for touch-ups throughout the day. It comes in three flattering shades and lasts up to eight hours so you won’t have to worry about visible pores, imperfections or shine while you’re busy going about your day. Suitable for all skin types and resists sweat and humidity. Buy from Sephora.com.

## 6. Too Faced Primed and Poreless Powder

![Too Faced Primed and Poreless Powder](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/o1/p6/bl2i1jpr_450x434.jpg)

If liquid primers aren’t your thing, Too Faced’s Primed and Poreless Powder is a fantastic alternative. You can lightly dust this powder on your bare face and both under or over makeup to keep skin perfectly smooth, shine-free and blur out imperfections like fine lines and enlarged pores. Available at Sephora.com.

## 7. Neutrogena Pore Refining Toner, Alpha and Beta Hydroxy Formula

![Neutrogena Pore Refining Toner, Alpha and Beta Hydroxy Formula](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/0f/sr/n7xm941w_500x450.jpg)

Toners can be just as effective at minimizing the appearance of pores as primers and powders. Neutrogena’s best-selling toner contains an alpha and beta hydroxyl formula to reduce the appearance of pores, get rid of dead skin cells and leave skin feeling smooth, soft and revitalized. What a great way to refresh your skin after a warm day! Find it at Walmart.com.

If you’re looking for help to minimize the appearance of pores, these beauty products are not to be missed.
