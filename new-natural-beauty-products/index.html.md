# 7 Natural Beauty Products Youll Love ...

I love finding new natural beauty products to try. Natural beauty products are key to avoiding toxic chemicals that steal your beauty, hurt the planet, and even hurt animals, believe it or not. Natural products don’t have to be expensive either. Here are my favorite natural beauty products that are new on the market this year. I’ve used all of these amazing products and highly recommend them, so be sure to pick up a few next time you're in search of a great, natural beauty product. You can find many at your local drugstore, and the others are available online.

## 1. Physician’s Formula BB Powder and Cream

![Physician’s Formula BB Powder and Cream](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/beauty/2013/05/1_physicians-formula-bb-powder-and-cream_517x327.jpg)

Physician’s Formula makes a large variety of effective, affordable, natural beauty products. Now the line has one of the most popular cosmetic products on the market: BB powder and cream. BB stands for beauty balm, and is hot right now in terms of beauty products. BB cream is great for coverage, balancing skin colors and uneven levels, covering blemishes, hiding wrinkles and for providing long lasting coverage. Physician’s Formula’s SPF BB cream foundation and powder are my two favorite products in this line. I love the concealer as well.

## 2. Mineral Fusion Mineral Beauty Balm

![Mineral Fusion Mineral Beauty Balm](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/beauty/2013/05/2_mineral-fusion-mineral-beauty-balm_600x600.jpg)

The popular brand Mineral Fusion now offers a new BB product as well. This beauty balm is a little pricier than others, but also one of the best rated on the market. It is hypo-allergenic, fragrance-free, is not tested on animals, and contains only natural ingredients. It won’t irritate your skin and can give skin a natural glow, without causing shine. It also protects your skin from sun damage and harmful UV rays.

## 3. Aubrey Organics Calming Skin Therapy Toner

![Aubrey Organics Calming Skin Therapy Toner](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/beauty/2013/05/3_aubrey-organics-calming-skin-therapy-toner_600x600.jpg)

Aubrey Organics is one of the most popular vegan companies on the market to produce quality skincare. Their new Calming Skin Therapy Toner is highly sought after with good reason. For starters, it can reduce redness and inflammation, provide moisture to the skin and it is hypoallergenic, so it won’t irritate your skin. This calming toner would be great to use after a day in the sun, since it contains aloe and sea aster, provides you with 8 hours of hydration, only contains organic ingredients, has no preservatives, and is of course, vegan. It also makes a great moisturizer and softens the skin, without making it greasy. For a reasonable price, definitely give this product a try!

## 4. The Body Shop Seaweed Collection

![The Body Shop Seaweed Collection](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/beauty/2013/05/4_the-body-shop-seaweed-collection_450x418.jpg)

The Body Shop is extremely popular for providing consumers with great beauty products at an affordable price. I specifically like that their products are natural and contain less junk than other brands out there. They have almost any beauty product you could want, and one of their newest collections is the Seaweed Collection. My favorite product is the seaweed cleansing wash, which is a mild, oil-free and soap-free foaming cleansing gel that deep cleans the skin. It opens up pores and gives your face a really clean, fresh feel. It rinses off excess oils, make-up and impurities to leave your skin fresh, clear and hydrated. The Seaweed collection line also features a moisturizer, night cream, SPF cream and more, so check it out!

## 5. Burt’s Bees Lip Shine

![Burt’s Bees Lip Shine](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/beauty/2013/05/5_burts-bees-lip-shine_230x321.jpg)

Burt’s Bees is one of my favorite brands for relying on an all-natural yet effective beauty product. The new lip shine product out by the company is absolutely awesome. First, it isn’t goopy or sticky, and the flavors all smell great! It also doesn’t taste funny, and keeps my lips moisturized, shiny and colorful for hours. The new product is also 100% natural, comes in 6 varieties and is extremely affordable. What’s not to love, right?

## 6. Stila All Day 10-in-1 HD Beauty Balm with SPF 30

![Stila All Day 10-in-1 HD Beauty Balm with SPF 30](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/beauty/2013/05/6_stila-all-day-10-in-1-hd-beauty-balm-with-spf-30_335x521.jpg)

Stila is one of my favorite natural brands that are also vegan friendly. Stila is known for its extremely effective and luxurious beauty products. The brand is popular with celebs and everyday women for being a great beauty product provider. Stila has just released a new beauty balm cream this year that reduces skin inflammation, builds collagen, protects skin from damage, fights blemishes, balances skin tones, gives a natural glow, provides thorough coverage and won’t cause clogged pores. The product is oil-free and paraben-free, along with cruelty-free. A bronzing version and illuminating version are also available, along with the basic one.

## 7. Stila Color Balm Lipstick

![Stila Color Balm Lipstick](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/beauty/2013/05/7_stila-color-balm-lipstick_335x403.jpg)

I’m in love with this new product by Stila! It is rich in beautiful hues that keep your lips moisturized, colored and shiny all day long, plus it contains many nutrients and ingredients to nourish the lips as well. It also comes in a great variety of colors, and contains a mirror on the product so that you can easily apply it on-the-go. My favorite thing about this product is it contains natural peppermint oil that not only freshens your breath, but also soothes your lips, aids as an anti-septic, and enhances your breath. Now you don't even need a stick of gum for that after-dinner refreshment. Just put on some of this gorgeous lipstick!
