# 10 Pros of a Beauty Sleep ...

Beauty Sleep is often treated as a myth, but not only does it really exists, it's also really necessary. Getting enough sleep is important for your health in general. You simply can't function when you don't get enough rest, mentally, physically, or emotionally. And yes, beauty sleep does keep you looking young, refreshed, and radiant. Don't believe it? Just check out the main benefits and pros of beauty sleep, and then head to bed!

## 1. Bright Eyes

Many people don't think about their eyes when they're brushing off beauty sleep as a myth. I don't mean dark circles, that's another point altogether. I mean, when you get the sleep you need, your eyes are brighter, the whites are clearer, and they won't be bloodshot. You'll automatically look more vivacious!

## 2. A Gorgeous Complexion – a Huge Benefit of Beauty Sleep

Getting enough sleep is also really important for your complexion. I won't say that too little sleep leads to acne all by itself, but it can be a contributing factor. Being well rested rejuvenates your skin, and if you want a radiant complexion, it's what you need.

## 3. A Better Memory

Beauty sleep also improves your cognition skills. If you've ever gone a long time without sleep, you know how hard it is to focus on anything. If you want improved concentration, a better memory, and the ability to just think clearly, plenty of sleep is necessary.

## 4. Smoother Skin

In addition to improving your complexion, the rejuvenation factor of good sleep leads to smoother skin as well. It won't sag or bag or lose so much of its elasticity. That might seem like a huge deal now, but it will be when you get a little older.

## 5. Quicker Reflexes

Just like beauty sleep gives you a better memory, it improves your reflexes, too. Without enough sleep, you can be sluggish and clumsy, so it's even hard to get through your daily life. When you're well rested, however, you'll find you have more flexibility and better coordination.

## 6. A Strong Metabolism – the Most Important Aspect of Beauty Sleep

The sleep you get can even help you lose weight. Why? Because it's essential to your metabolism. People who don't get enough sleep generally suffer from a decreased metabolism, which not only leads to sluggish energy but also makes it harder to burn calories.

## 7. Beautiful Hair

Even your hair benefits from enough beauty sleep. Without it, those luscious locks will soon turn dull, dry, and totally without luster. Because yes, even the follicles on your head need rest.

## 8. Less Stress

Stress, of course, exacerbates all of these conditions. Without sleep, you'll be far more stressed out than you would be otherwise. Everything will make you tense, whereas a good night's sleep gives you everything you need to deal with stressful situations. You can let them roll off your back much more easily.

## 9. No Dark Circles

Now let's talk about dark circles. Beauty sleep does indeed battle these bruised beauties. After all, a lack of sleep is one of the biggest causes of dark circles under your eyes. Give your eyes and your skin the rest they need, and you'll be amazed at the results.

## 10. A More Centered Life

Finally, getting enough sleep just improves life in general. You're more centered, you feel better, you look better, you react better. I know it's not easy to get the required hours of sleep every night, but you should always try.

Even though a lot of people joke about getting beauty sleep, we'd all be better off if we got more of it. Beauty sleep rejuvenates, it refreshes, and it helps you recuperate from bad days, tense occasions, and stressful situations. You don't have to do anything special to get your beauty sleep, beyond making sure that you can get at least six or seven hours of sleep a night, and don't pass up a nap if you have the time and the inclination. Now, do you have any tips to make getting to sleep easier?
