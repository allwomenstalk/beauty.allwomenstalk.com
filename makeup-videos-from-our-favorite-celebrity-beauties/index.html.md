# Makeup Videos from Our Favorite Celebrity Beauties ...

I usually assume that A-list stars and beauty icons have a whole entourage getting them ready every time they venture out but these **makeup videos from celebrity beauties** prove otherwise. I was browsing YouTube and found one, so decided to investigate who else had shared some great tips. I've got here an awesome collection of beauty and **makeup videos from celebs** to inspire and wow you.

## 1. Rihanna's Epic 10-Minute Guide to Going out Makeup

Beautiful before a drop of foundation even goes near her glowing skin, Rihanna is completely infectious as she shows - most expertly to give her her dues - how to apply a great look for going out in just 10 minutes.

## 2. Kendall Jenner Shares Her Morning Beauty Routine

No matter how you feel about the Kardashian/Jenner clan, you have to admit they do know a thing or two about beauty and makeup. Here she demonstrates with prowess how a supermodel goes about her morning beauty/makeup routine.

## 3. Miranda Kerr Applies Her Glowing Wedding Day Makeup

If you need some tips for your wedding day makeup, you can't go wrong with Miranda Kerr. She looked absolutely stunning in her Dior gown and perfectly made up face.

## 4. Drew Barrymore’s Genius Guide to on-the-Go Beauty

I know many of you reading this are busy women who really appreciate tips and tricks to save time. Drew Barrymore might be an A-Lister but she has the same problem. Watch as she shares her secrets to getting ready in a hurry.

## 5. Hailey Baldwin’s 5-Step Guide to Faking a California Glow

So summer is fast approaching in the Western Hemisphere and no doubt thoughts are turning to getting that sun-kissed glow. Learn how to fake it with model Hailey Baldwin.

## 6. Priyanka Chopra Reveals 3 All-Natural, DIY Skin Secrets

Indian skincare tips are equally suitable for other skin types, especially when natural products are used. Stunning beauty Priyanka shares her favorite skincare secrets.

## 7. Bella Hadid’s Jet-Lag Beauty Survival Guide

I already mentioned summer, so if you're traveling for vacation, there's the chance of dreaded jet lag and the number that can do on your skin. Try Bella Hadid's jet-lag beauty survival guide.

## 8. Rita Ora Does Day-to-Night Bombshell Beauty

How doesn't need to know how to transform their day makeup into night makeup with little effort? Watch as Rita Ora manages it with a few additons and a few strokes.

## 9. Kendall Jenner Shows 3 Ways to Craft Smoky Eyes

A list of makeup videos wouldn't be complete without a smoky eye tutorial. I know I've already featured Kendall Jenner but as she features 3 different smoky eye looks in one video, it's a must-see.
