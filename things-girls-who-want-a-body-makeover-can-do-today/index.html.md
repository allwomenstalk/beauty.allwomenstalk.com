# 7 Things Girls Who Want a Body Makeover Can do Today ...

**Body Makeover** dreams are like the career goals we had in college-audacious, inspiring, and occasionally intimidating. You see, achieving that perfect body isn't too different from acing a term paper; it involves strategy, dedication, and, most importantly, a positive mindset. As someone who spent a good chunk of 2023 experimenting with various fitness hacks, I'm here to share some actionable tips for 2024! Spoiler alert: unlike your last-minute cramming sessions, these tips won't leave you guzzling midnight coffee.

So if you're someone who often finds yourself entangled in the web of endless fitness trends, consider this your crash course! Let's remember, a **body makeover** isn't just about those dreamy abs or toned arms; it's about holistic well-being. Whether you're in the prime of your 20s or navigating the bizarre terrain of adulting, these tips are universal.

Let's kick things off with the first must-know: [Prioritize Nutrition](#1). Yes, my friend, food is not the enemy. While we're busy demonizing carbs and praising detox teas, we often overlook the importance of balanced nutrition. From loading up on healthy fats to including superfoods in your diet, making smart dietary choices can be revolutionary. And let's not forget, enjoying a leafy green salad has way less drama than a college cafeteria food fight!

Ah, but food is only part of the equation. Next up, we have the equally important [Strength Training is Key](#2). Incorporating resistance exercises into your routine can boost your metabolism like never before. And guess what? Lifting weights won't turn you into the Hulk! Rather, strength training shapes those well-defined muscles everyone envies. Plus, it's so much more rewarding than dragging yourself through a monotonous cardio session.

Moving on to something slightly mystical yet curiously underrated: [Mindfulness and Meditation](#3). Surprisingly, practicing mindfulness can anchor you amidst life's chaos, helping keep stress at bay. A harmonious mind often leads to a healthier body. Think of it as a mental detox session-no lemon water needed!

Ah, let's not forget the ever-trending topic: [Consistency is Your Best Friend](#4). Fitness isn't a sprint; it's a marathon. Flashy routines and extreme diets are the fitness world's equivalent of get-rich-quick schemes-appealing but often unsustainable. Embrace consistency; build habits you can sustain for the long haul. And trust me, it’s way more rewarding than crash diets that leave you disappointed and hungry.

In 2024, let's ditch the fads and embrace a science-backed, sustainable approach to a body makeover. Ready to find out the rest of the secrets? Let's dive in!

## 1. Combine Your Cardio and Strength Training

![human action, structure, muscle, bodybuilding, sport venue,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/pr/wm/xs11azmj590cdc8a9cc5c104629534_600x600.jpg)

If you generally separate your cardio workouts and your strength training ones, it’s time to stop. Experts agree that you can combine the two to enhance your benefits and cut back on the amount of time you spend exercising. This will help blast fat from your body and build lean, sculpted muscles at the same time. CrossFit and kettlebells are two exceptional examples of workouts that burn calories and build muscle at the same time.

## 2. Incorporate Tons of Variety

![barechestedness, clothing, person, lady, muscle,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/kv/vw/qolv8jwc590cdc794c751455761828_600x600.jpg)

Your body adapts to a workout with time so you may stop seeing progress. That’s why you should be doing lots of different things when you exercise. Lots of variety keeps your body challenged so you can keep your calorie burning and muscle building progress on track and keep seeing the results you want to see. Try biking one week, Zumba another and swimming on another.

## 3. Make Sure You Target Every Muscle

![hair, clothing, thigh, hairstyle, leg,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/th/p9/nzktbx6u590cdca8bc060189609376_600x600.jpg)

Instead of focusing on just your abs or just your arms, it’s important to include moves that target every single muscle group in your body. That way you create the perfect silhouette you want and balance the strength and appearance of your entire body. Your workouts should work your legs, arms, butt, back, abs, shoulders, chest and arms.

## 4. Now Overhaul Your Diet

![food, meal, produce, breakfast, dish,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/p6/v6/pj014kzm590cdcbb6a9e8206603852_600x750.jpg)

When you want a total body transformation, it has to work from the inside out. That means that in addition to plenty of exercise, you must change the way you eat. That means cutting calories and filling your plate with a variety of healthy foods from each food group. By controlling calorie intake, you can drop weight and help uncover the muscles hiding out beneath the flab.

## 5. Keep Your Energy up

![human action, person, sports, human positions, physical fitness,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wz/eo/jd62xc0f590cdce102b39154576244_600x525.jpg)

If you run out of energy, a total body transformation is going to be harder to complete. Experts suggest eating every three to four hours. Not only does this give you the nutrients you need to complete a workout, but it also prevents you from getting so ravenous that you eat everything in sight. Healthy snacks are a perfectly acceptable addition to a body makeover plan.

## 6. Eat Less CRAP

![leisure, human positions, sitting, vacation, leg,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/bn/v4/mlbgy8ap590cdd039d41c592559742_600x749.jpg)

CRAP stands for carbonated drinks, refined sugar, artificial sweeteners and colors and processed foods. These items are low in nutrients and high in fat, calories and sugar. The latter is terrible for your health and will only stand in the way of getting the body you want. Cut these items out and you’ll be well on your way.

## 7. Make a New Commitment Every Day

![human action, human positions, person, clothing, sitting,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/pg/kd/s3tz0alk590cdcec3e657141910357_600x749.jpg)

The thing about a total body transformation is that it won’t happen overnight. You will have to wake up each day and make a new commitment to the right choices to help you get where you want to be. Planning ahead and being prepared can help you do this.

Are you ready for a total body transformation? Which of these steps will you do first?
