# 10 of the Hottest Beauty Vloggers Right Now That All Pretty Girls Are Watching ...

I love watching beauty vloggers because you can pick up tons of beauty tips and tricks, see product previews and reviews, and watch some pretty fun makeup challenges go down. Think of them as entertaining yet equally informative videos that help you fine tune your application technique and get some insight into products from someone experienced in makeup. There are tons of in-demand beauty vloggers and the list below is just a preview of the many talented vloggers out there! 💄💅

## 1. Farah Dukai

I actually first learned about this beauty vlogger from Instagram. I’m a little obsessed with watching those 15-second makeup vids and she’s one of my favorites to watch. Farah has tons of DIY beauty and skin care tips in addition to makeup tutorials and celeb-inspired makeup. She’s also a great resource for home remedies for hair and skin care issues. Take a look!

## 2. Manny MUA

As you may already know, there are loads of beauty boys killing the makeup game and Manny MUA happens to be one of them. Personally, I think he’s especially skilled at eye shadow and I love watching and learning from him. He recently teamed up with Makeup Geek to create a custom eye shadow palette that’s most definitely worth checking out.

## 3. Nikkie Tutorials

Nikkie Tutorials was behind the Power of Makeup video that went viral awhile back and if you saw the video, you saw how talented she is! This girl can honestly do no wrong when it comes to makeup. She recently did a video using all kids’ makeup and she looked amazing. She’s another YouTuber who recently teamed up with a makeup brand for her very own palette. Look for her collab with Too Faced.

## 4. Nikita Dragun

I love me some Mama Dragun! If you haven’t seen her full face using Kylie lip glosses on IG, you’re seriously missing out. She posts lots of makeup tutorials from cut creases to how to create bigger lips and she does lots of Q&A videos on her transition and plastic surgery experiences, too. She’s definitely one to watch!

## 5. Chrisspy

Chrisspy is amazing at makeup but she’s also a master at transforming herself into characters like Pikachu and Dory. Although she’s skilled at dramatic transformations, she’s also great to watch to learn makeup basics like winged eyeliner to colorful smoky eyes. Don’t’ forget to watch her vlogs, too, she’s always up to something!

## 6. Teni Panosian

Teni Panosian is another fantastic resource for wearable makeup looks for school, work, and special occasions. You won’t see crazy transformations or any outrageous makeup on her channel but I think that’s what makes it unique. It’s all on-trend but classic. So, if you’re the type who prefers understated, timeless makeup; this channel is right up your alley!

## 7. Jeffree Star

Now Jeffree is no one to shy away from anything bright or colorful and you are going to die over the commentary he has for his makeup tutorials. But seriously, Jeffree does not play when it comes to makeup and watching him inspires me to be more daring with my makeup. If you love wearing super bright makeup or want to wear a bolder look, check out his namesake makeup line and give it a whirl!

## 8. Kaushal Beauty

I absolutely adore Kaushal Beauty. Her tutorials are easy to follow and she gives a breakdown of all the products she uses. The looks she creates are wearable and perfect to add that bit of chic glamour to your look.

## 9. Makeup by Shayla

Makeup by Shayla is one to watch out for. Her makeup techniques and flawless looks are amazing. She creates a mixture of makeup looks from dramatic bold lips to perfectly sun kissed highlight. I personally find her makeup products reviews really useful.

## 10. Kristen Leanne

Kristen Leanne has her own unique style. What I love about her is that she advocates cruelty-free makeup. If you want to try bold new makeup trends her videos are one to watch. One of my personal favourites is her neon makeup tutorial.
