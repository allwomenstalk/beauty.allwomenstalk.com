Here Are the 21 Hottest Beauty Products Everyone Will Use This Spring ...

Hourglass Ambient® Strobe Lighting Powder • Algenist POWER Advanced Wrinkle Fighter Moisturizer • Viktor &amp; Rolf Flowerbomb Extreme • Drybar Mr. Incredible the Ultimate Leave-in Conditioner • BECCA Aqua Luminous Perfecting Foundation • More ...

By Jennifer

The weather outside is frightful, but spring really is just around the corner, and with the new season comes a little more sunshine and a lot of new beauty products to fall in love with. Are you ready for a sneak peek of the hottest beauty products everyone will be using this spring?

1. Hourglass Ambient® Strobe Lighting Powder

Price: $38.00 at sephora.com
Available in four sweet shades, you'll love this powder because it creates such a natural-looking luminescence. Why wait til spring? Try it now!

2. Algenist POWER Advanced Wrinkle Fighter Moisturizer

Price: $100.00 at sephora.com
This is another brand-new beauty product I can't wait til spring to try! It promises to fight wrinkles (even mine!) without nasty parabens, sulfates, phthalates, synthetic dyes, GMOs, triclosan, or alcohol.

3. Viktor & Rolf Flowerbomb Extreme

Price: $135.00 at sephora.com
What better time than spring to fall in love with a new(ish) floral scent?

4. Drybar Mr. Incredible the Ultimate Leave-in Conditioner

Price: $26.00 at sephora.com
A lush-smelling leave-in condtioner that smooths, detangles, hydrates, and protects, with the even application and ease of an aerosol spray? Yes please!

5. BECCA Aqua Luminous Perfecting Foundation

Price: $44.00 at sephora.com
Luminous, dewy skin will be totes on-trend this spring, and this nearly weightless foundation can help you get the look.

6. MAISON MARTIN MARGIELA Lipstick on

Price: $125.00 at sephora.com
This floral-toned perfume is inspired by the last step in our beauty rituals: the application of our best lippie.

7. Boscia Charcoal Pore Pudding Intensive Wash-off Treatment

Price: $38.00 at sephora.com
Detox and purify your skin with this rich black and white charcoal treatment.

8. Josie Maran Whipped Argan Oil Face Butter

Price: $40.00 at sephora.com
Spring can't get here fast enough for my dry winter skin! This creamy, buttery argan oil cream can help in the meantime... and all through spring, too.

9. Bésame Cosmetics Brightening Face Powder

Price: $22.00 at sephora.com
Available in four shades, each to correct a set of skin sins, this powder is just amazing. I use the Violet, which tones down the sallow in the olive complexion and evens out the redness of my wind-chapped winter skin.

10. Kat Von D Shade + Light Two Tone Blush

Price: $25.00 at sephora.com
There are seven palettes in this line, all of which help you sculpt and highlight your pretty face.

11. MIU MIU

Price: $86.00 at sephora.com
You'll fall completely in love with this light, Lily of the Valley-toned perfume.

12. NUDESTIX Intense Matte Lip + Cheek Pencil

Price: $24.00 at sephora.com
Talk about a multi-tasker! This one pencil colors both your lips and your cheeks, and you can choose from 8 hot hues. My favorite is Belle, a plum-ish nude-y shade.

13. Laura Mercier Candleglow Soft Luminous Foundation

Price: $48.00 at sephora.com
Even if this foundation wasn't so lightweight and natural-looking, and even if it didn't add a soft glow to our skin, I'd love it because it comes in such a wide range of shades - two dozen of them - including shades for people of color.

14. Givenchy Rouge Interdit Vinyl Color Enhancing Lipstick

Price: $33.00 at sephora.com
Your new favorite lippie is here! The colors are intense (all 15 of them) and this lipstick is formulated to hydrate and plump!

15. Ciaté London Liquid Velvet™ Matte Lip Slick

Price: $19.00 at sephora.com
If you're looking for a new matte lip gloss for your spring fling, this is it... the colors are rich and vibrant, and I love the matte effect!

16. Dinoplatz Triceratops Multi-Face Palette

Price: $48.00 at sephora.com
This fab palette includes two contouring colors and a pretty blush, too.

17. Rita Hazan Ultimate Shine Gloss

Price: $26.00 at sephora.com
I don't know about you, but I want ultra-shiny hair year-round... so I'm going to start using this before spring, thank you very much.

18. Caolion Hot & Cool Pore Foam Cleansing Duo

Price: $24.00 at sephora.com
After trying this two-in-one hot-and-cold cleaning set, I'm still singing that Katy Perry song.

19. Too Faced Melted Chocolate

Price: $21.00 at sephora.com
If you love the Too Faced line of cocoa-infused makeup, you've just met your next crush.

20. Lancôme Visionnaire Nuit Beauty Sleep Perfector

Price: $88.00 at sephora.com
Apply this gel-to-oil treatment before bed and wake up even more beautiful.

21. SEPHORA COLLECTION the Escapader

Price: $18.00 at sephora.com
How about a new makeup bag to store all your new spring makeup and beauty products?
