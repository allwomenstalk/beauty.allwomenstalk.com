# 7 Lovely Beauty Products for Christmas ...

Are you trying to decide what beauty products for Christmas you should add to your wish list? These are some of the most popular gifts to consider. All of these products look amazing. I think it’d be great to receive any of these beauty products for Christmas.

## 1. Naked Basics or Naked2 Basics

![Naked Basics or Naked2 Basics](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/s4/zw/wbt81brk_300x160.jpg)

You’ve seen the full size Naked, Naked2 and Naked3 eye shadow pallets, but have you seen their Naked Basics or Naked2 Basics? It’s a smaller version, each having 6 eye shadow colors and selling for $29 each. They’re more of a matte collection and are perfect for everyday wear. They’ll work great for the office as well as many other daytime events. Be sure and check them out. They may just make your wish list of beauty products for Christmas.

## 2. Redken All Soft Shampoo and Conditioner Set

![Redken All Soft Shampoo and Conditioner Set](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/d6/iu/g2k064y8_450x449.jpg)

This is a beauty product I personally love. It always makes my hair look and feel amazing. My hair styles beautifully after being washed with Redken All Soft. Another point in favor of this shampoo and conditioner duo is that it works for many different types of hair. It works as fabulously for my fine textured hair as it does for my best friend’s thick, wavy hair.

## 3. MAC Objects of Affection Lip Gloss Set

![MAC Objects of Affection Lip Gloss Set](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/py/dz/czaeuzmp_600x490.jpg)

Girls, this lip gloss set is beautiful. If you haven’t seen it, you need to look at it. It contains 4 lovely MAC lip glosses for $35. One is a raspberry red and the other three are shades of very wearable pink. It’s definitely going on my Christmas wish list.

## 4. Philosophy Glowing Days Ahead Kit

![Philosophy Glowing Days Ahead Kit](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/i0/go/usiphke7_600x538.jpg)

You can’t go wrong with anything by Philosophy. It’s a great brand with high quality products. That includes this 6 piece facial care set. There’s a bit of everything in this gift set, including a facial cleanser and a 2 step micro peel treatment. Your face will look and feel beautiful with this arsenal of beauty products to use.

## 5. Bath & Body Works a Thousand Wishes

![Bath & Body Works a Thousand Wishes](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/tk/o7/dxrmhjq7_480x473.jpg)

No list of beauty products for Christmas would be complete without something from Bath & Body Works. This new fragrance has a scent perfect for winter. Bath & Body Works describes A Thousand Wishes as a fragrance made from a blend of sparkling champagne, crystal peonies and almond crème. It’s a light but lovely fragrance. There are many different choices in this fragrance line to choose from.

## 6. MAC Studio Brush Set

![MAC Studio Brush Set](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/pg/o3/dh9rlbqq_600x611.jpg)

Every girl needs a good quality brush set. This brush set by MAC is a great choice. It’s a total of 5 makeup brushes in a gorgeous sparkly case. The set includes a powder brush, foundation brush, blending brush, fluff brush and eyeliner brush. It’s a good basic set to build on.

## 7. Burt’s Bees Mani Pedi Gift Set

![Burt’s Bees Mani Pedi Gift Set](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ch/ee/xipa520h_600x305.jpg)

Burt’s Bees has an amazing line of products that almost always deliver really good results. The set includes a foot cream, hand cream and [cuticle cream](https://nails.allwomenstalk.com/best-cuticle-creams-for-nail-biters/). I have the cuticle cream and have found it works very well. This gift is packaged in a couple different ways so it could also contain a cosmetic bag or a nail file. One other point to make about Burt’s Bees products is that they always have an amazing scent.

Now it’s your turn and I’ve got to know. What beauty products are on your Christmas wish list? I can’t wait to read your answers!
