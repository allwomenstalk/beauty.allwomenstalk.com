By Lisa • 10 Comments

Protecting your skin from the sun is essential, so whether it’s December or July, you need to invest in the best facial sunscreens. Even when there’s cloud cover outside, the sun’s rays can still damage your skin. Incorporate a protective moisture into your daily skin care routine to avoid everything from wrinkles and sunspots to the risk of cancer. Start by taking a look at our reviews of the best facial sunscreens on the market.
