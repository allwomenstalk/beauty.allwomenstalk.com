# She Traveled the World to Prove ALL Women Are Beautiful ...

It wasn't easy - she caught some nasty viruses and dealt with less-than-ideal accommodations - but Romanian photographer Mihaela Noroc traveled to 37 countries in less than a year and a half to prove that all women, around the world, are beautiful. Here are some of her images - and they are stunning! What do you think of her "Atlas of Beauty"?
