# Spray on Beauty Products for Girls Who Hate Applying Them ...

You’ve probably used aerosol hairspray, but it’s not the only beauty product that comes packaged this way. The ease of which these items can be applied is particularly alluring to ladies who want to be ready to go in no time or who just don’t like the inconvenience of having to apply multiple products. If you want to branch out from your hairspray, try one of these innovative new things. You can find them in many drugstores in all price ranges so you’re sure to get just what you want and need.

## 1. You Are Going to Love Spray Lotion

If you don’t care for the time required to fully rub in lotion all over your body, you will adore spray lotion. It’s super lightweight and is designed to be absorbed into your skin really quickly. Spritz on the lotion while your skin is still damp so that it creates a barrier that locks in moisture and keeps your entire body hydrated and healthy.

## 2. Try a Root Concealer for a Great Hair Day

If you have grey hair, you are probably pretty diligent about keeping it covered. If you are seeing roots showing through, but can’t get into the salon anytime soon, try spritzing your roots with a concealer spray in an aerosol can. This product is perfect for touching up your hair anytime you need it so that you can face the world with confidence.

## 3. You’ve Got to Try Spray on Nail Polish

This sounds like a miracle if you ask me. I love having my nails painted, but I don’t like the time commitment involved in getting the perfect manicure. That’s why spray on nail polish may be my new favorite product. All you have to do is apply an adhesive to your fingers, then spray on the polish color. Perfect!

## 4. How about Waterproof Makeup?

The magicians at Sephora have come up with a waterproof spray-on cheek color that lasts all day long and goes on in seconds. This is the beginning of a new trend in the makeup world and you’re probably going to start seeing all kinds of makeup packaged in aerosol cans that makes it effortless to get the look you want.

## 5. Girls Rave about Aerosol Deodorant

You would never dream about leaving the house without deodorant, right? The new aerosol products are getting great reviews. They go on fast and dry almost right away so you’re ready to go in mere seconds. Many of them also contain moisturizers so they keep you fresh and dry, while also hydrating your skin with every use.

## 6. Hydrate Your Hair with Aerosol Conditioner

You’ve heard of dry shampoo, but have you ever tried dry conditioner? You can use an aerosol version to help you keep your hair tangle-free and easily manageable. A few simple spritzes can help you comb your hair out and create the style you love, even when you don’t have a ton of time on your hands to get the job done.

## 7. Soak up Hair Oil and Finish Your Style

An aerosol finishing spray is a great choice for ensuring that your hair isn’t too greasy and that it stays looking clean. This product will also help add volume to your mane and keep your hair from falling flat before the day is over.

Have you ever heard of any of these products? Which ones do you think you’d most like to use?
