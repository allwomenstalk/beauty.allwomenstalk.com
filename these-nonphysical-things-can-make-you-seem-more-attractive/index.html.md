# These Nonphysical Things Can Make You Seem More Attractive ...

I'm sure you're all very much aware of this, but beauty is not only skin deep. There are so many nonphysical things that guys take notice of! It really is all about what's on the inside. These five things are just some of many nonphysical things that can make you seem more attractive:

## 1. Being around People

It's weird but people tend to be rated as more attractive when they're part of a group than when they're alone. Of course, that doesn't mean you need to be around 20+ friends at all times... Hanging out with your BFF will work just fine. Being around people shows that you're approachable!

## 2. Being Passionate

It's important to have something you're passionate about. Whether it be music, books, or whatever else you could think of, having a passion gives people something to share. Guys will be intrigued by you and want to learn more about you, sparking their attraction!

## 3. Having a Sense of Humor

Girls are always going on and on about how they'd love a guy who can make them laugh. Well, guys are looking for the same thing! Humor is indicative of intelligence and creativity. Your ability to crack a joke will have boys swooning over you!

## 4. Doing Your Share

It's important to pull your own weight. That could mean it's your turn to suggest date ideas! Remember, it's a two way street. If you're not dating, pulling your own weight could be as simple as staying up late to finish up a group project. Doing your share and being reliable makes you a valuable social partner, which makes you seem very attractive.

## 5. Smiling

What's more attractive than a happy person? Smiling makes you look personable and approachable. I mean, wouldn't you be more attracted to someone who's smiling rather than someone who's frowning?

What other nonphysical things do you think makes someone attractive?
