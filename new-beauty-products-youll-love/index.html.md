# 7 New Beauty Products Youll Love ...

**New Beauty Products** come around with every season and it's always fun to find out what new trends are taking the stage. Now, it’s a new year and time to ditch those old beauty products that are collecting dust in your bathroom drawer and start fresh with new and exciting beauty products! Take a looksie at some of the brand spankin’ new products you can find at your local drug or department store, and see which new beauty product is right for you…

## 1. Garnier Skin Renew Miracle Skin Protector B.B. Cream

![Garnier Skin Renew Miracle Skin Protector B.B. Cream](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/beauty/2012/02/Garnier-Skin-Renew-Miracle-Skin-Protector-B.B.-Cream_108x300.jpg)

**Price: **$12.99 at [cvs.com](https://www.cvs.com/shop)

BB Cream was all over YouTube and beauty gurus around the world were raving about this innovative new beauty product. The only thing was, it wasn’t readily available in the U.S. and even if it was, the color range was very limited and expensive! Garnier to the rescue! BB Cream is famous for combining skin care with great coverage, it’s a notch above tinted moisturizer.

## 2. Guerlain Noir G Mascara

![Guerlain Noir G Mascara](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/beauty/2012/02/Guerlain-Noir-G-Mascara_171x300.jpg)

**Price:** $49.00 at [sephora.com](http://www.sephora.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)

This elegant tube of mascara comes with a built-in mirror and wait for it…is refillable! Once you’re finished with the tube, simply replace the old with a new one and you’ve got a brand new mascara wand and tube of mascara. This beauty product makes it easy to tote around your mascara for touch-ups with less waste.

## 3. Revlon Colorstay 16-Hour Eye Shadow

![Revlon Colorstay 16-Hour Eye Shadow](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/beauty/2012/02/Revlon-Colorstay-16-Hour-Eye-Shadow_298x300.jpg)

**Price:** $6.07 at [target.com](http://www.target.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)

I’ll buy anything Olivia Wilde peddles and this shadow looked amazing on her. This new beauty product will last you through the workday, to happy hour and beyond. With sixteen different color combinations, you’re sure to find the perfect palette of colors for you.

## 4. Chanel Le Vernis Nail Colour in June

![Chanel Le Vernis Nail Colour in June](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/beauty/2012/02/Chanel-Le-Vernis-Nail-Colour-in-June_138x300.jpg)

**Price:** $26.00 at [chanel.com](http://www.chanel.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)

Peach and apricot colors are predicted to be hot in the spring months and June is a great way to incorporate the "it" color into your everyday look. You might not get away with wearing peach pants to work, but peach nails will be a hit! Plus, Chanel polishes stay in top condition for a really long time, so that's an added bonus.

## 5. China Glaze Nail Polish

![China Glaze Nail Polish](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/beauty/2012/02/China-Glaze-Nail-Polish_134x300.jpg)

**Price:** $2.75 at [amazon.com](http://www.amazon.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)

Actually, lots of the new beauty products have to do with your nails. China Glaze offers a more affordable option to keeping your nails on trend for the spring. Check out the bright, music-inspired nail colors that will give your nails that electropop at your next GNO or day out.

## 6. Yves Saint Laurent Candy Palette Eye Shadow

![Yves Saint Laurent Candy Palette Eye Shadow](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/beauty/2012/02/Yves-Saint-Laurent-Candy-Palette-Eye-Shadow_278x300.jpg)

**Price:** $65.00 at [sephora.com](http://www.sephora.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)

Any beauty product with the word candy in its name gets my attention. YSL’s Candy Palette offers this sweet and delectable candy colored eye shadow palette that is sure to please any girly girl or makeup maven craving lots of color. You'll love the dazzling array of shades, and easily find something for day or evening.

## 7. Covergirl Lashblast 24 Hour Mascara

![Covergirl Lashblast 24 Hour Mascara](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/beauty/2012/02/Covergirl-Lashblast-24-Hour-Mascara_105x300.jpg)

**Price:** $7.99 at [target.com](http://www.target.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)

For those never ending days or nights, choose mascara that can keep up with you. Covergirl’s lastest 24 Hour mascara touts bold and luscious lashes that keep you looking easy breezy beautiful for an entire day.

Try out some of these fun new beauty products to add to your beauty arsenal. Adding some beauty products in bright colors is sure to put a spring in your step! What's your favorite new beauty product this season?
