# 10 Bold Beauty Products to Make Eyes Stand out ...

Make your eyes light up even brighter the next time you’re preparing for a special occasion with these beauty products to make eyes stand out! There are tons of eye makeup products that can help make your peepers really pop, we just have to find the right ones for us! Make your next makeup look the best and brightest ever with these 10 beauty products to make eyes stand out!

## 1. Benefit Creaseless Cream Shadow/Liner

![Benefit Creaseless Cream Shadow/Liner](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/beauty/2012/12/1_benefit-creaseless-cream-shadow-liner_216x216.jpg)

Products to make eyes stand out can liven up any makeup look with just a few swipes! Take Benefit’s cream shadow and liner, it comes in a variety of colors ranging from cream to deep blue and you can use it to line, contour or shade your eyes. The range of bold and beautiful colors is perfect to accentuate your eye color and it lasts all day long!

## 2. Urban Decay Heavy Metal Glitter Liner

![Urban Decay Heavy Metal Glitter Liner](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/beauty/2012/12/2_urban-decay-heavy-metal-glitter-liner_250x250.jpg)

This eye makeup product screams fun and festive! Don’t be afraid of glitter liner, even though this eye liner looks pretty intense and bright, it’s really quite wearable! Wear it alone over complementary eye shadows or layer it on top of your go-to eyeliner. This liner gives you a lot of control over the amount you apply so you get a clean, precise application!

## 3. Sephora Collection False Eye Lashes

![Sephora Collection False Eye Lashes](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/beauty/2012/12/3_sephora-collection-false-eye-lashes_224x88.jpg)

There’s nothing like batting long, lush eye lashes to get some attention! Sephora has a collection of super snazzy lash styles and lengths so you can create and customize your very own look. Use small sections to add length to just the outer corner of your eyes or use a full strip to add color or mile-long lashes!

## 4. Tarte EmphasEYES Inner Rim Brightener

![Tarte EmphasEYES Inner Rim Brightener](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/beauty/2012/12/4_tarte-emphaseyes-inner-rim-brightener_250x250.jpg)

Makeup tutorials often recommend lining the inner rim of your eyes to make them appear wider and more awake. Highlighting the inner corner of eyes is another effective way to wake up tired eyes and Tarte’s inner rim brightener does both! Ladies of all ages can benefit from an eye brightener so don’t be afraid to give this a try!

## 5. Laura Mercier Caviar Stick Eye Colour

![Laura Mercier Caviar Stick Eye Colour](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/beauty/2012/12/5_laura-mercier-caviar-stick-eye-colour_250x250.jpg)

This eye color was inspired by the brand’s caviar eye liner and takes a traditional beauty product and turns it into an easy-to-use and luxurious cream eye makeup. Use this stick to create colorful smoky eyes or as a basic eye shadow that provides not-so-basic color and coverage!

## 6. Violent Eyes Temporary Eye Appliques

![Violent Eyes Temporary Eye Appliques](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/beauty/2012/12/6_violent-eyes-temporary-eye-appliques_470x600.jpg)

If you shy away from using liquid eyeliners because they can be a bit challenging to use, try an applique! Violent eyes’ temporary eye liner tattoos help you achieve a bold dash of color on your eyes without all the fuss of using a liquid liner. Appliques come in solid, sparkly colors or glittery rainbow or Union Jack designs, perfect for parties and girls night out!

## 7. Make up for Ever Smoky Lash Mascara

![Make up for Ever Smoky Lash Mascara](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/beauty/2012/12/7_make-up-for-ever-smoky-lash-mascara_250x250.jpg)

Another fun way to liven up your eyes is to use a colored mascara! Colored mascara doesn’t have to be super bright and flashy to get you noticed. MUFE’s smoky lash comes in classic, muted colors that are safe for work but playful enough to use when you’re getting ready for a night out on the town. These super saturated colors are great for dark or light lashes!

## 8. Kat Von D Tattoo Liner

![Kat Von D Tattoo Liner](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/beauty/2012/12/8_kat-von-d-tattoo-liner_250x250.jpg)

One of the top beauty products that women own is a proper liquid eyeliner to create a sexy, dramatic look! Kat Von D’s Tattoo liner helps you create the most striking lined eyes in five different colors. I’m always a fan of using rich black liner to define the eyes but any of these intense colors will make a great addition to your look! You can also use this liner to create temporary tattoos, so get creative!

## 9. Illamasqua Powder Eye Shadow

![Illamasqua Powder Eye Shadow](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/beauty/2012/12/9_illamasqua-powder-eye-shadow_250x250.jpg)

Playing with mascara and eye liner are always fun but finding an eye shadow that really matches well with your eye color and skin tone is a real winner! Illamasqua has a big array of highly pigmented powder eye shadows that are sure to flatter your eyes and skin. Use Illamasqua's powder eye shadow to line or shade your eyes!

## 10. Guerlain Terracotta Loose Powder Kohl Liner

![Guerlain Terracotta Loose Powder Kohl Liner](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/beauty/2012/12/10_guerlain-terracotta-loose-powder-kohl-liner_250x250.jpg)

Here’s another multi-purpose product that looks as pretty on as it does in the package! This kohl liner comes in a unique loose powder form so there’s a tiny bit of fall out when you apply but ladies rave about the super long staying power! You can use this powder line on your eyelids like regular eye liner or on the waterline. There’s a bit of sparkle in the colors so it really makes your eyes shine!

The next time you feel like you’re in a makeup rut or just want to add some drama to your look, try out one of these products to make eyes stand out! Your eyes can communicate a lot without you even saying a word so make sure you’re communicating the right statement! What’s your favorite beauty product to make your eyes stand out?
