# The New Rules That'll Keep You Looking Young ...

There comes a point in every girl’s life when she starts to notice the signs of aging and she goes on a constant quest to fight them off. Are you there yet? I am too! That’s why I’m always on the lookout for anti-aging advice that I can use and share with you. Here are some fabulous new rules on looking young no matter what age you are, courtesy of the experts at Redbook magazine. You’re going to love them!

## 1. Start Using a Soap-Free Exfoliating Cleanser Twice per Day

![eyebrow, face, cheek, hair, nose,](https://img.allw.mn/content/nm/sh/kbkdphke58765dd0e6f19611996325_500x281.gif)

As you get older, your skin is less and less able to exfoliate itself, making it important to use a product that gets the job done for you. Experts recommend using a soap-free exfoliator twice a day to get rid of dead skin cells and other junk hanging out on your face. This helps prevent aging by keeping your skin fresh and clean. Get into the habit now and you’ll be set for a lifetime of looking youthful.

## 2. Switch to a BB Cream That Contains SPF for a Double Duty Punch

![hair, facial expression, face, person, nose,](https://img.allw.mn/content/nh/c7/jzbx9m9258765de50cb24899832305_500x328.gif)

Chances are you already use a BB cream. They are pretty miraculous, aren’t they? Now is the time to start using a BB cream that contains at least an SPF of 30. One of the best ways to fight the signs on aging is to wear sunscreen every day. The sun can lead to wrinkles and age spots so protecting your skin now can keep you from looking older than you are down the road.

## 3. Add a Retinol Product to Your Routine

![hair, face, facial expression, black and white, person,](https://img.allw.mn/content/st/fp/twvtmrn158765e16d0226877157309_500x281.gif)

Retinol is a magical ingredient that fights wrinkles, combats age spots and keeps your skin looking fresh and radiant. It essentially delays aging when you use it on a regular basis. Look for a night cream that contains retinol and use it before you go to bed each night. You’ll be amazed at what it can do for you.

## 4. Do a Once a Week Glycolic Peel, Especially as You Enter Your 40s

![face, clothing, black and white, head, monochrome photography,](https://img.allw.mn/content/yq/zy/rf18ydvq58765ec315287767489700_500x222.gif)

You can do a glycolic peel at home and the difference is impressive. The treatment fades age spots, renews your skin and gets rid of icky stuff that dulls your complexion. Talk to your dermatologist about which glycolic peel is right for you. This becomes especially important in your 40s as you skin starts to naturally look dull and old.

## 5. Use a Moisturizer That Contains Hyaluronic Acid

![hair, facial expression, face, white, black and white,](https://img.allw.mn/content/gr/ut/trszgxd758765ea589bd5668073473_500x417.gif)

Hyaluronic acid pulls moisture into your skin, helping give it that plumped, youthful look. In fact, nothing can age you more than dry, lifeless skin. It’s imperative to keep your skin adequately moisturized to help smooth wrinkles and lines and keep your skin from getting dull, dry and flaky. You can find hyaluronic acid in products of all prices so you’re sure to find one that fits your budget.

## 6. Use Creams with Natural Brightening Agents

![black, white, black and white, person, woman,](https://img.allw.mn/content/sk/ti/ik22ukb958765f290d14b316691378_500x265.gif)

These products help keep your skin glowing and radiant, no matter your age. They become more important as you enter middle age, but getting into the habit now can save your skin down the road. Dab a cream with brightening ingredients onto your face on a regular basis and you’ll love the results.

## 7. Start Taking a Supplement Designed for Healthy Skin

![hair, black and white, black, face, photography,](https://img.allw.mn/content/k6/lk/qxfpy2oh5876606d1b0f2918671756_650x365.gif)

Of course, you should discuss any supplement with your doctor before you start taking it, but a healthy skin vitamin can do wonders for keeping your face looking young. Look for one that promises to plump, hydrate and fight the signs of aging. And make sure to combine it with a healthy diet for the best results.

Are you actively fighting the signs of aging right now? What are you doing to stay young?
