# 9 Candy Themed Beauty Products to Treat Yourself to ...

Candy themed beauty products are a fun and guilt-free way to enjoy all the girly colors and scents of our favorite sweets! With Halloween candy still in the house, I thought this would be a great way for us to enjoy some of the sweetest and most tempting treats minus the cavities and calories. Check out this list of 9 candy themed beauty products to treat yourself to ASAP!

## 1. Philosophy I Love Candy Corn Shampoo, Shower Gel & Bubble Bath

![Philosophy I Love Candy Corn Shampoo, Shower Gel & Bubble Bath](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/beauty/2012/11/1_philosophy-i-love-candy-corn-shampoo-shower-gel-bubble-bath_250x250.jpg)

Philosophy has tons of dessert and candy themed beauty products for us to choose from. And since Halloween just passed, I had to include their I Love Candy Corn multi-purpose cleanser as one of the candy themed beauty products to try! If you’re not in the mood for this seasonal sweet, check out some of their other dessert and cocktail themed products for the whole body!

## 2. Cotton Candy Body Spray by Body Fantasies

![Cotton Candy Body Spray by Body Fantasies](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/beauty/2012/11/2_cotton-candy-body-spray-by-body-fantasies_300x300.jpg)

If you love super sweet scents like vanilla and cotton candy, you have to check out this beauty product inspired by candy! This body spray captured the exact cotton candy scent for a frisky and fun fragrance that you can wear anytime you want to feel extra girly or give you a little sugar high without the crash!

## 3. Victoria's Secret Beauty Rush Gloss in Taffy Go Lucky

![Victoria's Secret Beauty Rush Gloss in Taffy Go Lucky](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/beauty/2012/11/3_victorias-secret-beauty-rush-gloss-in-taffy-go-lucky_146x175.jpg)

You might not think of Victoria’s Secret and candy themed beauty products together, but their Beauty Rush lip glosses give us lots of candy inspired options! Taffy Go Lucky is a sassy hot pink color that contains a little bit of sparkle along with a sugary scent and flavor that’s sure to satisfy any sweet tooth!

## 4. Too Faced Chocolate Soleil Matte Bronzing Powder

![Too Faced Chocolate Soleil Matte Bronzing Powder](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/beauty/2012/11/4_too-faced-chocolate-soleil-matte-bronzing-powder_250x250.jpg)

This sweet beauty product inspired by candy is the real deal; this bronzer has real cocoa in it! Too Faced is a brand after our own hearts with their cocoa-laced sheer matte bronzer. This delectable powder will add a gorgeous glow and subtly sweet scent to dull autumn skin or anytime of the year!

## 5. Sally Hansen Bubble Gum Pink Nail Polish

![Sally Hansen Bubble Gum Pink Nail Polish](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/beauty/2012/11/5_sally-hansen-bubble-gum-pink-nail-polish_234x289.jpg)

Sally Hansen’s Bubble Gum Pink polish is candy themed beauty product that unfortunately doesn’t smell like the confection it was named after. But, you can still enjoy the playful pink color and the strength and shine formula of the nail polish for long-lasting luster and color! Wear this carefree color anytime you need a little pop of pink in your life!

## 6. Revlon Colorburst Lip Butter in Gumdrop

![Revlon Colorburst Lip Butter in Gumdrop](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/beauty/2012/11/6_revlon-colorburst-lip-butter-in-gumdrop_410x410.jpg)

This next beauty product inspired by candy is a gorgeous light purple lip butter! Gumdrops are known for being spicy and sweet and this lavender lip butter will have you looking and feeling just as good! The nice thing about lip butters is that they are a hydrating and pigmented so you don’t have to tote around a separate lipstick and lip balm!

## 7. LUSH Mmm Melting Marshmallow Moment

![LUSH Mmm Melting Marshmallow Moment](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/beauty/2012/11/7_lush-mmm-melting-marshmallow-moment_350x350.jpg)

The good folks at LUSH are the kings and queens of candy themed beauty products as well as a heap of other good stuff! Their Mmm Melting Marshmallow Moment is a marshmallowy and moisturizing bath bomb that’ll make bath time even sweeter! Drop this in your bath when you want to envelop your body with this candy scent and soothe and smooth your skin!

## 8. Bath & Body Works Holiday Traditions Winter Candy Apple Body Lotion

![Bath & Body Works Holiday Traditions Winter Candy Apple Body Lotion](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/beauty/2012/11/8_bath-body-works-holiday-traditions-winter-candy-apple-body-lotion_300x270.jpg)

Have you ever wanted to smell like a winter candy apple because this next candy themed beauty product allows you to do just that! This scent is just one of the zillions of sweet, fruity and floral scents Bath and Body Works offers us to indulge our every whim. This particular seasonal scent is a must-smell and would be ideal after a nice shower or bath to transport yourself to a winter candy land!

## 9. Lip Smackers Nerds Flavored Lip Balm

![Lip Smackers Nerds Flavored Lip Balm](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/beauty/2012/11/9_lip-smackers-nerds-flavored-lip-balm_300x300.jpg)

Nerds are a classic American candy and now you can enjoy these candy themed beauty products without the funky sugar high! Lip Smackers has just about every flavor of lip balm you can imagine so even if you’re not into Nerds, you’ll probably find your favorite flavor in their never ending roster of flavors! Swipe on some candy flavored balm whenever you crave a fun flavor and a hint of color!

Candy themed beauty products can offer us all the things we love about candy minus all the calories, tooth aches and sugar lows! Spoil yourself rotten with one or some of these cosmetic confections whenever you want to sweeten your look or just indulge your senses! Do you like candy themed beauty products? What product is your favorite?
