For those moments when you want your cheeks to capture a slightly flirtatious & whimsical spirit, the **Coquette/Mimi** duo is perfect. The bright pinky peach paired with a tawny beige creates a balanced tone that flatters a wide array of skin tones, infusing a warm touch of vitality.
