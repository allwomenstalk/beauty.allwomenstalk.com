# 7 Incredible Transformations on YouTube ...

If you haven’t seen some of these incredibly impressive transformations on YouTube, you’re missing out! There are so many talented makeup artists who can transform themselves into a cartoon character or celebrity all by cleverly applying makeup and dressing up. If you’ve ever wanted to transform yourself into your favorite celebrity or want see someone make themselves over into a totally different person, you’ve got to see these transformations on YouTube; you will be amazed!

## 1. Beyoncé by Dope2111

Dope2111 or Promise Tamang Phan is one of the best at makeup transformations on YouTube! Although she’s done tons of celebrity and character transformations, this is definitely one of my favorites. She really thought of everything when it comes to this makeup transformation from the shape of Beyoncé’s nose to her eyebrows. Although Promise and Beyoncé look nothing alike in real life, she did a fantastic job at recreating her features and makeup!

## 2. Justin Bieber by Kandee Johnson

Here’s another amazing YouTube transformation by Kandee Johnson. It’s hard enough to try to recreate another woman’s look, but it’s even harder to try to make yourself look like someone of the opposite sex! Miss Johnson did a fab job contouring and recreating the Biebs’ brows. With the earrings, snapback and sunglasses, I seriously did a double take when I saw this video!

## 3. Bruno Mars by Marina Mamic

Marina Mamic is another YouTube personality who has done numerous makeup transformations. Take a look around her channel and you’ll see she’s transformed herself into celebs like Cher, Lady Gaga, Megan Fox and even Barbie. In this video as Bruno Mars, I think she did a great job contouring her face and making up her eyes just like the soulful crooner. The finished product is quite impressive, don’t you think?

## 4. Jared Leto by Anastasiya Shpagina

I’ve admired Jared Leto for many years. I mean, it’s hard to resist those piercing blue eyes and that fabulous head of hair, so when I caught this makeup transformation by Anastasiya Shpagina, I almost fell out of my chair! The detail in the eyes, eyebrows and the fake facial hair is just amazing! She’s also made herself into Ian Somerhalder, Nina Dobrev and Miley Cyrus - check the videos out!

## 5. Angelina Jolie by Michelle Phan

Michelle Phan is one of my favorite YouTube beauty gurus and she’s done her fair share of makeup transformations, also. If you’ve ever wanted to recreate Angelina Jolie’s sexy bedroom eyes or pouty lips, this is the perfect makeup tutorial for you. Michelle perfectly recreated Angelina’s deep creased eyes and signature cat eye. Meow!

## 6. Jennifer Lawrence by Dope2111

I just had to include another makeup transformation by Dope2111! She is just so amazing at transforming herself and this is another extraordinary makeover where she turns herself into Jennifer Lawrence. With the light-colored wig and the colored contacts, I swear I thought I was looking at J-Law herself. Isn’t it wild how much difference makeup application can make?

## 7. Haifa Wehbe by Joseph Harwood

There are tons of Arabic makeup tutorials on YouTube and this is a really stunning eye makeup tutorial. I love the bright, vivid colors and the gorgeous eye shape that was created in this look. Joseph mentions that the look he created is much more dramatic than Haifa Wehbe’s, but it’s still a great way to try Arabian-inspired makeup. Look up his channel for other makeup transformations like Tyra Banks, zombie makeup, anime girl and more!

What do you think of these dramatic YouTube transformations? Honestly, I was astounded at how similar these beauty gurus made themselves look to their celebrity inspiration. It just shows how powerful makeup can be. Have you ever tried making yourself over to look like a celebrity or character?
