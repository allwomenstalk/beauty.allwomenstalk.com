# 11 Non\-toxic Products for a Flawless Face ...

Not so long ago, I decided to purge all of my chemical-laden beauty products and convert to non-toxic products instead. It took some time, and a lot of trial and error, but I've been fortunate enough to find some of the very best non-toxic beauty products on the market. From cleansers to moisturizers, concealers to foundations and powders for every skin type, I've compiled my ultimate list of non-toxic products for a flawless face.

## 1. HANA Organic Skincare Gentle Cleanser

![HANA Organic Skincare Gentle Cleanser](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/bj/bj/u4t2pugs_600x716.jpg)

A good cleanser is the fundamental basis of any skincare regime, and this is a truly beautiful, non-toxic one. A very simple formula of Certified Organic Rose Hydrosol, Castile Soap, jojoba and essential oils makes for a formula that mists on and works into a foamy cleansing luxury! It's not going to bust tough makeup, so it's ideal for a bare-faced morning cleanse to refresh and awaken your skin, leaving behind a clean, bright canvas! This is my go-to for the morning. This brand makes excellent non-toxic products.

## 2. Pure Anada Fruit Enzyme Exfoliating Cleanser

![Pure Anada Fruit Enzyme Exfoliating Cleanser](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/zw/hm/i5b2ml1r_600x709.jpg)

Naturally scented with pineapple, orange and grapefruit, this super-creamy cleanser is a slice of citrus heaven. The fabulous froth features jojoba beads to exfoliate. Pure Anada kills it with this fragrant gem! It leaves skin thoroughly cleansed and ultra-soft, swiftly removing all traces of dirt and makeup. Perfect for any skin-type, this is an amazing night cleanser! Plus, it's got super-clean ingredients and is very affordable, especially considering the extra large size of the bottle!

## 3. Rosemira Organics Moisturize Me Citrus Restorative Cream

![Rosemira Organics Moisturize Me Citrus Restorative Cream](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/si/tu/noex7a01_600x393.jpg)

Crafted with lemon rind oil to brighten and balance, aloe Vera to soothe, and jojoba oil to tone and moisturize, this cream firms, tones, AND moisturizes. Beautifully fragrant, non-toxic and luxuriously rich, it packs a hydrating punch for all skin types! Moisturizing is key for flawless skin, and I reach for this multi-purpose miracle daily.

## 4. Rosemira Organics Roses in Bloom Hydrating Toner

![Rosemira Organics Roses in Bloom Hydrating Toner](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/bz/gg/gunpkjrg_600x640.jpg)

Rose essential oil hydrates while Rose Hydrosol balances and tones, in yet another stellar non-toxic product from the high-end luxury that is Rosemira Organics. Spritz this on for a natural rose scent and refreshed, evened-out skin. No flawless face routine is complete without a good toner, and this one is a prime example!

## 5. Vapour Organic Beauty Stratus Instant Skin Perfector

![Vapour Organic Beauty Stratus Instant Skin Perfector](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/hj/k1/dp28kcmk_375x500.jpg)

Glide this silky smooth miracle on your skin prior to applying foundation and watch as your skin tone is instantly balanced, pores and imperfections are blurred and a velvety softness graces your face. A 3-in-1 moisturizer, colorless foundation and primer, this revolutionary stick is non-toxic and high performance.

## 6. Sante Naturkosmetik Coverstick

![Sante Naturkosmetik Coverstick](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/yo/fv/tvmi78uh_600x339.jpg)

Castor seed oil, Shea butter and jojoba seed oil star in the formula for this natural mineral concealer stick, which provides exceptional coverage in a creamy, easy-to-apply and very portable pencil form. Perfect for concealing everything from dark circles to blemishes, this is a must-have for a flawless looking face!

## 7. Sappho Organic Cosmetics Liquid Foundation

![Sappho Organic Cosmetics Liquid Foundation](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/j8/av/0wkn7f2n_430x472.jpg)

This Vancouver-based high-end cosmetics line was created by celebrity makeup artist JoAnn Fowler, who took matters into her own hands to offer her clients a non-toxic option with pro performance. Sappho Organic Cosmetics is renowned for this incredible foundation, and with good reason. The formula, filled with potent natural ingredients like organic aloe juice, organic calendula extract and organic green tea, offers buildable, flawless coverage for satin skin with a seamless finish.

## 8. Lauren Brooke Creme Foundation

![Lauren Brooke Creme Foundation](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/cv/rf/o6n0av9k_600x339.jpg)

If creme foundation is more up your alley, you can't go wrong with this whipped souffle. With key ingredients like Argan oil, green tea and zinc oxide (built-in sun protection!), this non-toxic formula is light-as-air, yet offers a beautiful coverage that can be worn sheer or built up to medium-full. Blends like a dream and wears like a champ!

## 9. Delizioso Skincare Natural Pressed Foundation

![Delizioso Skincare Natural Pressed Foundation](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fh/y6/a3hr5nrs_400x629.jpg)

For those who prefer a powder, this all-natural pressed foundation offers beautiful coverage and zero powdery mess. Easy, effortless application, nine skin-matching shades and a perfect finish without any dryness, flakes, or cake-face makes this high-end, non-toxic foundation a no-brainer, especially for those with oily skin.

## 10. Pure Anada Sheer Radiance Tinted Moisturizer

![Pure Anada Sheer Radiance Tinted Moisturizer](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/po/lb/f3ejpcah_600x407.jpg)

For those with dry or mature skin who are looking for a natural glow, a healthy dose of hydration and light coverage, this is the perfect solution. The incredibly creamy formula offers natural sun protection and a gorgeous glow that instantly perfects skin for a naturally flawless finish. It minimizes pores and imperfections while letting the natural beauty of your skin shine through.

## 11. Everyday Minerals Finishing Dust

![Everyday Minerals Finishing Dust](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/mu/ix/dg6n55tx_600x545.jpg)

To set your makeup and control shine, a setting powder is the final step in your flawless face routine. Everyday Minerals offers a truly natural, super simple formula with a cornstarch base to lock your makeup in place and send shine packing. Available in four shades, spanning the very fair to the deep, these powders impart a seamless, invisible finish. The perfect finishing touch!

I swear by these non-toxic gems. What products do you use for your most flawless face? Have you tried anything on this list? Let me know!
