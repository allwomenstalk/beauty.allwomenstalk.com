## 1. Bella Schneider Beauty Luminous Lifting Gold Rubber Mask

_ labelledayspas.com_
Price: $15.00
Brighten, tighten and refresh your face with this results-oriented peel off mask made from protein-packed rice powder. Simply mix the powder into the mask gel cup until it turns into a honey consistency, then generously spread on your entire face and neck area and leave it on for 10 minutes. When the mask forms to an elastic consistency that molds to your face remove it off from the top down. Rinse off the residue with a cool compress.
