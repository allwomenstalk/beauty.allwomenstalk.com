# 7 Hot Celebrity Beauty Lines to Check out ...

Have you noticed the recent influx of celebrity beauty lines these days? We’re used to seeing celebrity fragrances but it appears that celebs are taking things a step further by creating a collection of beauty products. So whether you love the celeb or maybe you love to hate them, you just might become a fan of one these celebrity beauty lines! Let’s check them out!

## 1. Nuance by Salma Hayek

One of the more recent drugstore celebrity beauty lines is the Nuance brand. Salma says her grandmother’s homemade beauty recipes inspired her beauty collection and made them without parabens and aren't tested on animals. Some of the standout products in the line are the Jojoba dual phase body oil and the Flawless Mineral Cheek Trio. Check out Salma’s collection of beauty products at CVS!

## 2. Kat Von D by Kat Von D

If you’re a fan of Kat Von D’s edgy style, you’re probably a big fan of her makeup as well! Her collection of makeup, primers and fragrances has been around for a few years but many ladies can’t get enough of the super pigmented eye shadows, smooth liquid eye liner and tattoo concealer! Look for Kat Von D’s products at Sephora stores and create your own rock n roll look!

## 3. The Queen Collection by Queen Latifah

Queen Latifah is one of the few Covergirls who got their own celebrity beauty line through the drugstore beauty brand. Her Queen Collection beauty products often receives praise for their creamy and lush lip colors, lasting matte pressed powder and Lash Fanatic mascara. If you love Queen Latifah’s style, you can also check out her line of clothing at HSN also!

## 4. Khroma Beauty by Kourtney, Kim and Khloe Kardashian

Khroma Beauty is the newest and possibly the most controversial beauty collection on this list. Aside from the brand’s legal woes, the sisters Kardashian created their own line of lashes, eye shadows, a nude lip gloss set and makeup palettes. So far, the reviews seem to be mixed, many people feel the quality is subpar whereas others are in love with the products. Check them out for yourself at Ulta and other drugstores!

## 5. Josie Maran by Josie Maran

Model Josie Maran created a line of natural and eco-friendly beauty products that accentuates natural beauty and doesn’t mask the skin. One of her most well-known products is Moroccan Argan oil which can be used for a variety of skin issues and can even be used to nourish dry hair. But that’s not all - the line also has natural mascara, hair serum and tinted moisturizer!

## 6. IMAN Cosmetics

Another fashion model turned entrepreneur is Iman. Iman has been in the fashion scene since 1976 and is considered to be a innovator in ethnic cosmetics. Her celebrity beauty line, Iman Cosmetics, was created in 1994 and continues to provide makeup for women of all skin tones and ethnicities. You can find Iman Cosmetics products at Target, Walgreens, Sears and drugstore.com

## 7. Katy Perry Lashes by Eylure

If you love Katy Perry’s bold and daring makeup looks, her line of faux lashes should be right up your alley! Bat your lashes and make the boys drool with Katy’s signature line of sweet and sassy lashes. There’s a pair for everyone ranging from the lash novice to the professional and even come pre-glued so there’s no fuss! Her lashes have rave reviews so pick some up next time you’re in need of some fringe benefits!

Celebrity beauty lines certainly have come a long way! The celeb beauty lines in the past were somewhat disappointing, (remember Jessica Simpson’s toxic tasting body frosting?) so it’s nice to see that there are some good quality beauty products out there! Do you buy beauty products from celebrity beauty lines? What’s your favorite?
