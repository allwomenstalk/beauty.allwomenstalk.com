# 7 Perfect Ways to Look Pretty without Makeup ...

Ever want to leave the house sans makeup but are afraid there are no ways to look pretty without makeup? That is so not so! There are lots of ways we can go without any beauty products and still look pretty as a picture. Still don’t believe me? Then let’s explore seven of the many different ways to look pretty without makeup together and you can try it for yourself!

## 1. Brows

![Brows](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/o7/h3/nq8mdz8l5505d15eea625907666195_442x442.jpg)

[@adrianalima](https://www.facebook.com/unsupportedbrowser)

One of the surefire ways to look pretty without makeup is to rock some clean and well-groomed brows. There’s no way you can get away with beat-up looking brows, with or without makeup, so let’s not even attempt to bypass this important step! Make sure your eyebrows are trimmed, plucked and groomed so they properly frame your face.

## 2. Exfoliate

![Exfoliate](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/bu/r9/hfknaje45505d1aab1568579049530_392x478.jpg)

[@ciara](https://www.facebook.com/unsupportedbrowser)

If you want to look good without makeup, you’ve gotta take good care of your skin! One of the keys to clear, healthy skin starts with exfoliating dead skin cells so you can show off fresh skin. Exfoliating a few times a week will keep skin fresh, smooth and radiant. Exfoliating regularly also helps your skin look better, which is always a plus when you’re going out without makeup!

## 3. Lashes

![Lashes](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/h5/n9/uyhwz2ki5505d1e83cc91117649370_480x720.jpg)

[@dianekrugerperso](https://www.facebook.com/unsupportedbrowser)

Another important aspect of looking good without makeup is to emphasize those lashes! Our lashes are sort of like the curtains to the windows to our soul so you know we’ve got to dress ‘em up! Celebrity makeup artist Carmindy suggests adding a thin coat of Vaseline to your lashes to make them look longer, fuller and darker! If you want to take your lash look up a notch, use an eyelash curler to really make them stand out!

## 4. Lips

![Lips](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/c4/ij/bkm9xibl5505d21fdea1e442340407_480x480.jpg)

[@badgalriri](https://www.facebook.com/unsupportedbrowser)

We mustn’t neglect our lips when we’re trying to look good without makeup! Our faces aren’t the only place that needs exfoliation; our lips need it, too! Make a quick lip scrub with sugar and olive oil or use a damp cloth to gently scrub your lips. Next, give them a coat of a moisturizing lip balm to give lips a nice sheen and to keep them hydrated.

## 5. Moisturize

![Moisturize](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/h1/d9/a5l4fpvk5505d254508f9950506141_480x480.jpg)

[@lupitanyongo](https://www.facebook.com/unsupportedbrowser)

No matter what type of skin you have, keeping your skin moisturized is essential to a smooth and glowing complexion. Moisturizer will help keep your skin hydrated and looking healthy and supple. Skipping moisturizer can cause skin to look dried out and dull, so keep skin soft and naturally luminous with a quality moisturizer with SPF to shield skin from the sun.

## 6. Eyes

![Eyes](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/gm/wz/wj87ho6q5505d298ca081229101506_434x450.jpg)

[@kendalljenner](https://www.facebook.com/unsupportedbrowser)

If you’re skipping makeup because you’re too tired or you overslept, redness reducing eye drops will be your best defense against bloodshot or tired-looking eyes. A quick way to refresh your eyes and look good without makeup is to use moisturizing and redness reducing eye drops to really make the whites of your eyes stand out and look more awake!

## 7. Teeth

![Teeth](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/t3/v8/zeagg1885505d2c22eb38764956525_480x720.jpg)

[@drewbarrymore](https://www.facebook.com/unsupportedbrowser)

Another easy way to look pretty without makeup is to whiten your teeth so your smile is always sparkly and bright! Use whitening strips or a gel pen to whiten and brighten teeth. Then, keep up all your hard work by using a whitening toothpaste and avoiding staining drinks and foods like coffee, wine, berries and beets.

As you can see, there are lots ways to look good without makeup! Wearing makeup is a fun way to accentuate your features and cover up blemishes, but we can certainly give our skin a break sometimes and go without.
