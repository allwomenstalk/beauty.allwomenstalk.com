# 10 Facts about Moisturizing Lotions and Moisturizing Products for Dry Skin ...

At the point when we happen to have dry skin, it is a difficult task to get it back on track when not using the proper routine. Dry skin tends to show up with flakes just like the dry skin of the scalp that starts developping dandruff. When we wipe it with a dry towel, it accumulates and falls off. The skin patches turn red. And we end up scratching our skin more and more as the dry skin happens to be very itchy most of the time.

This is the classic way that our skin reacts when brought to this condition. Dry skin can be as embarrassing as it is uncomfortable since applying foundation becomes a virtually impossible task. Not only it becomes red and itchy but it also becomes prone to premature aging.

**What Causes Dry Skin?**

## 1. The Heat

In this situation, the excess heat can be one of the main culprits of your skin's dryness. The excess heat aggravates the dryness in the first place. It sucks out the moisture of the skin. The dry air, the sun, hot showers, harsh soaps, and many other products can make the skin extremely dry.

## 2. Aging

The natural aging process of the skin is another culprit behind the dryness of the skin. Normally, the natural oils of our skin get depleted as we age. When aging gradually, our skin bids goodbye to the moisture and says hello to the dryness. The oil glands are no longer able to produce enough moisturize for the skin to be lubricated at all times.

## 3. The Diet

The diet may also have a considerable impact on the quality of your skin. Vitamins A and B are the fundamentals when it comes to keeping your skin hydrated. In case our skin lacks these vitamins, it becomes prone to aging and dryness.

## 4. Genes

Dry skin and wrinkles may also appear on your skin due to your genetic background. If our parents exhibit premature signs of aging, there is a great chance that we too are predisposed to the same condition. If you have skin disorders such as psoriasis, eczema, seborrhea, and dermatitis, you can be sure that you have dry skin, and you need to start treating your skin to elevate your condition.

## 5. Medical Conditions and Pharmaceuticals

There are a lot of conditions such as diabetes, and thyroid disorder can make our skin parched. When our skin happens to be a lot intense to the dryness, at that point, it is crucial to visit a dermatologist so that he or she can evaluate our skin. Some pharmaceuticals medicines can lead our skin to dryness, such as diuretics or antihistamines. These medicines are helpful for treating certain things within your body, but they may be devasting for your skin.

**What Is The Solution?**

## 6. Moisturizing Lotions Are Key

Now, what should we do if we face all these issues with our skin? What is the solution to these problems that can alleviate the off lines, irritations, and wrinkles on our skin? Well, you must have heard about the moisturizing lotions. Yes, that's right. Moisturizing lotions will be your best friends in this battle. The moisturizing products that work with your skin are the only solution to this condition.

## 7. What the Moisturizer Should do

Dry's skin only fix is moisturizing skincare products. The [perfection moisture](https://exoskinsimple.com/blogs/news/the-top-things-to-know-when-picking-a-modern-moisturizer) components can bring relief from dryness almost in no time. The reduce the dryness, we have to replenish the moisture by using skincare products and locking the moisture in. While looking for a moisturizing product, we have to make sure that we buy the right one that penetrates deep within our skin and helps our skin to be luxurious, smooth, and relieved from the dryness. After using the product, there should be no more redness, flaking, or itchiness. Reducing the appearance of wrinkles should be another objective in using a moisturizing skincare product.

## 8. Jojoba, Avocado, Soybean Oils and Other Ingredients to Look for

When you visit the market, you are likely to find a lot of moisturizing products available. Among thousands of products, you have to get the one that suits best on your skin without being harsh. You should look at the ingredient label, and get the one that comes with natural ingredients. Products that come with botanical-based oils such as jojoba, avocadoes, soybeans, and almonds are one of the best products you can find in the market today. Besides, some products may be useful for you that come with added vitamins and omega-3 acids. These components will help your skin to replenish the moisture.

## 9. Look for Almond Oil Moisturizer

Indeed, even before going to your usual moisturizers, consider the chemicals you use on your skin. In case you're utilizing bar cleansers that strip the skin of its healthy oils, you have to go to an even longer length to keep your skin hydrated. Again, look for almond oil in your skin cleansing products. Almond oil is amazingly rich in unsaturated fats, explicitly oleic, and linoleic unsaturated fats. These are fundamental unsaturated fats that your skin requires to achieve that full, youthful look.

As the essential unsaturated fats are exhausted with age, the cell layers of your skin are affected. The skin gets thinner and drier. Almond oil will nurture the skin, lock the oils in, and make your skin feel smooth and healthy. The almond oil properties are such that it will glide smoothly onto your skin. There is no better feeling than that. Also, the aroma of almond is exceptionally satisfying!

## 10. Check the PH Levels

Something else to consider when searching for your dry skin's chemical is to check the pH of the cleaning agent. The ideal would be a pH of 8.5. Anything more acidic would make your skin progressively inclined to dryness. Look for an excellent product for cleansing your skin, this will be the first step toward a superior skincare regime. The product that contains pure almond oil, hydrolyzed collagen (amino acids), and has a pH of 8.5 will be the best choice.

Battle with dry skin is definitely a long one, unless done properly with the right cleansers and moisturizers. As long as you pay attention to how your skin feels on a daily basis, it will take you very little to get back on track.
