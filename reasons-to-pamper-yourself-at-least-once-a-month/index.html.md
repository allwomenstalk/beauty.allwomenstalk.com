# 7 Reasons to Pamper Yourself at Least Once a Month ...

Ladies, don't you just love all the wonderful reasons to pamper yourself? Don't think for a second that you don't deserve a little pampering now and again, because it's definitely a necessity with the grime of daily life. Whether it's a mani/pedi, new haircut, shopping trip or Swedish massage, pick one of your favorite ways to relax and enjoy some solace once a month! Here are a few reasons to pamper yourself that might just convince you to make your next appointment.

## 1. To Feel Girly

One of the very best reasons to pamper yourself is to just feel girly! Break out the feminine side of you and get your nails done or a fancy up-do just because. Remember when you were really young and always loved painted nails, princess dresses and lipstick? That same joy can be experienced through a little pampering and the right mindset. Be thankful you're a women and bask in your girly side once in a while!

## 2. To Relax

My favorite reason to get pampered is to simply relax. It's hard to relax at times, and did you know some women physically can't relax? They're minds won't let them! It does take practice for those who don't relax easily. Learn to calm yourself and just enjoy a few moments of peace without distraction. That's why getting a massage or a pedicure is a wonderful tool for relaxing- you don't have other distractions around you!

## 3. To Feel Beautiful

I believe most all women like to feel beautiful and be told they are by their friends, family and significant others. Sometimes a little pampering can make you feel at your best. A new haircut and color can go a long way in how you feel about your physical appearance! And don't you just love getting your makeup done? Pick up some of your favorite beauty products and get a makeover while you're at it!

## 4. To Boost Your Confidence

I have terrible nails and cuticles and end up painting them often because they break and peel and don't look well kept. That's why when I get a professional manicure, I feel more confident! I know it sounds silly but when my nails look classy and pretty, I feel more put together and confident about my overall appearance. What boosts your confidence?

## 5. To Melt Away Stress

Is stress taking its toll on you? Try a therapeutic shopping trip with just you or a close girlfriend. A little retail therapy can be just the key to taking that edge of stress away. And don't forget about tension release massages! Those can work wonders in relieving built-up stress and anxiety.

## 6. To Have Some "Me" Time

Looking for some quality time alone? Take some time to pamper yourself! Don't underestimate the power of some alone time to recharge. Doing something you love while relaxing and getting pampered is incredibly beneficial!

## 7. To Have Something Simple to Admire

A fresh manicure, a new haircut or cute new outfit can bring feelings of happiness because you now have something pretty and simple to admire! I remember this poem from college that's still one of my favorites. It describes the reasons the author would paint her nails, and the one that stuck out to me most was that she did it so she would have something to look at in traffic. Now I can't help but glance down at my mani while stuck in traffic!

I would love to hear the ways you ladies love to pamper yourselves! Share them in the comments!
