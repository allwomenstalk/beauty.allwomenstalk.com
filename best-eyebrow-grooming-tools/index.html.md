# 7 Best Eyebrow Grooming Tools ...

It seems like eyebrow grooming tools are often overlooked in the world of beauty. Even though our brows beautifully shape our eyes and make a huge difference in how we look, we’re usually preoccupied with other eye makeup products to accentuate our eyes. If you’re looking for a polished look with flawlessly groomed eyebrows, definitely check out this list of the best eyebrow grooming tools!

## 1. Eyebrow Brush

One of the top eyebrow grooming tools to own is the eyebrow brush. We typically see the eyebrow brush paired with the eyelash brush, and they are also called eyebrow/lash combs or groomers. This little tool is essential when you groom eyebrows because it helps volumize and set brows in place. Did you get overly enthusiastic with the powder or pencil? Not to worry, this eyebrow tool also helps to blend color!

## 2. Eyebrow Pencil

The eyebrow pencil is another helpful eyebrow tool that we can use to frame our eyes. Using an eyebrow pencil not only helps us to fill in and shade sparse brows, but they also help emphasize your gorgeous eyes! Some tips to keep in mind while using an eyebrow pencil are to use a color that’s a shade or two lighter than your natural brow color and stay away from waxy formulas that can look unnatural.

## 3. Eyebrow Powder

Another super popular eyebrow tool is eyebrow powder. Some ladies prefer eyebrow powder to pencil as it’s easier to use and some say it looks more natural. Use brow powder with a good angled brush to shade in and fill in uneven or thin eyebrows to get fuller, more youthful looking brows!

## 4. Eyebrow Gel

Do you have unruly brows? Maybe they have a bit of curl to them or perhaps they just don’t like to cooperate. Either way, you can easily tame and straighten them out with just a quick swipe of eyebrow gel! This handy dandy eyebrow tool comes colored or clear to keep stray brows in place and keep them looking clean and sophisticated!

## 5. Mini Scissors

Tiny scissors might not be something you keep at home as an eyebrow tool, especially if you get your eyebrows done professionally, but they’re pretty convenient. Mini scissors trim stray or long hairs that are out of place and help you achieve a more even and uniform look. If you trim your cuticles, these scissors will also come in handy when you’re giving yourself a mani!

## 6. Tweezers

Tweezers are perhaps the most popular and important eyebrow tool to use when you groom eyebrows. Tweezers pluck any stray hairs and are used to help create arches and shape the brows. You’ve also got a few options when it comes to tweezers, there are slanted, pointed, rounded, arched, and straight tweezers that are all useful tools. While slant tip tweezers are the most commonly used, read up on all of the different benefits and see which works best for your needs!

## 7. Eyebrow Wax

Eyebrow waxes are a little less common but they work just as well as gels in grooming eyebrows. Waxes work similarly to eyebrow gels by taming and straightening out wild hairs. Eyebrow waxes also come in colored or clear formulas to shape or seal in brows colored in with pencil or powder. Use a colored wax to fake fuller eyebrows while creating a beautiful shape!

These eyebrow grooming tools can help you achieve an impeccable and pulled together look. Our eyebrows can easily make or break a look, so it’s essential to care for and groom them properly. Do you have any tips on grooming eyebrows? What are you favorite eyebrow grooming tools?
