# The Most Delicious Scents from Bath & Body Works ...

Are you wondering what are **the best scents at Bath and Body Works**? Bath & Body Works LLC is an American retailer and was founded in 1990 in New Albany, Ohio. It has expanded across the United States, Canada, Chili and Peru. In 1997, it was the largest bath shop in the United States. It specializes in shower gels, lotions, fragrance mists, perfumes, candles and home fragrances. So, what are the **the best scents at Bath and Body Works**?
