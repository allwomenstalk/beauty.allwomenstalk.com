# 5 Inspirational Ways to Leave the House Feeling Beautiful without Makeup ...

Want some **ways to leave the house feeling beautiful without makeup**? Decide to use those extra 20 minutes to get some beauty sleep? No problem! Get ready and leave the house feeling confident and beautiful in just 5 minutes. Here are the best **ways to leave the house feeling beautiful without makeup**.

## 1. Wash Your Face with Cold Water

Wash your face with cold water when you wake up. This will give you a little morning energy boost and close up your pores to keep them clear through the day!

## 2. Use Eye Cream…Everywhere!

Moisturize your face using eye cream in the morning. Eye cream reduces puffiness and tightens the skin. So why not put those benefits to use all over your face?!

## 3. Wet Those Brows and Lashes

Make your eyebrows and lashes pop, but without makeup. Use rose scented Vaseline to wet your eyebrows slightly and brush them into the shape you want. Then wet your eyelashes with the Vaseline and curl them with your eyelash curler. This will give you a glowy, effortless look.

## 4. Use Tinted Lip Balm on Your Lips and Cheeks

Use Burt’s Bees Tinted Lip Balm in your favorite shade to moisturize your lips and give your cheeks a little color. Using the same color on your lips and cheeks will also help to tie your look together.

## 5. Smile!

You are beautiful with or without makeup! Smile to show the world that you know strong, confident women and men are the most beautiful people in the world. Rock your beautiful, fresh and glowing face.
