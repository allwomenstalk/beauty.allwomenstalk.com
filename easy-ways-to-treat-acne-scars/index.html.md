# 8 Easy Ways to Treat Acne Scars ...

The worst part about acne has got to be the lasting scars. So how does one get rid of these scars and move on? Let Alda help you with her tips. Thanks Alda!

Okay, so a few weeks ago I did an article about home remedies for acne and some of you asked if I can do an article for treating acne scars so below are a few that hopefully will work for you to treat acne scars. I know it's not easy but with time and using the right things it gets better.

## 1. Cucumber

Cucumber is great for helping to keep the skin smooth and get rid of acne scars. If you haven't noticed by now that many different cosmetic beauty products have cucumber as a key ingredient because it helps your skin to get better.

## 2. Tea Tree Oil

Tea tree oil is fantastic and it helps to reduce the effects of acne scars. This oil comes comes from Australia and is used for healing acne scars and also preventing outbreaks from occurring. I usually buy tea tree oil at a whole foods market.

## 3. Aloe Vera

Aloe Vera has been used for a long time as a natural remedy to cure acne scars. The Aloe Vera plants are famous for their magically ability to help heal scars and wounds. The juice of the Aloe Vera helps to heal acne scars and fight breakouts. If you use aloe vera lotion or the juice on a regular basis, you will see great results. It's proven fact.

## 4. Fenugreek

Fenugreek is an important and great solution to treat acne scars. What I do is usually make a paste out of fenugreek leaves and use it as a face mask and leave it on for an hour or so. Another thing you can is boil Fenugreek seeds in water and use it on acne scars after it cools down! I love the feeling and the results I get from fenugreek.

## 5. Olive Oil

I mentioned it before but olive oil is great for the acne scars. It works like a miracle. Smooths, gets rid of redness and makes your face so much better. Leave it on as much as you want. Make sure to wash it off with a warm cloth when you decide to take it off.

## 6. Apple Vinegar

It's not just for dieting its also for acne scaring. Just put it on after you clean your face and leave it on for about 30 minutes. Rinse your face with warm or cold water and dry it. You'll see great results if its done everyday.

## 7. Egg Whites

I learned this from my grandma that when I was little I would get hurt and later get a scar. She would come up and put egg white on my face and the scar would be gone in a few days. Take an egg and just use the white part. Put it in a bowl and after washing your hands apply to your face a thin layer. Leave it on for 30-45 minutes and wash it off. Do this daily.

## 8. Honey

Honey is so sweet for your face that you'll see those scars go away. Just put honey on your clean face and leave it on for as much as you want. Make sure it doesn't drip all over your shirt because it usually does.

You can't get rid of acne scars overnight don't listen to anyone that says its possible! Try to use natural beauty products on your skin so you don't damage those acne scars even more. Eating a healthy diet, drinking water and exercising can also help reducing those acne scars. Another important thing is to not pick at your newly pimples because it will leave you with those scars that you try to get rid off. Enjoy your life free of acne scars.
