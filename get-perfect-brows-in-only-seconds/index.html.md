# Get Perfect Brows in Only 10 Seconds  ...

You don't have to spend an hour in front of the mirror to make your brows look perfect. According to [Marie Claire](https://www.marieclaire.com/beauty/), all you need is ten seconds. Here's what you should be doing:

## 1. Brush Brows down

![Brush Brows down](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/w9/z2/jeusmn3g5670329f6bc71918240338_480x720.jpg)

Grab a spoolie and brush down all of those hairs. "This help expose your skin, so the crayon has something to adhere to."

## 2. ADD COLOR and SHINE

![ADD COLOR and SHINE](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/q7/vh/rmiywvg65670329f592c1884807843_480x720.jpg)

Choose a color that matches the natural color of your brows. Then add color with super short strokes, to make them look like little hairs.

## 3. BRUSH BROWS BACK up

![BRUSH BROWS BACK up](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/no/vb/uvbnbzvb5670329fc907c144765525_480x720.jpg)

Now it's time to brush your hairs back into place with the spoolie. And you're all done!
