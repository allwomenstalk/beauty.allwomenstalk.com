# 7 Products for Refreshing Your Look in the Middle of the Day ...

I hate when I go to the bathroom at lunchtime and look into the mirror, only to see that my makeup is fading and my carefully styled hair isn’t looking that great anymore. It turns out that if I am more careful when I pack my beauty bag, I can quickly and easily refresh my look for the rest of the day. You can do the same by making sure you always have these products on hand during the day.

## 1. Mascara Resurrection is a Must Have

![hair, black hair, face, eyebrow, person,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/yh/ej/i9l582q7588fa7ddbada0428769718_600x600.jpg)

Urban Decay does a great version of this product, but any version will do the trick. It’s a softer version of mascara that works to refresh the product you put on your lashes this morning. It will help revive your lashes without adding so much extra mascara that you have that dreaded clumpy look that you hate so much. Just a quick swipe and you’ll see a huge difference.

## 2. A Perfume Pendant Keeps You Smelling Fresh

![clothing, cap, fur, fur clothing, fashion accessory,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/o6/gs/c9q265rx588fa8270275c461364412_600x600.jpg)

If you feel like your scent fades by midday, you’ll love wearing a perfume pendant. You simply load the necklace with your favorite perfume and you can reapply it as often as you need to throughout a busy day. Perfect!

## 3. Put Flyaways Back in Their Place with anti-Frizz Sheets

![hair, eyebrow, face, hairstyle, beauty,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/r2/hd/gr2xr2em588fa839edf5b118959201_600x723.jpg)

You’ve probably heard that dryer sheets can combat frizzy hair. Now hair product companies are marketing something similar that is specially designed for frizzy strands. Use an anti-frizz sheet to tame hairs that aren’t lying flat so that you can finish the day with the polished hair do you created this morning.

## 4. Refresh Your Facial Skin with a Skin Spray

![hair, black, clothing, black hair, person,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/vy/go/kmsuz1l2588fa85e47b8d905664761_600x600.jpg)

Sometimes called an essence, this magical product will help refresh your face and keep skin hydrated and healthy. Not only is this great for your skin, but most of these products also help create a new radiance when it comes to keeping your makeup looking as fabulous as it did when you left the house this morning.

## 5. You’ve Got to Have Blotting Sheets for Shine

![hair, human hair color, face, eyebrow, blond,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ij/ja/ed8gychn588fa894e8690787980855_600x600.jpg)

I hate when my skin gets shiny in the middle of the day. If you suffer from the same problem, your makeup bag should always have some blotting sheets. All you have to do is press them against your skin and they’ll soak up excess grease, leaving your with that matte look you crave. The best part? Most of them will keep your makeup intact.

## 6. Use No Rinse Body Foam after a Lunchtime Workout

![human action, clothing, human positions, blond, sitting,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/sk/nk/s9jmc4kx588fa945eb2fc676894320_500x500.jpg)

You had time for a quick run of the treadmill or a yoga class, but now you have to head back to work and can’t get a shower. No rinse body foam works like hand sanitizer. All you have to do is swipe it over your body and it removes germs, sweat, grime and bacteria so you can go back to the office clean and fresh.

## 7. Dry Shampoo Will Refresh Your Hair

![eyewear, hair, human hair color, glasses, face,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/j8/v6/dv3dwqv1588fa8fca1773761807841_600x600.jpg)

Dry shampoo is sort of like blotting papers, but for your hair instead of your skin. When you spray it on your strands, the product soaks up grease, helping revitalize your hair when it starts to fall limp and less than perfect. Dry shampoo is ideal for refreshing your hair after a workout.
