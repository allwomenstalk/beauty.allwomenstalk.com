# Would You Ever Get a Mystery Tattoo?

According to [BuzzFeed](https://www.buzzfeed.com/juliegerstein/a-bunch-of-people-let-a-tattoo-artist-tattoo-them-without-se), a tattoo artist named Scott Campbell recently gave 25 different people free tattoos--but they had no idea what they would be getting. In fact, they were completely separated from him by a wall until he was finished, so that the tattoos would be a complete surprise.
