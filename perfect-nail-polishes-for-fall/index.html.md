# 8 Perfect Nail Polishes for Fall ...

The best accessory this fall is lacquer. Prepare to say goodbye to your bright summer polishes, come September and begin brushing on the perfect nail polishes for fall. Fall’s fingertip colour palette draws its inspiration from the season’s runway collections including a range of jewel tones and metallics. Sport these rich statement shades for a chic look available at your fingertips.

## 1. Red Hot

[img.allw.mn](https://img.allw.mn/content/beauty/2012/08/1_300x300.png)
**Price:** $8 at _essie.com_

Red is a real classic when it comes to nail polish. This season you can take this traditional colour up a few notches by opting for a darker metallic red. Essie’s «Scarlett O’hara» is a deep ruby-red that is spot on for fall’s jewel tone palette.

## 2. Tempting Teal

![Tempting Teal](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/beauty/2012/08/21_225x225.jpg)

**Price:** $8 at [zoya.com](https://www.zoya.com/content/38/item/Zoya/Zoya-Nail-Polish-Frida-ZP640.html)

Teal is a super haute hue right now. The deeply rich and lush colour is a creamy combination of playful sophistication with an unexpected pop of boldness. A warm toned teal shade tones down the brightness of the hue, which is ideal for day or night.

## 3. Brown Cocoa

![Brown Cocoa](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/beauty/2012/08/3_brown-cocoa_600x920.jpg)

**Price:** $18 at [shop.nordstrom.com](http://shop.nordstrom.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)

Cozy up with this shade of brown as you would with a cup of hot cocoa on a chilly day. If you are more into warm, earthy tones this chocolate inspired colour is a simple replacement for your usual taupe tones. Don’t be put off by this deep dark dreamy brown as like most colours brown comes in so many shade variations that you’ll be able to find one that is best suited for you.

## 4. Nearly Nude

![Nearly Nude](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/beauty/2012/08/sephora-300x300_300x300.jpg)

**Price:** $5 at [sephora.com](https://goo.gl/Rx3JGg)

Sheer shades and nude tones are a staple to have in your manicure kit regardless of season. Nude tones are perfect for anytime of day but especially perfect for office wear if your work in a more reserved setting. The best thing about nude tones is that you can find shade with undertone hints of pink or brown, which helps match a variety of skin tones, making nude one of the most universal nail polishes ever!

## 5. Magnetic Mauve

**Price:** $4.79 at _target.com_

If you enjoy your pink but want to keep a subdued shade for fall then mauve is a classic alternative. This mauve colour is on trend when it comes to the retro ladylike dressing looks we’ve seen on the runway. Mauve adds a touch of soft feminine charm among the dark and jewel tones of fall.

## 6. Cool Gray

_ **Price:** $21 at narscosmetics.ca_

This crazy cool colour is a new and edgy take on your standard black polish. The colour is softer than black, which still gives it a rock ‘n’ roll feel while still being chic. Try it in dark stormy colours or choose a more metallic finish with flecks of silver in the polish. Either way this neutral shade makes a huge impact and compliments any look this season.

## 7. Purple Reign

**Price:** $5 at [ulta.com](http://www.ulta.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)

Bold berry shades get a fierce update this season dark undercurrents and metallic finishes. Purple’s pop of colour is fun and playful with funky textures and sparkle. If you are more into red shades try a blackened berry shade to amp up the sexiness and add a bit of gothic romance to your autumn look. These purple shades are still feminine but have an offbeat cool about them.

## 8. Fall Orange

![Fall Orange](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/beauty/2012/08/lipman-240x300_240x300.jpg)

**Price:** $16 at [neimanmarcus.com](http://www.neimanmarcus.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)

Orange is the quintessential shade of fall. Its bright colour adds a festive touch and pop. This polish by Deborah Lippmann combines full-bodied reds with intense golds to create this shimmering rust shade. The colour is still earthy and warm but has a bold intensity with jewel like quality for a rich and luxurious look that brightens up any fall day.

Feel the fall fever at your fingertips this season with the perfect nail polishes for fall in warm, jewel and earthy tones to add autumn’s finishing touch. What is your favourite fall nail polish?
