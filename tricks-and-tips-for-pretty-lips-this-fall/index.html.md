# 7 Tricks and Tips for Pretty Lips This Fall ...

Wouldn’t we all like to have some tips for pretty lips this fall? Fall and winter can be fairly harsh on our lips with the dropping temps and dry air. But you needn’t worry; it is possible to have beautifully kissable lips all year round when you follow these tips for pretty lips. You will have gorgeous lips in no time.

## 1. Exfoliate Daily

![Exfoliate Daily](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/09/10211024_9871_466x700.jpg)

One of the best tips for pretty lips is to exfoliate your lips daily. You can do this several different ways. One of the easiest and quickest ways to exfoliate your lips is to use a wet toothbrush with nothing on it and rub it back and forth across your lips in a gentle manner. This will remove the dead skin and leave your lips looking fuller and healthier. There are also lip exfoliators that you can purchase in the health and beauty section.

## 2. Keep Moisturized

![Keep Moisturized](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/09/10211052_9591_426x640.jpg)

Your lips will look much prettier if you keep them moisturized. The best way to do this is to keep a lip product on them. This includes using a lip balm at bedtime. Keeping it on your nightstand can help you to remember to apply it before you go to sleep. Following this habit will help you to avoid chapped lips this fall.

## 3. Swap out Your Lip Colors

![Swap out Your Lip Colors](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/09/10211102_4672_521x783.jpg)

Just like you swap out your summer clothing for fall clothing, you should swap out your summer lip colors for fall lip colors. This is actually one of the best parts of fall for me. How fun to wear something different or shop for new lip colors. Put away your sheers and go for a lip color that has a bit more depth to it. Think muddy pinks and lip colors with a hint of plum.

## 4. Drink up

![Drink up](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/09/10211132_0461_500x598.jpg)

One way to keep your lips pretty this fall is to keep them hydrated. Every part of our body needs water, including our lips. When your lips are dry, they will look smaller and be more likely to chap, crack and bleed. Drinking an adequate amount of water each day will help them to look healthy and look plumper, which is something we all want. Keep a water bottle nearby to keep your lips looking totally kissable.

## 5. Stop Licking Them

![Stop Licking Them](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/09/10211202_9606_400x600.jpg)

This is a terrible habit and one I have caught myself doing from time to time. This makes your lips dry out very quickly, as well as crack and bleed. The best way to break a bad habit is to replace it with a new one. When you catch yourself starting to lick your lips, stop and go get some lip balm to apply. Eventually, your new habit will take the place of the old one and you won’t have to think about it any more.

## 6. Lay down the Smokes

![Lay down the Smokes](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/09/10211212_9438_500x620.jpg)

Cigarettes are terrible for your health, all the way around. But they are bad for your lips as well. Smoking will cause many wrinkles around your lips from years of using your lips to inhale the smoke. It can be a hard habit to break but it can be done. There are even medications your doctor can prescribe to help you break this awful habit.

## 7. Apply an anti-aging Cream around Them

![Apply an anti-aging Cream around Them](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/09/10211353_7539_433x640.jpg)

As we age, our lips begin to age, as well as the skin around them. This can make your lips look unattractive as well as causing your lipstick to bleed. You can help to prevent this by applying an anti-aging cream around your lips. You do not have to purchase a separate anti-aging product for this. I use just a touch of my eye cream around my lips at bedtime.
