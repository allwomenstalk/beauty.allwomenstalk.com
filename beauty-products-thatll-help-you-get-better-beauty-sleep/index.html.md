# Beauty Products Thatll Help You Get Better Beauty Sleep ...

I think we can all agree that getting enough sleep is vital to looking and feeling good. Having too many late nights can wreak serious havoc on our skin including those dreaded dark circles and even accelerate the aging process! But let’s not focus on the negative, we’re here to talk about rad beauty products that help you get better beauty sleep. That’s right, try out these beauty products to maximize your beauty sleep and wake up looking like a million bucks. Check them out for yourself below!

![Farmacy Sleep Tight Firming Night Balm](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/m9/of/ssi241205617121bb7019376358270_300x232.jpg)

There are many reasons to fall in love with Farmacy’s Night Balm. First off, Farmacy products are formulated with farm-grown Echinacea GreenEnvy which is a patented ingredient that contains the highest concentration of a natural antioxidant that helps protect and firm up our skin. Another unique feature of this product is that it’s a balm that transforms into an oil-gel. Apply this to your clean face before bed and wake up to softer, more youthful skin.
Available at Sephora for $48

![SEPHORA COLLECTION Green Tea Sleeping Mask](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/lp/z1/pkpty8j45617121bf2938905379355_300x248.jpg)

Have you used any of Sephora’s masks yet? I’ve tried their sheet masks, but this Sleeping Mask is new to me and I’m excited to try it. It comes in 8 different varieties which all address different skin care concerns and melts into your skin so that the active ingredients work while you’re snoozing. It’s also super portable for pampering on the go.
Available at Sephora for $4

![DERMALOGICA Clear Start Breakout Clearing Overnight Treatment](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/zb/nc/u17eqrei5617121d2f9c3983443802_500x360.jpg)

Wake up to a clearer complexion with this soothing and effective acne treatment that works while you sleep. It’s cooling, calming, and purifying so it combats redness and irritation. The formula is also invisible and easily absorbed so you can rest easy. Now the best defense against breakouts is a good night’s sleep!
Available at Ulta for $19.50

## 4. Vaseline Intensive Care Advanced Relief Healing Serum

![Vaseline Intensive Care Advanced Relief Healing Serum](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/up/fr/sozj6guc5617121e224d8156680446_420x469.jpg)

I don’t know about you, but I almost always apply lotion before I hit the sack. Vaseline’s Healing Serum is the perfect product to apply before bed because it’s easily absorbed and leaves behind a slight fragrance that’s sure to lull you into a blissful sleep. This is also ideal for the colder weather because it’s formulated especially for severely dry skin.
Available at Walmart for $7.97

## 5. Pantene Pro-V Repair and Protect Overnight Miracle Repair Serum

![Pantene Pro-V Repair and Protect Overnight Miracle Repair Serum](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/i5/ma/tc2cztug5617121eb474e067504053_480x459.jpg)

Pantene didn’t forget about our hair. This repair serum is made especially to restore your hair while you sleep. Don’t worry, this isn’t greasy or oily so it won’t leave behind any residue on your pillow. Wake up to smoother, softer, and shinier hair without doing a thing! Works for all hair types.
Available at Target for $3.04.

## 6. ANATOMICALS Be Good with Your Hands in Bed Hand Cream

![ANATOMICALS Be Good with Your Hands in Bed Hand Cream](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fr/jn/uitflqaz5617122025560725172231_600x581.jpg)

During the fall and winter, the skin on our hands can take a real beating. Combat dryness and irritation with Anatomnicals’ cream that’s an intense moisturizing treatment for your hands. Make the treatment even more effective by wearing some gloves or even wrapping your hands with plastic wrap so you soak in all the moisture and wake up to velvety-smooth hands you’ll be proud to show off!
Available at Ulta for $6.99

## 7. Ciaté London Knight in Shining Armor

![Ciaté London Knight in Shining Armor](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/hz/zr/y462peks5617122099900116652178_300x291.jpg)
