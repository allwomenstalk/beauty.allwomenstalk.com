# 7 Lines of Celeb Beauty Products That Are Actually Worth Their Price ...

You hear it all the time. Celebs come out with their own line of shoes, workout gear, swimwear, and much more. Celeb names are also behind untold numbers of beauty product lines too. In some cases, you shouldn’t be duped into buying them just because they have a recognizable name on the label. Instead of wondering which ones are best, you can simply read this list and find out which ones are worth their price tag. Here you go!

## 1. The Honest Company – Jessica Alba

Jessica launched her line of natural beauty products after becoming a mother and wanting products that weren’t full of harmful ingredients. You probably know about the Honest Company for its diapers, but it also offers so much more. You can get sunscreen, lotion, bubble bath, shampoo, conditioner, toothpaste, lip balm, shaving oil, body scrub, soap and hand sanitizer. And the prices are affordable and you totally get plenty of bang for your buck!

## 2. Flower – Drew Barrymore

We all want Drew Barrymore’s effortless, but gorgeous look. Well, now we can get it with her pretty recent line of beauty products, called Flower. The company specializes in lipstick, lip gloss, eyeshadow, mascara, powder and nail polish with price tags that any girl can afford. In fact, you can find them at Wal-Mart. What more could a girl ask for?

## 3. Alterna Haircare – Katie Holmes

Katie is the co-owner of this premium haircare line that actually has caviar in some of its products. The products are designed to completely transform your strands by infusing them with ingredients that lift, hydrate and volumize your head of hair. You are going to love how you look. The best news is that the products aren’t as expensive as you might expect.

## 4. JWoww Skincare – JWoww

She of Jersey Shore fame now has her own line of skincare products. You can get anything from cleanser to moisturizers to tanning products. If you idolize JWoww’s skin, you can emulate her look with all the best products from her line. You have to admit – her skin is pretty smooth and clear.

## 5. Josie Maran Cosmetics – Josie Maran

If natural products are what you’re after, this former Victoria’s Secret Angel has what you crave. Her line of products are infused with argan oil and other organic ingredients that promise to enhance your natural beauty. You can get all sorts of great items, including body butters and hair care products.

## 6. BeautyMint – Jessica Simpson

You are in luck no matter where you live because BeautyMint is online, which means you can shop for great beauty products anytime the mood strikes. The site promises to help you create a personalized routine and offer the products that go with it. You will be so glad you invested in these wonderful beauty products from the lovely Jessica Simpson.

## 7. Nuance – Salma Hayek

Salma is definitely a gorgeous lady, so you know her comprehensive line of beauty products are going to be worth the price. You can find a full range of skincare products, cosmetics and beauty products. The affordable price will shock you and you can find all of the items at CVS. Each promises to be free of parabens and aren’t tested on animals.

Which one do you want to try? Let me know how it goes!
