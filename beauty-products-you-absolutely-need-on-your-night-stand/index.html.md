# 7 Beauty Products You Absolutely Need on Your Night Stand ...

There are some beauty products every girl needs to have on her nightstand. Most women have two times of day when they apply beauty products. One is in the morning before makeup and the other is at bedtime. Having beauty products on your nightstand will help you be more likely to put them on and give your skin the TLC it needs and deserves.

## 1. Lip Balm

The skin on your lips needs special care and that’s especially true in the winter. Harsh cold air can dry your lips out. While you want to apply lip balm during the day, it’s especially important to apply it at night. During the day your lip balm probably won’t stay on long because you have to eat and you’re applying other lip products such as lipstick. Applying lip balm at night gives your lips time to absorb all the moisturizing benefits.

## 2. Hand Cream

The same principles that apply to your lips carry over to your hands. It’s difficult for hand cream to truly do its job during the day when you’re washing your hands off and on. It works better at nighttime because it has more time to do its thing. If you deal with dry hands then applying a hand cream at bedtime can help resolve that problem. Eucerin’s Intensive Repair Hand Cream is a great product to try.

## 3. Anti-aging Serum

Anti-aging serum is a product you want to have on your nightstand. Even if you aren’t seeing any signs of aging, it’s a good idea to use this product. As the old saying goes, an ounce of prevention is worth a pound of cure. Using an anti-aging serum before the signs of aging show up can delay their appearance. If you’re already seeing signs of aging, this product can slow them down.

## 4. Moisturizer

Everyone needs a moisturizer. It’s good to use one both morning at night. Your morning moisturizer needs to have an SPF in it to give you sun protection but of course you don’t need that at night. So, it’s good to have two different moisturizers.

## 5. Eye Cream

I’ve listed these facial products in the order you should put them on. First serum, then moisturizer and lastly, eye cream. Eye cream can help you to avoid those unattractive fine lines. It can also help the skin in your eye area to stay firm. The skin in your under-eye area is especially thin so it needs extra attention. Neutrogena Healthy Skin Eye Cream is a good product to try.

## 6. Lash Serum

If you’d like to see your eyelashes become thicker and longer then this’s a great beauty product to have on your nightstand. Lash serums are a relatively new product but they’re producing good results. I’ve been using one for the last few months and my husband commented that my eyelashes looked longer. I’ve been using the one by Mary Kay but ther a’re several available that you can choose from. You just apply it lightly to your lash line after you put on all of your other beauty products.

## 7. A Glass of Water

Last but certainly not least, a glass of water is the most important beauty product you need on your nightstand. Water will help your skin in many ways. First and foremost, it helps you to stay hydrated and fights dryness. It also gives your skin a healthy glow that no product can compete with. Water is one of the keys to healthy, beautiful skin.

These are 7 beauty products you need on your nightstand. What’s your nighttime beauty routine? I’d love to hear from you.
