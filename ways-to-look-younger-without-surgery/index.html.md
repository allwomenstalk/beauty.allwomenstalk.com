# 10 Ways to Look Younger without Surgery ...

Let's face it, a large number of women have received or are planning to receive plastic surgery services, but maybe you're one who wants to know **ways to look younger without surgery**. In 2016 alone, approximately 119,000 women underwent a facelift procedure to help them look young and vivacious. For those who haven't gone under the knife yet, studies show that 1 in 5 women are planning to get plastic surgery. If you want to look younger and feel better, you don't have to have major surgery. Here are several **anti-aging procedures that don't require surgery**. You're going to love them!

## 1. SKIN TREATMENTS

This is a great option for those who don't feel that they need a major change but would still like to see some results. With various peels and masks available, you're sure to find something to firm your skin. Talk to your dermatologist about a professional peel or facial, which are two examples of **how to look younger without surgery** that you will love. Of course, you can also do masks or exfoliation at home to keep your skin looking youthful and fresh.

## 2. DERMAL ROLLING

This procedure involves the use of rolling small needles across the surface of your face. By intentionally damaging the skin, collagen is produced and fine lines, scars, and wrinkles are reversed. If you need a more dramatic effect, your doctor can use a roller with longer needles. Don't try this if you have skin conditions like eczema or psoriasis, unless directed by your dermatologist.

## 3. CHEMICAL PEELS

A chemical peel is excellent for age prevention and gives a small collagen boost. With low levels of glycolic acid, lactic acid, and trichloroacetic acid, dead skin cells are removed. Higher levels can help increase collagen production, minimizing lines and wrinkles.

## 4. MICRODERMABRASION

In this simple procedure, crystals and sand are used to buffer dead skin cells off the face. The professionals say that microdermabrasion can be great for reversing sun damage, improving skin's texture, and giving you a youthful glow.

## 5. INJECTABLES

A step up from chemical peels, injectables are commonly referred to by brand names like "Botox" and "Juvederm". Injecting fillers into lines and wrinkles can help fill them in and give you a younger look. Keep in mind that the effect is temporary so you'll have to go back every so often for a boost.

## 6. DERMAL FILLERS

These can last from six months to a year, and the results are usually very appealing to clients. They can also help to stimulate the growth of collagen, providing more permanent anti aging results.

## 7. LIQUID FACELIFTS

A liquid facelift is excellent for women who don't want to go under the knife but want more results than fillers or a chemical peel. By using a combination of dermal fillers and muscle relaxers, you achieve an end result of beautiful, ageless skin. You'll need to see your dermatologist to have this procedure done.

## 8. LASER PROCEDURES

This procedure uses heat to stimulate collagen production, providing a painless and effective anti-aging treatment. There are a few common options, including heat treatments and pulsating laser treatments. Your doctor can help you figure out which one is best for you.

## 9. INTENSE PULSED LIGHT (IPL)

Collagen is stimulated with the help of a pulsating light which helps the skin to improve its color and texture. Multiple treatments are recommended for the best results, but you're going to love what this IPL can do for you.

## 10. HEAT-BASED TREATMENTS

There are a variety of heat based treatments that can be used to help prevent or assist in reversing signs of aging. Radio frequency often referred to as Thermage, can last for multiple years with only one session. An Ultrasound treatment is also available, with 1-2 sessions recommended for best results. Finally, Infrared treatment can be completed in 2-3 sessions with noticeable results.

Which one do you want to try?

If you've considered going under the knife, talk to your doctor. You may see great results from less invasive options, and leave the face lift for further down the road. With the help of injections, laser procedures, or chemical peels you can bring youth and life back into your skin.
