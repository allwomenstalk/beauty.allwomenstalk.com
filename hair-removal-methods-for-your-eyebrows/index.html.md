# 7 Hair Removal Methods for Your Eyebrows ...

We're all paying extra special attention to our eyebrows lately, which is why it's important for each of us to find the hair removal method that suits us the best. Every woman is different, so what one person may swear by, another person will hate. Even if you think you've tried every hair removal option available, there's probably still one or two things out there you could try. Here are a few of those different types of hair removal methods for your eyebrows:

## 1. Threading Your Eyebrows

Threading involves, you guessed it, thread. When you go into a salon, a professional will twist a piece of thread over your skin. The process will remove pieces of your eyebrows so that they're shaped into the desired design. Although some people claim that it's painful, a lot of women don't believe that it hurts all that badly. The only way to find out your opinion on it is to get it done yourself!

## 2. Waxing Your Eyebrows

![Waxing Your Eyebrows](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/gc/yp/pjfxcroi5588b4160973a692810625_600x600.jpg)

If you've never had any part of your body waxed before, the sensation might feel a bit odd. You'll start by sitting down in a chair and having a professional apply hot wax onto your face. Then a strip will be applied to the area you want your hair removed. Once it is pulled off, your eyebrows will have a whole new shape!

## 3. Shaving Your Eyebrows

You can shave your eyebrows, just like you shave your legs or your bikini area. However, since the area is small, you have to be extra careful. There are special eyebrow razors that you can buy that will fit into the area better, so if you're looking to shave, you should invest in one of them.

## 4. Sugaring Your Eyebrows

Sugaring is pretty much waxing without the strips. After combining sugar, lemon juice, and water, the paste will be warmed and then placed against your skin. Then you can remove the product with your fingers alone! It should remove your hair for up to six whole weeks.

## 5. Hair Removal Creams for Your Eyebrows

There are hair removal creams that you can place over certain areas of your eyebrows. However, you should make sure to buy products that are meant to be used on your face. You don't want to apply something too harsh that'll end up harming your beautiful skin.

## 6. Tweezing Your Eyebrows

Tweezing is the simplest method of eyebrow hair removal. It's also the best way to avoid making mistakes, because you'll be dealing with one hair at a time. If you think it's too painful to pluck out your hairs one by one, then you can always apply ice to the area ahead of time. It will help numb your skin so that the pain isn't as extreme.

## 7. Laser Hair Removal for Your Eyebrows

You might not want to spend hundreds of dollars on laser hair removal, especially on such a small spot like your eyebrows. However, if you get the procedure done, you won't have to worry about dealing with eyebrow growth ever again.

Hair removal isn't always painful. If one method is too intense for you, then there are several other methods that you can test out until you find the one that fits your body best. What method of eyebrow hair removal do you usually use?
