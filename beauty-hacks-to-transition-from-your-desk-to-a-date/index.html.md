# Beauty Hacks to Transition from Your Desk to a Date ...

If you don't have time to run home before your hot date, you can get ready for it at work. You don't have to do much. Just follow these tips from [Elle:](https://www.elle.com/beauty/advice/a30138/desk-to-date-beauty-hacks/)

## 1. PREP YOUR COMPLEXION

![PREP YOUR COMPLEXION](https://img.allw.mn/content/ho/rq/mh0b468q56263968781b2332497767_400x225.gif)

Use your favorite repair serum on your face. It'll hydrate and revive your complexion.

## 2. CRANK up the VOLUME

![CRANK up the VOLUME](https://img.allw.mn/content/ti/dx/zyf8gz8s562639676b789677303746_500x280.gif)

Lift up your flat hair by using a dry shampoo. Then you should switch your part to the opposite side of your head to lift your roots.

## 3. WALK the (TIGHT)LINE

![WALK the (TIGHT)LINE](https://img.allw.mn/content/r1/cx/nwcwiodn5626396998969242811214_400x225.gif)

You need dramatic lashes for your late night parties. That's why you should use a fine point eyeliner to tight line your upper and lower lids.

## 4. JUST ADD WATER

![JUST ADD WATER](https://img.allw.mn/content/i7/ie/vz6o5kjx5626396a4dd24402019315_275x244.gif)

Grab a face mist and spritz it on. The moisture will get rid of your cakey foundation or concealer.

## 5. GET RED-Y

![GET RED-Y](https://img.allw.mn/content/bs/kg/fskkni3w562639723d601131129223_320x180.gif)

Red is the best lipstick color to take your look from day to night. Apply it to your lips, use some blotting paper, and then apply a second coat.
