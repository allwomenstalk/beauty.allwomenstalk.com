# The Many Endless Ways You Can Use Coconut Oil for Beauty ...

Coconut oil is having a moment right now. The reasons are many, but the best one is that it can do wonders for your skin, hair and health. You probably hear about a new way to use it all the time. This list will give you some of the top ideas for incorporating coconut oil into your beauty routine. However, they aren’t the only ones so don’t be afraid to use it for other purposes if your doctor gives you the all clear. In the meantime, try these fantastic ideas for a more beautiful you, starting right now.

## 1. Get Rid of Wrinkles

![hair, face, black hair, clothing, nose,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/pj/a5/myyg6wt358c687f0a6531126865223_600x600.jpg)

Coconut oil hydrates skin, which can help combat the appearance of fine lines and wrinkles. Dab just a bit around your eyes before bed and wake up looking refreshed and young.

## 2. Remove Your Makeup

![hair, human hair color, clothing, black hair, brown hair,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/yq/qz/qnwd6if458c687fa269f9384473657_600x722.jpg)

Want something natural to get rid of your cosmetics at the end of the day? Try using a swipe of coconut oil and say goodbye to mascara, eye shadow, liner, blush and foundation. Cool!

## 3. Heal Dry, Chapped Lips

![hair, human hair color, face, black hair, nose,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/sx/pz/cs7xiazv58c6881f3e124468294482_600x600.jpg)

Nothing is worse than dry, cracked lips, but coconut oil might be the miracle you’ve been looking for. Use just a small dab on both lips and as the oil soaks in, it will hydrate and heal chapped lips.

## 4. Soothe Dry Skin

![hair, clothing, human positions, blond, supermodel,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/d3/d2/fh4wm0h658c688354a5a7207898764_600x600.jpg)

You can rub coconut oil directly onto your skin to instantly hydrate and soothe it, but adding it to your bath can also help. As you soak, the coconut oil will infuse your entire body with moisture for smooth, supple skin all the time.

## 5. Fight Body Odor

![winter, season, vacation, snow,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/zt/tu/bmcpwqrz58c688499a899088496510_600x749.jpg)

If you’re a fan of natural beauty products, coconut oil can be a great alternative for traditional deodorant. Apply the oil where needed and you should notice a dramatic reduction of body odor.

## 6. Wash Your Face

![hair, face, human action, eyebrow, person,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/cr/nm/gvonuhsl58c6887b5a506838448674_465x598.jpg)

When combined with other homemade facial cleanser ingredients, such as tea tree oil and raw honey, you can get a really great clean when you suds up. A clean face is important and coconut oil might be just what you’ve been looking for.

## 7. Heal a Sunburn

![hair, clothing, photograph, image, girl,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/us/sr/aflnwr5x58c688aba0683677012317_600x600.jpg)

You know you should be wearing sunscreen, but if you got a little too much sun anyway, coconut oil can soothe and heal the affected areas. Simply massage it into your burn and you should notice relief right away.

## 8. Treat Cold Sores

![hair, person, clothing, glasses, girl,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/y0/xi/t573etb958c688d5aa672960079364_600x749.jpg)

If you get cold sores on your mouth, you know how miserable they can be. Dab just a touch of coconut oil on your sores and you can effectively reduce the discomfort and healing time.

## 9. Keep Your Cuticles Healthy

![nail, finger, nail care, pink, manicure,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/gm/tw/art7clhy58c688e978ff3916787916_600x600.jpg)

Dry, cracked cuticles are no fun, but coconut oil can remedy the situation in no time. Massage a drop into each cuticle to condition and hydrate the cuticle and nail for healthy and gorgeous fingers.

## 10. Wash Your Hair

![hair, clothing, hairstyle, long hair, brown hair,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/kr/fi/tz7y584o58c689031af10301192982_600x733.jpg)

Experts say that using coconut oil to cleanse your hair is a really good idea because it preserves color and health. Use it with apple cider vinegar for the best results.

## 11. Condition Your Hair

![hair, clothing, bridal accessory, bridal veil, fashion accessory,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/li/u7/d1pavc0b58c689166bd1e314653491_600x730.jpg)

Don’t you just love a double duty product? Coconut oil can cleanse your hair, but it can also condition your strands. Massage a couple of teaspoons of the oil throughout your mane, then rinse for shampoo commercial worthy hair you’ll love.

## 12. Highlight Your Cheeks

![person, clothing, lady, black hair, girl,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/dm/c5/n92q02l158c6892aebdf4896968046_600x600.jpg)

Dab just a touch of coconut oil on your cheekbones and you instantly highlight them for a lovely look any day of the week. You can also use the oil to highlight your eyes and brows.

## 13. Make an Exfoliator

![hair, face, clothing, person, facial hair,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/yi/ok/re5fa9nf58c689442db39490677158_600x600.jpg)

Exfoliating your skin helps remove dead cells and lets your young, fresh skin shine through. Combine coconut oil with an equal amount of sugar and gently massage it all over your body for smooth, supple skin everywhere.

## 14. Lighten Stretch Marks

![hair, clothing, beauty, lady, blond,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/w3/fk/xkjh08mu58c6897d42ad0918296707_600x749.jpg)

Stretch marks often occur with pregnancy, but can also crop up anytime rapid weight gain occurs. Massaging coconut oil into the affected areas on a daily basis can help reduce the appearance of your stretch marks.

## 15. Heal Cracked Skin

![clothing, black hair, person, red, lady,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/u8/u4/wuem6ojo58c6899b384b3007794011_600x678.jpg)

Whether you have cracked heels or hands, using coconut oil as a moisturizer can solve the problem in no time.

## 16. Fight Skin Fungus

![white, clothing, sleeve, footwear, leg,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/cf/mh/w5mgfe4o58c689b3a675f661999029_600x600.jpg)

Coconut oil is a natural anti-fungal ingredient and is a great way to combat athlete’s foot or other similar skin infections.

## 17. Get Rid of Cellulite

![clothing, pattern, gown, design, dress,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/dl/om/xv8fx31158c6896a87f46094885072_600x665.jpg)

OK, you can’t really get rid of it, but you can make it look a whole lot better by gently massaging coconut oil into the places that are affected by cellulite, such as your thighs or butt.
