# 10 of the Best Beauty Products for Sensitive Skin ...

Sensitive skin used to make shopping for beauty products quite difficult but not anymore! Big brands like Neutrogena and Olay have worked really hard to release products specifically for those with sensitive skin. Here are some of the best beauty products that'll leave your sensitive skin feeling great!

## 1. Simple Skincare Oil Balancing Cleansing Wipes

![Simple Skincare Oil Balancing Cleansing Wipes](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/az/da/yg0sh61y_600x394.jpg)

Simple's oil balancing cleansing wipes is perfect for sensitive skin! You can use to remove make up or just to refresh your face. You can find out more info on [simpleskincare.com](https://goo.gl/bafBxu) and even find the store nearest to you. Thanks to the hydrating glycerin in it, the wipes won't leave your skin dehydrated or make it sting!

## 2. Olay's Foaming Face Wash

![Olay's Foaming Face Wash](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/or/cp/ghyw3wx8_600x568.jpg)

This face wash is made specifically for sensitive skin. It's formula is 100% soap-free, Oil-free, and Fragrance-free. Plus, the face wash is only $5 and available on [olay.com](https://goo.gl/lz2pt0).

## 3. Micellar Lotion Cleansing and Make-up Remover

![Micellar Lotion Cleansing and Make-up Remover](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/mp/eo/cu92vgoh_300x376.jpg)

This (3-in-1) lotion is a gentle no-rinse cleanser, toner and make-up remover! It removes impurities and makeup and is based on cleansing and softening your skin. It's alcohol, soap, and oil-free care for the face and eyes. Get more information on [aveneusa.com](http://www.aveneusa.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com).

## 4. Burt's Bees Eye Cream

![Burt's Bees Eye Cream](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ij/d1/irnwp4j3_600x576.jpg)

This eye cream is made for those with sensitive skin! "Our natural lightweight under-eye cream helps reduce the appearance of puffiness and is clinically proven to hydrate the delicate eye area without causing redness or irritation," the product description reads. "Formulated with softening Cotton Extract that can help sustain moisture which can help shield skin from potential irritants such as harsh soaps or facial cleansers." Read more at [burtsbees.com](http://www.burtsbees.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)

## 5. Cover Girl Clean Foundation

![Cover Girl Clean Foundation](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/i1/sf/h4qnp7cl_500x443.jpg)

If you have sensitive skin, it's probably very difficult to find a foundation that works for you. It's important to make sure you're picking one that contains hydrating glycerin and is fragrance- and oil-free like this one from [drugstore.com](https://www.walgreens.com/)!

## 6. Dove Nourishing Body Wash

![Dove Nourishing Body Wash](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/hl/xg/v9jmtyec_500x484.jpg)

If regular soap leaves you feeling itchy after your shower, switch to Dove's Sensitive Skin Nourishing Body Wash! It'll leave your skin itch-free and feeling super soft. Learn more on [drugstore.com](https://www.walgreens.com/)

## 7. Honest Hand Soap

![Honest Hand Soap](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/o9/kp/p0ruy6bj_378x426.jpg)

Jessica Alba cares about your sensitive skin! Her company, Honest, has soap with pure plant-based ingredients. It cleans your hands but doesn't strip them dry. Buy some from [honest.com](https://goo.gl/DnLMkH).

## 8. Gillette Venus Sensitive Shaving Gel

![Gillette Venus Sensitive Shaving Gel](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/c4/xu/w6ypxbcd_296x874.jpg)

Shaving your legs can leave your skin feeling super sensitive. This shaving gel will change that! The product description reads, "This dye- and fragrance-free formula helps to minimize the risk of skin irritation." Read more on [gillettevenus.com](https://goo.gl/ZjPBlg).
