# Here's How You Can Get Clear Skin in Just 2 Weeks ⌛️ ...

Girls, perfect acne-free clear skin is in your reach, seriously. And it's crazy how easy it is to get that clear skin in just 2 weeks! Read on!

## 1. ITEMS NEEDED for Your Clear Skin

• Water
• Facial cleanser (Noxzema, Cetaphil, Olay)
• Coconut oil (virgin, cold pressed, unrefined)

## 2. MORNING ROUTINE for CLear Skin

• Wash your face with hot 💦 (only as hot as you can stand)
*Hot 💦 opens your pores
• Use your daily cleanser and massage your face
*You do not have to let the cleanser sit for it to work
• Rinse your face with cold💦
• *Cold 💦 closes your pores (FYI you WANT to do this step because leaving your pores open allows them to become clogged again)
• Let your face air dry
• Use a dime size of coconut oil to massage on your face

Pretty Easy Right!? So here is the 🔑 to making this work!

## 3. NIGHT ROUTINE for Clear Skin

• USE THE MORNING ROUTINE!!

*Only put coconut oil if your face becomes dry after the wash.
Eventually your face will have moisture after your night routine, you will not need the oil to go to bed!

NOTE: You are training your skin! Training takes time and discipline. You will see a difference in your skin if you follow these steps every day!!

*If you are clearing your face from acne do not give up!
*This regimen may not work for everyone if you need to change some things to work for you and your type skin go for it!!

*Do not pick at your skin or try to cover your imperfections! (Minimal coverage if you must!)

REMEMBER!

## 4. Use Your Hands with the Entire Process

*use a towel or a face brush to scrub off dead skin or if your pores seem to be large (this can give your face a deeper cleaning)

*use a towel or face brush once or twice a week (using it more can make your face too sensitive or raw)

## 5. This Process Should Only Take 3 Mins

The process seems long when you're doing it, but take your time and take pride in cleaning your face!

## 6. Coconut Oil is a Natural Moisturizer It is All You Need!

Do not use lotion, it clogs your pores, coconut oil will work so much better for clear skin!

## 7. Give This Regimen 2 Weeks to Work Well!

You have to train your skin to get clear results - keep going for 2 weeks and you'll be amazed.

## 8. Wash Your Face Twice a Day for 2 Weeks and on

Do not skip or you may find a bump growing the next day!
