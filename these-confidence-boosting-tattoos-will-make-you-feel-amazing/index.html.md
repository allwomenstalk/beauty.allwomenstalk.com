# Confidence Boosting Tattoos to Get if You Want to Feel Amazing ...

Your tattoo doesn't have to consist of a pretty picture. You can get words, instead. Some of them are more powerful than you even realize. To prove it, here are a few confidence boosting tattoos that would make you feel unstoppable:

## 1. She Believed She Could, so She Did

Anything is possible, as long as you believe. It's cliche, but it's the truth. That's why you should always think positively.

## 2. Know Your Worth

Never forget how valuable you are. You're a gorgeous, intelligent girl who deserves the very best. Don't let anyone convince you otherwise.

## 3. Love Yourself

Your heart is huge. There's more than enough room in it to fit love for yourself. So give yourself the affection you deserve.

## 4. I Took a Walk in the Woods and Came out Taller than the Trees

The next time you're feeling low, take a walk through nature to put things in perspective. It'll remind you of how beautiful the world is, and in turn, how beautiful you are for being a part of it.

## 5. Pain is Temporary, but Quitting Lasts Forever

If you have a goal you want to achieve, never give up on it. If you do, you'll regret it someday.

## 6. You Don't Need Anyone's Permission to Be like You Want to Be

Who cares what other people think of you? Their approval isn't important. As long as you're happy with yourself, you're doing something right.

## 7. Stand Tall and Believe

Keep your head held high, because you're just as amazing as all of the other people around you. There's no reason to be ashamed of who you are.

## 8. Never a Failure. Always a Lesson

Whenever you come short of reaching your goals, you're not a failure. You're just in the middle of the journey that'll lead you to success.

## 9. Be at Peace, Not in Pieces

Don't fall apart when things get rough. You're a tough woman who can keep it together. You just have to be strong.

## 10. Hold on. Pain Ends

Nothing lasts forever, not even pain. That's why you have to keep holding on. Eventually, things will get better and you'll be happy again.

## 11. I Will Make Better Mistakes Tomorrow

You can't stop yourself from making mistakes. Of course, you can always make smarter mistakes tomorrow than you did yesterday.

## 12. Keep Moving Forward

You can never stop moving forward in life. You need to continue moving, so that you continue to grow.

## 13. Be - You - Tiful

You're the most beautiful when you're being yourself.

## 14. Don't Look Back. You're Not Going That Way

There's no reason to focus on the past. It's over and done with.

## 15. Sometimes You Gotta Fall before You Can Fly

Bad things happen before good things happen.

## 16. We All Have Our Issues

Every single person in this world has problems they need to deal with. You're no different than anybody else.

## 17. Stay Strong

You're a strong woman. Remember that.

If you didn't want ink before, you will now! After all, these types of tattoos will help you get through even your toughest days. Do you have any confidence boosting tattoos of your own?
