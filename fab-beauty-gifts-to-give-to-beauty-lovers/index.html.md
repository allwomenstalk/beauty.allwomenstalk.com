# 15 Fab Beauty Gifts to Give to Beauty Lovers ...

If you’re still shopping for holiday gifts, why not give out some of these beauty gifts that any beauty novice or expert will love! Giving beauty gifts are a great way for your friends and family to try out a new beauty product that they otherwise wouldn’t get to try! The best thing about beauty gifts is that they come in super chic packaging, they’re fun to try out and they’ll get to enjoy the gift over and over again!

## 1. Butter LONDON Lips & Tips

![Butter LONDON Lips & Tips](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/beauty/2012/12/1_butter-london-lips-tips_233x282.jpg)

You can’t go wrong with beauty gifts that offer a splash of color to your lips and fingertips! Butter London is renowned for their long-lasting polishes in rich, bold colors. Pair a polish with a matching vibrant lippie and you’ve got your girlfriend perfectly matched for any occasion!

## 2. Lipstick Queen Holiday Lipstick Trio

![Lipstick Queen Holiday Lipstick Trio](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/beauty/2012/12/2_lipstick-queen-holiday-lipstick-trio_315x280.jpg)

If you have a lipstick lover on your list of gifts to buy for, give her a trio of lip colors! This awesome threesome has a shade for everyone. Whether she loves vibrant reds, sexy nudes or pretty pinks and plums, she’s bound to find a lip color that she likes for every occasion!

## 3. Burt’s Bees Tips N’ Toes Set

![Burt’s Bees Tips N’ Toes Set](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/beauty/2012/12/3_burts-bees-tips-n-toes-set_300x282.jpg)

If you need to purchase something for a natural beauty lover, Burt’s Bees is affordable and natural! This set comes with everything you need from lip balm to cuticle cream for soft and nourished skin all over. Wrap this up in a reusable bag and you’ve got a natural and eco-friendly gift anyone would appreciate!

## 4. Marc Jacobs Rollerball Trio

![Marc Jacobs Rollerball Trio](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/beauty/2012/12/4_marc-jacobs-rollerball-trio_250x250.jpg)

Fragrances are always a great choice when it comes to gifts, especially when you’ve got three different scents to choose from! I have yet to meet anyone who doesn’t love Marc Jacobs’ fragrances and this colorful and cute set contains three of his fresh, floral perfumes in a convenient rollerball!

## 5. E.l.f. 10-piece Brush Set

![E.l.f. 10-piece Brush Set](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/beauty/2012/12/5_e-l-f-10-piece-brush-set_410x410.jpg)

A perfect gift for beauty lovers and newbies is a makeup brush set! Every beauty addict needs a few extra brushes and if your girlfriend is new to using makeup brushes, this is a great set to learn on! This handy set contains all your basic brushes so you’ve got all your bases covered with these ergonomically designed brushes!

## 6. Clarins Feed 10 Kit

![Clarins Feed 10 Kit](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/beauty/2012/12/6_clarins-feed-10-kit_250x250.jpg)

Give a gift of beauty that gives back! Clarins’ Feed 10 Kit features some of their best skin care products including a lip protector, sunscreen, multi-purpose skin cream and primer all tucked away in festive and feel good red pouch. Each set provides 10 healthy school meals through the United Nations World Food Programme!

## 7. Fresh Sugar Kisses Mini Lip Trio

![Fresh Sugar Kisses Mini Lip Trio](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/beauty/2012/12/7_fresh-sugar-kisses-mini-lip-trio_248x184.jpg)

Winter lips call for sugar kisses from Fresh’s lip treatment trio. I was always hesitant to try high end lip treatments but these really are worth the hype! Give the gift of super soft lips in three different, subtle tints. These chic lip treatments come with screw-top caps so you never have to worry about losing your cap!

## 8. Philosophy Home for the Holidays Set

![Philosophy Home for the Holidays Set](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/beauty/2012/12/8_philosophy-home-for-the-holidays-set_250x152.jpg)

Treat your friends or family to an array of moisturizing and fragrant skin care, hair care and lip glosses all made with seasonal scents and flavors! Who can resist the inviting fragrance of cranberries, cherries, peppermint bark and cinnamon buns? Give this beauty gift to a super busy family member so she can get in some relaxation time!

## 9. LORAC Eye Candy Full Face Collection

![LORAC Eye Candy Full Face Collection](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/beauty/2012/12/9_lorac-eye-candy-full-face-collection_250x250.jpg)

Oh baby, this might be the Holy Grail beauty gift for beauty and candy lovers! LORAC created a limited edition collection of holiday colors in six palettes that look and feel as smooth as chocolate candy bars! Once you get the lavish gold box in your hands, you might want to keep a couple of palettes for yourself and wrap up the rest!

## 10. Benefit How to Look the Best at Everything

![Benefit How to Look the Best at Everything](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/12/14000303_3886_249x247.jpg)

Benefit is known for its cute little make up kits that come in adorable packaging at an affordable price. That is one of the main reasons why they make such great gift ideas! This particular makeup kit comes with their PROfessional primer, Hello Flawless foundation, Bo-ing concealer, powder and brush applicator. I don't think anyone will be able to resists this cute and convenient makeup kit!

## 11. Soap and Glory Beginner's Luxe Set

![Soap and Glory Beginner's Luxe Set](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/12/14001644_8416_249x199.jpg)

If you are still shopping for the friend who loves her pampering sessions, this luxe set would make the perfect gift idea. It consists of Soap & Glory's most popular items: full-size body butter, shower gel, shower scrub and scrub gloves, all packed in a girly makeup bag. Give your friend the gift of smooth and silky skin this holiday season and she will surely appreciate it.

## 12. Sephora Favorites- Draw the Line

![Sephora Favorites- Draw the Line](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/12/14001934_6167_249x183.jpg)

It is usually hard to find the perfect eyeliner that doesn't smudge. However with this luxurious gift, you will be able to give your friend or family member the chance to try a variety of eyeliners and potentially end their search for the perfect one. This gift set comes with six eyeliners in a variety of shades and formulas. There are blacks and browns, liquid and pencils. Anything you would want or ever need!

## 13. Too Faced All I Want for Christmas

![Too Faced All I Want for Christmas](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/12/14003917_7859_249x127.jpg)

The name literary says it all! This makeup kit has everything a beauty junkie would put on her Christmas list: blush/eye shadow trio compact, lip cream, eyeliner, lash injection mascara, shadow insurance and cosmetic pouch. This 6-piece full face collection features some of the best Too Faced products that customers keep on raving about. So stop the search for your perfect beauty gift because it cannot get any better than this.

## 14. Nails Inc. Bling It on Rose Gold

![Nails Inc. Bling It on Rose Gold](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/12/14002949_7806_249x231.jpg)

Rose Gold has been very popular this season, so why wouldn't you want a splash of rose gold goodness on your nails? This nail kit is perfect for the beauty junkies, who go above and beyond when it comes to nails. It comes with a four piece nail art set with rose gold glitter pot, foil effect polish, application tray and a dusting brush.

## 15. Buxom Red Hot Set

![Buxom Red Hot Set](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/12/14003649_9450_249x247.jpg)

The beauty of makeup sets is that you get a variety of mini products for a reasonable price. You are able to try out several brands and maybe even find one that you fall in love with. So if you are dwelling on what to get your friends and family for Christmas, take a shot with this mascara set and let them experiment on their own!

Every season cosmetics companies pull out all the stops and create the most fabulous beauty gifts that are as nice to look at as they are to use! If you’re all finished buying your holiday gifts, why not pick up a special beauty gift for yourself? There’s nothing wrong with treating yourself. Do you like to give beauty gifts?
