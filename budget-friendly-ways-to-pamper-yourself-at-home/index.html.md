# 10 Budget Friendly Ways to Pamper Yourself at Home ...

Every woman deserves to be pampered like a princess and needs a list of **budget-friendly ways to pamper yourself at home**. Unfortunately, we do not all have royal funds in our bank accounts. If you are on a small budget and looking for ways to pamper yourself, then this list is for you. Here are the best **budget-friendly ways to pamper yourself at home**.

## 1. At Home Facial

There are so many relatively inexpensive facial scrubs and masks on the market. Treat yourself, to an at home facial, to leave your face looking like you just came from the spa. I recommend a clay exfoliation mask, for silky smooth skin. This is definitely one of my favorite **budget-friendly ways to pamper yourself at home**.

## 2. Bubble Baths

One of the ultimate ways to pamper yourself is a nice hot bubble bath! Light a few candles, put on your favorite song, and relax.

## 3. Hobby Time

A personal favorite of mine, doing something like your favorite hobby is pampering yourself in a different kind of way. All of us have tendencies to get caught up in life, and not make time for our interests. By dedicating a little time to your interests, you are separating yourself from everyday life, and reestablishing independence.

## 4. Wine and Girls

Grab a bottle of wine, the remote, a few friends, and turn on Netflix, for a night in. One of the best ways to pamper yourself can be as simple as having fun with the girls.

## 5. Salon Relaxation

When you have an extra 15 or 20 dollars in your bank, treat yourself to a shampoo, and scalp massage at a salon. It feels amazing, and you get a brief moment of celebrity treatment, for not much money.

## 6. Sleep in

Not getting enough sleep can have a negative impact on your body as a whole - less energy, bags under the eyes, and a sluggish feeling in general. When the work week is done, do yourself a huge favor and catch some extra sleep. You will feel better, look better, and your body will thank you.

## 7. Subscription Boxes

Who doesn’t love a little gift every now and again? Be your own best friend and pamper yourself by getting a monthly subscription box. For about 10 bucks a month, you will receive a box with make-up, jewelry or whatever you decide to order, sent right to your door.

## 8. Talk It out

We all have that one friend we call when we need a good laugh or a deep meaningful talk, but if you have a busy life, it can be hard to find the time. Pamper your spirit, and let yourself get caught up in a conversation.

## 9. Just for the Moms

I know from personal experience that once we have children, we rarely get to cook a dinner that we really want. Now don’t get me wrong, I love spaghetti as much as the next person, but after your 400th dinner of it in two years, you began to daydream about the time in your life when you got to pick what you ate. So, put down the noodles, show yourself some attention, and cook what you love every so often.

## 10. Relax

A fantastic way to pamper yourself with a low budget is to simply relax. Curl up on the couch with a book and a cup of tea and forget the world for a while.
