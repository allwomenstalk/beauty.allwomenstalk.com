# 7 Genius Time Saving ⏱ Beauty Tips You Need in Your Memory ...

Most of us don't have hours to get ready in the morning. That's why every second you save counts. If you follow [Women's Health Mag's](https://www.womenshealthmag.com/beauty/g19966164/cut-down-your-morning-beauty-routine/) advice, you should be able to get ready in a hurry:

## 1. SKIP the MORNING SHOWER

![SKIP the MORNING SHOWER](https://img.allw.mn/content/bm/pn/g7jcrve4563768e7a811b047191623_300x156.gif)

Most of your time in the morning is spent washing your hair, drying it, and styling it. You can reduce your workload by showering during the nighttime instead of the daytime. Then all you'll have to do is brush your hair a bit and style it.

## 2. STORE YOUR MAKEUP in ONE PLACE

![STORE YOUR MAKEUP in ONE PLACE](https://img.allw.mn/content/q1/s2/at8owl17563768e7eed83336208019_500x271.gif)

Make sure all of your beauty products are in the same room, and even the same container. If you know where everything is, you won't have to run around the house searching for it all.

## 3. PREP YOUR SKIN the NIGHT before

![PREP YOUR SKIN the NIGHT before](https://img.allw.mn/content/lc/ib/gd1v2m1a563768e7bfc7f606486781_250x136.gif)

If you wash your face every single night before you go to bed, it shouldn't take you all that long to prep your skin in the morning. All you'll have to do is splash some cold water over your face and call it a day.

## 4. STYLE YOUR HAIR WHILE YOU SLEEP

![STYLE YOUR HAIR WHILE YOU SLEEP](https://img.allw.mn/content/yt/ja/d2rbqcpw563768eabdf32614231141_500x333.gif)

If you want your hair to look curly in the morning, sleep in curlers. If you want your hair to look wavy, sleep in a bun.

## 5. USE YOUR LIP COLOR as YOUR CHEEK COLOR

![USE YOUR LIP COLOR as YOUR CHEEK COLOR](https://img.allw.mn/content/l7/ut/gmy8h3z45637691931ba0517643870_480x270.gif)

If you use your lip color as your cheek color, you won't have to search through your makeup bag for multiple products. You'll already have everything you need right in your hands.

## 6. OPT for BB CREAM

![OPT for BB CREAM](https://img.allw.mn/content/wh/g0/rqzmxju65637694647837427130525_272x240.gif)

This product will "even out your skin tone and act like a foundation, but it also moisturizes and primes your face for the rest of your makeup." That means it'll save you plenty of time.

## 7. Save LIP COLOR for YOUR COMMUTE

![Save LIP COLOR for YOUR COMMUTE](https://img.allw.mn/content/t1/nt/zmrubkkg563768e90c261872395840_500x284.gif)

You usually have to reapply your lipstick throughout the day anyway, so you might as well wait until you reach your destination to color your lips.
