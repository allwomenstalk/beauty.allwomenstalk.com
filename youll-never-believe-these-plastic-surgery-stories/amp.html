You'll Never Believe These Plastic Surgery Stories ...

By Lyndsie • 12 Comments

I don't believe in plastic surgery shaming. When Renée Zellweger went through so much bullcrap last year, I thought it was awful. You're going to look at this post and think it's about calling these unfortunate people ugly or taking pleasure in what they've gone through, but no. It's easy to laugh at plastic surgery fails. It's much harder to consider why these people feel the need to get plastic surgery. As you'll see, women are often the culprits of bad plastic surgery because we can't win. It's worse in Hollywood, where women are told that youth is everything and they must look young forever, but those same women are torn down and ripped on for turning to cosmetic procedures to keep their youthful looks. This is also true for women in the real world, outside of the celebrity side of things. However, even men fall prey to the belief that they need to look young forever, or perfect, or just generally better. These are some of the results of that mentality.

1 Jocelyn Wildenstein

Jocelyn Wildenstein's comes to mind any time the subject turns to plastic surgery tragedies. Jocelyn is famous for making herself look more feline through plastic surgery – and she is completely happy with her look, by all media accounts, at least. What's sad to me is that she began her plastic surgery journey because of her then-husband. After catching him in bed with a much younger woman, Jocelyn attempted to reignite her husband's passion for her by changing her look.

***

She underwent countless procedures, which included extensive facelifts, lip augmentation, and eyelid surgery to resemble the exotic wild cats her then-husband adored. Despite the fortune she spent on procedures that radically altered her appearance, Jocelyn, also known as "Catwoman," possesses a level of confidence and self-assuredness that could be envied. Yet it's a stark reminder of how deeply personal and complex the reasons behind one's choice to go under the knife can be.

2 Mickey Rourke

In many ways, you can understand Mickey Rourke's procedures, as unfortunate as they are – they're the result of the injuries he received during his long hiatus from acting. He became a boxer, which is pretty badass, but it did a number on his face. He's completely open about it, however, doesn't try to hide his face and won't let anyone shame him. And I say good on Mickey for that.

3 Rose McGowan

I see so many people talking ish about Rose's face, which I don't think looks all that different. She still has the same impish, snarky smirk and full lips I fell in love with as a teen and she's still drop dead stunning. What kills me is that so few people take the time to understand why she had any surgery in the first place. They spew really vicious comments that not only aren't really true, at least in my opinion, but that also lack sympathy or empathy, considering that Rose had cosmetic surgery following a car accident.

***

It seems as though the lines between personal choice and public speculation have become alarmingly blurred. It's like we're forgetting the human element amidst the clamor for celebrity gossip. Rose McGowan, for instance, has been more than candid about the procedures she's undergone, yet many choose to gloss over the fact that these decisions often aren't for vanity but for healing. Whether mending physical scars or boosting confidence, is it really our place to judge? Next time you hear a whisper about someone's changed appearance, maybe take a beat and consider the road they've walked. Isn't compassion the real beauty standard we should be striving for?

4 Farrah Abraham

Teen Mom's Farrah is the most divisive controversial girl to come out of the series. She's had several cosmetic procedures, but let's talk solely about her lips. Consider this a tale of caution: cosmetic procedures can have unforeseen consequences. You never know when you might be allergic to something. You never know if the doctor you trust is using subpar materials. You never know what the final results will look like.

***

Farrah's lip injections became infamous after a procedure went awry, leaving her with an exaggerated pout she hadn't bargained for. Photos of her swollen lips went viral, sparking conversations and warnings about the risks of filler injections. This reality star's experience underscores the importance of researching and understanding the potential side effects and complications of any beauty enhancement before going under the needle. It's a stark reminder that even seemingly simple procedures can have dramatic and unintended outcomes. Always consult with experienced professionals and consider the possibility of having to navigate unexpected results.

5 Michael Jackson

Michael Jackson's story is so sad. I know he's a controversial figure, so for the moment, let's focus on the facial side of things, not the, erm, other rumors that plagued him. I'm not here to speak ill of the dead, either. Although he only copped to a small handful of procedures, it was obvious to anyone with eyes that MJ had a lot of work done. It was likewise obvious to everyone – even people who didn't know the man personally – that his drastic facial surgeries pointed to a deep-seated unhappiness with himself. That's sad for anyone.

6 Renée Zellweger

You know what's unbelievable about this plastic surgery story? A. it shouldn't have been a story, and b., Renée doesn't even care what any of us think. Good for you, Renée. You're gorgeous.

***

Renée Zellweger, known for her roles in popular movies such as "Bridget Jones's Diary" and "Chicago," has been the subject of plastic surgery rumors for years. However, in a 2014 interview with People magazine, Zellweger addressed the speculation and stated that she was happy and healthy and that her appearance was simply due to living a more relaxed and fulfilling life. Zellweger's response to the plastic surgery rumors is refreshing and empowering, as she refuses to conform to society's narrow standards of beauty and instead focuses on her own happiness and well-being.

7 Kylie Jenner

Let me preface this by saying that I don't care what Kylie does or doesn't do to her lips. This is a story of note because of how many young girls look at Kylie's lips and, even now, take on the Kylie Lip Challenge. That is so dangerous – painful, unsightly, and potentially disfiguring.

***

With or without the enhancements, Kylie's impact is undeniable. Her pout became her signature and sparked a beauty trend that’s still going strong. However, the Kylie Lip Challenge, where fans suctioned their lips in hopes of mimicking her look, not only went viral but also raised concerns over the extreme measures young girls were willing to take. It's critical to remember that beauty standards, especially those influenced by celebrities, should not put anyone's health at risk. True beauty, after all, isn't about replication; it's about embracing your own unique features.

8 Tara Reid

Tara Reid's story is so unfortunate: a botched surgery, an unlucky snap or three, and her flaws are suddenly spread out all over the world. Well, okay, so that's not all that happened and she has had more than one bad procedure, but still. It was such an unfair blow to her career. The procedures she had done solely because of the industry she's in ended up completely backfiring on her. You have to feel sympathy for that. She's had those botched jobs fixed, however, and seems much happier and more confident now.

***

But even after the initial shock and recovery period, it's clear that Tara Reid has come out stronger on the other side. Not everyone can bounce back from such a public ordeal, especially in Hollywood. It goes to show her resilience and strength of character. The actress didn't just repair the physical – she rejuvenated her spirit. Nowadays, she's stepped back into the spotlight with renewed energy, ready to leave those misfortunes in the past. Her experience is certainly a cautionary tale of plastic surgery's potential pitfalls but also a testament to personal fortitude.

9 Tori Spelling

Tori has been very open about the procedures she's had done, from her nose job to her boob job. But let's talk about her boob job. I sincerely feel bad for her and I can only imagine how self-conscious she feels. I kind of watch “Botched” a lot and I've seen the same dent on patients appearing on the show, which makes me wonder if Tori ever wants to get her breasts fixed.

10 Meg Ryan

Meg Ryan is another actress who, I really believe, got Botox and fillers and what-have-you to keep up with Hollywood's youthful expectations. This lovely woman is beautiful no matter what her age and I will always be sad that she felt she needed to do this.

11 Dennis Avner

Otherwise known as the Catman and Stalking Cat, Dennis Avner isn't a cautionary tale, nor does his extensive plastic surgery qualify as a fail. Is it unbelievable? Absolutely. It's out-of-this-world. However, Dennis is happy. He went to great pains to look like a tiger, which is his totem animal. His body modifications include transdermal implants for his whiskers, subdermal implants, filing and sharpening of his teeth, and a variety of tattoos, lip, and nose procedures.

12 Heidi Montag

Heidi is quasi-famous for a variety of reasons, most notably her tumor – you might know him as Spencer. Ah ha ha. But seriously, she's quite well-known for getting ten different procedures in a single day. Just one day! She regrets pretty much all of them now and has even had some surgeries to fix them.

***

Heidi's quest for Hollywood perfection led her down a path that's as eye-opening as it is cautionary. The star's complete transformation made waves, but it's the aftermath that really gets you thinking. From her dramatically enhanced bust to the re-sculpting of her facial features, it's a tale of beauty procedures that reads like a grocery list. And now, with a hint of remorse in her voice, she speaks out about the intense physical and emotional pain that came post-op. Let's just say, it's a bumpy road back from extreme makeover territory.

13 Katie Price

Katie Price most definitely serves as a cautionary tale, much like Heidi Montag here in the states. There comes a point where plastic surgery makes you completely unrecognizable – and maybe that's what Katie wanted, I don't know. Maybe that's what Heidi wanted. But it's kind of sad.

***

Katie Price, once a glamour model known for her natural beauty, has undergone numerous cosmetic procedures over the years. From voluptuous breast augmentations to facial fillers and botox, she has transformed her appearance quite dramatically. There's an undeniable pressure in the limelight to maintain a youthful look, and Katie has admitted to struggling with her self-image. Yet, the ceaseless pursuit of perfection through the surgeon's scalpel paints a worrisome picture. Her journey is a stark reminder that sometimes, less is more, and self-acceptance is a beauty that no surgery can enhance.

14 Erik Sprague

Eric Sprague, also known as the Lizardman, is also extremely happy with his extraordinary choices, which are designed to make him look like a lizard, obviously. He was one of the very first people to get his tongue bifurcated. That, his filed teeth, implanted horns made of Teflon, and tattoos certainly make him look quite reptilian.

15 Jennifer Grey

So, it's not true that no one puts Baby in the corner. Baby put herself in the corner. This is one of my least favorite cosmetic surgery tales, but I hate saying that because Jennifer appears happy and she looks lovely. However, she also looks … ordinary. Which, okay, that was her aim so I get it. But she took the one thing that made her stand out as an individual and made it look like everyone else. I thought she was breathtaking before. She really is still gorgeous, but to me, this is just proof that cosmetic surgery all too often turns people into cookie-cutter images of each other.

***

Jennifer Grey, famously known for her role in Dirty Dancing, underwent rhinoplasty that dramatically transformed her distinctive look. While the surgery was successful by conventional standards, it altered her unique appearance to such an extent that she even joked about being unrecognizable in Hollywood. Her decision to have cosmetic surgery was her own, driven by personal desire rather than industry pressure. Yet, it sparked considerable discussion about the price of fitting in versus preserving one's individuality. Her talent remains undeniable, but some fans still pine for the original Baby—a reminder that sometimes imperfections are a star's greatest asset.

16 Pete Burns

Pete Burns has spoken extensively about his unhappiness with many of his cosmetic procedures, especially the polyacrylamide injections in his lips, a decision that required bundles of money and over a year to fix. This is a reminder that cosmetic surgery is sometimes incredibly dangerous, especially if you aren't dealing with professionals.

***

Burns' experience underscores the significant risks involved in cosmetic enhancements. Despite the lure of achieving a certain aesthetic, his journey through reconstructive procedures serves as a cautionary tale. It's essential for anyone considering plastic surgery to thoroughly research and choose certified, reputable surgeons. Otherwise, the consequences can be both emotionally and physically taxing, with outcomes like Burns', who lamented his results and the lengthy, costly path to remediation.

17 Michaela Romanini

Michaela is a beautiful woman. You can still easily see that. The sad story here is her collagen addiction – and by now, unfortunately, I think it qualifies as an addiction. This is a problem that happens too often as a result of body dysmorphia, which is truly tragic. Can you imagine looking in the mirror and never being happy with a single thing you see? Can you imagine seeing a distorted image that no one else sees? What hell.

***

Michaela's journey is a stark reminder of the pressures society places on women to conform to certain beauty standards. This intense scrutiny can lead to a relentless pursuit of perfection. Her fixation with collagen injections pushed her natural beauty into the background, overshadowed by an unending desire to enhance and alter her appearance. It is a cautionary tale, highlighting the need for self-love and acceptance in a world that often asks so much of our physical selves. Michaela's experience shows how beauty enhancements, when taken to extremes, can become an elusive chase with real consequences.

18 Amanda Lepore

I am not saying one single bad word about Amanda Lepore and no one can make me. Amanda is an inspiration to many members of the LGBTQIA community and from her bubbling breasts to her luscious lips, this high fashion muse is fabulous. She is unrecognizable from who she used to be in so many ways – that's the only reason why you won't believe this particular plastic surgery story.

***

Amanda Lepore's transformation is quite possibly one of the most talked about in the world of glamour and celebrity. Dubbed as one of the most famous transsexual icons and performance artists, her numerous surgical procedures have taken her from a Jersey boy to a voluptuous icon. Lepore's adventures with plastic surgery include rhinoplasty, buttock implants, and a myriad of other enhancements. And while her journey may have been complex and, at times, controversial, Amanda embodies confidence and self-expression at its most extravagant. She's living proof that beauty really is in the eye of the beholder.

19 Axl Rose

I … I don't have anything nice to say about Axl. So I'm not going to say anything at all.
