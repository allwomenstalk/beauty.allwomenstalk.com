# Fall's Must\-Have Nail Polish & Watch Combos ...

According to E's #Fabulist, these are the must-have combos of watches and nail polishes. I agree with (and am dying to buy) every single one of these combos! Watches and nail polishes are some of the easiest ways to accessorize your outfit so be sure to check it out!

## 1. LA MER + JIN SOON

![LA MER + JIN SOON](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/vv/h8/ngshoqag_600x417.jpg)

I am 100% obsessed with the La Mer Collections Black Magic chain watch, $120 ([lamercollections.com](https://www.lamercollections.com/shop/Black-Magic-Chain-Watch/))! Pairing it with the Jin Soon nail polish in Heirloom, $18 ([jinsoon.com](http://www.jinsoon.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)) makes for a perfect look.

## 2. BETSEY JOHNSON + BUTTER LONDON

_ Obsessed with orange? Rock the Betsey Johnson Orange watch, $75 ([betseyjohnson.com](http://www.betseyjohnson.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)) and the Butter London nail lacquer in Brick Lane, $15 (butterlondon.com_).

## 3. MODIFY + FORMULA X

_ Start a conversation with the Modify Rainbow Chevron watch, $45 (modifywatches.com_) and the Formula X Brushed Metallics nail polish in Prophecy, $12.50 ([sephora.com](http://www.sephora.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)).

## 4. RIVER ISLAND + CHANEL

![RIVER ISLAND + CHANEL](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/tj/ae/z7q0zxbt_600x415.jpg)

Go dark with the River Island Leopard Bling watch, $23 ([us.asos.com](https://goo.gl/oSvHvq)) and Chanel Le Vernis in Orage, $27 ([chanel.com](http://www.chanel.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)).

## 5. KATE SPADE + FORMULA X

![KATE SPADE + FORMULA X](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/lc/sb/zcf4kv91_600x417.jpg)

Make a statement with the Kate Spade Rio Wave Metro watch, $195 ([katespade.com](https://www.katespade.com/sale/)) and Formula X Brushed Metallics nail polish in Fate, $12.50 ([sephora.com](http://www.sephora.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)).

## 6. MICHAEL KORS + DEBORAH LIPPMANN

![MICHAEL KORS + DEBORAH LIPPMANN](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wf/dn/sgp78eqt_600x430.jpg)

Bling it up with the Michael Kors Silver Glitz watch, $275 ([shopbop.com](http://www.shopbop.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)) and pair it with the Deborah Lippmann nail polish in Take the "A" Train, $20 ([deborahlippmann.com](http://www.deborahlippmann.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)).

## 7. OASIS + 3.1 PHILLIP LIM for NARS

![OASIS + 3.1 PHILLIP LIM for NARS](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/h1/e2/tmf1ap3k_600x431.jpg)

I want (need) this Oasis Blue Floral watch, $48 ([us.asos.com](https://goo.gl/vMMotB)) with the 3.1 Phillip Lim for Nars nail polish in Hell Bent, $20 ([narscosmetics.com](http://www.narscosmetics.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)).

## 8. OLIVIA BURTON + ESSIE

![OLIVIA BURTON + ESSIE](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/e0/ei/fsrqiq8k_600x444.jpg)

Pair a fun color like the one the Olivia Burton Wonderland watch, $65 ([oliviaburton.com](https://www.oliviaburton.com/uk/home?)) with a calming color like the Essie nail polish in Take it Outside, $8.50 ([essie.com](https://www.essie.com/nail-polish/whats-new)).

## 9. MAY28TH + DEBORAH LIPPMANN

_ This fall, rock some summer vibes with May28th Palm Print watch on your wrist! It's $44 (may28th.me_) and looks incredible paired with the Deborah Lippmann nail polish in Autumn in New York, $20 ([deborahlippmann.com](http://www.deborahlippmann.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)).

## 10. ALDO + JIN SOON

![ALDO + JIN SOON](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/u0/yc/jjts1lpq_600x417.jpg)

For a classy and chic look, pair the Aldo Miraon watch, $25 ([aldoshoes.com](http://www.aldoshoes.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)) with the Jin Soon nail polish in Mélange, $18 ([jinsoon.com](http://www.jinsoon.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)).

## 11. MARC by MARC JACOBS + ESSIE

![MARC by MARC JACOBS + ESSIE](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ok/jw/oqirerka_600x444.jpg)

I LOVE the idea of pairing these two bold colors together. Make a splash with blue and red by pairing the Marc by Marc Jacobs Henry Dinky watch, $175 ([revolveclothing.com](https://www.revolveclothing.com/VerifyHuman.jsp?url=%2Fr%2FDisplayProduct.jsp%3F&source=botcfbs)) and the Essie nail polish in Style Cartel, $8.50 ([essie.com](https://www.essie.com/nail-polish/whats-new)).

## 12. ASOS + ZOYA

![ASOS + ZOYA](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/lv/by/c49m9387_600x418.jpg)

I need this color in my life! It's the ASOS Girlfriend Watch, ([us.asos.com](https://goo.gl/LtzyFd)) $43 paired with the Zoya Nail polish in Veronica, ([zoya.com](https://goo.gl/BUuSRL)) $9.
