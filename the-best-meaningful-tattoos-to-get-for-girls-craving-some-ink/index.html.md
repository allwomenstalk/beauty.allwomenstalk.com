# The 7 Best Meaningful Tattoos to Get for Girls Craving Some Ink ...

Tattoos are a great trend that has risen over the past few years, but like all trends, our likeness of our own can fade. Tattoos with meaning behind them, however, stand the test of time and will be meaningful to you throughout your life. Here are 7 of the best meaningful tattoos.

## 1. Semi-Colon Tattoo

![face, nose, cheek, close up, skin,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ba/qx/thfn19pr58e474dd62a21372922247_600x849.jpg)

To support those with depression and self-harm, this tattoo symbolizes the choice to continue life, as a semi-colon continues a sentence.

## 2. Latin Tattoo

![clothing, tattoo, arm, mouth, muscle,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/xw/zt/b4xy45yb58e476f18d455715625339_450x604.jpg)

Translated from Latin, this tattoo means "knowledge is power" and is a popular quote by Sir Francis Bacon.

## 3. Arrow Tattoo

![tattoo, arm, fashion accessory, leg, hand,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/sa/ln/ebhqcro758e4778b571a4434772036_595x594.jpg)

This tattoo symbolizes how best to look at life. When life drags you back with hard times, it means it is about to launch you into something great.

## 4. 3 Triangles Tattoo

![arm, tattoo, leg, hand, organ,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/hr/p5/oimgszf959242aee498d2981678007_600x566.jpg)

The three triangles can represent many things. The most popular being past, present, and future or body, soul, and spirit.

## 5. Psalm Tattoo

![face, close up, photography, beauty, skin,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/j8/r6/u9mqov5s58e478fa12b26372566885_600x563.jpg)

Meaningful for obvious reasons, a psalm tattoo can show your faith and beliefs. This tattoo is not for fair-weather believers, but for those who are truly strong in their beliefs.

## 6. Chinese Symbol Tattoo

![hair, face, white, black and white, tattoo,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/mi/dj/dq86fyzs58e47a1f263ee492483781_488x700.jpg)

Chinese symbols are a way to tattoo a meaningful word to you with one symbol (or more if you prefer). Many popular Chinese symbols are for beauty, grace, destiny, and courage.

## 7. Last Heartbeats Tattoo

![tattoo, face, arm, skin, lip,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/bz/yg/a9jifikf58e47ad69f0a2827733816_600x800.jpg)

A very meaningful tattoo, this symbolizes the actual last heartbeats of a loved one that has passed away. This tattoo is a great example of a tattoo that will never lose substance.

These are the 7 most meaningful tattoos and are great examples of tattoos that will last in substance for a lifetime. Which one will you decide to get?
