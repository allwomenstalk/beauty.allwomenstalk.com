# 7 Quotes about Beauty to Stick to Your Mirror ...

Everyone has insecurities, but there are quotes about beauty that will make you feel better about yourself. There is too much pressure to be externally beautiful that people forget the importance of internal beauty. Would you rather have a best friend who is gorgeous, or who is always there for you? The answer is obvious, so it's ridiculous to believe that your looks are everything. Here are some quotes about beauty that will remind you to keep your priorities in order:

## 1. Coco Chanel

![Coco Chanel](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/hs/0o/gdd4dmsk_500x500.jpg)

You should never pretend to be someone that you're not. You have to realize that you're an amazing person, even though you're flawed. You shouldn't be ashamed to be yourself because you're an incredible girl! This is one of the quotes about beauty that emphasizes the importance of your personality.

## 2. Sophia Loren

![Sophia Loren](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/rz/aj/dupu1nm3_500x375.jpg)

Beauty is all about perception--everyone sees things differently. You don't need a man to tell you that you're beautiful in order to feel special. You need to realize your worth on your own. It sounds selfish, but your opinion is the only one that matters. That means you should ignore the negative things that others say about you.

## 3. Marc Jacobs

![Marc Jacobs](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ha/n2/g7aqqxpk_500x260.jpg)

Think about your best friend. Even though you love her, you can probably list a few of her flaws. Of course, they don't make you want to hang out with her less. You still adore her, because her flaws are what makes her her. They add to her personality, and you wouldn't want her to act any other way. If she did, then she wouldn't be the same girl you became friends with.

## 4. Marilyn Monroe

![Marilyn Monroe](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/cz/k0/w7xshpge_500x332.jpg)

It's upsetting to see how many women genuinely believe that they aren't skinny or pretty enough. You're perfect just the way you are! Society tricks us into believing that certain things are important, but they're not. You have to realize that magazines and stores want to make money. That's why they push you to buy their expensive clothes and make-up.

## 5. John Green

![John Green](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ct/dw/zfofxx17_500x476.jpg)

Do you see the theme that's emerging? There's no such thing as perfect. Everyone has flaws, but that doesn't make them any less valuable. Even if you feel like you're alone, you're worthy of love. One day, others will realize that. All that matters now is that you realize it.

## 6. Miley Cyrus

![Miley Cyrus](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/vf/xc/nq2ez80i_400x302.jpg)

Some people don't believe that Miley is a good role model, but she has had some wise words of advice. No matter what people say about her, she continues to do what makes her happy. She ignores the 'haters' and enjoys herself, because that's [what life is all about](https://inspiration.allwomenstalk.com/this-is-what-life-is-all-about/). As she has said, you should remember that everyone is imperfect. Whether you're a young girl in school or a famous singer, you have flaws. It doesn't matter whom you are.

## 7. Audrey Hepburn

![Audrey Hepburn](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ne/vx/h2vo76z8_570x475.jpg)

Whenever you look in the mirror, remember that there are things that are more important than your looks. You shouldn't have a meltdown if your hair is a little messy or your winged eyeliner didn't come out perfectly. Others are in the same boat, so they'll understand. Who is your celebrity role model? What's the best advice that they've ever given?
