# Shockingly Cheap Drugstore Beauty Products Celebrities Use ...

Celebrities typically have their personal glam squad to help them look their best, but that doesn’t mean they don’t love a good bargain! Turns out there are some tried and true products that celebs and their makeup artists turn to regardless of the many high-end products they have at their disposal. So just because your bank account isn’t at Rihanna status, that doesn’t mean you can’t get the celebrity look way less! Keep reading to see if some of your favorites made the list and discover some new items while you’re at it!
