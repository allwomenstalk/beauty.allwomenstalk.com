Where was my life before I discovered the best bronzers on the market? Bronzers are so amazing for highlighting your skin, and giving you that beautiful sun-kissed glow you get at the beach. I used to rely on tanning in the sun to get a bronzed look, but when I became more interested in taking care of my skin, I turned to bronzers. I have been hooked ever since, and in my opinion, a great bronzer does two things: first, it gives you that gorgeous sun-kissed glow, and second, it doesn’t look fake or flake off. After that, there are many options to choose from from SPF factors, special shimmer factors, or different colors for the season. Of course, price is also a factor to consider. Check out our list of the best 11 bronzers on the market, and be sure to tell which one is your personal favorite!
