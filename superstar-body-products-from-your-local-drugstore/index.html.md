# Superstar Body Products from Your Local Drugstore ...

Body products don’t have to cost you a fortune. There are plenty wonderful body products to choose from right at your local drugstore. It’s fine to enjoy your splurges, but you don’t want to overlook these superstar body products. Some of them may even make the list of products you can’t live without.

## 1. Dove Beauty Bar

![Dove Beauty Bar](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ky/qo/wt9s1iuq55d34e58d61c9786578640_350x160.jpg)

If you’re a bar soap kind of girl then you can’t go wrong with the Dove Beauty Bar. This lovely little beauty product has been a bathroom staple for almost 60 years now. It’s perfect for your skin because it’s made with one quarter moisturizing cream. It’s a difference you can feel. Don’t be surprised when your skin becomes noticeably softer after a few showers with this jewel.

## 2. Caress Evenly Gorgeous Exfoliating Body Wash

![Caress Evenly Gorgeous Exfoliating Body Wash](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ia/ia/biwx8v2s55d34dfe9feb1152291982_450x450.jpg)

This body wash has made it into my shower again and again over the last few years. It’s one that I have on a regular rotation. I just can’t get enough of how luxurious it makes my skin feel and the wonderful scent it leaves behind. The fragrance of burnt brown sugar and Karite butter is positively addicting. It’s one I’ve never tired of.

## 3. Sonia Kashuk Yellow Alluriana Body Polish

![Sonia Kashuk Yellow Alluriana Body Polish](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/cj/n2/j0lcaaag55d34f4d8fede928961341_480x412.jpg)

Sonia Kashuk body products have a strong following and it’s no wonder. They’re quality products at a good price. This body polish will help you get the freshly scrubbed feeling you’re craving. You can exfoliate to your heart’s content with this body polish. Formulated with a mixture of grapefruit, verbena and musk, it’s a delight for the nose as well. And if you love all of your body products to coordinate, you can have that with this line of fragrance.

## 4. Bodycology Scarlet Kiss Moisturizing Body Cream

![Bodycology Scarlet Kiss Moisturizing Body Cream](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/gt/qd/ufhhgz3855d34fbe1d5d3371581849_450x450.jpg)

This is another drugstore superstar body product I come back to time and time again. I love the fragrance that results from this mixture of pomegranate, peach and vanilla. This body cream is rich and decadent, making it a treat to use. It’s very moisturizing and a perfect choice to fight off dry skin. And at around $4, it’s a steal!

## 5. Burt’s Bees Lemon and Vitamin E Bath and Body Oil

![Burt’s Bees Lemon and Vitamin E Bath and Body Oil](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/iv/bx/adav9rnt55d34ff91b396566255523_600x496.jpg)

Maybe your skin needs a little more help in the moisturizing department. For girls with extremely dry skin, there’s Burt’s Bees Lemon and Vitamin E Bath and Body Oil. Use this product in your bath or apply it directly to your skin afterward. Either way, it’ll make dry skin issues a thing of the past. This lovely blend of good for your skin ingredients will make you fall in love with the product.

## 6. Tree Hut Peppermint and Sugar Foot Scrub

![Tree Hut Peppermint and Sugar Foot Scrub](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/r4/m5/blzqev0055d3502e511ce291598301_600x600.jpg)

Feet sometimes need a special product to keep them soft and supple. They take a lot of abuse and deserve a special treatment. This peppermint and sugar foot scrub is just the product you need. It helps to scrub rough areas away and leave your feet baby soft. Tree Hut has a wonderful line of products, including this one.

## 7. Aquaphor Healing Ointment

![Aquaphor Healing Ointment](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/um/m3/yf7akufh55d350b8380ce838191142_500x447.jpg)

Lastly, when special skin issues arise, Aquaphor Healing Ointment is what you need. It’s specifically formulated for dry, cracked and irritated skin. It can treat Eczema and Rosacea as well as more common skin ailments. It’s highly recommended by dermatologists. Rated at 4.7 stars on Amazon, it’s a must for every bathroom cabinet.

These are 7 superstar body products you need for your bath and body needs. What body products are your favorites? I can’t wait to be inspired by your favs!
