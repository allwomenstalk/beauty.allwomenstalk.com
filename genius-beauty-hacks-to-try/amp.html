Frequently Asked Questions

How can I make my makeup last all day?
Use a good primer before applying makeup and set it with a setting spray for longer wear.
What’s a quick hack for fuller-looking eyelashes?
Apply a coat of mascara, then dust some loose powder before adding a second coat to boost volume.
How do I reduce under-eye puffiness fast?
Place chilled spoons or cucumber slices on your eyes for 5-10 minutes to soothe puffiness.
Can I prevent lipstick from smudging?
Line your lips with a lip liner before applying lipstick and blot with tissue to keep it in place.
What’s an easy way to soften dry skin?
Use a natural oil like coconut or almond oil after showering to lock in moisture.
How can I refresh my hair without washing it?
Spritz dry shampoo at the roots to absorb oil and add volume instantly.
Is there a quick fix for chipped nail polish?
Apply a thin layer of clear topcoat over the chip to smooth it out and prevent peeling.
How do I make my eyeshadow colors pop more?
Apply a light concealer or eyeshadow primer on your lids before adding color.
What’s a simple hack to highlight cheekbones?
Use a highlighter or lighter shade of concealer on the tops of your cheekbones for a natural glow.
Can I use household items for beauty remedies?
Yes! Ingredients like honey, yogurt, and oatmeal can be great for DIY masks and exfoliators.
