# 7 Date Night Beauty Must\-haves to Snag Now ...

Before you get ready for your next date, I recommend having some of these date night beauty must-haves on hand! Date nights are kind of a big deal so you want to look and feel your best, and there’s no reason to not pull out all the stops when you’re getting ready. Whether it’s your first date with your crush or your weekly date night with your partner, these fabulous date night must-haves will help ensure you look like the knockout you really are!

## 1. Bold Lipstick

![Bold Lipstick](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/dy/an/ve/zxqcjdiw_599x871.jpg)

When you’re on a date, you most likely want to draw attention to your lips so one of the top date night beauty must-haves is your favorite color of lipstick. Aside from brightening up your face, lipstick will help you flaunt your lips and show off your bolder side. You can splurge on a brand new color you’ve been wanting to try or bring along your signature lip color. Either way, bold lips are memorable and sexy!

## 2. Perfume

![Perfume](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/xy/g8/wv/h0f7dbyz_349x303.jpg)

If you’re going out on a date, you can’t forget perfume or body spray! If you happen to be heading out after work, throwing a solid or rollerball perfume in your bag is a good way to give yourself a touch-up while you’re out. Aside from smelling good, wearing perfume can give you an extra boost of confidence and it’s a fun way to show your individuality. Plus, there’s said to be a strong connection between scent and memory, so if your date happens to smell a similar fragrance as the one you wear, he’ll think of you!

## 3. Setting Spray

![Setting Spray](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/rf/0i/fj/xcsl568h_349x512.jpg)

Makeup setting spray is one of the date night beauty essentials that comes in handy no matter what the occasion. Setting spray is key to helping your makeup keep up with you all night or helping control excess shine if your skin is oily. One of the tricks I like to use is to spray your bare face with setting spray once and then again after you apply all of your makeup for extra staying power.

## 4. Blotting Papers

![Blotting Papers](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/jq/g7/j6/dovdmzt7_399x385.jpg)

This is another date night beauty product for my gals with oily skin. If you’re prone to oil, keep these babies in your bag so you can nix shine in an instant. There are tons of drugstore brands that work great, but Shiseido makes some that are coated in Hydroxyapatite which absorbs oil like a champ and won’t bother your makeup.

## 5. Eyeliner

![Eyeliner](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fp/ti/dk/fiap79fa_395x479.jpg)

Whether it’s liquid, gel or pencil, eyeliner is a date night and beauty classic. Since eyeliner sometimes has a tendency to fade after a few hours, it’s worth it to keep an extra one as a back-up. Your eyes are another area you want to emphasize since your date will be looking into them, so if you normally don’t wear eyeliner, date night is a great night to rock it and try something new.

## 6. Eye Drops

![Eye Drops](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/u1/fy/pb/lqhq8wfz_399x340.jpg)

If you’re going out after a long day, perk up your eyes by using some refreshing eye drops to hydrate and whiten your eyes. No matter how perfect your outfit, hair or makeup are, red eyes are not something you can get away with. It’s a sure-fire way to look tired and you’ll want to look irresistibly fresh-faced and ready for the night ahead. Look for eye drops that help reduce redness and give off a cooling sensation when applied.

## 7. Nail File

![Nail File](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/gl/hc/we/tofpdjzt_299x214.jpg)

The last of the date night beauty essentials to have is the nail file. No matter whether you have natural or artificial nails, you never know when a nail emergency might come up and you don’t want to be caught unprepared! Nail files are handy to have to fix any broken or chipped nails, but if you use a metal file, it can also double as a tool to help open things so you don’t ruin your nails or even as a weapon to protect yourself if the need ever arises.

In addition to the aforementioned date night beauty must-haves, don’t forget breath mints or gum so you can freshen up in case you get close to your date at the end of the night! If you’re meeting someone brand new for the first time, be sure you tell someone where you’re going just to stay on the safe side. Aside from that, the most important thing is to have fun! Hopefully these products will help you forget about any possible beauty blunders and focus on your date. What are some of your must-haves for date night?
