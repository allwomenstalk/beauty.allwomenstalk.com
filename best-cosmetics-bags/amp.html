13 Best Cosmetics Bags ...

By Lisa • 13 Comments

We all need good cosmetics bags in our lives. Even if you don’t wear that much makeup, cosmetics bags provide us with a stylish place to keep all of our health and beauty goodies together. If you’re in the market for a new makeup bag, I found some great choices that are worth looking into.

1 Kate Spade New York Medium Heddy Cosmetics Bag

Kate Spade knows a thing or two about making a good bag, so we can count on her to create a fashionable and functional makeup bag. This bag is on the smaller side, but it’s perfect for ladies who only carry a few must-haves and don’t carry a large handbag. The nylon lining makes cleaning makeup spills a breeze.

Price: $70.00 at shop.nordstrom.com

2 Trish McEvoy Makeup Planner System

This cosmetics bag is for the makeup maven who loves organization. No need to throw all of your expensive makeup together when have a compartment for everything. This smart planner is customizable so all of your makeup is visible and easily accessible. Comes in petite and large sizes, also.

Price: $68.00 at trishmcevoy.com

3 Tory Burch Stacked Logo Travel Bag

The Stacked Logo Travel Bag makes a fab travel companion. It looks great, opens up to zippered compartments for easy organization and has a hook so you can hang up your stuff and get ready anywhere.

Price: $150.00 at shop.nordstrom.com

4 Stephanie Johnson Sumatra Zip Makeup Bag - Medium

Stephanie Johnson’s makeup bags and jewelry rolls are big amongst celebs for their stylish shapes and designs. The medium size Sumatra bag gives you enough room to stash all your beauty faves without hogging all the space in your purse or tote. The sassy black and white ikat design with lime green trim perfectly complements the earthy bamboo zipper pull.

Price: $38.00 at neimanmarcus.com

5 LeSportsac Cosmetics Case - Sweetheart Travel Bag

LeSportsac makes some of the most colorful and functional bags and their cosmetic bags are no different. This nylon makeup bag is easy to clean, lightweight and has plenty of pockets so that you can store all your beauty basics together in one sweet bag.

Price: $55.00 at bloomingdales.com

6 Bobbi Brown Organizer

Ideal for those with more cosmetics, the Bobbi Brown organizer is a favorite amongst the makeup obsessed masses. Its interior pockets are designed to hold up to seven brushes and feature two removable mesh pouches.

Price: $60.00 at barneys.com

7 Trina Aurora Cosmetic Box

I love the sleek design of Trina Aurora's Cosmetic Box. Aside from being super stylish, it’s very easy to carry around and use with its removable pencil case, inner zippered compartment and top handle. You’ll have all your beauty essentials at your fingertips with this small but roomy cosmetic bag.

Price: $40.59 at soap.com

8 Sonia Kashuk Geometric Print Roll up Valet

Travel in style with Sonia Kashuk’s Geometric Print Roll Up Valet! This chic cosmetics bag makes getting ready on the go a breeze. The polyester lining and shell make clean-ups fast and the multiple compartments make sorting simple.

Price: $35.00 at target.com

9 Marc by Marc Jacobs Cosmetics Case - Dreamy Logo Neoprene

Store your makeup in a cosmetic bag that’s as stylish as you are! Marc Jacobs’ Dreamy Logo case has a zip-top opening which extends to the sides so you have plenty of room to store all of your favorite beauty products and essential beauty tools.

Price: $88.00 at neimanmarcus.com

10 Vera Bradley Large Cosmetic Case

Vera Bradley’s collection of colorful quilted accessories make an ideal cosmetic bag for travel or just to keep your favorite cosmetics close by. Made from cotton, this durable yet lightweight makeup bag is easy to clean, use and love. If you like to keep all of your cosmetics together without any compartments, this bag is perfect for you.

Price: $30.00 at ebags.com

11 Sephora Collection the Vacationer

Whether you’re going on a vacation or just a night out on the town, you want to have your grooming essentials nearby for touch-ups or beauty emergencies. Sephora’s Vacationer contains a brush holder and a net pocket so all of your cosmetics and tools are neatly tucked away and ready to use at a moment’s notice.

Price: $42.00 at sephora.com

12 Apple & Bee Organic Cotton Cosmetics Case

For the ladies who just like to keep the bare necessities on hand, this simple and slim cosmetics case is perfect for just that. This eco-friendly makeup bag made of organic cotton is small enough to fit in a small purse but big enough to carry your favorite lip products, eye liner or anything else you want to keep close by.

Price: $34.00 at shop.nordstrom.com

13 Modella Transparent/Black Makeup Bag

Sometimes you just need a cosmetic bag that’s simple. With Modella’s makeup bag you can easily locate your beauty products at a glance and there’s enough room for all of your makeup needs. This durable bag is perfect for toting around, easy to clean and at a price under $5, you can’t beat the value!

Price: $4.88 at walmart.com

All of these makeup bags have unique features that make them easy-to-use and easy to keep all of your items neat, organized and out of the way. What makeup bag do you currently use? Which one of these bags would you want to try out?
