# 7 Best Chapsticks for Winter ...

The hardest part about Winter for me, is being able to make it outside without having dry, cracked, lips... so I'm always looking for the best Chapsticks and lip balms! The weather itself has a big role in making normally kissable lips totally untouchable! I've gone above and beyond to find the best Chapsticks for those Winter woes!

## 1. Burt's Bees Original

![Burt's Bees Original](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/beauty/2012/12/11099-00-1_xl-253x300_253x300.jpg)

[burtsbees.com](https://www.burtsbees.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
Burt's Bees is my personal favorite brand of Chapstick! With their wide variety of flavors, this brand never seems to disappoint when it comes to providing a smooth, moisturized finish on my lips! The balm in the yellow tube contains coconut and sunflower oil that helps nourish, condition, and seal in hydration. The formula also contains peppermint oil, which allows you to feel your lips tingle as it works in moisture. You can also pick up the tinted lip balm for a touch of color. I highly recommend it; it's definitely one of the best Chapsticks!

## 2. Vaseline

![Vaseline](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/beauty/2012/12/2_vaseline_256x296.jpg)

[vaseline.us](http://www.vaseline.us/products/vaseline-lip-therapy/original/)
Vaseline never ceases to amaze me on the countless things you can do with it! This cute little pocket sized Vaseline can not only protect and moisturize those sensual lips, but, it also can be handy for soothing dry winter skin! Talk about a two for one deal!

## 3. Carmex

![Carmex](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/beauty/2012/12/3_carmex_242x360.jpg)

[mycarmex.com](https://www.mycarmex.com/classic-lip-balm/original-jar/)
Carmex made it to my top 3 because it's medicated! I don't know about you but, sometimes during the Winter I am prone to getting Winter blisters and cold sores. Yuck! Carmex helps me not only moisturize and protect, it also helps prevent and treat those nasty little Weather fiends!

## 4. Eucerin

![Eucerin](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/beauty/2012/12/00383_Ampliada-300x300_300x300.jpg)

[eucerin.com](https://select.eucerin.com/)
This Chapstick, just like Vaseline, has always done wonders on my lips! The Eucerin/Aquaphor products are fantastic in many ways! I personally enjoy the fact that this little gem helps reduce the redness around the lips for those dry, cracked moments! Kudos Eucerin!

## 5. Blistex

![Blistex](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/beauty/2012/12/5_blistex_213x264.jpg)

[blistex.com](https://www.blistex.com/?option=com_content&amp%3Bview=article&amp%3Bid=135&amp%3BItemid=116)
Blistex provides a variety of different types of Chapstick for any personal needs! My personal favorite is the Blixtex Five Star Lip Protection! Perfect for soothing, protecting, and hydrating for the cold weather! It also has SPF 30! Brilliant if I do say so myself!

## 6. Chapstick

_ chapstick.com_
Now, what kind of list would I have if I didn't include Chapstick itself?! Chapstick makes it to my number six spot - with its medicated version. The medicated version soothes and cools sore lips. If you're like me with all the lip licking, my lips get very dry- quick! Thankfully, Chapstick helps moisturize, soothe, and protect!

## 7. Burt's Bees with Eucalyptus Oil

![Burt's Bees with Eucalyptus Oil](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/beauty/2012/12/01920-00_xl-253x300_253x300.jpg)

[burtsbees.com](https://www.burtsbees.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
Burt's Bees makes it back onto my list with another amazing product! This product is the lip balm with Eucalyptus oil, it literally defies dry lips! And, the scent is pretty amazing too!

These brands have helped my lips and I hope they can help yours! Do you have any favorite Chapsticks that you think defy those crazy Winters? What do you think about my list? Let me know!
