# 7 Beauty Products from around the World ...

Beauty products from around the world can vary from the famous favorites to the lesser known cult items. With international travel and the internet making the world a more global place, sharing our favorite country-specific products has never been easier. Take a quick trip around the globe with the following beauty products from around the world!

## 1. Embryolisse Lait-Crème Concentré

![Embryolisse Lait-Crème Concentré](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/beauty/2012/11/1_embryolisse-lait-cr-me-concentr_101x276.jpg)

Let’s start our look of beauty products from around the world in one of the most beautiful countries in the world, France. It’s been labelled as a bit of a miracle cream, so no wonder so many people want to get their hands on it! While it’s harder to get a hold of in other countries, this moisturizing cream is readily available in pharmacies throughout France.

## 2. Moroccanoil Oil Treatment

![Moroccanoil Oil Treatment](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/beauty/2012/11/2_moroccanoil-oil-treatment_163x241.jpg)

While the main ingredient of argan oil originates from Morocco, this cult beauty buy is actually a product of the United States of America. It can be used on hair as a conditioning, styling, or finishing product and is often praised in beauty columns the world over.

## 3. Wei East Ageless Pearl Cream

![Wei East Ageless Pearl Cream](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/beauty/2012/11/3_wei-east-ageless-pearl-cream_273x241.jpg)

The next stop in our quest for exploring beauty products from around the world is China. This anti-aging and anti-wrinkle cream uses pearl particles in its formula which is quite luxurious when you think about it.

## 4. Butter London Nail Polish

![Butter London Nail Polish](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/beauty/2012/11/4_butter-london-nail-polish_114x208.jpg)

When it comes to nail polishes, there are a few brands that have quickly gained cult status and Butter London is up there with the best of them. Their nail lacquers are non-toxic and come in a variety of fashionable colours. Best of all? Their products are sold all over the world.

## 5. Korres Body Butter

![Korres Body Butter](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/beauty/2012/11/5_korres-body-butter_146x285.jpg)

Hailing from Greece, the Korres brand is well known for its focus on natural products. Their body butters are especially lush. Available in Jasmine, Guava, Quince, Fig, and Japanese Rose, the problem is just deciding which one to buy!

## 6. Lucas’ Paw Paw Ointment

![Lucas’ Paw Paw Ointment](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/beauty/2012/11/6_lucas-paw-paw-ointment_114x288.jpg)

A cult favorite for beauty editors and bloggers around the world over, this ointment can be used as a lip balm or skin salve. It’s made from fermented papaya and also has antimicrobial properties. It’s perfect for those who prefer fuss-free beauty products.

## 7. Labello Lip Balm

When it comes to comparing beauty products from around the world, Labello is Germany’s version of the humble chapstick. There are a range of different formulas and flavors available, but you can’t really beat the original Classic Care product.

Who knew that your humble makeup bag could host a United Nations of beauty products? You don’t always have to travel to the host countries to buy these beauty products from around the world either, since many of these brands are available globally.
