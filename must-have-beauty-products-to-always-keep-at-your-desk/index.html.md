# 7 Must\-Have Beauty Products to Always Keep at Your Desk ...

If you’re working the usual 9 to 5, there are must-have beauty products out there to help keep you feeling fresh and fabulous. That’s right: you can still look put-together even after spending hours catching up on e-mails and dreaming up big presentations. If you've ever wanted to go from day to evening in no time flat or found yourself feeling a little blah in the middle of the day, then you've probably needed some of these awesome fixes yourself. Here are 7 beauty treats for your desk –now go out and get ‘em, working girl!

## 1. Hand Cream Trio Collection

![Hand Cream Trio Collection](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/beauty/2012/05/1_300x300.jpg)

**Price:** $30.00 at [usa.loccitane.com](http://usa.loccitane.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)

If you suffer from dry and cracked hands, these scented creams are must-have beauty products for your desktop. Choose from classic, Cherry Blossom, or Pivoine Flora to keep your fingers silky smooth. I keep my cream on hand (literally) before big meetings. Remember, ladies: you want the big wigs to remember your ideas and not a scratchy handshake.

## 2. Pixi Succulent Lip Twin

![Pixi Succulent Lip Twin](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/beauty/2012/05/2_130x300.jpg)

**Price:** $18.00 at [soap.com](https://goo.gl/huxrRJ)

Lip gloss is a must-have beauty product in general, but it’s especially important to keep in your desk drawer for a little afternoon pick-me-up. I adore this lip balm and cheek crème duo for its soothing oils and extracts. And how could I resist a two-in-one treat? Make sure you check out all the assorted shades as well, there are some gorgeous colors in this line!

## 3. Got 2b Fat-tastic Fresh & Full Dry Shampoo

![Got 2b Fat-tastic Fresh & Full Dry Shampoo](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/beauty/2012/05/3_got-2b-fat-tastic-fresh-full-dry-shampoo_300x300.jpg)

**Price:** $5.99 at [drugstore.com](https://www.walgreens.com/)

The problem: you slept through your alarm and had to skip a morning shower. The quick fix: this volumizing, freshening dry shampoo. I prefer clean tresses as much as the next gal, but when I’m in a pinch, this little bottle can work wonders for my locks. Remember, dry shampoo is also a great option when you don't want to use a traditional shampoo every day.

## 4. Sally Hansen Color Quick Nail Color Pen

![Sally Hansen Color Quick Nail Color Pen](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/beauty/2012/05/4_sally-hansen-color-quick-nail-color-pen_300x115.jpg)

**Price:** $7.48 at [drugstore.com](https://www.walgreens.com/)

Nail aficionados, here’s your biggest must-have beauty product! When you’re stuck opening mail or packing boxes all morning, your nails can look like they’ve gone to battle. But with this handy pen, you can give yourself a quick mani (without your boss noticing). As someone who hates chipped nails, I appreciate its easy application and super quick drying time. Plus, it contains conditioning vitamins and UV protection. This tiny pen really packs a punch!

## 5. Charlotte Ronson a Perfect Touch Rollerball Oil

![Charlotte Ronson a Perfect Touch Rollerball Oil](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/beauty/2012/05/5_charlotte-ronson-a-perfect-touch-rollerball-oil_300x300.jpg)

**Price:** $20.00 at [sephora.com](http://www.sephora.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)

This must-have beauty buy is perfect for the summer –or on mornings when you’re wishing for warm weather. Inspired by lazy days on the sand, you’ll love this rollerball’s sunny citrus, floral, vanilla, and musk notes. Whenever I slather this on, I imagine I’m perched on a boardwalk bench watching the waves. It’s just what I need on a slow work day!

## 6. Antiperspirant-Deodorant Roll-on

![Antiperspirant-Deodorant Roll-on](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/beauty/2012/05/6_300x300.jpg)

**Price:** $11.00 at [clinique.com](https://goo.gl/rQaQCj)

If you need a little extra protection after rushing to meetings all day, this deodorant will keep you odor and sweat free. Say goodbye to sweat stains on your fresh white frocks! It's also a good idea to have a spare on those mornings when you're rushing around so much that you forget this vital step. It happens to the best of us, but with this, it won't even matter!

## 7. Stila in the Light Palette

**Price:** $39.00 at [sephora.com](http://www.sephora.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)

This must-have beauty palette features 10 eye shadow shades and waterproof liner. These gorgeous neutrals will work with any outfit, so if you’re heading out for post-work cocktails with the girls, keep this collection handy. It even includes a lookbook with tips and tricks, which I find useful for creating looks to remember.

Must-have beauty products are absolutely vital. It definitely helps to have some handy makeup and beauty products stowed away -you never know when you might need them. I always keep a host of bright beauty products at my cubicle. What are some of your must-have beauty products? Let me know if you're planning to try any of these!
