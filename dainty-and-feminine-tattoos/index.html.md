## 14. Wanderlust

![tattoo,handwriting,arm,human body,chest,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/p8/us/cnu4o_tattoo_handwriting_arm_human-body_chest_600x600.jpg)

For those intoxicated with the deep desire to travel the world!

The "wanderlust" tattoo, as a profound expression of the nomadic spirit, is not just a piece of text or pattern on the body, but more like a symbol of a lifestyle. In addition to tattooing these patterns on the body, you can also put them on [**Luggage Tags**](http://www.luggagetags.com/).

In this way, it not only becomes a symbol of your travel but also extends your desire for freedom and adventure to every journey. On the luggage tag, you can engrave the coordinates of the place you most yearn for in your heart or travel-related elements such as a compass and a globe.

In addition, you can choose a material that echoes it according to the different tattoo styles, so that the tattoo spirit and the design of the luggage tag can be integrated. Such a luggage tag can inspire you to embrace more unknowns and challenges and continue to pursue further dreams.
