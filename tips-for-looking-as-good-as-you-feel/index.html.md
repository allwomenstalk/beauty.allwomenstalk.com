# 7 Tips for Looking as Good as You Feel ...

It's way more important to feel good than it is to look good, but it's even better when you actually look as amazing as you feel. It's not impossible to do, as long as you tweak a few things about your lifestyle. It'll be hard to get used to at first, but soon you'll fall into a healthy routine. Here are some of the best tips for looking as amazing as you feel that you should start following immediately:

## 1. Give Yourself Time

Time is the most important tool you could ever use in order to look amazing. Give yourself enough time to sleep, so that you don't have bags under your eyes. Give yourself enough time in the morning to wash your face and apply your make-up. Give yourself enough time to workout to get the body you've always wanted. Time is the cure to all your worries.

## 2. Drink a Lot of Water

Make sure that you stay hydrated, no matter what. It's easy to remember to drink water when you're under the heat of the sun at the beach. It's harder to remember when snow is falling on your grass. However, you need to remain hydrated, no matter what the weather outside looks like. Your body still needs it.

## 3. Carry a Mirror

Don't you hate when you've had an amazing day, but when you go home, you realize that you had food stuck in your teeth? In order to avoid those little embarrassments, make sure that you carry around a compact mirror. Don't become obsessed with it, because it'll make you seem like you're shallow and overly concerned with appearances. Just glance into it every hour or so to make sure that nothing is out of place.

## 4. Use Specific Products

Don't forget about the beauty products that your skin needs. You should always moisturize your face. You should always brush your teeth. You should always condition your hair. If you forget to do these things, you won't see a change right away, but it'll impact you greatly somewhere down the line.

## 5. Smile All the Time

It's a simple thing, but smiling can make you look like an entirely different person. If you're feeling good, then why wouldn't you want to smile? Let loose and show off your gorgeousness. Smiles cause more smiles, so you'll set off a happy chain reaction.

## 6. Wear Bright Colors

Stop wearing so much black. Yes, it's a flattering color, but it's also a dreary one. Head to the store and buy some more colorful clothing. You don't need to buy neons, but at least get something in red or navy. They're still dark colors, but they'll make you look a little more awake and alive.

## 7. Exercise when You Can

You already know how important exercising is. It'll make you stronger on the inside and on the outside. If you want to look your best, exercising is the best way to get the glow you've been hoping for. You don't have to workout every single day, of course. Just add it to your weekly routine.

If you're feeling amazing, you might as well strive to have your exterior match your interior. That way, you'll be happier than you've ever been before. What else do you do to make sure that you're looking as fabulous as you're feeling?
