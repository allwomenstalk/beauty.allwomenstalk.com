# 8 Best Face Masks Not to Miss ...

Face masks are an excellent addition to our skin care routine, they take minimal effort and give the greatest rewards. They really can supercharge our skin, help with anti-ageing and give skin a gorgeous plump and glowing appearance. A good mask doesn't always come cheap but they do go a long way and should last you a long time; I see them as an investment in my skin. It's a good idea to use once or twice weekly and mix them up so your skin gets the benefits from different masks. As a mask expert/addict, the following list are my 7 best face masks that are my favourite ones to use.

## 1. L'Occitane Immortelle Biologique Cream Mask

![L'Occitane Immortelle Biologique Cream Mask](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/beauty/2012/08/locciatane_215x215.jpg)

Price: $62.00 at [usa.loccitane.com](http://usa.loccitane.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)

A deeply hydrating face mask, this is one of the best face masks for dry or uncomfortable skin, particularly in cold weather. Made from the Immortelle essential oil which has proven anti-ageing qualities, this also makes an astonishingly good anti-ageing mask. Skin literally glows after.

## 2. Payot Dermaforce

![Payot Dermaforce](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/beauty/2012/08/payot_121x251.jpg)

Price: $35.00 at [essentialdayspa.com](http://www.essentialdayspa.com/payot-dermforce-masque-mu-p_14195.htm)

An excellent anti-ageing mask that protects agains environmental pollutants, smooths skin, softens line and gives a radiant glow that lasts for about a week after. It also makes your skin feel extremely soft. Truly one of the best face masks out there.

## 3. Sisley Radiant Glow Express Mask with Red Clay

![Sisley Radiant Glow Express Mask with Red Clay](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/beauty/2012/08/red-225x300_225x300.jpg)

Pricing at [sisley-cosmetics.com](https://goo.gl/0BKyWu)

One of the best face masks, but used differently. You put this on a clean face before you apply your makeup, leave for 5 minutes then wipe off and your skin will look unbelievably smooth, radiant and glowing. Ideal for special occasions or nights out, this really works and it is incredible.

## 4. Sisley Express Flower Gel Mask

![Sisley Express Flower Gel Mask](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/beauty/2012/08/fleur-225x300_225x300.jpg)

Pricing at [sisley-cosmetics.com](https://goo.gl/fXjIQ3)

Sisley's other mask works in the same principle, apply to a clean face and leave for 3 minutes before applying makeup. This mask tones and firms your skin leaving you looking refreshed and wide awake, it is excellent for dehydrated skin that's showing lots of tired lines. No need to rinse off.

## 5. Liz Earle Intensive Nourishing Treatment Mask

![Liz Earle Intensive Nourishing Treatment Mask](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/beauty/2012/08/liz-196x300_196x300.jpg)

Price: $24.00 at [us.lizearle.com](https://www.lizearle.com/)

One of the best face masks for dry, stressed skin. It's deeply rehydrating, it will calm and moisturise even the most sensitive and stressed skin. A really great mask.

## 6. La Roche Posay Hydraphase Intense Masque

_ Pricing at laroche-posay.com_

A fantastic soothing and rehydrating mask, this is like a thermal bath for dehydrated and sensitive skin. Extremely soothing and non-irritating, skin feels like velvet after. No need to rinse off.

## 7. Dermalogica Multivitamin Power Recovery Masque

_ Price: $46.00 at [dermalogica.com](http://www.dermalogica.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)

One of the best face masks for rescuing stressed skin. A highly antioxidant mask, it instantly revitalises dull, dry, dehydrated skin while it smoothes, calms and repairs. It also smells fab!

The best time to use a face mask is at night before you go to bed. My pro tip is to ignore the instructions, sleep with it on and let your face soak up all the goodness overnight and wash it off in the morning. Your skin really will look amazing! It's also a very good idea to use a face mask after a night drinking, your skin really will thank you the next day! Have you tried any of these? Do you know of any other excellent masks?
