# 7 Cheap Household Products You Can Use for Beauty ...

Have you ever thought about using some of your common **household products for beauty**? It’s not as crazy as it sounds! While I won’t ask you to use Comet as a body scrub, there are lots of cheap and simple household beauty fixes for all of your beauty dilemmas! Peep this list of 7 cheap household products you can use for beauty and get pretty for pennies!

## 1. Baking Soda

Did you know that baking soda is one of the most versatile household products for beauty? You can use baking soda with a dab of water to use as a zit zapper, sprinkle some on your toothbrush to whiten teeth or use with honey as a light scrub! Don’t waste any more boxes of baking soda for deodorizing your fridge, use it to beautify!

## 2. Olive Oil

This **household item for beauty is not** only good for your body; it’s great for your skin too! Some ladies swear by using olive oil as an all-natural moisturizer, eye makeup remover or scalp treatment. If oil is too heavy for your face, try using olive oil as a body oil instead and get gorgeous glowing skin!

## 3. Cinnamon

If you fancy a little spice in your life, cinnamon **is a great household product for beauty**! Aside from tasting great, cinnamon can double as a bronzer in a pinch or as a fragrant pimple prophylactic. Combine 1 teaspoon cinnamon with 3 tablespoons of honey and apply to any unsightly blemishes at night and rinse the next morning for clear skin!

## 4. Dryer Sheets

If you like to keep your hair routine simple, you’ll love dryer sheets as a **homemade beauty product**! Dryer sheets come in a variety of scents and you can use a sheet to smooth your hair and reduce any static or flyaways. Don’t want to waste the sheet? Throw it under your car seat as an air freshener or use it to remove lint from your outfit!

## 5. Cooking Spray

Cooking spray is **a great kitchen beauty aid** that you can use in lieu of pricey quick-dry top coats for your manis! The next time you’re in a rush after you give your nails a fresh coat of varnish; spray some unscented and original style cooking spray on your talons for a cheap alternative to a fast-drying top coat. No joke!

## 6. Toothpaste

Toothpaste is a classic **kitchen beauty helper as a** spot treatment for acne but it does a host of other unexpected things such as clean and whiten your nails or soothe a burn from a rogue curling iron. If you’re ever away on a sleepover and caught without your go-to hair product, look for a gel toothpaste and apply sparingly to hair as a quick alternative for a fresh look. The best part is that you'll definitely be minty fresh!

## 7. Petroleum Jelly

Petroleum jelly **is an age-old household beauty secret** that has a plethora of beneficial uses! Dab some jelly on your cuticles before you start your mani to easily wipe away any mistakes. This inexpensive household product for beauty also works great on feet to avoid blisters from painful shoes. Simply apply to problem areas and avoid suffering for the sake of fashion!

Was your favorite household product for beauty covered on this list? This list just goes to show that natural and cheap beauty fixes are very possible and can be found in your own house! What cheap and easy kitchen beauty product do you use and how? I’d love to hear your ideas and suggestions!
