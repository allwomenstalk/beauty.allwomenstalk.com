# 5 Silk Pillowcases for the Best Hair and Skin Ever ...

I’m sure you know by now **why you should use a silk pillowcase**. The smooth surface of silk is much better for your hair because it prevents breakages and also, silk doesn’t absorb moisture from your hair in the same way as cotton or man-made fabrics, leaving your hair shinier and healthy. If you want your gorgeous blowout to last longer, you really should consider a silk pillowcase. **Silk pillowcases** are good for your skin too. The silk provides a gorgeously soft surface for smoother and healthier skin, and like with your hair, doesn’t pull moisture from your skin. Some say that [satin pillowcases](https://beauty.allwomenstalk.com/beauty-benefits-of-sleeping-on-a-satin-pillowcase/) have equal benefits but there’s just something that is lovely and decadent about a silk pillowcase. Here are 5 beautiful **silk pillowcases to suit all budgets**.
