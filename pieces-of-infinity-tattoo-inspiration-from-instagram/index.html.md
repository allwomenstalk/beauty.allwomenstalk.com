# 17 Pieces of Infinity Tattoo Inspiration from Instagram ...

There's nothing wrong with getting the type of tattoo that thousands of people already have, like an infinity sign, because you can put your own spin on it. Make it your own! If you need an idea of how to do that, here are a few pieces of infinity tattoo inspiration from Instagram:

## 1. Flower

If you want to add a pop of color to your tattoo instead of getting a simple, black infinity sign, then you can throw some flowers into the mix.

## 2. Barbed Wire

Getting a tattoo of barbed wire is an easy way to make yourself look more badass. No one will mess with you when you have ink like this.

## 3. Words

If there are a few words that mean a lot to you, then you can turn them into an infinity sign. It's a cute twist on a classic idea.

## 4. Feather

A feather has just the right curve to use as part of your infinity sign. Like the person who posted this Instagram pic, you can even turn your tattoo into a way to honor your new baby!

## 5. Paw Prints

If you don't have a child, but love your puppy like he's your child, you can get a tattoo like this one. It'll let you show your love of animals.

## 6. Watercolor

Do you prefer colorful tattoos? Then get some blue, pink, and yellow ink splashed over your infinity sign.

## 7. Stars

If you like words as much as you like pictures, then you can mix the two together. You might be able to think up a design as adorable as this one.

## 8. Breast Cancer Ribbon

Instead of getting a regular breast cancer ribbon, you can put a twist on it. It'll show your support.

## 9. Bicycle

Is this a bicycle or is it an infinity sign? Well, it's both!

## 10. Birds

You don't have to close off your infinity sign. Instead, you can have birds flying out from the end of it.

## 11. Love

You've been told not to get a tattoo of your boyfriend's name. Of course, if you're going to do it anyway, you might as well get an adorable design like this one.

## 12. Sisters

Even though you might not want to get a matching tattoo with your man, there's nothing wrong with getting one with your sister. After all, you'll be siblings forever.

## 13. Hearts

These infinity signs look just like little hearts touching each other! How cute is that?

## 14. Tiny

You don't need to get a huge tattoo. You can get a super small one on the inside of your finger.

## 15. Anchor

This is a unique design. It's not something that you'd see everyday.

## 16. Forever and Always

If you're a Taylor Swift fan, then you might like the looks of this tattoo. Of course, even if she's not your favorite singer, you can still appreciate this delicate design.

## 17. Rope

Anything that has meaning to you is worth getting. If a tattoo like this speaks to you, then you might want to get something similar to it.

These tattoos are all equally adorable, so hopefully they've given you an idea of what you want to get next! How many tattoos do you currently have?
