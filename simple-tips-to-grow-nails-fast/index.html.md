# 9 Simple Tips to Grow Nails Fast ...

Are you looking for some simple tips to grow nails fast so that you can partake in some DIY manis or sport some long talons for a special occasion? I’ve got your back, girls! I’ve scoured all my resources looking for the easiest and most effective ways to get long, beautiful nails to show off those pretty spring manicures! Read these 9 easy tips and get growing!

## 1. Take Your Vitamins

The most important tip to grow nails fast is to make sure you get your daily dose of vitamins, namely vitamins A, B, C and D. You can definitely get your recommended daily amount by foods or you can also pop a multivitamin to have all your bases covered. Taking vitamins will help strengthen your nails and promote nail growth. Prenatal vitamins and Biotin are said to stimulate nail growth too, but taking a daily multivitamin should work just as well.

## 2. Get Your Blood Flowing

Did you know that getting the blood flowing to your fingers is one of the most effective tips to grow nails fast? If you type often, play piano, cook or do basically anything involving your hands, you’re helping yourself to get a pair of longer fingernails faster. I can attest to this tip to get nails growing working. I type frequently and it feels like as soon as I trim my nails, it’s time to trim them again!

## 3. Cleanse and Moisturize

One of the most important tips to grow nails fast and keep them strong and healthy is to properly clean and moisturize your hands and cuticles regularly. Use cuticle oil to moisturize and apply a protective clear coat every other day to bare nails to prevent cracking, peeling and provide a shield between your nails and the elements.

## 4. Get Balanced

Eating a healthy and consistently balanced diet is one of the best ways to trim some inches off of your waist and to grow nails faster and stronger. Yo-yo dieting is horrible for not only your body but your nails too! Make it a habit to eat balanced meals and get your daily intake of lean protein, fiber and fresh produce to get a set of claws rivaling Rihanna’s super-long set of nails!

## 5. Get Your Rub on

This is one of the weirdest tips to grow nails fast, but if you crave a long set of nails, it’s worth a try. Take a clove of garlic and rub it on your nails for 30 minutes and rinse. To avoid smelling like vampire repellant, rub a lemon wedge on your fingers and nails. Another option is to chop garlic and put it in your polish and apply to nails. Garlic is said to help strength and harden wimpy nails and you can often find garlic extract on the list of ingredients for nail hardeners.

## 6. Put the File Away

I love to shape my nails, but over-filing fingernails can wreak havoc on your nails and prevent you from growing nails fast. Avoid over-filing your nails and sawing back and forth when you do file them down. Don’t ever share your nail file with anyone as the emery board is porous and you can easily spread bacteria so replace it often and avoid getting nail cooties.

## 7. Get Juicy

Another DIY tip to grow nails fast is soak your fingers in orange juice. An easy tip to encourage nail growth is to soak nails in OJ for 10 minutes then rinse off. Orange juice is packed with Vitamin C and folic acid, both which are awesome to induce nail growth. If you can’t stand the smell of garlic, this at-home treatment to grow nails might be a better option!

## 8. Protect and Serve

What’s the easiest way to protect and get nails to grow? Protect your hands and fingers from anything abrasive such as dish detergent or cleaning with harsh household cleaners. Also, avoid using acetone nail polish remover more than once per week as it can weaken and dry out your nails. Wearing gloves while washing dishes and cleaning will save your hands from drying out as well as saving your nails.

## 9. Stay Hydrated

The last and perhaps most important tip to grow nails fast is to stay hydrated and drink lots of water. We all know how essential it is to drink at least 8-10 glasses of water per day for health and weight loss. If we needed another reason to drink up, we now know we need to hydrate ourselves consistently to grow nails long and strong. Keep a big water bottle with you daily and make it goal to finish it each day!

Getting short nails to grow longer and stronger is possible and I hope you found these 9 simple tips to grow nails fast helpful! Do you have any of your own tried and true tips to grow nails fast? I’d love to hear your tips and tricks!
