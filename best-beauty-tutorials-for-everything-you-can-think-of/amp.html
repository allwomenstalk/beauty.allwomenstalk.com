25 Best Beauty Tutorials for All Your Needs ...

A Quick, Everyday Makeup Routine • For All Us Greasy-haired Girls • Back to School Makeup! • A Must-watch for Eye Makeup Beginners • An Epic Tutorial Featuring Everyone's Favorite Urban Decay Palette • More ...

By Heather

Everyone needs a beauty tutorial at some point or another, right? Searching for them can be something of a hassle, though, and it seems like most articles just focus on one aspect – just hair, just makeup, just nails. Not here. No way! I've got 25 of the very best beauty tutorials for pretty much everything you can think of, all in one handy place! From hairstyles to skincare and makeup looks, you'll definitely find at least one must-have tutorial that will help with your beauty routine!

1. A Quick, Everyday Makeup Routine

Need a new go-to routine for a quick makeup look? Here you go! You can substitute your own products and shades, but this is a great base for a fast routine! How do you pull together your speediest routine?

2. For All Us Greasy-haired Girls

Sometimes, you don't have time to wash your hair. Other times, you just don't feel like it. No problem, it's good to give your hair a break! If your hair tends to get oily after a few days without shampoo, this tutorial is for you!

3. Back to School Makeup!

Who doesn't want to look their best on their first day of school – or work, for that matter, or an internship? This video is so helpful if you're stumped about how to do your makeup before a big first day.

4. A Must-watch for Eye Makeup Beginners

Need to polish your eye makeup game? Eye makeup is hard to perfect, but practice makes perfect. Even if you're brand new to the wide world of makeup, this tutorial will soon have you applying your eye makeup like a pro!

5. An Epic Tutorial Featuring Everyone's Favorite Urban Decay Palette

If you love Naked 2, you'll love this tutorial! You'll learn tips, techniques, and fierce looks that will allow you to use your palette to its maximum potential. Don't worry, it's perfectly acceptable to go snag a palette just to get the most out of this video.

6. Contouring Made Easy!

Contouring is easy … once you know how to do it. It takes a lot of practice, too, though. This is one of the easiest, clearest tutorials I've ever seen, and I really think you'll benefit from it if you want to contour but aren't sure how to start.

7. How to Contour like a Contouring Artist

Then again, if you want to contour like one of the pros, study this tutorial. Remember, as you practice, you'll also find a technique that works perfectly for your face. You might make a few adjustments to bring out your best features, but this will give you the know-how you need to get to that point.

8. All the Drugstore Magic You'll Ever Need

Not everyone can afford expensive or even mid-range makeup. For me, a trip to Sephora or Ulta or MAC is usually a luxury, reserved for my very favorite products. And that's fine because there are tons of drugstore brands and products that are absolutely amazing! This tutorial shows you how to use them to their very best advantage.

9. A Fantastic Beginner's Guide to Makeup

New to the makeup game in general? You need some basic tips to get you started! This tutorial is ideal for beginners who aren't used to applying or wearing makeup. It features tips you'll use long after your makeup game reaches epic levels.

10. Pretty, Pretty Prom Style

This tutorial is absolutely great for choosing a lovely look for prom, but really, you can use it for just about any special occasion. Try this look when you need something formal, flattering, and just a little fancy, whether it's prom, Homecoming, or an awesome party!

11. The Secret to Big, Curly Hair

Looking for bouncy locks with lots of volume? Here you go! Follow this tutorial and you'll end up with big, bountiful, beautiful curls!

12. Pull off the Perfect “Ah, I'm Running Late!” Hairstyle

We all run late at some point or another. Better to have a quick, go-to hairstyle at the ready so you don't have to sacrifice your tresses to a too-quick routine. Even when you only have seconds to spare, this tutorial ensures you'll run out of the house with fabulous hair!

13. Hairstyles for Short Hair

Looking for some cute ways to style short hair? There are so many adorable looks you can choose from! For starters, you'll find some truly amazing hair inspo in this vid!

14. The Almighty Waterfall Braid

Find out how to pull off the perfect waterfall braid with this tutorial! Love it! Waterfall braids are so pretty, and after you practice a few times, it might become your signature hairstyle.

15. Easy School Hair

It can be hard to get your hair styled before school or work, too. Fortunately, this tutorial helps you maximize your time and your hair! What do you think?

16. An Amazing Korean Skincare Routine

Korean skincare products are getting so much publicity now and for good reason! Follow the tips and scout out the products in this tutorial, and you'll have the best skin of your life. Seriously, this video opened my eyes to so many new products!

17. A Gorgeous Evening Makeup Routine from Michelle Phan

Oh, you know this tutorial is absolutely incredible! Michelle Phan is so talented and she comes up with so many stunning makeup looks. I want to try this one the next time I go out on the town!

18. Awesome Beauty Hacks, Revealed!

There are so many fantastic hacks in this video! I don't want to ruin it – you seriously need to watch this. Is there anything better than a really great beauty hack?

19. Serve Face like Jeffree Star

Who doesn't want to serve face like Jeffree? Even if you never intend to go full glam, you'll find a look you want to recreate. At the very least, always make these your lipstick goals.

20. Fabulous in-flight Beauty Tips

This is a must-have for any traveler, whether you fly frequently or only occasionally. Being in the air can wreak havoc on your skin, but if you follow the tips in this video, you'll always look airport chic – even seconds after landing!

21. A Step-by-step Guide to Stiletto Nails

Do you love this sinfully sharp nail trend? Watch this tutorial and you can learn how to do it yourself! Not only can you save a lot of money by skipping the salon, but you can also make your nails as long or as short as you like.

22. An Easy Nail Guide Tutorial

It's okay if you're not quite ready for stiletto nails. Here's an easier, simpler guide to follow. Nail care doesn't take long, but it's so important, and it can make such a difference in your manicures.

23. Acrylic Nails 101

You can take advantage of DIY acrylic nails, too. I actually learned a lot from this tutorial. For instance, I figured out why my attempts at acrylic nails always look just awful.

24. Fun and Easy Splatter Party Nail Tutorial

Need a fun, quick manicure that doesn't require a ton of skill? This tutorial has you covered! Splattered nails always look good because, really, it's so hard to get this technique wrong.

25. Ombre Nail Art

Ombre nail art is a little more difficult, but it's so pretty! This tutorial should help you perfect the technique. With some practice, you'll end up with beautifully polished nails featuring a stunning ombre fade.

Did I miss anything? Let me know in the comments, we'll add it in!

Frequently Asked Questions

What are the best beauty tutorials for beginners?
Start with simple makeup basics like foundation application and natural eye looks to build confidence.
How can I find tutorials suited for my skin type?
Look for tutorials that specify your skin type, such as oily, dry, or combination, to get tailored advice.
Are there tutorials for quick everyday makeup?
Yes, many tutorials focus on 5-10 minute routines perfect for busy mornings.
Can I learn skincare routines from beauty tutorials?
Absolutely, many tutorials cover step-by-step skincare tailored to different skin concerns.
What tools do I need to follow beauty tutorials?
Basic tools include brushes, sponges, and common makeup products which are often listed at the start of tutorials.
Do beauty tutorials cover hair care and styling?
Yes, many include hair styling tips, from easy waves to braids and updos.
Are there tutorials for special occasions like weddings?
Definitely, you can find detailed guides for bridal looks and other event-specific styles.
How do I choose tutorials for my age group?
Look for age-specific tutorials that address different concerns and styles suitable for your stage of life.
Can beauty tutorials help with acne-prone skin?
Yes, many tutorials focus on using non-comedogenic products and techniques to cover acne gently.
Where can I find the best beauty tutorials online?
Popular platforms like YouTube and Instagram have diverse creators offering expert tutorials for all levels.
