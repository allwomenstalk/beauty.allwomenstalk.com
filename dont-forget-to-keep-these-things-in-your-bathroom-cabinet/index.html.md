# Dont Forget to Keep These 7 Things in Your Bathroom Cabinet!

There are some staples that you need in your bathroom cabinet. They’re necessities that will prove themselves useful time and time again. I simply don’t know how to get by without them. They’re things I use on a regular basis.

## 1. Cotton Rounds Are a Multipurpose Product

I cannot imagine removing my nail polish without these magical little circles of cotton. One cotton round can go very far in removing nail polish from several fingers. Cotton rounds are also good for other purposes. They’re great at applying medication or removing eye makeup. Once you start to use them you’ll wonder how you ever did without them.

## 2. Dove Beauty Bar is the Best Beauty Bar Available

Dove Beauty Bar is more than your average bar of soap. It’s ¼ moisturizing cream, which keeps your skin soft and smooth. It’s great for everyday use or just for when your skin has a flare up of dryness. Dermatologists recommend it and you’ll probably find you love it, as well. I can’t imagine not having it in my bathroom cabinet. It’s a great choice for sensitive skin, too.

## 3. Vaseline Can do so Many Things for You

Vaseline can be used for so many purposes. It’s a wonderful skin protectant if you’re dealing with chapped cheeks or lips. It can feel soothing to skin that’s irritated. It can also be used to give your skin a bit of shine. Some women even use a tiny bit of it to accentuate their lashes in place of mascara.

## 4. Leave in Hair Treatment Can Strengthen Your Hair

Yes, you use conditioner in the shower but your hair may need something more. A leave-in hair treatment can be the perfect product. It gives your hair another layer of protection. I personally like the Tresemme Split Remedy Leave-in Conditioning Spray. I can honestly say I haven’t found a split end since I’ve been using it over the last year.

## 5. Tweezers Help Keep Your Brows in Tip-top Shape

Tweezers are a must for your bathroom cabinet. They’re useful for more than just arching your eyebrows, too. You can use them to remove an ingrown hair, remove a splinter and other purposes. All tweezers are not created equal, though. It can take some trial and error before you finally find a pair you love.

## 6. Your Favorite Body Lotion Gives Your Skin a Soft, Subtle Scent

Body lotion is a way to wear fragrance in a subtle way. You can add perfume on top of it, of course. But if you only want a soft scent that isn’t overpowering, body lotion is the way to go. Besides adding fragrance, it’s wonderful for your skin. You may get compliments on how soft your skin feels from your partner, too.

## 7. A Good Quality Cuticle Stick Keeps Your Nails Looking Neatly Manicured

A cuticle stick is the key to having lovely, manicured nails. You can’t have a proper manicure if your cuticles are ragged. A cuticle stick takes up very little room and will prove itself useful on a very frequent basis. I use mine every time I do my nails. This is a very inexpensive beauty tool that greatly improves the look of your hands.

These are 7 things you need in your bathroom cabinet. What are the necessities you can’t do without in your bathroom cabinet? I’d love to hear from you.
