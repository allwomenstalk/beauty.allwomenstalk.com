# 11 Essential Beauty Products for Winter That We Can't Live without ...

When the weather turns cold outside, it’s time to turn to some must-have beauty products for winter – you’ll wonder how you ever managed without them! From creams to protect your skin to luscious hair masks and lip balm, there are some beauty products for winter that are an essential if you want to make it through the coldest months of the year looking top-to-toe gorgeous.

## 1. Elizabeth Arden Eight Hour Cream Skin Protectant

![Elizabeth Arden Eight Hour Cream Skin Protectant](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/10/17044706_8158_599x424.jpg)

This amazing cream is a must-have, multi-tasking beauty product that deserves a place in every woman’s make up bag. It’s a great moisturizer, and can be used on your face as well as any dry or rough spots – elbows, cracked heels and knees. It’s also ideal for re-hydrating sore, chapped lips or to add a sheen to cheekbones for naturally beautiful skin. This is one of the beauty products for winter you won’t want to be without!

## 2. Vitamin E Oil

![Vitamin E Oil](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/10/17045402_2376_599x507.jpg)

Whichever brand you choose, vitamin E oil is a great skin protectant. You can use it all over your body to soothe, protect and moisturize and it’s perfect for keeping skin soft and supple when the temperature drops outside. Just a few drops applied to your skin at night can help with redness and irritation from central heating.

## 3. Moroccan Argan Oil

![Moroccan Argan Oil](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/10/17050346_2640_599x322.jpg)

Argan oil is great to protect and moisturize your hair, and you can find masks designed for use on hair, or neat bottles of the oil, which you can use for your body as well. Argan oil is light and won’t weigh your hair down, and a hot oil treatment once every few weeks will help your tresses stay shiny and soft all winter long!

## 4. Astral Moisturizing Cream

![Astral Moisturizing Cream](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/10/17050606_8399_599x453.jpg)

This staple moisturizer can be used all over your body, and in the UK, it’s a cult classic! Cheap and cheerful, it really does the job, leaving your skin soft and protected.

## 5. Liz Earle Cleanse and Polish Hot Cloth Cleanser

![Liz Earle Cleanse and Polish Hot Cloth Cleanser](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/10/17055009_9273_595x507.jpg)

Everyone’s skin needs a little extra help to get glowing in the winter months, and Liz Earle’s Hot Cloth Cleanser really helps to remove dead skin cells, leaving you with a fresh, revitalized complexion. I don’t use it every day, but once a week it helps to give my skin a boost!

## 6. Jurlique Rosewater Balancing Mist

![Jurlique Rosewater Balancing Mist](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/10/17050732_4416_599x519.jpg)

In the winter, I usually skip toner and rebalance my skin with a light mist of this lovely rosewater spray instead. It leaves your skin feeling soft and refreshed, and the scent of roses will follow you around all day!

## 7. Neutrogena Cracked Heel Foot Cream

![Neutrogena Cracked Heel Foot Cream](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/10/17055124_4426_598x943.jpg)

When your feet are tucked away in gorgeous boots all season, it’s easy to forget about sore, cracked heels. Treat them with his super-moisturizing cream from Neutrogena which sinks in straight away and leaves them feeling soft.

## 8. Yes to Carrots Super Rich Body Butter

![Yes to Carrots Super Rich Body Butter](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/10/17051959_7564_599x447.jpg)

I love using this body butter after a long bubble bath, just before I go to bed. It leaves skin soft and protected and smells yummy! Plus, it’s good for your skin as it’s paraben free and boasts all-natural ingredients.

## 9. Neal’s Yard Remedies Beauty Sleep Concentrate

![Neal’s Yard Remedies Beauty Sleep Concentrate](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/10/17054127_7572_569x544.jpg)

This gorgeous serum concentrate sinks into skin whilst you sleep so you wake up with a fresh, smooth complexion. It’s easily absorbed and helps to replenish moisture whilst you get some shut-eye.

## 10. Carmex Lip Balm

![Carmex Lip Balm](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/10/17053519_9784_599x533.jpg)

We all need a great lip balm to protect lips from chapping – Carmex has a satisfying menthol tingle which calms and moisturizes sore, chapped lips and protects them from the elements.

## 11. Nicky Clarke Intensive Moisture Mask

![Nicky Clarke Intensive Moisture Mask](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/10/17053847_5520_599x527.jpg)

This gorgeous hair mask contains Moringa oil, an ingredient found in many high-end skincare lines, and it has the same benefits for your hair. A once-a-week treatment with this mask will help your hair survive all those months of being bundled under a hat!

Without these beauty products, my skin certainly wouldn’t survive the winter. Everyone has their own staples that they can’t live without, and I’d love to hear what some of your favorite products are for the winter months!
