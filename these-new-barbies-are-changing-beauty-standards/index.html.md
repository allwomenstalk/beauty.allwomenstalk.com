# These New Barbies Are Changing Beauty Standards ...

Have you seen the new line of Barbies that are being released? According to [Mental Floss,](https://www.mentalfloss.com/article/74592/mattels-new-barbie-comes-variety-skin-tones-and-body-types) they come in curvy, petite, and tall! That means Barbie is no longer a stick-thin girl with unobtainable proportions.

![](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/c5/sw/ueegg47c56ab7ba85f4fc217592124_600x403.jpg)

Now, she come in all different shapes and sizes. This new set of dolls even has twenty-two different eye colors and seven different skin tones. It's a way to make every little girl out there feel beautiful.

![](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ak/jh/mz5awtee56ab7b82595f7123433641_600x320.jpg)

The days of playing with unrealistic Barbies are over, thanks to Mattel. Hopefully, this means that the future generation will be more comfortable in their skin.
