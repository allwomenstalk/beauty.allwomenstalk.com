# 10 Best Powders and Pencils for Perfect Eyebrows ...

Eyebrows play a big role in our appearance so it makes sense to hunt down and use only the best products for eyebrows to complement and frame our faces. Over the years the number of beauty products created strictly for filling in or enhancing our eyebrows has greatly increased so I wanted to pick 10 of the best powders, pencils, creams and pencils that will give you lush and impeccable eyebrows!

## 1. Make up for Ever Aqua Brow

![Make up for Ever Aqua Brow](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/beauty/2012/08/1_make-up-for-ever-aqua-brow_450x450.jpg)

**Price:** $20 at [sephora.com](http://www.sephora.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
Beauty fanatics will attest to this top beauty product for enhancing brows. Aqua Brow is a gel that comes in three shades, light blonde, blonde and ash and helps to reshape and fill in sparse eyebrows. As the name indicates, the formula is water proof and leaves you with a natural yet long lasting finish that looks great all day!

## 2. Illamasqua Eye Brow Cake

![Illamasqua Eye Brow Cake](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/beauty/2012/08/2_illamasqua-eye-brow-cake_450x450.jpg)

**Price:** $20 at [sephora.com](http://www.sephora.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
Ladies rave about this brow powder as one of the best products for eyebrows around. Illamasqua is known for creating highly pigmented cosmetics so you know this powder will pack a colorful punch that lasts. The power comes in five colors ranging from a dark grey to taupe.

## 3. Anastasia Beverly Hills 'Ultra Long Wearing' Brow Kit

![Anastasia Beverly Hills 'Ultra Long Wearing' Brow Kit](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/beauty/2012/08/3_anastasia-beverly-hills-ultra-long-wearing-brow-kit_600x920.jpg)

**Price:** $30 at [shop.nordstrom.com](http://shop.nordstrom.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
Anastasia is known for creating some of the top beauty products for enhancing brows and their brow kit is no exception. The brow kit comes with a marker-like brown pen, duo brush with a spoolie and angled brush and mini tweezers so you can pluck, brush and paint on color to your naked eyebrows and make them more tidy and groomed.

## 4. Clé De Peau Beauté Eyebrow & Eyeliner Compact

![Clé De Peau Beauté Eyebrow & Eyeliner Compact](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/beauty/2012/08/4_cl-de-peau-beaut-eyebrow-eyeliner-compact_552x424.jpg)

**Price:** $70 at [shop.nordstrom.com](http://shop.nordstrom.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
This brow kit is one of the pricier beauty products for [perfect eyebrows](https://beauty.allwomenstalk.com/beginners-eyebrow-shaping-tips-for-girls-wanting-perfect-brows/) but perennial makeup artist and celebrity favorite brand Clé de Peau fashioned this genius palette of powders to line eyes and fill in brows. Fans of the brand swear that the extra cost is worth every penny for the natural-looking color and luxurious formula.

## 5. Tarte EmphasEYES for Brows High Definition Eyebrow Pencil

![Tarte EmphasEYES for Brows High Definition Eyebrow Pencil](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/beauty/2012/08/5_tarte-emphaseyes-for-brows-high-definition-eyebrow-pencil_450x450.jpg)

**Price:** $19.50 at [sephora.com](http://www.sephora.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
A list of the best products for eyebrows has to include an eyebrow pencil and Tarte makes a lovely double-sided pencil with a super thin pencil to create soft and genuine-looking brow hairs. The brow pencil is water-resistant and made free of parabens, sulphates and their nasty counterparts so you can feel good about using it!

## 6. Origins Just Browsing Brow-shaping Color

![Origins Just Browsing Brow-shaping Color](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/beauty/2012/08/6_origins-just-browsing-brow-shaping-color_327x400.jpg)

**Price:** $16 at [macys.com](http://www1.macys.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
Origins makes one of the top beauty products for enhancing brows with their Just Browsing gel. Ladies adore the brow gel that allows them to add color and volume while taming and grooming their eyebrows all in one fell swoop. Better looking brows in one sweep- sounds like my kinda product!

## 7. Milani Brow Fix Kit

_ **Price:** $5.99-6.49 at target.com_
In order to qualify to be a best product for eyebrows, the product has to be long-lasting, come in a good range of colors and is easy to use. Milani’s brow fix kit fills the bill and then some. This tiny compact houses a brow highlight, two brow colors, an angled brush, sponge and tweezers, what a beauty bargain!

## 8. Too Faced Brow Envy Brow Shaping & Defining Kit

![Too Faced Brow Envy Brow Shaping & Defining Kit](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/beauty/2012/08/8_too-faced-brow-envy-brow-shaping-defining-kit_450x450.jpg)

**Price:** $35 at [sephora.com](http://www.sephora.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
Too Faced made the deluxe version of the Milani Brow Fix Kit which is also a best product for eyebrows. Too Faced is notorious for suckering us in with their super cute packaging but this kit is definitely worth every penny! You get your brow powders, highlight, brow pencil, tweezers, brushes and stencils!

## 9. E.l.f. Studio Eyebrow Kit

![E.l.f. Studio Eyebrow Kit](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/beauty/2012/08/9_e-l-f-studio-eyebrow-kit_410x410.jpg)

**Price:** $3 at [target.com](http://www.target.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
Can you believe this $3 kit is one of the top beauty products for enhancing eyebrows?! It just goes to show that price doesn’t always equal quality. The creamy consistency of the powder and highly pigmented brow wax makes for the perfect combo of cheap and chic.

## 10. Laura Mercier Eyebrow Pencil

![Laura Mercier Eyebrow Pencil](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/beauty/2012/08/10_laura-mercier-eyebrow-pencil_450x450.jpg)

**Price:** $20 at [sephora.com](http://www.sephora.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
This unique pencil made this list of best products for eyebrows for its distinct triangle shape and for delivering long lasting color while being easy to apply. Ladies most often rave about how great a match the pencil is to their hair or brow color so if you’ve had a hard time finding a good color match, try this baby out!

There are so many different options in makeup that it was quite difficult to narrow a list of the 10 best products for eyebrows but all the products on this list are very well-loved and cherished by beauty addicts everywhere so you’re bound to find your perfect brow kit or tool! Have you used any of these products? What is your best product for eyebrows?
