# 7 Hot Orange Lipsticks to Try out ...

Orange Lipstick is a trend that has enjoyed much popularity lately. It’s a great alternative to the ruby red lip, and is an on-trend way to update your beauty routine. From tangy tangerines, to rich corals, there’s an orange lipstick to suit most skin tones. With a little bit of experimentation, you’re bound to find a colour that best suits you. Take a look at some orange lipstick below.

## 1. Yves Saint Laurent Rouge Pure Shine Sheer Lipstick in Blood Orange

![Yves Saint Laurent Rouge Pure Shine Sheer Lipstick in Blood Orange](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/dm/ov/jcwm3y5k54c934a93dd26246010017_163x248.jpg)

**Price:** $30.00 at [shop.nordstrom.com](http://shop.nordstrom.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
This is a shiny orange lipstick that simply glides on. This sheer lipstick has a shimmer finish, and comes in a colourful blood orange colour. It has a hydrating formula and provides a veil of colour. It also comes in a stylish gold-tone case, making it perfect for the fashion-wary beauty buyer.

## 2. Make up Forever Rouge Artist Intense in Satin Bright Orange

![Make up Forever Rouge Artist Intense in Satin Bright Orange](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ro/co/sd0s817l54c934a9e5408672054826_250x248.jpg)

**Price:** $19.00 at [sephora.com](http://www.sephora.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
This lipstick is highly pigmented. It has a long-lasting formula and gives a true-to-colour result. This particular formula is made from a blend of waxes and is formulated without parabens. It has a satin finish, which is perfect for creating a glossy lip.

## 3. Topshop Lips in Ohh La La

![Topshop Lips in Ohh La La](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/pj/jc/gsjpl9jf54c934aa9ebd9303241976_167x249.jpg)

**Price:** $16.00 at [us.topshop.com](https://goo.gl/u3waHF)
This orange lipstick from Topshop has a velvety finish. The formula is designed to nourish and moisturise lips, all while giving them a smooth finish. This warm coral colour would be ideal for wearing during the day.

## 4. Rimmel London Lasting Finish Lipstick in Coral in Gold

![Rimmel London Lasting Finish Lipstick in Coral in Gold](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ml/ln/b541ncat54c934ab5989f187771165_145x249.jpg)

**Price:** $9.00 at [asos.com](https://goo.gl/K5It9f)
If you’re on a budget, this Rimmel lipstick is a must-try. It has a long lasting finish and has a long-lasting formula. This pigment rich lipstick is designed to provide full coverage. ‘Coral in Gold’ is a warmer toned orange lipstick, so would suit warmer skin tones.

## 5. Barry M Lip Paint in Coral

![Barry M Lip Paint in Coral](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/yc/rh/ooz9for354c934ac075e8808288495_127x247.jpg)

**Price:** $8.10 at [asos.com](https://goo.gl/KqSAqd)
This is another affordable orange lipstick. It has a pigmented colour-rich formula and moisturising properties. This lipstick has a smooth texture and provides a long-lasting finish. This vibrant orange hue is perfect for complementing an electric evening look.

## 6. Dior Rouge Dior Lip Colour in Blazing Red

![Dior Rouge Dior Lip Colour in Blazing Red](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/hn/ue/e3wlzmj154c934acb9006263490587_250x248.jpg)

**Price:** $32.00 at [sephora.com](http://www.sephora.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
This Dior lipstick has an ultra-nourishing formula. On the red end of the orange lipstick scale, it’s perfect for those who still prefer the drama of a red lipstick. If you’re willing to splash out, then this lipstick is worth a try.

## 7. Estee Lauder Pure Colour Gloss Stick in Orange Poppy

![Estee Lauder Pure Colour Gloss Stick in Orange Poppy](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/iy/y3/lbmlyomk54c934ad6485f260017535_163x240.jpg)

**Price:** $20.00 at [shop.nordstrom.com](http://shop.nordstrom.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
A glossy lipstick is the best way to work the orange lip. This orange lipstick is designed to provide a high shine finish. It is formulated with conditioning and hydrating properties in mind. The glossy, semi-sheer finish is perfect for wearing on an everyday basis.

Pulling off the orange lipstick look can be tricky, so if you’re starting out stick to sheer and glossy formulas. Also, go for an orange lipstick with a hint of blue to it, as opposed to yellow, as this will complement more skin tones. What are your thoughts on the orange lipstick trend?
