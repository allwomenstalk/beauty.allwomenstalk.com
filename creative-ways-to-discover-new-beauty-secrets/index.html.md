# How to Discover a New Beauty Secret Every Day ...

Are you always on the lookout for new beauty secrets? Most women are. These are some creative ways to get new beauty secrets that I have discovered. Finding out the perfect beauty secret can make a huge difference in your overall hair, makeup and beauty routine.

## 1. Ask for Them

One of the very best ways to discover new beauty secrets is to ask for them. When you see someone and you admire the way they apply their eyeliner or you notice that their skin just has a natural glow, ask them their secret. Most people take this as a compliment and are happy to share with you. If you want to know where they got that fabulous pink lip gloss, ask them.

## 2. Follow Some Beauty Blogs

Beauty blogs can be so helpful in teaching you new techniques and keeping you in the know about the latest, greatest products. You can follow too many and end up feeling overwhelmed, though. I follow a couple that really work for me. The trick is to find the some that suit your beauty style. You can do a quick search on Google for best [beauty blogs](https://beauty.allwomenstalk.com/fantastic-budget-beauty-blogs-to-follow/) and will be pointed in the right direction.

## 3. Search on Pinterest

Pinterest has a wealth of information on beauty secrets. I could seriously spend all day just surfing them. Not all of them will be hits, of course, but there are certainly many good ones there to find. You just have to sift through them and find the ones that work for you.

## 4. Ask Your Hairdresser for Extra Time

Do you love the style that your hairdresser gives you when she does your hair, yet you can’t ever duplicate it when you get home? Many women feel your pain! If this is the case with you, ask her if you can schedule some extra time with her to learn how she creates that style. More than likely you will catch on quick once she begins showing you. It is worth a little extra cash to get that knowledge.

## 5. Take Advantage of Free Makeovers

There are many department stores where you can get free makeovers. Getting a makeover isn’t a permanent change; you can wash it off if you don’t like it. But you might love it and you might love the products that they use on you. If you don’t want to commit to that, you can always ask the associate for their advice on choosing makeup colors. If you would prefer a private makeover, Mary Kay is a company that can provide that.

## 6. Be Observant

Another tip you can follow is to just be observant. You can actually learn a lot just from observing how others do their hair and makeup. You may be able to figure out that extra teasing gives volume at the crown or that a certain color of eyeliner is great for green eyes. For example, I am always on the lookout for what shades of lipstick work for blondes since I am one. Even if I don’t get the name of the lipstick, I can match the shade.

## 7. Be Fearless

Lastly, you can discover new beauty secrets by choosing to be fearless. Don’t be afraid to experiment with beauty. You may find a new way of styling your hair or applying your makeup that changes your routine forever. You might discover the perfect way to make homemade body scrub. Your friends will all admire you for being such a trendsetter.

There are many great ways to discover new beauty secrets. What are some ways you do this? I can’t wait to hear your ideas!
