# 7 Teeth Whitening Products That Really Work ...

There’s a plethora of **teeth whitening products** on the market, but which ones truly work to give you that **pearly white smile**? It’s important to remember that not all teeth are suited for **teeth whitening** and that it may be wise to **consult your dentist first before beginning a whitening regimen**.

## 1. What to Know Before You Whiten

Now, before you hop in your car and rush to the store **to buy the newer trending whitening kit or toothpaste**, it is important to know what’s actually going on behind many of those “brighter smile” promises.

When it comes down to it, a large majority of whitening toothpastes use a different combo of the same ingredients, such as **bleaching agents and abrasives**, to scrub away surface stains.

**According to Dr. Thomas Dobie**, an [orthodontist in Branford, Connecticut](https://dobierevolution.com/branford-orthodontics/), and founder of **Dobie Revolution Orthodontics**, these products usually include ingredients like **hydrogen peroxide, carbamide peroxide, silica, or pyrophosphates**.

_“The aim of these agents is to remove extrinsic stains from the enamel,”_ he explains, _“thus lightening the tooth color.”_

Sounds simple, right? While that would be nice, **every trend doesn’t always deliver exactly as promised.**

Take charcoal toothpaste, for example. It has become quite popular on social media platforms, but **Dr. Dobie** points to a [2021 study](https://link.springer.com/article/10.1007/s00784-021-03903-x) in the Journal of Clinical Oral Investigations that compared it to regular fluoride toothpaste over 12 weeks, eventually finding no real difference in tooth shade between the two.

_“Consumers should be cautious before jumping on the latest trend in hopes of instant gratification,”_ he says.

In other words, not all whitening products are created equal, so it's always best to stick with options that have real science behind them and to **follow the recommendation of your dentist or orthodontist**.

Now, let's get into some of the products you might be looking at when starting your **tooth whitening journey.**
