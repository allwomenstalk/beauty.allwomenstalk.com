# 7 Ways to Get Rid of Freckles ...

I know so many girls who want to know **How To Get Rid Of Freckles**. Now, I'm all for freckles! I think they make you unique and give a very natural element of beauty to the girl who possesses them. However, not every girl who has freckles believes this way, and is often looking for ways to get rid of freckles. I'd rather that girls be informed of natural ways to fade their freckles then spending a ton of money and time on bleaching creams that are bad for your skin. So with that in mind I've written this article on natural ways to get rid of freckles! Please continue reading for some great info!

## 1. Strawberry Paste

Strawberries are one of the best **ways to get rid of freckles**! Strawberries have a lot of citric acid in them, which is proven to help fade freckles. Here's how: Mash up a few fresh strawberries and apply them to face. Leave on for about 5 minutes, then rinse off. Cranberries will work if you don't have strawberries on hand!

## 2. Parsley

Parsley is one very natural and effective **way to get rid of freckles**! This is what you need to do. Mix natural parsley juice with equal parts lemon juice, orange juice and red current juice. Then, cover your skin with the liquid just as if you were applying moisturizer and allow it to dry. Finally, apply your daily moisturizer over top. The citric acid in the juices will help to naturally get rid of freckles and make your skin glow. Do this every other day for results.

## 3. Soured Milk

This may sound a little gross, but it's a proven **way to get rid of freckles**! The natural lactic acid in soured milk provides gentle peeling of the skin without harshly over-drying it. Rub soured milk onto your face for about 60 seconds and then rinse well to help get rid of freckles.

## 4. Radish

One of the most unique **ways to get rid of freckles** that I've ever discovered involves the use of radishes. Yes, that's right. Radishes! They contain a natural bleaching agent that helps to lighten freckles. Here's how: Grate a radish and mix it with the juice of one lime. Blend it with the juice of one lime in a food processor, then apply to your face. Allow to dry and then rinse it off. It's that simple!

## 5. Citric Juice

If you want to know **how to get rid of freckles** the most natural way possible, then you need to visit the produce section of your local grocery! Pick up a lemon, orange, and lime. Squeeze the juices all together into a bowl, then add a teaspoon of plain yogurt so that you have a paste. You will want to let this paste dry thoroughly before rinsing it off, and follow up with moisturizer so your skin won't over-dry itself.

## 6. Castor Oil

We've all been threatened at one point or another with castor oil, mostly by well meaning mothers or grandmothers. Some of you may have even experienced the horrors of castor oil! But the truth is, castor oil is one of the best **ways to get rid of freckles**! Castor oil equalizes skin pigmentation and can be applied overnight, so no need to wash and rinse or mix and mash. Just slather it on, go to sleep, and watch your freckles fade!

## 7. Vitamin E Oil

I love vitamin e oil, and not just because it's a great **way to get rid of freckles**. Vitamin E oil is loaded with nutrients your skin needs to be healthy, and over time it fades freckles. Pop a vitamin E oil capsule with a pin and apply the contents to the area desired. It definitely smells better than castor oil! But you will love the results.

Now that you know **How To Get Rid Of Freckles** the natural and effective way, you will be well on your way to smooth, and flawless skin! However, if you have freckles I would encourage you to embrace them and stop looking for ways to get rid of freckles! Your freckles make you unique and lovely! Ladies, do you think that looking for ways to get rid of freckles should be embraced or discouraged?
