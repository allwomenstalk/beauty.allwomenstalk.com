# 5 Amazing Homemade Spa Treatments for Women on a Budget ...

If you want to look good or enhance your beauty without much cost, take a moment to read these helpful tips. Here are the five spa treatments that can easily be done in the comfort of your own home with just a few natural ingredients you already have in the kitchen. So chill out and relax after a long day of stressful errands.

## 1. Deep Hair Mask Conditioning

Our hair needs extra attention especially when it goes through a long day or days of various outdoor activities. Consider your evening spa to be an excellent time to deeply condition your hair. Just use the coconut oil. First, wash the hair and then, apply a bit of coconut oil - especially to the ends and wrap the hair tight with a soft towel.

## 2. Face Mask

Face masks are considered an excellent option to both deeply clean and moisturize the skin. You may apply the natural type of face mask - like honey for oily skin and sour cream for dry skin or alternatively buy an inexpensive clay face mask. Just make sure to place 2 rounds of cucumber or any eye mask on your closed eyes for maximum benefits of this spa treatment.

## 3. Aromatherapy

Atmosphere is really important in making a relaxing spa treatment. Make sure that your aromatherapy oil keeps on burning in your bathroom or you may want to add a few drops of lavender oil. This will help you sleep well afterwards, too.

## 4. Steam Facial

Doing a steam facial is best before applying your face mask. After cleansing your face from makeup, allow the pores to open through filling up a huge bowl with some essential oils, boiling water and staying over this bowl for good 5 minutes. For better results - cover your head with a towel, but be careful with the hot water, sit comfortably, so that you can stay in this position and not spill the hot contents on your precious legs.

## 5. Exfoliating Body Scrub

Body scrubs are an excellent way of exfoliating the skin especially when done on a daily basis. Coffee and coconut oil are the best ingredients to use for a home made body scrub. Coffee is also known to lessen the tiredness of the eyes. This type of scrub can be used on your entire body, including your face.

Just watch yourself destress and get even more beautiful with these simple home made spa treatments. Tell us in the comments which one is your favorite!
