# 7 Annoying Common Beauty Problems Solved ...

We all have beauty problems, but what if our **beauty problems were solved easily**? Well ladies, I've got the top 7 common problems in beauty that we all face and I've got ways to go about beauty problem solving! Not only do we cover everything from blemishes to blotched bangs, but we've got streaky skin and hair dye problems too! So take a look at our top 7 beauty problems solved list!

## 1. Botched Bangs

So you've decided to cut your own bangs and now you've blotched them a bit, is that right? Well ladies, this is acutally one of the beauty problems solved that isn't a big deal at all! You can actually use some styling cream to weigh your hair down a bit to make your bangs seem longer. For a really, really unfortunate bang job, you can also pull your bangs to the side and that way you'll be able to secure them with a bobby pin.

## 2. Blemishes

Blemishes are a part of life, but this is one of the beauty problems that you can get rid of too. The first thing that you want to do is start cleansing the area with a cotton pad soaked in alcohol which has antiseptic properties. The will hurt, but it'll actually dry up all of the dirt and oil in your blemishes! After that, add in some oil free moisturizer and you should see your blemishes go down immensely! See ladies, beauty problem solved!

## 3. Brows Too Thin

I've actually done this so many times. I'll pluck and pluck and pluck and all of a sudden, I look surprised all of the time. The first thing that you need to do to get this beauty problem solved is to pickup a soft textured pencil that is the exact same color as your eyebrows. Basically from there, just fill in all of the gaps that you are missing your hair and you'll be just fine!

## 4. Splotchy Teeth

With all of the different whitening strips and just how busy we are, it's super easy to actually forget you're wearing your whitening strip right? If you happen to do that, don't worry! You can actually just take a break from the strips and even start to use just whitening toothpaste. Instantly, you start to see a huge difference!

## 5. Streaky Skin

So you were in a rush and you applied way too much tanner and now your skin is super streaky. Does that happen a lot? Well, you can actually put some baby oil on a cotton pad and whip that over any of the streaked parts. This will actually allow you to get rid of the streaks easily!

## 6. Too Dark Hair Color

So I am the person that dyes my own hair and I've totally made the mistake of picking a color that was too dark. So, how do you go about getting this beauty problem solved? Well, you actually will have to head up to the salon and get some highlights. These will actually lighten up your hair without damaging as much as bleaching!

## 7. Hair Dye on Skin

Because I dye my own hair, this is honestly one of the biggest **beauty problems solved for me**! There are tons of different ways that you can get any and all of the dye off of your skin. A few include using toothpaste, nail polish remover and even petroleum jelly! I've tried them all and any solution works great!

As you can see, there are tons of different comment beauty problems and all of those **beauty problems can be solved right here**! So ladies, what common beauty problems solved have you come in contact with? Did I miss any? List 'em below!
