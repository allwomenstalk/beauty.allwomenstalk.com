# 8 Best Drugstore Beauty Products under $15 ...

Beauty at budget prices? What more could we ask for! Thanks Veronica for your wonderfully cost-effective list of drugstore beauty products. We owe you one!

If you're anything like me, you like looking good without over paying to do so. I have done some research and I have came up with, in my opinion, is the best 10 beauty products that you can get at drugstores. Being on a budget is hard, but staying looking good will come easy with these products.

## 1. Fruit of the Earth Vitamin E Lotion

This Fruit of the Earth Vitamin E Skin Care Cream at $8 is awesome! Not only does it moisturize your skin wonderfully, it also has Vitamin E in it to help with scars and minimize signs of aging. It has aloe vera, wheat germ, sunflower seed oil, collagen and elastin in it to give your skin the ultimate moisture bath.

## 2. Garnier BB Cream

This $12 BB cream is lightweight and provides sheer coverage. Instantly feel the results from its vitamin C and mineral elements all packed into this bottle! With each and every time you put it on you will see five results: renewed radiance, brightened complexion, evened skin and blurred imperfections, 24 hour hydration and SPF 15 protection from UV rays.

## 3. Revlon New Complexion One-Step Compact Makeup

This foundation at $11 is a revolution! It literally is an all in one make-up, it covers everything without feeling heavy or looking cakey because it features DermaBreathe science, which gives you smooth, even-toned skin that is able to breathe. The cream-to-powder glides on even, is oil free, irritant free and has SPF 15 in it!

## 4. The Falsies Volum' Express Mascara

This mascara at $5 does just as it claims, makes your lashes look big and full like fake eyelashes would! It has a pro-keratin fiber formula and a slightly curved brush to give your lashes that long, full look you've been craving. Waterproof is also an option with this mascara which makes it perfect for summer pool parties!

## 5. Crest 3D White Glamorous White Toothpaste

This toothpaste at $5 helps remove 90% of surface stains on your teeth, while protecting teeth and tooth enamel with a safe anti-cavity formula. Give your mouth that minty fresh, sparkling clean and pearly whites they deserve!

## 6. St. Ives Swiss Formula Blemish & Blackhead Control Apricot Scrub

This apricot scrub at $7 does wonders for oily/acne prone skin types. It is oil-free and has a refreshing apricot skin, while it gently removes dead skin and blemishes. With its 2% salicylic acid content, you can be sure it is helping your face clear up one scrub at a time!

## 7. Burts Bees Replenishing Lip Balm

This lip balm is infused with the antioxidant pomegranate oil so it replenishes natural moisture and helps restore texture to give you smooth, healthy lips!

## 8. Spring Valley Pharmaceutical Grade Tea Tree Oil

While there are many uses for tea tree oil, I love to use it for acne! It is 100% pure Australian oil that is used as a natural antiseptic and a topical skincare agent. Tea tree oil also helps soothe head colds and infections of the throat and chest when applied like a balm. Another helpful use for tea tree oil is its a natural antiseptic and it helps with skin ailments, burns, cuts, insect bites and spots!

So there you have it ladies, the best eight everyday beauty products you can get from almost any drugstore near you! I have personally used all of these products and do not like to go without them now. Even better that it fits within my monthly budget, as I am sure it will with yours as well.
