# 7 Things to Know before Ordering Beauty Products Online ...

There are lots of things to know before ordering beauty products online. Knowing them can help you make the best choices and get the items you want at the prices you want. Shopping online always carries a risk because you don’t get to touch and inspect an item before you put it in your cart and pay for it. That doesn’t mean you can’t find great stuff, but these things to know before ordering beauty products online are important to helping you choose the right merchandise.

## 1. Watch out for Fakes

One of the most important things to know before ordering beauty products online is that you need to be aware of sites that are going to trick you. They can tell you’ll be getting a product with certain ingredients, scents or colors, but you might wind up with something entirely different. That’s why it is important to buy only from reputable sources and to remember that if it seems too good to be true, it probably is.

## 2. Lack of Support

If you buy from the big names, the site likely lists the information you need if you want to talk to a representative. If not, you may be out of luck if you have questions or concerns about an item you’ve purchased. Scour the site for contact info and if you can’t find it, consider buying elsewhere. Even with contact info, there is no guarantee that you’ll get the same level of customer service you would in a store so be prepared for that.

## 3. Reactions

Since you can’t try an online product without buying it, you may end up with something that causes a reaction and no chance of getting your money back. While some manufacturers do offer this type of guarantee, others don’t. If you want to try a new ingredient, it’s best to purchase it from somewhere that will let you sample it or will guarantee it if you aren’t satisfied.

## 4. Contamination

There is no guarantee that you’re getting an unopened product, even if the photo online shows it as such. Again, if you buy from the big names, this is highly unlikely, but if you opt to purchase from other suppliers or outlet markets, you can’t be entirely sure. Make sure there is a return policy and if you do receive a questionable item, don’t use it. Send it back right away.

## 5. Rock Bottom Prices

Who wouldn’t love finding their favorite high end beauty product for mere pennies? While it can be enticing to grab the old credit card and stock up, slow down and use caution. Sometimes, these items are fakes or are not what you think you’re getting. Keep in mind that if a company is offering a sale or has reduced a price, you’ll be able to find it on their website rather than getting it elsewhere. Like I said before – if it seems too good to be true, it probably is.

## 6. Read the Reviews

Most websites publish the reviews of other customers. Listening to your peers is a great way to find a product that is perfect for you. It’s also invaluable for finding out which companies and products to stay away from. If you can’t find reviews on the site, Google the company’s name with the word “reviews” and you’ll be good to go.

## 7. Range of Prices

Often, buying online can save you mega dollars, but that isn’t always the case. True, you can save money this way, but sometimes it’s cheaper to go to the store and save yourself the shipping cost of having your items sent to your house. You can easily find beauty products for any budget online though, but you might have to do some searching to find them.
