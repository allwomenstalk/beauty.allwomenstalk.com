# 7 Perfect Eyeglasses for Every Face Shape: A Modern Style Guide

While women who once feared the "four-eyed" look in their youth may now be embracing 24/7 contact lens wear, finding unique eyeglasses for your face shape can enhance your entire look. Glasses can be more economical than disposable contacts and significantly reduce the risk of eye infections. Plus, an adorable optical accessory can really make you stand out. You won’t bat an eye at these 7 picks for perfectly designed eyeglasses tailored to your face shape.

## 1. Cat Eye Coquine in Purple by Bon Look

![Cat Eye Coquine in Purple by Bon Look](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/10/23035323_7736_479x133.jpg)

[BonLook.com](https://www.bonlook.com/) provides a selection of some of the cutest designed eyeglasses at competitive prices. With a virtual try-on option and recommendations based on your specific shape, these vintage-inspired designs will have you swooning. This lovely purple cat eye frame is vibrant without being too overwhelming. At $99 including prescription lenses, these frames are fantastic.

## 2. Vogue Cat Eye in Tan

![Vogue Cat Eye in Tan](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/10/23035323_3342_599x150.jpg)

Leave it to [Vogue Eyewear](https://www.vogue-eyewear.com/) to design a cat eye frame in such a wonderful camel shade. The light plastic design is as easy on the eyes as it is resting on the bridge of your nose. If you have an oval, square, or heart-shaped face, you’re in luck—the Vogue model VO2763 from [LensCrafters.com](https://www.lenscrafters.com/) is just for you!

## 3. DKNY Two-tone Translucent Oval

![DKNY Two-tone Translucent Oval](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/10/23035324_3021_599x134.jpg)

Forget about those old school labels when you put on these two-toned tortoise pattern and translucent pink glasses from [DKNY](https://www.dkny.com/). Their light oval shape is a perfect fit for an oval or square-shaped face. Even if you have a rounder jawline, these are worth a try. The split pattern and color make this a hot eyewear trend for every face!

| Face Shape | Recommended Style | Key Feature |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| Oval | Cat Eye, Oval | Balanced proportions |
| Square | Round, Oval | Softens angles |
| Heart | Cat Eye, Bottom-heavy | Balances forehead |

## 4. Warby Parker Design Nedwin Summer Green

![Warby Parker Design Nedwin Summer Green](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/10/23035323_0940_543x134.jpg)

Who wouldn’t love to be a woman in green glasses? They’re earthy, unique, and incredibly sweet. At $95 including anti-reflective prescription lenses and shipping, this Summer Green Nedwin design from [WarbyParker.com](https://www.warbyparker.com/) is simply adorable. If you have dark hair, these will especially pop.

## 5. Prada Burgundy Eyeglasses

![Prada Burgundy Eyeglasses](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/10/22233528_2296_599x155.jpg)

These color-dipped [Prada](https://www.prada.com/) glasses are optical haute couture. The ultra-thick frames and the fade from deep red to translucent are as sweet as a candy apple. You can find this luxe look for your oval, square, or heart-shaped face at major retailers.

## 6. Ray-Ban 5256

![Ray Ban 5256](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/10/22233529_0450_599x171.jpg)

These sleek square frames from [Ray-Ban](https://www.ray-ban.com/) are nerdy-chic and a must-have accessory. Bold black frames with silver-lined legs and small embellishments at the temples make this the perfect look for anyone who proudly embraces their vision with a pair of classic frames. This pair works for heart-shaped, oval, round, or square faces.

- Choose frames that contrast with your face shape.
- Consider your skin tone when picking colors.
- Check the bridge fit for maximum comfort.

## 7. Bisou Bisou BT 357

![Bisou Bisou BT 357](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/10/22233529_7469_599x189.jpg)

This pick is partially rimmed, creamy brown, and perfectly proportioned for those with a round or oval-shaped face. This metal frame accentuates the eyes and is offset with a thick embellished leg. This modern take on eyeglasses will leave you fresh-faced and ready to see the world. If you love this style, you might also enjoy our guide on [Cat Eye frames](#1) or [luxe designer looks](#5).

Women with glasses have never looked better with all the funky, modern, and vintage-inspired options available. Give your eyes a break from contact lenses and pick out a pair of frames that fits your face and your personal style. What eyeglasses have been leaving you wide-eyed lately?
