# Ways to Get Rid of Frown Lines ...

Nobody wants to get older and especially not look it. People go great lengths and are willing to spend large amounts of cash to keep themselves looking youthful. If you have had a hard life or smoked for a large portion of your adult hood the cracks and lines can begin to show on your face. There are many ways you can get rid of frown lines from natural home remedies to surgery. Here is a list of **ways to get rid of frown lines**.

## 1. Forehead Lift

A forehead lift is treatment which will last years rather than a short term Botox fix. The surgery will remove excess skin and fat, it will also tighten the muscles leaving your forehead wrinkle free. The surgery only takes one to two hours you will be under anaesthetic and won’t feel a thing during the procedure. You may experience pain in the recovery process, how much pain varies from person to person. When you are recovering you should aim to rest up and relax for at least a week. This does mean you may need to use up some of your sick leave from work. However when you are back in the office you will be looking absolutely fantastic everyone will be asking how did you get rid of frown lines?

## 2. Absorbable Injectable Fillers

If you are not crazy about the idea of going under the knife but want a result that is almost just as good look into having “ absorbable inject able fillers”. These fillers contain collagen and will get rid of frown lines. The side effects of this treatment are minimal and you will be left feeling good and looking even better. The downside of this treatment is that it will only last around six months so you will have to get it done over and over.

If you don’t fancy going under the knife or being injected with needles why not try something natural. Surgeries can be expensive and leave you feeling bruised.

## 3. Facial Exercises

I would advise you not to try these on the bus or in the pub as you may be dismissed by others as a “weirdo”. These facial exercises will make you look younger in a matter of moments, it takes time. The idea is to target the small muscles which you don’t use every day. Look online for the best facial exercises

## 4. Give up Smoking!

Smoking makes you age, fact. Smoking affects almost every aspect of your health as well being detrimental to your looks and purse. The more money you save on cigarettes the more money you will have to save up for surgery or some fabulous creams.

## 5. Wrinkle Cream

There are many creams on the market which boast to being the best of the bunch. Don’t be lead into thinking they are the ones for you without reading the labels. Look for creams with caffeine in as it has been proven to tighten the skin. You also need to look for a cream which is suitable for your skin type. Don’t be afraid to ask the shop assistant what cream they recommend for your skin type. If you find the right one creams are without a doubt the best way to reduce wrinkles without going under the knife.

## 6. Face Masks

You don’t even need to fork out on these, you will possibly find all the ingredients you need in your fridge! An egg and cream face mask can work wonders, just mix an egg white and a tablespoon of milk to make a paste. Then apply to the face with cotton wool or your fingers. This isn’t a mask which will give long term effects however it will get rid of frown lines for the evening.

You can’t stop the ageing process but you can help your skin in some way to avoid the outward ravages of time.
