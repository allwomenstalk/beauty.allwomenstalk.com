# 10 Things That Can Trick You  into Feeling Ugly ...

Unfortunately, way too many women hate what they see when they look in the mirror, and that shouldn't be the case. You're not ugly at all. However, it's normal to feel like you are. Here are a few things that can fool you into feeling ugly, even though you're absolutely beautiful:

## 1. Not Getting Any Likes on Your Selfies

![Not Getting Any Likes on Your Selfies](https://img.allw.mn/content/rw/l3/oyhyz7zy56c7213e38b98601061717_500x264.gif)

Since it can take you a few minutes to strike the perfect pose and tilt your phone at the perfect angle, you'll expect to get dozens of likes after you post a picture on Instagram. When that doesn't happen, you can feel crushed. Of course, your friends might not have even seen the picture. During certain times of the day, you aren't going to get many likes, even if you look flawless.

## 2. Staring in the Mirror for Too Long

![Staring in the Mirror for Too Long](https://img.allw.mn/content/oo/ln/mdifypoc56c7213e43269230213432_480x270.gif)

It's unhealthy to look into the mirror for long periods of time. Even if you look amazing, you're going to find flaws on your face and body if you stare for long enough. That's why you should only give yourself a few minutes to get ready in front of the mirror in the morning. Then, when you check your reflection throughout the day, make it quick.

## 3. Getting Turned down by Your Crush

![Getting Turned down by Your Crush](https://img.allw.mn/content/pd/xv/c7i3q34c56c7214034f01507554870_500x250.gif)

When your crush rejects you, or when he flirts with another girl, you might feel uglier than ever. However, your looks have nothing to do with him not liking you. If you're not together, it's because it's not meant to be. There are plenty of men out there that would consider you gorgeous. You just have to wait until you find them.

## 4. Having a Bad Hair Day

![person, woman, girl, beauty, lady,](https://img.allw.mn/content/t2/hp/n0yrm0od58b4daa7e75ee256606481_500x259.gif)

Having a bad hair day can feel like the end of the world. How can you feel attractive when you know your hair isn't cooperating with you? Well, there are a few things you can do to fix that dip in your confidence. You can either put on a hat, braid your hair, or cover it up with a bandana. That way, you won't have to worry about it anymore.

## 5. Being in a Bad Mood

![Being in a Bad Mood](https://img.allw.mn/content/fa/o3/acalkcfu56c7213eb0ec6127472502_400x200.gif)

When you're in a bad mood, you'll put a negative spin on everything. That means that you'll assume that you look horrible, even though you actually look fabulous. In order to feel beautiful again, you should do something to lift your mood. When you feel good, your positivity will return.

## 6. Having Trouble with Your Makeup

![Having Trouble with Your Makeup](https://img.allw.mn/content/ne/am/hbmxaw9f56c7213eac93b008075531_500x281.gif)

We all love to look at YouTube tutorials and fashion gurus on Instagram. We wish we could do our makeup like them, which is why we can get so frustrated when the wings of our eyeliner don't match. Of course, you don't need to wear makeup in order to feel beautiful. You're gorgeous without it, and you're gorgeous when it's a little uneven.

## 7. Having Your Plans Canceled

![Having Your Plans Canceled](https://img.allw.mn/content/df/yi/i39kvwxf56c721406e342106364166_500x223.gif)

It doesn't matter if your friends or a boy cancel plans. Either way, it can make you feel undesirable. To make yourself feel better, you should get dressed up as if you were leaving the house. In fact, get out! You don't need other people with you in order to have fun at dinner or a movie.

## 8. Feeling like You're Being Ignored

![hair, color, human hair color, red, face,](https://img.allw.mn/content/xb/x9/ax7kdly558b581e2291b2240495402_500x209.gif)

Sometimes there are those people who are rude enough to ignore us, and this can make us feel ugly and/or undesirable, but if you don't hear back from someone right away it's important not to assume they are ignoring you. Give them a couple of days to get back to you, instead of thinking the worse and using it as a way to view yourself negatively.

## 9. Getting Break-outs and Blemishes

![face, person, nose, black hair, beauty,](https://img.allw.mn/content/n2/nt/b85lu50l58b582325d14e030247144_500x250.gif)

Getting blemishes is nothing to feel embarrassed about, honestly! Everybody goes through periods when their skin seemingly has a mind of its own, resulting in things like break-outs and blemishes. When this happens, it's all too easy to look in the mirror and deem yourself ugly, when in reality all you need to do is pop a bit of concealer on the blemishes, remember that they will disappear usually within a couple of days to a week, and you're still as beautiful as you always have been!

## 10. Any Insecurities You Have

![hair, face, black, black and white, person,](https://img.allw.mn/content/bl/ur/xf2wa96858b5825976c05758040311_499x327.gif)

No one likes to feel insecure, that's for sure. But insecurities are a part of life and it's completely normal to have them. Of course the exact nature of insecurities will vary from one person to the next, but if they have one thing in common it's that they can often leave us feeling ugly as well as just plain insecure. If you can, consider what the reasons are as to why you feel insecure about it. Understanding your insecurities is the first step to overcoming them, and then the next step is to come up with a few ways you can move past it and gradually become less insecure!

You're beautiful, even if you don't believe it. Do any of these things ever fool you into feeling ugly?
