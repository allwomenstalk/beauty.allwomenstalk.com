# 16 Essential Beauty Items to Make You Look Beautiful in the Summer Heat ...

Once the temperature rises, keeping up with our beauty routines remains a daily challenge. Here are the 5 best beauty items that will help keep you looking your summer best even in the sweltering heat and humidity.

## 1. Mineral Foundation

![face powder, powder, product, product, powder,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/sj/ap/bb2q9xpp595d82f931c1d002784915_600x600.jpg)

Switching from liquid foundation to a dry mineral-based foundation will assist your makeup to stay intact with less worrying about your coverage melting off during the day. Used with a nice moisturizer, light concealer, and a good dusting of mineral foundation, you should have full coverage that lasts all day. The 3 excellent recommendations for mineral foundations include:
- [bareMinerals Original Loose Powder Foundation](http://www.sephora.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
- [L’Oreal Paris True Match Naturale Mineral Foundation](https://www.lorealparisusa.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
- ﻿‌[Ulta Mineral Powder Foundation](http://www.ulta.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)

## 2. Tinted Moisturizer

![product, product, skin care, cosmetics, cream,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ti/uq/jk8hbdyp595d83246397f353289479_600x600.jpg)

However, if you wanted to forgo foundation completely and head out the door wearing as little makeup as possible, some tinted moisturizers can act as an SPF protectant, moisturizer, and provide great coverage, as well. A list of popular tinted moisturizers that do the job perfectly with the lightest made-up feel include:
- [Laura Mercier Tinted Moisturizer](https://faveable.com/best-moisturizer)
- [Tarte Amazonian Clay BB Tinted Treatment](http://www.ulta.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
- [Bobbi Brown Tinted Moisturizing Balm](http://shop.nordstrom.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)

## 3. Blotting Papers

![product, product, brand,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/v9/mv/e28a4i59595d8330c6e9c862441179_500x355.jpg)

If having excess oil, shine,and sweat makes you concerned, one simple solution would be to keep some blotting papers available in your purse or bag. An all time favorite on the list would be:
- [Clean & Clear Oil Absorbing Sheets](https://www.amazon.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
Other fine blotting sheets that work well are:
- [Palladio Rice Paper](https://www.walmart.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
- [Neutrogena Shine Control Blotting Sheets](https://www.amazon.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com).

## 4. Frizz Control

![product, product, lotion, liquid, health & beauty,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/jz/jz/jcnqyyl4595d833c9eb19462979150_400x400.jpg)

Whether you have curly or straight hair, no one is immune to the havoc that humidity plays on our hair. While we can’t control the heat or humidity, we can do our best to minimize its effects on our hair. Some of the best frizz-control hair products include the classic go-to:

- [John Frieda Frizz Ease](http://www.ulta.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
A constant favorite:
- [Living Proof Weightless Styling Spray](http://www.sephora.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
And dependable:
- [Moroccanoil Treatment](http://www.sephora.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com).

Your hair will thank you for it!

## 5. Glowing Skin

![product, skin care, product, lotion, cream,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/gr/eb/qdumjcs8595d834b5e231775707358_400x400.jpg)

Finally, whether you are spending your days by the pool or safely air-conditioning indoors, the natural sun-kissed glow can be enjoyed by all with the right body lotions and bronzers. [Jergens Natural Glow](http://www.ulta.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com) works well acting like a self-tanner and a moisturizing body lotion to give a more natural looking summery glow. You can also wear bronzer as well and add some color and depth to your makeup routine to accentuate your features and get a natural sun-kissed glow on your cheeks. Some popular bronzers include:
- [NYX Cosmetic Matte Bronzer](http://www.ulta.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
- [NARS Bronzing Powder](http://www.sephora.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
- [Benefit Cosmetics Hoola Matte Bronzer](http://www.sephora.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)

Product Source: [faveable.com](https://faveable.com/best-bronzer)

As the days grow longer and the weather heats up, we can take advantage of the season’s natural beauty by working with the weather to highlight our best features and minimize the more challenging elements with the right beauty products!

Once mastered, your summer beauty routine will become a yearly tradition!
