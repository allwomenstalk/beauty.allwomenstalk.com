The AWS Teams Favorite Beauty Products of 2015 ...

Becca X Jaclyn Hill Shimmering Skin Perfector® in Champagne Pop • Anastasia Brow Wiz • Urban Decay Naked Basics Palette • Philosophy Iced Mint Lemonade Lip Polishing Sugar Scrub • Benefit Cosmetics They're Real! Mascara • More ...

By Jennifer

The editors here at AWS will all remember 2015 as the year of perfect brows, luminous skin, and graphic liner, attainable with our best beauty buys and favorite products of the year. Which of your faves made the list? Let's find out!

1. Becca X Jaclyn Hill Shimmering Skin Perfector® in Champagne Pop

Price: $38.00 at sephora.com
Pinky swears by this light-weight powder for the perfect amount of shimmer and shine.

2. Anastasia Brow Wiz

Price: $21.00 at sephora.com
Available in 9 natural-looking colors, Pinky says, "Your eyebrows will ALWAYS be on-fleek with this product!"

3. Urban Decay Naked Basics Palette

Price: $ at sephora.com
This set is a classic, really, and a must-have. Pinky loves it because of its "perfect warm tones."

4. Philosophy Iced Mint Lemonade Lip Polishing Sugar Scrub

Price: $15.00 at sephora.com
If you're worried about dry, chapped lips, protect your pout with this gentle scrub; I use it daily.

5. Benefit Cosmetics They're Real! Mascara

Price: $24.00 at sephora.com
I'm super-picky about mascara: it has to darken and lengthen my lashes, but it absolutely cannot clump. That's why I adore this product!

6. Benefit Cosmetics High Beam Liquid Face Highlighter

Price: $26.00 at sephora.com
I love, love, love this luminizer! A few drops on my cheekbones and browbones and I'm all fresh-faced.

7. Bumble and Bumble Surf Infusion

Price: $29.00 at sephora.com
Who doesn't love the loose waves and ultra-shine of beach hair? I know I do!

8. Josie Maran 100% Pure Argan Oil

Price: $48.00 at sephora.com
Ideal all-natural, year-round hydration for your skin, hair, and nails. I use it daily.

9. Benefit Cosmetics Roller Lash Curling & Lifting Mascara

Price: $24.00 at sephora.com
Lisa says, "Hands down, the best mascara I've ever used. It really curls the lashes and makes them look great!" She does have stellar lashes, too - you can feel the breeze when she blinks.

10. Boscia Purifying Cleansing Gel

Price: $28.00 at sephora.com
It's pricey, Lisa admits, but it thoroughly cleans without drying out your skin.

11. Smashbox Iconic Photo Finish Foundation Primer

Price: $36.00 at sephora.com
Lisa says this is the only primer that gets rid of ALL the shine.

12. Egg Mousse Soap Cleanser

Price: $20.00 at sephora.com
When I showed my grandmother the bottle, she said, "Well, of course! I've been using egg on my face for years!" Maybe that's why she's 91 and doesn't look it.

13. Marc Jacobs Daisy

Price: $78.00 at sephora.com
This summer-light fragrance is Fawn's favorite. Want to change it up for fall? Try Dot.

14. Josie Maran Coconut Watercolor Eyeshadow

Price: $18.00 at sephora.com
Fawn's favorite shade is Playa del Pink, but the other five colors are very nice, too.

15. Olivia Palermo X Ciaté London Nail Collection

Price: $24.00 at sephora.com
With three reds, one navy, and one neutral to choose from, Fawn alternates and wears a new one each week.

16. Fresh Sugar Nourishing Lip Balm Advanced Therapy

Price: $30.00 at sephora.com
Fawn says, "I use this year-round to keep my lips soft and kissable... it smells and tastes pretty good, too!"

17. Urban Decay Heavy Metal Glitter Eyeliner

Price: $20.00 at sephora.com
"This liner is amazing," Fawn says, "especially for the graphic liner trend." It's available in 7 metallic shades.
