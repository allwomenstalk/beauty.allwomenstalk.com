## 1. What is Clean Beauty?

Diving straight into the heart of the matter, **clean beauty** is more than just a buzzword; it's a commitment to healthier and more ethical personal care. So, what's the real scoop behind this movement? Imagine slathering your skin with only the purest ingredients-mixtures that shun the questionable chemicals which could do more harm than good. That's clean beauty, in essence.

But it's not just about being safe; it's about being responsible. Clean beauty brands go the extra mile to ensure sustainability in their processes. A 'clean' label doesn't come easy. It's a dedication to transparency, sourcing ingredients that are both kind to your skin and the environment. When you're scrolling through TikTok, and you stumble upon one of these brands, like [Youth To The People](#3) or [ILIA Beauty](#4), you're not just looking at a trending hashtag-you're witnessing a commitment to beauty that cares, inside and out.

Biossance isn't just another clean beauty brand; their commitment to **sustainability** and solid science sets them apart. When discussing why [TikTok loves these brands](#13), Biossance is a prime example of integrity meeting innovation in a bottle.
