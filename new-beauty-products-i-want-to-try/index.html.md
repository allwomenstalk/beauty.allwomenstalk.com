# 10 New Beauty Products I Want to Try ...

The beginning of the new season is yet another great excuse for us to make a list of new beauty products to buy and try them out! I don’t know about you, but I’m always on the hunt for my next great beauty product and I can’t wait to show you some of these fantastic new products! Keep reading for 10 new and super cool beauty products that yours truly is dying to try and I’m sure you will too!

## 1. The Perfect Fall Nail Polish

![The Perfect Fall Nail Polish](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/beauty/2012/09/1_the-perfect-fall-nail-polish_350x537.jpg)

One of the new beauty products I definitely want to try is a non-traditional yet perennially pretty fall nail varnish. There are some great fall colors out already. While I enjoy doing holiday nail art, I love deep, dark colors that aren’t your typical vampy nail like dark blood red or pure black. Check out Chanel’s Vertigo that's a delicious dark chocolate color that'll make your nails shine!

## 2. Wine Lips

A trend and beauty product to try out this fall is wine-colored lips. I don’t think I pull off really dark lips all that well so I’m glad there’s a huge range of colors to choose from! One of my ideal shades that I definitely want to try out is Nars’ Pure Matte Lipstick in Valparaiso, it’s a deep burgundy with mango butter formulated into it so it won’t leave lips super parched and looking dry!

## 3. Eye Serum

![Eye Serum](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/beauty/2012/09/3_eye-serum_250x250.jpg)

I love serums and they’re one of the fall beauty products to try in the colder months to come! Serums serve to target and improve a particular skin issue so they’re great to have in addition to a multi-purpose moisturizer. The serum I have my eye on is Lancome Genefique Yuex Light-Pearl Eye-Illuminating Youth Active Concentrate. I’ve tried eye creams and gels but never ventured into the world of eye serums so I’m excited to give this a try!

## 4. Liquid Lipstick

The first lip beauty product to try out this fall was a matte wine color but I’m also looking forward to sporting a shiny liquid lip! I like to stay on trend when it comes to makeup and fashion but I also like to go off on my own too! Although this liquefied lip gloss and lipstick hybrid isn’t exactly at the top of fall beauty trends, I think it’ll add some excitement to any fall look. Check out Shiseido’s Makeup Lacquer Rouge in Savage is a great girly color to wear anywhere!
[amazon.com](http://www.amazon.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)

## 5. Fuss-free Polish

Another one of the beauty products I want to try is a nail polish that is comes in a range of on-trend colors and is as easy on the eyes as it is to apply. I’ve never been a nail polish snob so I’m open to all brands and price ranges. One of the nail polish collections that piqued my interest is Maybelline New York’s Color Show Nail Lacquer. It’s chip resistant, comes in four different collections and features a variety of bold colors!
[target.com](http://www.target.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)

## 6. Long Lasting Liner

![Long Lasting Liner](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/beauty/2012/09/6_long-lasting-liner_250x250.jpg)

I’m a total eyeliner addict; I appreciate the virtues of both pencil and liquid liners and it also tops my fall beauty wish list. The problem with pencil lines is that although they’re easy to use and look good, it’s hard to make them stay put! One of the eyeliners that I’m raring to try is from Too Faced. Their Perfect Eyes Waterproof Eye Liner is creamy, long-wearing and comes in some sassy fall colors but I’m always faithful to black.

## 7. Satiny Skin

![Satiny Skin](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/beauty/2012/09/7_satiny-skin_250x250.jpg)

I have to have our faces covered so a foundation is also on my list of beauty products to try this fall! I like the coverage of a liquid foundation but I also love the skin-nourishing benefits of the BB cream but I want more! Okay, maybe not more but I want my skin to feel as good as it looks! New Japanese beauty brand KohGenDo has an Aqua Foundation that looks mighty interesting and promising. It has a pH balanced formula with lots of minerals for a healthy-looking complexion.

## 8. Spellbinding Blush

![Spellbinding Blush](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/beauty/2012/09/8_spellbinding-blush_220x272.jpg)

My fall beauty wish list also includes a super pretty blush to add some much-needed color and warmth to my face during my fall facial blahs. I like to experiment with different formulas and colors but I have yet to find a blush that lasts all day and looks natural without making look like I’ve spent the entire day in a sauna. Ever since I came across e.l.f.’s Studio HD blush that comes in a fun pump, I’ve been really eager to try it out!

## 9. Beauty Mist

A refreshing facial mist and makeup setter is another one of the new beauty products to try this fall. I’ve used a few setting sprays in my day and it was more like a form of punishment than anything else. I discovered e.l.f’s Makeup Mist and Set and was instantly intrigued. Their affordable mist helps moisturize and hydrate your skin but also serves to set your makeup, how cool is that?!
[target.com](http://www.target.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)

## 10. All-natural Moisturizers

![All-natural Moisturizers](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/beauty/2012/09/10_all-natural-moisturizers_410x410.jpg)

I have to make sure to include some natural and paraben-free moisturizers on my list of beauty products to try out this fall. I try as hard as possible to check the labels on my makeup but I’m much better at checking out my skin care products. One of the brands that immediately came to mind was EOS and they have a cool limited edition Breast Cancer Awareness set that comes with a lip balm and lotion that’s got my name written all over it!

That’s just the tip of the iceberg when it comes to new beauty products to try! There are so many more new products coming out that I can’t wait to get my hands on and play with! I’ll miss the bright summery colors but I’m looking forward to trying out some darker shimmery shades and different lip colors! What are some of the new fall beauty products that you want to try?
