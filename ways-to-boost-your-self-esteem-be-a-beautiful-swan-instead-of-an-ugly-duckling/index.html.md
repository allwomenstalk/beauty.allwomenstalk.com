# 7 Ways to Boost Your Self Esteem to Be a Beautiful Swan Instead of an Ugly Duckling ...

If you're a girl struggling with how you feel about yourself, then you need some **ways to boost your self-esteem**. Have you ever felt like you’re the ugly duckling from a good ol' fairy tale?

That everyone's perfect except you? Then keep reading because these few **tips for boosting your self esteem** will change your life. But first things first. Forget about all the nasty stuff you have come to believe about yourself. You're going to be a brand new confident girl, so forget your past.

## 1. Take a Piece of Paper and Write down Everything That You Love about Yourself

![table, furniture, still life,](https://img.allw.mn/content/ws/o9/d4u7143t599dc21b4ec96474029994_245x149.gif)

Read those things out loud and believe in them. Start appreciating those things and you've got the best of the many **ways to boost your self-esteem.**

## 2. Start Accepting Compliments - You Are Worth It

![hair, human hair color, blond, hairstyle, chin,](https://img.allw.mn/content/au/jl/wz7sev6n599dc2b197c53934710171_244x180.gif)

You truly are beautiful, smart, funny and kind. And people wouldn't take time to compliment you if they didn't think so.

## 3. Talk to Yourself in a Kind Way

![girl, black hair, scene,](https://img.allw.mn/content/ms/y9/zgyu93lu599dc39315451182729920_500x281.gif)

Stand in front of the mirror and talk to yourself. You'll figure out much more than you would have if you didn't. Talk just like you would talk to one of your best friends. Be your own bestie.

## 4. Dress the Way YOU Feel Comfortable, Yet Sexy and Confident

![dress, gown, dance, performing arts, dancer,](https://img.allw.mn/content/cu/xo/cx2kz2bo599dc3e3dab66221006450_500x281.gif)

This will give you the guts to go out there and take what you want. We all feel better when we dress nicely.

## 5. Do Something That You Love That Makes You the Happiest Person on the Earth

![person, facial expression, black and white, smile, beauty,](https://img.allw.mn/content/bh/xm/id05pl54599dc477bbfa3846902971_500x300.gif)

Doing what you love can give that extra spark to your eyes and give you an extra dose of confidence.

## 6. Take Time to Pamper Yourself Once in a While

![water, girl, amenity, bathing,](https://img.allw.mn/content/um/p4/pdbxagil599dc0cbed22f911992991_500x202.gif)

A nice warm bath or trip to the spa will do you good. Take care of your body - it's the only one you got.

## 7. Know That You Are One of a Kind - Nobody Can Be YOU

![song,](https://img.allw.mn/content/qz/s5/srprsnpd599dc4cc16519421061614_480x264.gif)

You are special. You are unique. And you have only one life.
Make the best out of it. Live the best life that you can. And remember you are loved.
