# Natural Beauty is Expressed in These 143 Fabulous Pictures ...

Looking at Instagram often gives me makeup inspiration but @meliniiimartin's account actually motivates me to go makeup-free! Melina's IG photos are often taken makeup-free and she looks absolutely stunning. She takes photos with makeup too but it's usually really subtle and her features really shine through. Although I absolutely love makeup, I think not wearing makeup is really refreshing, too. It's really nice to let your skin breathe and show off your natural features once in awhile! Check out her photos, we think you'll be inspired to take more makeup-free selfies in the future!
