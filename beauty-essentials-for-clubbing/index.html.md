# 8 Essential Beauty Essentials for Clubbing: Going Out Clutch Must\-Haves

Ladies, do you need a little help picking out your going out essentials for a fun night on the town? When you’re heading out with friends, you want your must-have items readily available without dragging a heavy shoulder bag along. Fortunately, keeping a curated beauty essentials list in your clutch ensures you stay gorgeous and fresh all night long without feeling weighed down! Read on for eight compact clubbing essentials you will definitely want to keep in your bag.

## 1. Hair Tie

Let’s start off this list of beauty essentials for clubbing with a personal non-negotiable: a reliable hair tie. You might step out the door with voluminous waves or sleek, pin-straight hair, but after an hour of dancing, heat and humidity take over. Keeping hair elastics on hand allows you to sweep your hair up into an effortless top knot or pony tail whenever you hit the dance floor. You can find convenient multipacks at retailers like [Target](https://www.target.com).

Top quick hair options to pack include:

- Spiral elastics that prevent creasing

- Mini claw clips for quick updos

- Soft silk scrunchies that avoid breakage

Pairing your hairstyle switch with a quick lip refresh using a [lip stain](#3) keeps your whole look polished in seconds.

## 2. Blotting Papers

Blotting papers are an absolute lifesaver regardless of the venue. They come in ultra-thin packages that slide seamlessly into even the sleekest clutch, absorbing excess oil without disturbing your foundation. If your face starts to show shine, avoid piling on heavy face powder right away, which can easily look thick and cakey under bright lights. If you ever find yourself in a quick bind without blotting sheets, clean paper napkins work surprisingly well to absorb shine in a flash.

## 3. Lip Product

Long-wearing lip stains and liquid lipsticks are ideal when you need your lip color to last through drinks and dinner. If you prefer high-shine glosses or tinted balms, simply make sure to bring your product along for quick touch-ups throughout the evening. Packing a hydrating lip balm is also smart in case your lips feel dry. Before reapplying, a quick check alongside your [pressed powder](#8) mirror ensures crisp application.

## 4. Breath Mints or Gum

While mints and gum are technically wellness items rather than makeup, they are just as crucial when you're out socializing. After enjoying cocktails or appetizers, keeping your breath minty fresh gives you instant confidence. Keep a compact pack in your bag so you are ready whenever you get into close conversation or run into your crush!

Great options to keep on hand:

- Sugar-free peppermint gum

- Micro breath sprays

- Dissolvable mint strips

## 5. Eye Liner

Waterline eyeliner can easily smudge or fade after a couple of hours of dancing and warmth. Carrying a travel-sized pencil liner lets you quickly redefine your lash line in the restroom mirror. If you don't typically wear eyeliner, a tiny shadow stick or compact eye palette from [Sephora](https://www.sephora.com) works just as well. Whatever product you choose, keeping it compact is key!

## 6. Nail File

A nail file might not seem like a typical beauty essential, but the moment you snag a nail is when you desperately wish you had one! A chipped or jagged nail can snag on your outfit or ruin your evening if left unhandled. Slim emery boards take up virtually zero space, so tucking one into your purse lining gives you instant peace of mind.

## 7. Lash Glue

If you love wearing false eyelashes, carrying a mini tube of lash glue is essential. Corner lift can happen at the most inconvenient times, disrupting an otherwise flawless eye look. Even if you are a pro at applying lashes, keeping dark or clear adhesive in your clutch takes care of sudden mishaps. You can pick up pocket-sized lash adhesives at [Ulta Beauty](https://www.ulta.com) for quick touch-ups.

## 8. Pressed Powder

While compact powders take up a bit more room than [blotting papers](#2), their built-in mirrors make them extraordinarily useful. A light sweep of translucent powder sets your makeup and tames stubborn shine while giving you a private mirror to check your overall look discreetly.

| Essential Item | Primary Function | Clutch Placement |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Blotting Papers | Absorbs excess oil without disturbing makeup | Side slip pocket |
| Lip Product | Maintains color and hydration throughout the night | Main compartment |
| Mini Lash Glue | Quick emergency repairs for false lashes | Zippered coin pocket |

Depending on your personal makeup routine, your exact selection of going out essentials will vary. Focus on multi-tasking products and mini travel sizes to maximize every inch of space in your handbag. What are your personal must-haves when getting ready for a night out with friends?
