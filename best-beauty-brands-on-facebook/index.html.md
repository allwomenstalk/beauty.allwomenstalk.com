# 8 Best Beauty Brands on Facebook ...

We all have our favorite beauty products, but have you checked out some of the best beauty brands on Facebook? If you haven’t taken a look at the Facebook pages of some of these hot beauty brands, you’re missing out! You can get access to promotions, contests, tutorials and more fun stuff so let’s check them out!

## 1. Physicians Formula

This beauty brand on Facebook offers frequent giveaways, contests and beauty challenges, so it’s definitely worth taking a look around! Physicians Formula even has a Win It Wednesday where you can win a product for answering questions or finding a secret word!

## 2. MAC Cosmetics

One of the best beauty brands on Facebook is MAC Cosmetics! Keep tabs on their company page to stay updated on events, live chats with MAC makeup artists, video tutorials and contests. They have some really outstanding Halloween makeup looks up right now!

## 3. Wet N Wild Cosmetics

A popular drugstore beauty brand on Facebook is Wet N Wild. They have a pretty amazing page where you can enter sweepstakes and look at photos for beauty inspiration. Currently there are sweepstakes to win a trip to meet Kaley Cuoco and win Fergie swag!

## 4. Tarte Cosmetics

Natural cosmetics giant Tarte is another one of the best beauty brands on Facebook. Visit their page for info on events, new products, makeup tutorials, take a peek at their Pinterest boards and shop some of their best-sellers!

## 5. Rimmel London US

This beauty brand Facebook page offers a little bit of everything! Stop by Rimmel London US for the scoop on new products, enter contests, and upload your own “scandalous mugshot” using their Scandaleyes mascara!

## 6. Urban Decay Cosmetics

A popular beauty brand on Facebook is the cheeky and fun Urban Decay Cosmetics. Take a look around their page for useful product reviews, events at stores and tons of photos from the brand, consumers and features in magazines for info and inspiration!

## 7. Smashbox Cosmetics

Smashbox’s website offers up a lot perks so it’s no surprise that they’re one of the best beauty brands on Facebook! Check out their page for product features, events, create your own photo layout and add photos and stories to their Breast Cancer Awareness Campaign!

## 8. Illamasqua

This British cult beauty brand on Facebook features lots of virtual glam goodies like amazing makeup photos, an interactive look book with pages of products and how-to’s and you can even get 15% off your first purchase when you “like” their page!

Although there are many beauty brands online, these eight brands make up the some of the best beauty brands on Facebook! Keep an eye on these pages on Facebook to help you learn how to use a new product, win something fab or even be featured in one of their photo albums! Do you have a beauty brand page that you visit regularly? What’s your favorite company Facebook page?
