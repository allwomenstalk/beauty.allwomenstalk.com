# 9 High\-end Beauty Products That Are Actually Worth the Price ...

So you want to invest in some **high-end beauty products but don’t want** to waste your money on crap that doesn’t work? I hear you and I’ve been there! We all want a little taste of luxury when it comes to our beauty routine and there’s nothing wrong with spending a little more on something that works wonders. Take a look at these 9 high-end beauty products that are actually worth the price.

## 1. Shiseido Benefiance WrinkleResist24 Intensive Eye Contour Cream

![Shiseido Benefiance WrinkleResist24 Intensive Eye Contour Cream](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/beauty/2012/03/1_shiseido-benefiance-wrinkleresist24-intensive-eye-contour-cream_232x208.jpg)

**Price:** $55.00 at [sephora.com](http://www.sephora.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
This eye cream covers every eye aging concern you could have, creases in the inner eye, crow’s feet, undereye wrinkles, lid creases and vertical lines under the eye. The container appears small but just a little dab will do ya. Consistently applying this cream can do wonders for the appearance of your eye area and it’s super moisturizing.

## 2. Bumble and Bumble Prep

![Bumble and Bumble Prep](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/beauty/2012/03/2_bumble-and-bumble-prep_250x250.jpg)

**Price:** $6.19 at [sephora.com](http://www.sephora.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
Most Bumble and Bumble hair products go above and beyond their product description and it couldn’t be truer for their Prep hair detangler and styling primer. Mist this on wet hair to detangle and prep tresses for styling or spray on styled hair for an extra boost. This high-end beauty product was designed to make detangling a breeze and refresh hair without weighing it down.

## 3. Patricia Wexler M.D. Intensive Night Reversal & Repair Cream

![Patricia Wexler M.D. Intensive Night Reversal & Repair Cream](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/beauty/2012/03/Patricia-Wexler-M.D.-Intensive-Night-Reversal-Repair-Cream_250x223.jpg)

**Price:** $46.50 at [amazon.com](http://www.amazon.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
This high-end beauty product has legions of fans singing its praises of delivering soft, supple skin after applying it at night. This is another product where a little goes a long way so don’t let the small container fool you. If you’ve been looking for a night cream that really repairs and revives your skin, you’ve found your Holy Grail night cream!

## 4. Dior DiorShow Iconic

![Dior DiorShow Iconic](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/beauty/2012/03/4_dior-diorshow-iconic_250x250.jpg)

**Price:** $28.00 at [sephora.com](http://www.sephora.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
Dior’s iconic mascara is cult classic and one of the highest rated **high-end beauty products on the** market. This mascara takes multi-tasking to the next level by separating, curling, lifting and lasts 24 hours without smudging. If you're tired of eye makeup that can't keep up with you, pick up this mascara ASAP! You can even forgo the eyelash curler with this mascara!

## 5. Yves Saint Laurent Touche Eclat Radiant Touch

![Yves Saint Laurent Touche Eclat Radiant Touch](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/beauty/2012/03/5_yves-saint-laurent-touche-eclat-radiant-touch_250x250.jpg)

**Price:** $40.00 at [sephora.com](http://www.sephora.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
Touche Eclat is another prestige beauty product with a huge following. It might look like nothing more than a chic pen, but it’s a highlighter and concealer that provides sheer yet consistent coverage and illuminates the skin. Tote this pen along in your bag and feel free to do touch-ups throughout the day; this product is sheer and totally buildable.

## 6. NARS Blush

![NARS Blush](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/beauty/2012/03/6_nars-blush_232x184.jpg)

**Price:** $28.00 at [sephora.com](http://www.sephora.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
This award-winning blush features over 20 gorgeous shades to color you beautiful and give you a natural yet flattering wash of color on your cheeks. Use this luxury beauty product as a highlighter or wear alone or layered with another cheek color as blush. The swatches might appear like it’s loaded with shimmer, but it’s actually quite understated so don’t worry about looking like a Vegas showgirl gone wrong.

## 7. L’Occitane Ultra-Rich Body Cream Rich in Shea Butter

![L’Occitane Ultra-Rich Body Cream Rich in Shea Butter](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/beauty/2012/03/L’Occitane-Ultra-Rich-Body-Cream-Rich-in-Shea-Butter_250x250.jpg)

**Price: **$42.00 at [sephora.com](http://www.sephora.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)The perfect high-end beauty product for those who crave a thick, nourishing body cream that actually lasts! Ladies adore this cream for its light scent and super-moisturizing yet non-greasy finish. This body cream is highly concentrated in Shea butter and contains tons of nourishing ingredients which leave your skin feeling soft, supple and pampered.

## 8. Guerlain Terracotta 4 Seasons Tailor-Made Bronzing Powder

![Guerlain Terracotta 4 Seasons Tailor-Made Bronzing Powder](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/beauty/2012/03/8_guerlain-terracotta-4-seasons-tailor-made-bronzing-powder_250x250.jpg)

**Price:** $75.00 at [sephora.com](http://www.sephora.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
This luxury bronzer is pricey but according to the masses of women who have fallen in love with it, it’s definitely worth its weight in gold. Not only is made without any nasty chemicals, it’s got you covered for the whole year with a dedicated bronzing powder for each season. Whether you use the bronzers individually or mix it up, the beautiful combination of colors is sure complement most skin tones.

## 9. Lancome L’Absolu Rouge Lipstick

![Lancome L’Absolu Rouge Lipstick](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/beauty/2012/03/9_lancome-labsolu-rouge-lipstick_250x250.jpg)

**Price:** $29.50 at [sephora.com](http://www.sephora.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
Unsure if you should splurge on a high-end lipstick? Lancome’s L’Absolu Rouge Lipstick is another high-end beauty product that you should not go another day without! Take your pick from 18 stunning shades of pinks, reds and nudes which are all enriched with SPF 12 and Pro-Xlyane which helps to reshape and replenish the look and feel of your lips!

Are you ready to attack the beauty counter and get some of these 9 splurge-worthy beauty products? If you’re still wary about investing you hard-earned cash, you can always ask for samples before taking the plunge and going full-size!
