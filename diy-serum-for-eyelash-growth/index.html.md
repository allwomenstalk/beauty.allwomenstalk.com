# DIY Serum for Eyelash Growth ...

Looking for a **DIY serum for eyelash growth**?

There is a quote that I like. "The beauty of a woman is not in her hair or makeup." Beautiful shining eyes with super long eyelashes are always attractive. It is possible to have long lashes without eyelash extensions. There are a lot of serums on the market, but if you're looking something natural and if you want both length and volume here is a natural **DIY serum for eyelash growth**.

INGREDIENTS
2 tbsp coconut oil
2 tsp. castor oil
2 tsp vitamin E

Take a container and add all ingredients. It's better to use a glass container. First, add 2 tbsp of coconut oil. The magical effect of [coconut oil for hair](https://food.allwomenstalk.com/ways-that-cooking-with-coconut-oil-can-help-you/) is known. It works for eyelashes too. Coconut oil gives the perfect moisturizing effect and thickens lashes.

Then add 2 tsp of castor oil. Castor oil is secret ingredient for your lashes. With castor oil, eyelashes grow fast and long without breaking.

Finally, add vitamin E oil. Vitamin E is also beneficial for lashes.
Shake and mix all oils together. It is ready.

You can store your eyelash serum in a jar and use a mascara brush to apply on cleanly washed lashes. Swipe a layer on before applying mascara, or use at night to let the oils work their magic.

You can also transfer your serum into an empty mascara container. It would be easy to carry with you.

Use it as your daily routine and you will see how your eyelashes grow and thicken in a week or a month. All the ingredients aim to treat each individual lash. Get sky-high eyelashes in a short period of time. And be ready for compliments.
