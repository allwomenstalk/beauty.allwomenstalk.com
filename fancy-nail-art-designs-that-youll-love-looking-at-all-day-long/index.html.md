# 24 Fancy Nail Art Designs That You'll Love Looking at All Day Long ...

Fancy nail art is rocking the world of beauty right now. Get the look you crave by using these ideas for inspiration. Don't forget to describe your favorite fancy nail art ideas for me to try.

## 1. Love in Purple and Silver

![nail,finger,nail care,purple,manicure,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ln/09/gf3uk_nail_finger_nail-care_purple_manicure_598x545.jpg)

Via [Nail Ideas | Beauty Tutorials](http://www.ibeautytutorial.com/nail-ideas-290/)

This is one of the best fancy nail art designs and is super fun because it incorporates a heart on the third nail that's the same color as the base on several of your other nails. Add some glitter on one nail and as stripes with the heart and you're good to go.

## 2. Flowery Fun

![nail,color,finger,nail care,pink,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/mj/ud/nilrp_nail_color_finger_nail-care_pink_594x599.jpg)

Via [media-cache-ec0.pinimg.com](http://i.pinimg.com/1200x/61/ba/87/61ba8730ff972b9c61296eb8541c352a.jpg)
