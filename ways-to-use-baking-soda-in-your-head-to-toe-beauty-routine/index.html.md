# Genius Ways to Use Baking Soda  in Your Head to Toe Beauty Routine ...

I'm here to share with you **ways to use baking soda in your head to toe beauty routine**. This kitchen staple is super cheap and can do wonders for many aspects of your beauty. So, get ready to look gorgeous from head to toe with these **ways to use baking soda in your head to toe beauty routine**.

## 1. For Your Hair

Get beautiful manageable hair by adding a quarter size amount of baking soda to your daily shampoo. This will make your crowning glory soft and beautiful, it’s also a great, natural hair cleanser. This is one of the best **ways to use baking soda in your head to toe beauty routine.**

## 2. For Your Teeth

Baking soda, along with a hydrogen peroxide solution, will naturally clean and polish your teeth. You can also give your toothpaste a boost by dipping your toothbrush with toothpaste into baking soda. Two teaspoons of baking soda in a glass of water is also great for soaking your partial or dentures. It leaves them clean and odor free.

## 3. Freshen Your Breath

Freshen your mouth and breath with 1 teaspoon of baking soda in half a glass of water; swish, gargle and rinse, this will neutralize the odor not just cover it up.

## 4. Ease Heartburn

If you are experiencing heartburn or acid indigestion, completely dissolve ½ teaspoon of baking soda in a half of glass of water and drink it every 2 hours. Consult a physician if you are on a sodium restricted diet. Do not administer to children under the age of 5.

## 5. Exfoliate

Baking soda is excellent as an exfoliating facial and body scrub. Mix 3 parts baking soda to 1 part water and rub in a circular motion, then rinse. I use it once a week but its gentle enough to use every day.

## 6. Wash Your Hands

Make a gentle hand cleanser and softener to take the place of harsh soap. Use 3 parts of baking soda to 1-part water.

## 7. Soften Your Skin

For soft feeling skin and a relaxing bath, add ½ cup of baking soda to your bath. At the end of a long day, relax your feet in a tub of warm water with 3 tablespoons of baking soda.

As shown baking soda is not just for cooking, it’s great for beautifying and it has many health benefits. It’s also very economical and safe to use.
