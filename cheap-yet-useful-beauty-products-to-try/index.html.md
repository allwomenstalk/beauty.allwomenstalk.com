# 7 Cheap Yet Useful Beauty Products to Try ...

I am always thrilled when I can find useful beauty products. I am even happier when I can find items that are both helpful and inexpensive, but sometimes that can be difficult. I usually try several products before I find one that I love. I wanted to share some of my favorite useful beauty products with you. Perhaps, some of them will become your favorites, too.

## 1. Vaseline Original

![Vaseline Original](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ny/xy/v0ikiyaq_293x300.jpg)

Vaseline is one of the most useful beauty products available. You can use it to treat dry skin. It can also be used to treat minor cuts and burns. Some people even use Vaseline as an eye makeup remover. My daughter has sensitive ears, so we like to apply Vaseline before inserting earrings. You can also apply it under eye shadow for a shiny look or under blush for a more dewy effect.

## 2. St. Ives Timeless Skin Facial Moisturizer

![St. Ives Timeless Skin Facial Moisturizer](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/yq/my/x6ckp87r_299x260.jpg)

I have sensitive skin, so I’m always wary of new facial products. Many of them tend to cause breakouts and irritations. I first saw this product on an episode of The Dr. Oz Show, but I didn't decide to try it until it was recommended by a friend. I have now been using this moisturizer for a couple of weeks, I’m very satisfied with it. It doesn't irritate my skin and leaves it feeling very smooth.

## 3. N.Y.C Eyeliner Pencil

![N.Y.C Eyeliner Pencil](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/e1/o3/cemruwit_332x375.jpg)

If I could only have one makeup item, it would be eyeliner. I like using liquid and gel eyeliners, but pencil is my absolute favorite. I have purchased many different brands and have found that N.Y.C eyeliner pencil works just as well for me as many expensive brands. It also goes on smoothly and doesn't smudge. It also stays put for several hours.

## 4. Suave Keratin Infusion Dry Shampoo

![Suave Keratin Infusion Dry Shampoo](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/kk/zb/bl6hq52f_299x251.jpg)

Dry shampoo is a product that’s fairly new to me. I decided to try it after my hairdresser recommended it. I choose this brand because I have been pleased with Suave Keratin shampoo and conditioner. I typically wash my hair every other day, but this product allows me to go an extra day when needed. I really like that it makes my hair look shiner and feel cleaner.

## 5. Nivea Lip Butter Smooth Kiss

![Nivea Lip Butter Smooth Kiss](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/q5/bt/cy3dc1ow_268x239.jpg)

Every winter I suffer from terribly chapped lips, but this year has been different. When I tried Nivea Smooth Kiss Lip Butter it quickly became my go-to lip product. I love that it is lightweight, but long lasting. It is available in different flavors, but I prefer this one because its scent free. I can’t wait to try some other Nivea products.

## 6. Jergens Natural Glow Daily Moisturizer

![Jergens Natural Glow Daily Moisturizer](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/yt/dr/vo7eax54_113x300.jpg)

I have tried many sunless tanning products and this one is my favorite. I love that it is a tanner and a moisturizer in one. It contains both antioxidants and Vitamin E. I really like that I can gradually get a natural looking tan. It doesn't leave my skin looking orange, like others tanning products do. You can purchase it in two different shades, depending on the look you’re going for.

## 7. Caress Evenly Gorgeous Exfoliating Body Wash

![Caress Evenly Gorgeous Exfoliating Body Wash](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/rd/qh/tko8bh1l_125x300.jpg)

I have tried several different body washes, but I always go back to this one. It is very inexpensive and can be purchased at most drugstores. My skin always feels smooth and super clean after I use it. It has a wonderful scent, which seems to fill my entire bathroom for hours. I love this item so much that I often add it to birthday gifts.

I hope you find some of these beauty products helpful. Do you have any beauty products that you would like to recommend to me. I can't wait to hear what they are.
