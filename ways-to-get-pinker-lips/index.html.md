# 9 Ways to Get Pinker Lips That Are Totally Kissable ...

The best curve on a lady is her smile, and I’ve found that some of the ways to get pinker lips are actually what makes that curve so irresistible. One of my biggest turn-ons has to be a gorgeous smile, and pink lips. I’ve always loved trying out new ways to get pinker lips, and now I’m spilling all the secrets.

## 1. Pucker up & Suck It up!

Honestly ladies, one of the simplest ways to get pinker lips is by drinking more water. Doctors, trainers, and even tabloids all tell us that we need to drink more water, so now is the time for action! Take your body weight and divide it in half and that’s how many ounces of water you should drink a day. If you’re very active, like me then you’ll have to drink a lot more water, but meeting the minimal requirements is a sure way to start getting the those lovely lips luscious.

## 2. Sip ‘N’ Go!

I’ll be the first to admit that I reach for my cup of coffee every morning without a straw, but using a straw is another one of the awesome ways to get pinker lips. See, dark liquids actually stain and discolor our lips, however if you sip your darker liquids out of a straw, then the pinking process begins.

## 3. Tidy up!

A lot of the time I tend to forget that my lips are skin too, but one of the best keep secrets about ways to get pinker lips is exfoliating. Exfoliating your lips is easy because you can do it while you’re getting ready in the morning or even before bed. Take the bristles of your toothbrush and just gently rub them over your lips for about 30 seconds. Once you’re done, apply a lip balm to help heal the irritation, and then you and your smooth pink lips are good to go!

## 4. Power to Protection!

I’m not sure if you ladies know this, but SPF is another one of the ways to get pinker lips. Applying any type of lip protectant that contains SPF to your lips ensures healthier, pinker, and softer lips! Slather it on!

## 5. Tinted Say What?

I’m sure by now many of you have heard of tinted moisturizer, but never fear, there are tinted Chapsticks that help us achieve our goal of mastering ways to get pinker lips. Ladies, a tinted lip balm goes a long way. Not only does the balm help soften our lips and keep them moist, it gives the illusion that we have pinker or redder lips like we want. I personally use Baby Lips by Maybelline to give me a tint.

## 6. Eyes, Lips, Face, Oh My!

Another one of the rad ways to get pinker lips is through using products you already have. One day, I decided that I didn’t want to spend any money on lip products that may or may not work for me. I took my EOS balm and applied it to my lips, then I took my index finger and tapped it in my blush, and finally I applied it to my lips. I received some very pink results, so I tried the same thing with my eyeshadows as well, and I found an easy and cheap way to get pinker lips. Budget friendly beauty is what I call it.

## 7. Rub-a-Dub-Dub

Every night when I take off my makeup or just cleanse my face, I take a wipe to my lips and make sure all my lipstick is off, because it’s another one of the secret ways to get pinker lips. Sometimes lip products have contents in them that clot the natural oils in our skin, so the cleaner the pinker!

## 8. Keepin’ It Kool!

Can we just take a moment and appreciate the fact that using the Kool Aid Man from our childhood is one of the ways to get pinker lips? Moment appreciated! Okay gals, if you’ve ever spilled Kool Aid and wiped it up with a wet rag, then you know it stains something terrible. However, the stain from the powder is actually what gets our lips pinker! Lick your finger, then dab it ever so lightly in a Kool Aid pack, and finally apply it! This will give you pink lips for 24 hours, and it cost less than a dollar!

## 9. Maintain the Stain!

One of the last ways to get pinker lips is by maintaining the stain. If you use the Kool Aid trick that I recommend, apply it as needed and before you know it, you’re natural lip color will be the red or pink Kool Aid you used. Any type of lip stainer helps achieve those pink lips. Simple, inexpensive, and tasty is the best way to maintain the stain.

As I said before, the best curve on a lady is her smile, and with these tips on getting pinker lips, any lady can be as curvy as she’d like. From simple sips to kickin’ it with the Kool Aid Man, attaining pinker lips is possible and easy. Do you have any tricks up your sleeve for making your lips pinker? If you do, please share!
