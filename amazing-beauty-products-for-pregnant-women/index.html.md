# 7 Amazing Beauty Products for Pregnant Women ...

It seems that there are now more beauty products for pregnant women than ever before. There are all-natural products, pamper products for Mum, scented products for baby, weaker versions of everyday products…you can’t turn around without seeing a new set of beauty products for pregnant women, and if you announce that you’re expecting, you can guarantee that you’ll receive at least one. Not all products are created equal, though. Here’s a lowdown of the top beauty products for pregnant women, so you know exactly what to put on your wish list…

Even the packing makes this body oil stand out from other beauty products for pregnant women! It’s a cute pink bottle, and it’s packed with goodness. From Omega 3, 6 and 9 to sweet almond and jojoba, it’s got everything that you need to keep your skin nourished, firm, elastic and glowing. In short, you’ll get the pregnancy glow that you’ve always imagined. Amazing.

## 2. Zita West Vital Essence 1…

![Zita West Vital Essence 1…](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ft/cw/av/o5s74txi_599x495.jpg)

Okay, so this is a blend between a beauty product and a health supplement, but it has definite beauty benefits. As well as being filled with micro-nutrients that are vital during the first stages of pregnancy and will give your baby the best start, they are sure to make you positively radiant. You’ll feel a lot better if you aren’t deficient in nutrients, too.

## 3. Elemis Pro-Collagen Cleansing Balm…

![Elemis Pro-Collagen Cleansing Balm…](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/uj/bo/dd/odhkwp7q_599x309.jpg)

Want a striking complexion? This is the product for you. It combines starflower, elderberry and optimega oils, meaning that it will cleanse, nourish and smooth, as well as moisturize. It comes in a great pot that you can carry in your handbag, your maternity bag and your nappy bag, to keep you looking amazing all the way through. A few minutes rubbing it into your face is super relaxing, too!

## 4. Louise Galvin Deep Conditioning Treatment…

![Louise Galvin Deep Conditioning Treatment…](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/sz/r7/xh/bplcutxm_499x478.jpg)

Worried about your hair during and after pregnancy? This is the answer. Not only does it have the look of a luxurious spa treatment and smell like heaven, but it restores hair's moisture and strength, and keeps your colour looking great. It’s full of vegetable proteins and honey derivatives too, so your hair is left soft and silky, without being weighed down. There’s a reason that it’s won so many beauty awards!

## 5. Sanctuary Therapist's Facial Oil…

![Sanctuary Therapist's Facial Oil…](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fc/am/h1/s4a6gm7g_499x452.jpg)

Keep yourself feeling pampered throughout pregnancy and beyond with this Sanctuary Therapist's Facial Oil. It’s designed to make you look youthful and glowy. It’s a therapy oil, too, meaning that it smells gorgeous, and it’s perfect for taking five minutes to really reconnect with yourself and de-stress. I’m totally in love with this facial oil, and I’m not even pregnant!

## 6. Eucerin Even Brighter Day Cream…

![Eucerin Even Brighter Day Cream…](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wc/f3/sg/z1nyn5zn_599x421.jpg)

If you’re looking for a complexion boost that will also reduce pigmentation, this is the day cream for you. It reduces the appearance of pigmentation, and evens out the skin into a bright complexion. Pregnancy pigmentation isn’t uncommon, but this is one of the first effective creams that is actually mild enough to use all the way through pregnancy. It’s definitely worth getting in the habit of wearing it!

## 7. Weleda Stretch Mark Massage Oil…

It’s proven to reduce stretch marks across the bum, stomach and thighs, and it has an amazing success rate if you start using it early. It’s a lovely way to enjoy your changing body, too.
