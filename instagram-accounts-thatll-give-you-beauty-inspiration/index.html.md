# 7 Major Insta Accounts for Girls Who Need Serious Beauty Inspo ...

Sometimes seeing is believing, which is why you should totally be following Instagram accounts for beauty. Not only can you get the visual inspiration you need to master new and lovely looks, but you also get tutorials, tips and tricks that makes it super easy to get the look you crave. Not all beauty accounts are created equal and some are better than others. Here are some outstanding Instagram accounts that you need to add to your list. Trust me – beauty will never be the same again.

## 1. [@michellephan](https://www.facebook.com/unsupportedbrowser)

![Hemmo1996, person, black hair, beauty, singer,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/gs/b0/cem8k7i557bf2edfaf4a8359096968_600x600.jpg)

With over four million YouTube followers, you know she has to be good. Michelle is the guru for all things beauty and her Instagram account is one that you can’t afford not to follow. She isn’t afraid to try new things and show you how they look so that you can decide if they are right for you or not. She also helps you master new make-up trends and take the fear out of trying new things. Perfect!

## 2. [@intothegloss](https://www.facebook.com/unsupportedbrowser)

![hair, chin, blond, nose, hairstyle,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/bc/jp/q5acl2y357bf2f035416e365563107_600x600.jpg)

This account is an extension of the matching blog and is perfect for busy girls. The author, Emily Weiss, used to spend her days in the magazine industry so she’s up on all the latest when it comes to beauty. Follow her for the inside scoop on upcoming trends and get all the celebrity beauty inspiration you can handle.

## 3. [@chiaraferragni](https://www.facebook.com/unsupportedbrowser)

![@chiaraferragni](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/u4/pu/a7dhbap955b93d57b5744420869961_600x600.jpg)

This Italian girl knows what’s up when it comes to hair and make-up. In fact, she can give you all the inspiration you need when you want to try something new or jazz up your current look. She can help you decide on a new hairstyle or master a night on the town make-up look that will knock everyone’s socks off.

## 4. [@RealbarbarapalVin](https://www.facebook.com/unsupportedbrowser)

![eyebrow, beak, hair, chin, beauty,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/q0/x2/mlo3l8ny57bf2f81bc312532010990_600x600.jpg)

This account is full of all the goodness of the model who writes it. Who better to inspire your beauty ideal than a stunning runway looker? She shares her best pro shots and her everyday life looks so that you can be inspired to switch up your look based on what’s on the day’s agenda.

## 5. [@byrdiebeauty](https://www.facebook.com/unsupportedbrowser)

![hair, hairstyle, black hair, cornrows, head,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/gi/u5/m59664xt57bf30057065e745820420_600x749.jpg)

If the latest trends and easy to do steps are what you want, this is the account for you. You’ll get all the info you need on the newest and best products as well as new trends to try. If glamourous is your motto, this account will help you achieve that each and every day. Have fun!

## 6. [@jinsoonchoi](https://www.facebook.com/unsupportedbrowser)

![clothing, hair, photograph, model, hairstyle,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/nw/pj/d0jcqkt157bf305d65b6c689203294_600x746.jpg)

If your idea of beauty is fabulous nails, I’m right there with you. That’s why I love this Instagram account. It’s packed with great nail art ideas and can give you a look that works for any holiday, event or occasion. All you have to do is page through all the great photos and you won’t be able to help but be inspired to grab your polish and get to work.

## 7. [@beautyblitz](https://www.facebook.com/unsupportedbrowser)

![eyebrow, beak, nose, eye, beauty,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/kq/id/upjti7v557bf30c8a90c5331250148_600x600.jpg)

True to its name, you are going to be awed at the sheer amount of awesome beauty stuff on this account. Not only do you get all sorts of great info about the latest in the world of beauty, but the fun comments and captions are sure to make your day. Fun and beauty all rolled into one? Sign me up!

Do you follow any Instagram accounts? Will you be adding any of these to your list?
