# 6 Undeniable Benefits of a Proper Skincare Routine …

**Building a skincare routine** is a form of self-care that has long-reaching **benefits for your mind and body**. In addition to helping your skin stay healthy, the **time you spend on your skincare can become a soothing, rejuvenating ritual.**

If this is your first time building a skincare routine, know that **everyone’s skin has different needs**. Taking the time to explore whether you could benefit from a [firming face cream](https://volulift.imageskincare.com/pages/home) or maybe a dark spot corrector helps you **get the best results from your efforts.**

Whether you’re trying skincare for the first time or are a seasoned pro, **understanding the benefits of a consistent routine** can motivate you to prioritize your skin's needs.

## 1. Boost Mental Health

In today’s fast-paced world, having a skincare routine gives you an opportunity to take a breather. Building routines into your day can help you [better manage overall stress](https://www.nm.org/healthbeat/healthy-tips/health-benefits-of-having-a-routine). Adding a skincare routine to your bedtime ritual can also help improve your sleep.

Applying products in the morning offers a moment for reflection and goal setting. Starting your day with intention can leave you feeling refreshed and accomplished.

## 2. Improve Skin Tone and Texture

![](https://img.allw.mn/96466b0e1ac4eb84a455efe419a78f_1024x1307.jpg)

Your skin serves a powerful role by protecting your body from the onslaught of germs that you encounter on a daily basis. Yet, your skin also needs a little help with removing buildup, such as dead cells and debris that accumulate throughout the day.

An ideal skincare routine includes solutions for resolving discolorations and blemishes that occur naturally over time. For instance, incorporating a [vitamin C serum](https://imageskincare.com/collections/vitamin-c) into your skincare routine can provide benefits that help your complexion appear brighter and more toned.

Topical skincare products containing vitamin C can promote better wound healing and help with hydration, resulting in brighter and healthier-looking skin. Keep in mind that you’ll want to follow the instructions for using your new serum in your skincare routine. Most vitamin C serums are used once or twice daily for best results.

## 3. Reduce Visible Pores

Natural oils in your skin serve a protective purpose by keeping it supple and moisturized. However, excessive oil buildup can lead to clogged pores, which may become visible over time. To remove oil from your face without completely stripping it of moisture, it's helpful to use clarifying serums and creams.

Once you’ve cleansed your skin, you can use periodic face masks to tone and brighten, helping to reduce the appearance of your pores. Smaller pores contribute to a smoother surface, which also helps with makeup application.

## 4. Defend Your Skin Against Environmental Damage

![](https://img.allw.mn/d0b61322e155714bc711d9982e3395_1024x1268.jpg)

Many skincare products are designed to protect your skin from environmental stressors, such as UV rays and air pollution. For instance, adding products that contain a high-performance sunscreen can help [protect your skin from the sun’s UV rays](https://health.clevelandclinic.org/why-you-should-wear-sunscreen-every-day).

Since it’s best to wear sunscreen every day, adding it to your morning skin care routine makes it easy to remember to apply your favorite product before you go outside. In the evening, cleansing your skin helps remove pollutants and other irritants that collect on your face throughout the day. A routine that includes both morning and nighttime care provides your skin with the best defense.

## 5. Address Common Skin Conditions

While diagnosing skin conditions like eczema should be left to a doctor, your skincare routine can help manage symptoms. For instance, using gentle exfoliation products can help smooth flaky skin, allowing for better absorption of soothing moisturizers.

Applying lotion to your skin each morning and evening also helps create a protective layer that prevents it from drying out in the wind or cold temperatures. Taking care of your skin this way ensures it remains comfortable and healthy, helping you feel your best in daily life.

## 6. Smooth Away the Appearance of Wrinkles

![](https://img.allw.mn/11c5154fc9c5c80f62b25b55272105_1024x1268.jpg)

One of the primary benefits of establishing a skincare routine is preventing the signs of aging, including fine lines and wrinkles. If you’re concerned about small lines appearing on your skin, you can add products containing [hyaluronic acid](https://imageskincare.com/collections/hyaluronic-acid) to your routine.

Although the name of this ingredient sounds complicated, it’s a substance that [your body produces naturally](https://my.clevelandclinic.org/health/articles/22915-hyaluronic-acid). Known for helping to keep cells hydrated, you’ll find that including products with hyaluronic acid in your routine gives your skin a plumper and more youthful appearance, helping to smooth the look of wrinkles over time.

## Create a Skincare Routine That Puts Your Best Face Forward

Skincare needs change over time, which is why it is important to **take a customized approach to building your new routine** You can start by using a **skin quiz to build your regimen**, or **explore products that you know have worked in the past.**

Whatever you choose, **consistency is key**. Make sure to **practice your new skincare routine each morning and evening** to begin revitalizing your face and body. With consistency, **you’ll soon see the results** of your efforts reflected in your overall appearance while **enjoying greater confidence and mental clarity.**
