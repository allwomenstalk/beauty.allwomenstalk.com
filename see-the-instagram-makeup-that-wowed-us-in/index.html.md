# See the Instagram Makeup That Wowed Us in 2015 ...

It's a love/hate relationship when it comes to makeup, but 2015 brought a lot of lips, bold colors, and lengthy lashes! Check out the IG makeup looks that wowed us!

## 1. JACLYN HILL

The Queen herself slayed this color combo! The reds and warms were all the rage this year-- she rocked it for sure!

## 2. PATRICK STARRR

I think he blew the internet away, most importantly IG! He brings makeup to a new level and his looks are to die for!

## 3. KATHLEEN LIGHTS

Kathleen Lights brings lipstick and colors to life! Her bold lip looks inspired tons of IG followers!

## 4. NIKKIE TUTORIALS

UGH! Look at that wing! Her technique on lining the eyes is easy and gorgeous!

## 5. KYLIE JENNER

Kylie shares her make up skills on her app! And, I know a lot of people wonder how she gets glammed all day, everyday!

## 6. KENDALL JENNER

This rockstar always highlights her natural beauty!

## 7. LASHES

LASHES FOR DAYSSS! I think the eyes speak volumes, if your brows on fleek and your shadow is gorg. You have to make sure the overall look is complemented by your lashes! Guys, your lashes have to do the talking!
