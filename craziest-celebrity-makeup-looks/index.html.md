# 8 Craziest Celebrity Makeup Looks ...

Celebrities have been known to set trends with their hair and makeup and while most celebs these days are pretty tame when it comes to their appearance, I rounded up 8 crazy celebrity [makeup looks](https://makeup.allwomenstalk.com/classic-eye-makeup-looks-that-never-fail-to-impress/) of stars who aren’t afraid to take risks with their looks. This just goes to show that some celebs just can’t stop pushing the envelope and love to express their creativity through their appearance. Join me on this journey though celebrity makeup ridiculousness and revel in their bravery!

## 1. Nicki Minaj

![Nicki Minaj](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/beauty/2012/09/1_nicki-minaj_600x337.jpg)

Nicki Minaj is one of stars with the most crazy celebrity makeup looks! That girl can pull off Marge Simpson hair, rainbow colored makeup and outrageous outfits galore and we can’t get enough! Nicki is often spotted with insane eyelashes, blindingly bright hair and eye makeup which she manages to make look cute and yet crazy at the same time!

## 2. Ke$ha

![Ke$ha](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/beauty/2012/09/2_ke-ha_600x819.jpg)

Ke$ha gets my top vote for craziest celebrity makeup! Ke$ha’s not afraid to wear bright face paint, blue lipstick, Clockwork Orange-inspired eye makeup and glitter galore! She’s definitely not scared of color, breaking traditional makeup rules and thinking out of the box when it comes to makeup. You’ve gotta love her bold and daring personality and her willingness to take risks we’d never take!

## 3. Lady Gaga

![Lady Gaga](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/beauty/2012/09/3_lady-gaga_500x333.jpg)

Another one of the reigning queens of crazy celeb makeup is Lady Gaga. From day one this pop superstar took chances with her appearance and now we expect nothing but the extreme when it comes to Lady Gaga’s makeup and fashion. Whether she has jewels glued onto her face or facial prosthetics, Lady Gaga knows how to make heads turn and keep us guessing with what she’ll do next!

## 4. Christina Aguilera

![Christina Aguilera](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/beauty/2012/09/4_christina-aguilera_554x668.jpg)

Once upon a time in a land not too far away, Xtina was guilty of rocking some of the craziest celebrity makeup! If you remember her Dirrrty and Lady Marmalade days, she fearlessly wore lavish lashes, barely-there eyebrows and heavy mascara and red eye shadow. Christina wears much more natural and flattering makeup now but she went through her crazy celeb makeup phase too!

## 5. Marilyn Manson

![Marilyn Manson](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/beauty/2012/09/5_marilyn-manson_600x397.jpg)

Did you think a male would make it on this list of craziest celebrity makeup?! Marilyn Manson doesn’t wear the most becoming makeup but he knows the shock value of wearing daring and unusual makeup. I commend the guy for taking chances and being a non-conformist when it comes to his appearance but I’ve gotta admit I’m kinda mad at the guy for rocking the hell out of those crimson lips!

## 6. Lil’ Kim

![Lil’ Kim](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/beauty/2012/09/6_lil-kim_550x389.jpg)

Sigh, Lil’ Kim used to wear tasteful makeup in her pre-plastic surgery days but once she got some work done it’s like she went wild and insists on always wearing the craziest celebrity makeup! If you check out some of her more recent photos, she can be seen with crazy-big eyebrows, mismatched foundation and bronzer and super brightly colored cheeks. I’m all for being different but her makeup is bordering on bizarre!

## 7. Courtney Love

![Courtney Love](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/beauty/2012/09/7_courtney-love_500x391.jpg)

I get that Courtney Love likes to look a little wild and rocker-edgy but she has a tendency to look disheveled and unkempt which is why she made this list of crazy celebrity makeup looks. From smeared lipstick to a serious case of raccoon eyes, Courtney Love likes to look eccentric! We all know she’s beautiful but I can’t help but wish she’d go back to her look in the Hole days rather than her current curious look.

## 8. Lindsay Lohan

![Lindsay Lohan](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/beauty/2012/09/8_lindsay-lohan_600x463.jpg)

Lindsay is another starlet who went from cute and chic to one of the craziest celebrity makeup. Post Samantha Ronson and probation, Lindsay seems to have taken a liking to thick, drawn-on brows, pale face makeup or super tan bronzer and peculiar cheeks and lips. She’s another gorgeous star who just has a bad makeup artist so I hope she gets some new help soon!

What do you think of these crazy celebrity makeup looks? Do you like it when celebs go all out with outrageous looks or do you prefer a more classic and refined look? I think part of being a celebrity is to look good but to also be daring so I appreciate the great lengths some of these celebs go to shock and amaze us!
