# 8 Amazing Beauty Products for the Gym ...

Need some help picking out beauty products for the gym? We know it’s not always the best idea to wear makeup while we work out, but we sometimes end up doing it anyway for one reason or another. So if we’re going to get our sweat on while we wear makeup, let’s get the best beauty products for the gym! Keep reading for 8 beauty products that will survive a workout and then some!

## 1. The Body Shop’s Vitamin E Gentle Facial Cleansing Wipes

![The Body Shop’s Vitamin E Gentle Facial Cleansing Wipes](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/beauty/2013/01/1_the-body-shops-vitamin-e-gentle-facial-cleansing-wipes_326x289.jpg)

Since we’re talking about beauty products for the gym, makeup wipes might seem like an odd addition to your gym bag, right? Not really, these wipes aren’t just for removing makeup. These wipes are enriched with vitamin E, which makes it easy to freshen up your body after your workout and conditions skin!

## 2. Suave Professionals Keratin Infusion Dry Shampoo

![Suave Professionals Keratin Infusion Dry Shampoo](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/beauty/2013/01/2_suave-professionals-keratin-infusion-dry-shampoo_220x220.jpg)

Dry shampoo is definitely one of my must-have gym friendly beauty products. If you have oily roots like me or maybe you just like to refresh your scalp before you head out of the gym, dry shampoo can instantly help your scalp feel clean, revitalized and your hair will smell great!

## 3. Goody Ouchless Hair Elastics

![Goody Ouchless Hair Elastics](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/beauty/2013/01/3_goody-ouchless-hair-elastics_192x248.jpg)

Hair elastics are another one of the essential beauty products for your gym bag. It’s pretty hard to have a good workout when you’ve got your hair whipping back and forth, right? Hair elastics will keep your hair out of your face and you can easily put your hair in a pony or braid when you’re rushing out the door!

## 4. L'Oreal Voluminous Waterproof Volume Building Mascara

![L'Oreal Voluminous Waterproof Volume Building Mascara](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/beauty/2013/01/4_loreal-voluminous-waterproof-volume-building-mascara_300x300.jpg)

If mascara is one of your beauty staples at the gym, waterproof mascara is the way to go! Waterproof mascara is obviously a little more difficult to remove at the end of the day but so worth it if you want to your mascara to last through a strenuous workout. Voluminous is famous for building major volume and lengthening lashes, so this is a great mascara for anytime!

## 5. Maybelline Color Whisper by Color Sensational

![Maybelline Color Whisper by Color Sensational](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/beauty/2013/01/5_maybelline-color-whisper-by-color-sensational_161x400.jpg)

Don’t forget to include a lip tint when you’re packing up beauty products for your gym bag! If you crave a little more color than a plain lip balm but want a more lightweight formula than a lip butter, try out Maybelline’s new Color Whisper lip colors. They’re gel-based so they glide on smooth and don’t contain any shimmer, so the color is much more natural looking!

## 6. Maybelline Dream Fresh BB Cream

![Maybelline Dream Fresh BB Cream](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/beauty/2013/01/6_maybelline-dream-fresh-bb-cream_177x400.jpg)

Even out your skin tone, protect your face from the sun and brighten your skin all without any heavy oils or ingredients. Most skin experts will agree that if you must wear any foundation when you work out, oil-free formulas are the way to go. Dream Fresh BB is that, plus it comes in a larger range of colors so finding your color match will be much easier!

## 7. Urban Decay Glide-on 24/7 Eye Pencil in Perversion

![Urban Decay Glide-on 24/7 Eye Pencil in Perversion](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/beauty/2013/01/7_urban-decay-glide-on-24-7-eye-pencil-in-perversion_32x448.jpg)

UD’s 24/7 eyeliner is one of the hardest working eyeliners around, so naturally it’s one of the gym-friendly beauty products you need in your gym bag! If you just don’t feel complete without defining your eyes, this 24/7 eye pencil will take you from school or work to the gym and back. The formula is creamy, glides on super smooth and won't budge ‘til you say so.

## 8. Make up for Ever HD Microfinish Powder

After you apply all these gym-friendly beauty products, you’ll want to set it all with a totally translucent powder that’ll help blur any imperfections and even out your complexion. This powder is talc-free so it won’t dry out your skin but it’ll still help suck up excess oil and keep skin looking radiant.

Enlist the help of these gym-friendly beauty products to help you look and feel your best. Again, it’s best to go au naturel while you work out so your pores won’t get clogged, but if you feel better with some makeup up then by all means, go for it! What are your gym bag must-haves?
