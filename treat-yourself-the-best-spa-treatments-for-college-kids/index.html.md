# The Best Spa Treatments for College Kids ...

The spa is a wonderful, magical place where you get to feel both beautiful and pampered. Many spa treatments can seem geared toward more mature women but there’re still many options for you if you’re a college girl. These are 7 of the best ones.

## 1. Have a Facial

You’re never too young to take good care of your skin. Taking good care of your skin in your late teens and early 20s can help you to have healthier, younger looking skin in the decades to come. A facial is a wonderful way to take care of your skin. It’s a great way to have your skin cleansed on a deeper level and detoxified. You’ll love the difference you see and feel.

## 2. Experience Eyelash Tints

This can be a wonderful spa treatment for college kids. This is especially true if you have light or pale lashes. Being a natural blonde, this would be a dream come true for me. If you’ve always had to apply mascara to make your lashes visible then this is what you’ve been waiting for. Even those blessed with naturally dark lashes may want to go a shade or two darker.

## 3. Treat Your Tresses

Having a luxurious spa treatment for your hair is heavenly. You can choose to go for a deep conditioning treatment to revitalize your hair. The deep conditioners used at spas are generally much higher quality than the ones you use at home. Deep conditioners can help repair damaged hair and strengthen it to protect against future damage. This is a spa treatment anyone can benefit from.

## 4. Get a Glamorous Blowout

A good blowout is something you’ll never forget. If you’ve had one then you know how wonderful they are and how you want to just keep your hair looking that way forever. Treat yourself to a blowout at the spa. You can usually pick up a few tricks to use at home by watching how it’s done, too.

## 5. Have Your Makeup Done

Having your makeup done by someone else always offers you a fresh perspective. It’s good to see how someone else does it. They may choose different colors for you. They could choose to accentuate a different feature than you usually do. It could offer you some inspiration to keep you out of a makeup rut.

## 6. Indulge in a Hot Stone Massage

A hot stone massage is pure pleasure. If you’re the type of girl that loves the cozy feel of sitting by the fire or slipping into your fuzzy pjs in the winter then this is the spa treatment for you. It’s a combination of a massage with hot rocks strategically placed where they will give you the most pleasure. You’ll leave feeling totally relaxed. Your stress will just float away as you enjoy this lovely spa treatment.

## 7. End with a Pedicure

Of course, no trip to spa is complete without a pedicure. A pedicure is a treatment that stays with you quite a while after you’ve left the spa. It’s a treat almost every woman enjoys. Whether you choose French tips or a bold color with a floral accent, it’s a spa treatment that’s perfect for college girls. You can make use of all your pretty sandals to show off your new pedicure.

These’re 7 of the best spa treatments for college kids. Which ones do you hope to enjoy? I’d love your input.
