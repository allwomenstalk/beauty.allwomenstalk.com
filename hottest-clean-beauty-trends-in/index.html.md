# The Hottest Clean Beauty Trends in 2018 ...

Are you looking for the **hottest clean beauty trends in 2018**? As we leave 2017 in the distance, so too do we leave all of the fads and trends that arrived in the world of fashion and beauty over those last twelve months. However, it wouldn’t be the industry that it is without a whole new heap of trends coming in for the year to come, and this year it looks like its going to be all about clean beauty. This is a movement towards products that are as kind for your body and for the environment as possible, so with no further ado let’s get in to to the **hottest clean beauty trends in 2018**.

## 1. Anti-Pollution Skincare

One of the **hottest clean beauty trends in 2018** is anti-pollution products. Not only is pollution bad for your internal health, it’s also bad for your skin. Visible facial aging, especially premature aging, is something that we all want to prevent, so it’s no wonder that anti-pollution products are going to be big in 2018. Things like cleansers, face masks, and SPF based items are all going to be leading the charge when it comes to what it the most popular clean beauty product group this year.

## 2. Oil Cleansers

Oils don’t just moisturize; they can actually cleanse your skin too. This works when massaged into the skin so the oil can bind to grime and surface dirt, allowing it rinse away easily. Oils can be much gentler for sensitive skin than moisture stealing alternatives like regular soap, so it might be worth getting on board with this particular trend if you are prone to dryness.

## 3. Crystal Infused Products

Healing crystals were a massive thing in 2017, and 2018 will see crystals become prevalent in skincare too! A perfect example of this is the Kashmir Saphir Perfecting Mask, which contains elements of blue copper, blue lotus, and blue sapphire, all said to have firming, regenerative and restorative properties.

## 4. Organic Eye Shadows

2018 is going to see an increase in the number of brands that are producing amazing organic eyeshadows. Rather than smudging toxic chemicals near your eyes for the sake of beauty, this year thanks to names like Ecco Bella, Alima Pure, and Lily Lolo, you can enjoy the same old classic beauty with the added benefit of them being totally organic!

## 5. Facial Rollers

These little tools are handheld massagers that are crafted from various materials and in different forms. The goal is to roll them across your face to boost blood circulation and reduce the presence of under eye swelling, and they have also been promoted as helping your face to absorb beauty products more effectively!
