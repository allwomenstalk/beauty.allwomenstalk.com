# 9 Brilliant Beauty Products Every Glamour Girl Needs ...

I know you hear about so-called brilliant beauty products often, but I promise you that these nine products are worth taking a look at! In my opinion, most beauty products are pretty awesome already because they do so much for my skin and facial features. But, the nine oh-so-brilliant beauty products that I want to introduce you to go above and beyond the call of beauty duty!

## 1. Marc Jacobs Beauty Highliner Gel Crayon

![Marc Jacobs Beauty Highliner Gel Crayon](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/beauty/2013/08/1_marc-jacobs-beauty-highliner-gel-crayon_249x248.jpg)

Since the illustrious Marc Jacobs debuted his beauty collection, I had to start this list of brilliant beauty products with his highliner. What’s highliner you ask? Well, it’s called a gel crayon and they say it’s like a gel liner in a pot that’s enclosed in a crayon-pretty snazzy, right?! It’s long-wearing, waterproof and has incredible color! Get yours at Sephora for $25.

## 2. DuWop Foundation of Youth

![DuWop Foundation of Youth](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/beauty/2013/08/2_duwop-foundation-of-youth_299x271.jpg)

I’m not too familiar with DuWop except for their Lip Venom, but their Foundation of Youth is said to be kinda like a face lift in a tube! This easy-to-apply foundation blurs fine lines and wrinkles and helps minimize the appearance of large pores. As a bonus, this beauty product also contains Matrixyl 3000, which is a proven anti-aging ingredient! Get it at Dermstore.com for $38.

## 3. Clinique Black Honey Lip Duo

![Clinique Black Honey Lip Duo](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/beauty/2013/08/3_clinique-black-honey-lip-duo_249x248.jpg)

If you’re one of the many Black Honey devotees in the world, this lip duo will be your next must-have beauty product! Take the almost lipstick, almost gloss lip product on one end and pair it with the Long Last Glosswear with SPF, and your lips are covered no matter what the occasion! Tuck this in your purse for perfect pucker anytime. Find it at Sephora for $21.

## 4. MAC Prep + Prime BB Beauty Balm SPF 35

![MAC Prep + Prime BB Beauty Balm SPF 35](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/beauty/2013/08/4_mac-prep-prime-bb-beauty-balm-spf-35_374x495.jpg)

MAC Cosmetics makes a lot of genius beauty products that are on my beauty wish list and their new BB cream has just rose to the top of the list! I’m cray about BB creams, but I hate the limited selection of colors. Lucky for me, this particular BB cream comes in a nearly sheer beige that works with lots of skin tones! This works as a primer, foundation and a sunscreen so it’s a true multi-tasker! Get it at Nordstrom for $30.

## 5. Benefit High Beam

![Benefit High Beam](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/beauty/2013/08/5_benefit-high-beam_249x215.jpg)

I’m usually reluctant to try highlighters because I’ve had some pretty scary experiences with them, but I can trust Benefit’s High Beam to give my complexion a healthy, radiant glow! You can use this clever little beauty product to highlight the brow bone area or cheeks on a variety of skin tones for a subtle, soft shimmer. Many people love to use this without any other makeup for a dewy finish. Get it at Sephora for $26.

## 6. Guerlain Terracotta 4 Seasons Tailor-Made Bronzing Powder

![Guerlain Terracotta 4 Seasons Tailor-Made Bronzing Powder](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/beauty/2013/08/6_guerlain-terracotta-4-seasons-tailor-made-bronzing-powder_249x247.jpg)

Although this is definitely one of the more expensive beauty products, this bronzing powder is awesome! The reason this beauty product boasts such a high price tag yet still has women wanting more is because it comes in four graduated matte bronzing tones to custom blend your perfect color. Use it to sculpt, highlight and perfect your complexion any time of the year! Buy it at Sephora for $75.

## 7. PANTENE EXPERT COLLECTION AGE DEFY ADVANCED THICKENING TREATMENT

![PANTENE EXPERT COLLECTION AGE DEFY ADVANCED THICKENING TREATMENT](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/beauty/2013/08/7_pantene-expert-collection-age-defy-advanced-thickening-treatment_299x397.jpg)

Our hair can go through a lot of abuse from frequent styling, exposure to the elements and through the use of heat products. Pantene understands this and created an age defying thickening treatment for aging hair that adds fullness, softness and helps make hair easier to manage! Find it at Walmart for $18.97.

## 8. Smashbox Fade to Black Photo Op Eye Shadow Palette

![Smashbox Fade to Black Photo Op Eye Shadow Palette](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/beauty/2013/08/8_smashbox-fade-to-black-photo-op-eye-shadow-palette_429x150.jpg)

Eye shadow palettes come and go so I normally don’t get overly enthusiastic about them. However, Smashbox’s Fade to Black Fall 2013 Collection’s Photo Op Eye Shadow Palette is something to get excited about! You can get the palette in either Fade In or Fade Out, but both palettes come with rich matte and shimmer shadows that you’ll want to use all season long! Find it at Smashbox.com for $42.

## 9. John Frieda Full Repair Style Revival Heat-Activated Styling Spray

![John Frieda Full Repair Style Revival Heat-Activated Styling Spray](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/beauty/2013/08/9_john-frieda-full-repair-style-revival-heat-activated-styling-spray_299x398.jpg)

This last beauty product is especially for my heat styling ladies! Not only is this a heat protectant spray, it also adds shine, body and a flexible hold to your hairstyle! Now you can give yourself blowouts, curls, waves or pin-straight hair while protecting your tresses and giving them a silky and high-gloss finish! Pick yours up at Ulta for $9.99.

Some of these fab beauty products are new, some are a little older but they’re all great! These marvelous beauty products will have you looking and feeling beautiful all day or evening long! What do you think is the most brilliant beauty product you’ve used so far?
