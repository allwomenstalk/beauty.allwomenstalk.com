# 11 Delightfully Fantastic Gift Ideas for the Beauty Junkie ...

If you are looking for some gift ideas for beauty junkies, have I got some fabulous suggestions for you! I think we all have a certain someone in our life who is totally obsessed with beauty products and there is nothing they want more than more beauty products. So whether that makeup addict is you or someone else you know, be sure not to miss out on these 11 gift ideas for beauty junkies.

## 1. Urban Decay Naked 3 Palette

![Pink, Beauty, Eye, Flower, Eyelash,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ra/kb/fvkhl1ml5e93889d07341206132060_600x751.jpg)

The Naked 3 Palette has to be one of the top gifts ideas for beauty junkies this year. She has probably waited for the arrival of this palette with bated breath since she first heard about it. Naked 3 comes with 12 gorgeous rose-toned neutral eye shadow colors and a dual ended eye shadow brush. Perfect for everyday and special occasion looks. Available at Sephora for $52.

## 2. E.l.f. Studio 11 Piece Brush Collection

![Brush, Makeup brushes, Cosmetics, Tool,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/za/sg/hxbibau95e9388a8e27c0983540826_600x600.jpg)

Any makeup addict would be absolutely thrilled to receive a set of makeup brushes and e.l.f.’s Studio makeup brushes are sturdy, easy to use and gives you precise application. Since these brushes come in a handy compact case, this would make a great gift for someone who travels a lot or likes to do touch-ups on the go! Available at eyeslipsface.com for $30.

## 3. NYX Cosmetics Decadent Delights Nail Art Collection

![Material property, Plastic, Plant, Magenta, Toy,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/im/cp/xjjpbdiw5e9388b4e5c17965709091_600x761.jpg)

This is an amazing gift idea for the woman in your life who cannot go a few days without changing her nail polish. This set comes with 18 nail colors in a variety of shades and finishes so she can create a ton of different looks. Perhaps the best part of this gift is that you can join in on the fun! Available at Ulta for $19.99.

## 4. Philosophy 3-in-1 Shampoo, Shower Gel and Bubble Bath

![Product, Beauty, Pink, Water, Skin care,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/n2/zs/rofg3vl55e9388c2d4b95468516082_600x800.jpg)

If your beauty addict pal loves her bath and body products, philosophy’s multi-tasking shampoo, shower gel and bubble bath is a must. It comes in a variety of mouth-watering fragrances like raspberry sorbet, melon daiquiri, cinnamon buns and more. So not only will she smell great, this 3-in-1 product saves space and will help make relaxation time even better. Available at philosophy.com for $16.50.

## 5. NYX Cosmetics the It List Pink Lip Glosses

![Red, Cosmetics, Material property, Hair coloring,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/r5/n4/loiqo24f5e9388ca82569116166668_600x647.jpg)

Here we have another beautiful gift idea for the girl who can never have enough lip glosses or just hasn’t found the right pink. NYX’s The It List comes with five lippies in a range of hues and formulas from matte to glossy so there is a lip gloss to satisfy her every beauty whim. This set contains the infamous butter gloss that won an Oprah’s O-ward for spring 2013 makeup. Available at Ulta for $19.99.

## 6. Burt’s Bees Tips N’ Toes Kit

![Burt’s Bees Tips N’ Toes Kit](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/12/18023646_4049_299x269.jpg)

The Tips n’ Toes Kit makes a really awesome gift for the girl who has everything. If she has more makeup than she knows what to do with, treat her to some natural skin care products that will help keep her fingers and toes happy and hydrated all winter long. She will get a lip balm, hand salve, cuticle cream, hand cream and foot cream so she will be sandal-ready come spring. Available at Walgreens for $12.99.

## 7. Juice Beauty Organics to Go Kit

![Product, Beauty, Skin care, Material property, Cream,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/lo/xz/qek2tv8g5e9388d8c864e966140499_600x900.jpg)

Do you know someone who is interested in exploring the benefits of organic skin care? Juice Beauty makes makeup, skin and hair care products that are all organic, gluten-free and made with pesticide-free fruits, vegetables and plants. This 5-piece kit includes a cleansing milk, hydrating mist, exfoliating peel, antioxidant serum and moisturizer. Available at Ulta for $29.

## 8. Babo Botanicals 3-in-1 Bubble Bath, Shampoo and Wash in Lavender Meadowsweet

![Product, Liquid, Skin care, Lotion, camomile,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/t2/xa/bfelosiy5e9388e4da729285579171_600x600.jpg)

I like to think of this product as “philosophy for babies.” This is an all-natural, plant-based body wash, shampoo and bubble bath made for babies but loved by all. If you have a beauty addicted friend who’s expecting, has kids or just needs to unwind, this will be their next favorite bath item. It smells of sweet lavender and contains certified organic ingredients. Available at Ulta for $16.50.

## 9. SEPHORA Favorites Rollerball Collection for Her

![Product, Pink, Brown, Liquid, Room,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/vb/gs/omh4g6bs5e9388f4ddc2a489510636_600x750.jpg)

Does the beauty addict in your life go through perfume like water? Then she needs Sephora’s rollerball collection stat! This set is similar to their fragrance sampler, but instead of vials she gets five rollerball samples of their most loved fragrances plus a certificate to get a full-sized bottle of her favorite . Available at Sephora for $85.

## 10. Carol’s Daughter Olive Oil Infusion at-Home Treatment

![Product, Beauty, Liquid, Plant, Skin care,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/hs/t6/rjsuta5q5e9389021fd84859993810_550x550.jpg)

This is an amazing gift idea for the hair obsessed! Carol’s Daughter olive oil infusion treatment comes with a hair smoothie, hair oil, plastic cap and applicator tip so this hair treatment is the real deal. If your friend or family member has been going through some frustrating hair issues, this treatment will detangle and moisturize her troubles away. Available at Sephora for $25.

## 11. Pacifica Indian Coconut Nectar Take Me There Set

![Product, Red, Material property, Hair coloring, Skin care,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/a9/qm/rpzael2b5e93890d106a9480841317_600x411.jpg)

Perfect for the vegan or minimalist beauty in your life, the Take Me There set features a travel-sized body butter, roll-on perfume and lip tint in a delectable coconut scent. If she’s not crazy for coconuts, there is also vanilla, guava, blood orange and gardenia. Available at Target for $17.99.

I hope this list gave you some ideas on what to get your beauty junkie. There are so many tempting gift ideas out there that you just might want to pick up a little something for yourself! Do you have a go-to beauty gift that you like to give? I would love to hear your beauty gift suggestions!
