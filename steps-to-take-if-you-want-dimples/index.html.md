# Steps to Take if You Want Dimples ...

Dimples are so beautiful but some people aren’t born with them. But you can change that by following the steps and you will have them in about two weeks!

## 1. LIP EXERcises

All you have to do is suck in your checks and try to push your lips outward or watch a YouTube video! It’s called fish lips. It helps your cheeks get slimmer which means DIMPLES! If you have braces it will snag your checks so watch out!

Press and hold your fingers in the same spot for a few minutes every day. (Pencils work the best). PLEASE DO NOT, DO NOT let your nail go in the imprint It will just mess up your “dimples” that a yet to come! Trust me, be careful!

## 2. SMILE SMILE SMILE 😁😆

This will make it permanent!

Smile for 30 mins straight, showing a FULL set of teeth!!! it will start to hurt for the first 5 mins but beauty is pain!!!???? Really are you getting anywhere if it’s all fun and games?

If it’s hurting, it’s working🤗😁

This is the most important tip because if you have clear skin like CLEAR CLEAR OMG CLEAR skin which is rare, but if you can somehow get close to that OMG your dimples will show up and you will slay everyone else who has a regular smile 💞💖💗💖💗💓💓😍🥰
