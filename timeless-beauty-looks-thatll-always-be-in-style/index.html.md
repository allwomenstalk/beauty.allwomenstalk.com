# 6 Timeless Beauty Looks Thatll Always Be in Style ...

Nowadays, fashion trends are just that, trends. They come and go. Sometimes, it's hard to keep up with what's hot and what's not! That's why it's great to have certain looks that you can always depend on, the ones you know will always be in style... here's five of them:

## 1. Red Lips

![Red Lips](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/aj/ew/fd69dwwb_426x639.jpg)

There's a reason why red lips have never gone out of style. The red lip is the most sophisticated and [sultry makeup](https://makeup.allwomenstalk.com/amazing-sultry-makeup-tips/) effect a woman can master. Seriously, a good red lip can make you feel like you're about to conquer the world.

## 2. Smokey Eye

![Smokey Eye](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/x6/ab/tzfpzlyo_600x900.jpg)

A good smokey eye instantly adds a mysterious, sexy edge to your look and calls attention to those beautiful eyes of yours.

## 3. Defined Brows

![Defined Brows](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/op/kn/bcx2i699_600x899.jpg)

Embrace your thick brows! Stars like Cara Delevingne, Sophia Bush, Lucy Hale, and Lily Collins have proved time and time again that define eyebrows are gorgeous!

## 4. Winged Eyeliner

![Winged Eyeliner](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/d6/p7/pvmo1ugf_500x691.jpg)

Winged eyeliner helps elongate the eye, adding instant sex appeal to any look. It's a trend that originated years ago and is still making its rounds in the beauty world today.

## 5. Beachy Waves

![Beachy Waves](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/hq/gd/ghwdle0b_518x731.jpg)

Loose waves give you a natural, beach-babe appeal. The look is flattering for all face shapes and has managed to withstand the test of time.

## 6. Deep Side Part

![Deep Side Part](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/v9/n9/qz1dok42_400x544.jpg)

The deep side part is a hairstyle that commands attention and is both glamorous and sexy. It looks best when paired with big barrel curls! Stars like Sienna Miller and Amanda Seyfried have worn this trend on various occasions.

Which of these beauty looks are your favorites?
