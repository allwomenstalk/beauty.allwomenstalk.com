# 15 Life\-Changing Beauty Hacks You Must Start  for Perfect Skin ...

I'm here to share some **life-changing beauty hacks**. These are some tricks to make your skin flawless, your face younger, and your life easier. The most interesting part of this whole thing is that you can do most of these things without paying a dime. Most of the items you need to do these pretty simple things can be found in your kitchen. It is no secret that beauty products are super expensive, but they are definitely worth it. If we can just adopt certain tricks that make us prettier and we do not have to rip out pockets for it, wouldn’t we love them? We have compiled a list of **life-changing beauty hacks** to help you out.

## 1. SLEEP on YOUR BACK

You should sleep on your back if you want to avoid wrinkles. It has been revealed that squishing your face in the pillow makes your skin wrinkle easily. It is already an established fact that sleeping more makes your skin look younger. However, if you cannot get your beauty sleep, you can still avoid those wrinkles. That's one of the best **life-changing beauty hacks** you'll ever find!

## 2. GIVE YOURSELF a FACIAL MASSAGE

Move your fingers in an even circular motion from the inner to the outer corners of your eyes. Repeat this three to four times. You can also tap your face in rotating circular motions. This will increase circulation and also reduce lines.

## 3. HIDE FOUNDATION STAINS with SHAVING CREAM

When you stain your clothes with foundation while applying makeup, you do not have to change your clothes. You can simply apply a little bit of shaving cream on it to hide the stain.

## 4. ADD a LITTLE BIT of HEAT to YOUR PENCIL EYELINER

You bring your pencil eyeliner close to a lighter, match, hot iron, or blow hot air on it using the hair dryer, this turns the eyeliner into a gel formula. This will give you a much smudgier formula for a perfect look.

## 5. PUT YOU PAINTED NAILS in CHILLED WATER to MAKE THEM DRY FASTER

Waiting for your freshly painted nails to dry can be pretty exhausting. You might end up messing it all up if you are not patient enough. One way to dry up your freshly painted nails is to dip them in ice cold water. That does the trick.

## 6. SHRINK a PIMPLE with LISTERINE

You can apply a little bit of Listerine on pimples. The alcohol in the Listerine will cause a pimple to fade. This helps in removing those pimples in minutes.

## 7. DRINK LEMON WATER

Drink a warm glass of lemon water when you wake up in the morning. Squeeze lemon juice into warm water and drink it like you generally drink water. This detoxifies your system and gives you a clearer skin.

## 8. SPRAY YOUR HAIRBRUSH with PERFUME

Spraying your hairbrush with perfume will leave your hair smelling really good after you have used the hairbrush.

## 9. UNIQUE NAIL POLISH

If you have an eyeshadow you do not use because it is overly sparkly, you might want to try this. Crush the eyeshadow and add it to your nail polish. This will give you a glittering new color.

## 10. USE VASELINE to MAKE YOUR PERFUME LAST LONGER

Rub Vaseline on your pulse before applying perfume. This will make the perfume stay on you for a longer period.

## 11. USE BB CREAM for a LIGHTER MAKEUP

You can use BB cream instead of foundation to make your makeup feel lighter. The BB cream not only acts as a foundation, it also acts as a primer and moisturizer. It also gives you a more natural look.

## 12. USE CONDITIONER when YOU HAVE NO SHAVING CREAM

If you run out of shaving cream when you need to shave your legs, you are still in luck when you have conditioner. Apply conditioner in the place of shaving cream. This will also help you to have a clean shave while keeping your legs soft.

## 13. WEAR BOBBY PINS the RIGHT WAY for TIGHTER GRIPS

Always use the wavy side of your bobby pin when you want to hold your hair. The smooth side easily slips through the hair and that make it loosen its grip on the hair.

## 14. REVIVE DRY MASCARA with SALINE SOLUTION

When your mascara dries up within three months, you can make it work again with saline solution. Just add a few drops of and you're good to go.

## 15. Do THIS to MAKE YOUR LIPSTICK LAST LONGER

After applying the first coat for your lipstick, gently dab your lips with a tissue. You can proceed to apply your second coat. This makes your lipstick last longer.
