# Inspiring Beauty Resolutions That Will Make You Look Better than Ever ...

**New Year's beauty resolutions** are fun to make and work on. They also offer you a lot of payoff! When you keep beauty resolutions you’ll look better than ever. Here’re 7 to consider making.

## 1. Don’t Skip SPF

![human action, hair, face, photograph, person,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/gh/vr/ygsowzq658637c2b51dbe984713223_600x749.jpg)

Sun protection is so important. Not only does it protect you from the dangerous rays that can cause skin cancer but it protects your skin from premature aging. Let’s be honest. None of us want wrinkles to appear before their time. Being vigilant with sun protection can prevent that from occurring. There’re many moisturizers that already have SPF in them which will save you both time and money.

## 2. Try Some New Makeup Trends

![eyebrow, face, hair, cheek, nose,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ef/ls/ljeaxpi858637a58c685e025627981_600x600.jpg)

It’s easy to find yourself in a makeup rut. You’re doing your makeup the same day after day. While the finished look may be nice, it can get rather boring. Challenge yourself to try some new makeup trends this year. Aim for a new lip 💄 or eye look at least once every season.

## 3. Treat Yourself to Some New Makeup Brushes

![hair, face, clothing, nose, black hair,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/pm/r4/xhc0aykt58637a16d5977188345730_600x749.jpg)

I have a confession. I had let my makeup brushes go until they were looking rather ragged and worn despite regular cleaning so I decided to replace all of my old ones in 2016. I’m not tossing them all, just the ones that are worse for the wear. New makeup brushes are a treat. They feel luxurious on your face and your makeup applies so much more beautifully with brushes that’re in good condition.

## 4. Go for That Fierce Hairstyle You’ve Been Daydreaming of

![hair, black hair, face, person, hairstyle,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/j2/c9/px9gyxo458637afa29d12595372984_600x600.jpg)

Most of us have at least one hairstyle we’ve been thinking on for a long time but we just haven’t taken the plunge to try it. This’s the time! A new year is the perfect time for a new start! Be daresome and go for that fierce hairstyle you’ve been dreaming of. Whether it’s a sleek, new cut 💇 or an edgy hair color you’ve been dying to try, go for it!

## 5. Add a Weekly Skin Treatment into Your Skincare Routine

![face, eyebrow, hair, cheek, nose,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fl/eh/a3o0s4a358637bafb1df7922050822_600x600.jpg)

Daily skincare is very important. But it’s also good when you can add in a weekly skin treatment. There’re many options that you can choose from. An exfoliating treatment or skin mask are two simple skin treatments you can do at home. Your face will feel fresh and invigorated afterward. And not only do you feel the results but you can see them too.

## 6. Wash Your Makeup off Every Night

![hair, face, eyebrow, black hair, clothing,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/yl/ro/pter0e5c58637c05f1376152787521_600x600.jpg)

This one is hard! I used to awful about going to bed with my makeup on. But I finally realized it’s sabotage to your skin and now take very good care of it. Your skin needs time to breathe and recover and it can’t do that if it’s caked in makeup. This’s one of the top tips dermatologists give their patients in order to have clearer, more youthful skin. Keep a tub of makeup removing wipes on your nightstand for those nights you’re just too exhausted to do the whole cleanser and water routine.

## 7. Use an Eye Cream at Bedtime

![hair, face, eyebrow, person, nose,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/f8/bv/y1qwjffa58637cb9ce451603473697_600x600.jpg)

It’s never too early to start using an eye cream. The area around your eyes is one of the first to show signs of aging. Eye cream can help delay that for several more years. It’s good to use it twice a day but bedtime is the most important time. It has all night to work while you sleep.

These’re 7 beauty resolutions that’ll make you look better than ever. Do any of these inspire you? What’re your beauty resolutions for 2017?
