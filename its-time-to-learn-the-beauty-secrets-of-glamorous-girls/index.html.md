# It's Time ⌛️ to Learn the Beauty Secrets of Glamorous Girls ...

We all see a girl from time to time who blows us away with her glamour. Instead of feeling jealous, you can hone your beauty routine and be the girl that makes the other green with envy when you walk by. Looking glamorous doesn't have to be hard and you are going to be surprised at how easy it is to be the best looking girl in town. Here are all the secrets you've been looking for.

## 1. Make Sleep Work for You

![blond, I'm, looking, forward, sleeping.,](https://img.allw.mn/content/s6/id/qvf2sr7m586ca7fad41cd081476248_500x282.gif)

You should be getting at least 7 hours of sleep every night, which gives your skin a chance to restore and look fresh every morning.

## 2. Switch to a Satin Pillowcase

![hair, blond, clothing, girl, mouth,](https://img.allw.mn/content/gq/z6/zt65rljn586ca7fbc65da722300661_480x360.gif)

While you're at it, swap out your pillowcase for a satin one, which helps cut down on the lines and wrinkles on your face.

## 3. Always Wear Sunscreen

![human action, person, sense, interaction,](https://img.allw.mn/content/ck/b1/egnlsrxz586ca7fb9e9f0713652064_350x263.gif)

The most glamorous girls never forget the SPF. It will ward off the signs of aging and enhance your natural beauty.

## 4. Drink a Smoothie

![wheel, lighting, eye,](https://img.allw.mn/content/h2/ry/du7ka4di586ca7fb71e35897946373_565x377.gif)

Smoothies are loaded with fruit, which boosts your antioxidant intake, which is great for your skin and overall beauty.

## 5. Use Whitening Toothpaste

![human action, hair, person, blond, mouth,](https://img.allw.mn/content/a3/wp/cr4autdj586ca7fb9d9f5606068277_500x262.gif)

When you think glamour, you probably think bright white teeth so start using a whitening toothpaste right now.

## 6. Go Easy on the Foundation

![muscle, arm, sense, human body,](https://img.allw.mn/content/jj/j6/eoo0riuc586ca7fcb0f66806392256_450x253.gif)

Too much foundation never looks good so learn to master the art of getting it perfect and you'll be instantly glamorous.

## 7. Curl Your Lashes

![hair, face, eyebrow, nose, blond,](https://img.allw.mn/content/or/i1/o0k4g9cb586ca7fd8ff95900523317_497x344.gif)

All the glamorous girls will tell you that simply curling your lashes will make you look instantly better.

## 8. Choose a Signature Lipstick

![hair, human hair color, face, lip, eyebrow,](https://img.allw.mn/content/wc/ld/zp7vxbcj586ca7fdcc915834731731_245x155.gif)

When you have a signature color, you have the makings of a glamorous look anywhere you go.

## 9. Use Facial Toner

![face, hair, cheek, eyebrow, nose,](https://img.allw.mn/content/th/bf/qlc4i53q586ca7fe66e56975841402_245x300.gif)

This is a hotly debated topic, but give it a try and see if you like the results. If you do, go for it!

## 10. Curl Your Hair

![face, hair, blond, mouth, finger,](https://img.allw.mn/content/fd/pu/rn3fbh1g586ca7fe60995358301063_500x250.gif)

Don't have time for a stick straight blowout? Embrace waves of curls instead to prevent the dreaded bedhead look.

## 11. Choose Your Signature Scent

![human action, hair, person, girl, hairstyle,](https://img.allw.mn/content/x7/y4/y8htpsc5586ca7ff38e69031466017_500x277.gif)

Like a signature lip color, you should definitely find out what your signature perfume scent is.

## 12. Take Your Makeup with You

![handbag, bag, pattern, design, fashion accessory,](https://img.allw.mn/content/fw/m6/xb0nw981586ca8005a8cf613077595_500x600.gif)

Want to look glamorous all day long? Make sure you take your makeup with you for quick touch ups.

## 13. Drink Lots of Water

![human action, hair, face, nose, person,](https://img.allw.mn/content/vh/b2/nkqrige0586ca8016177d050651207_245x300.gif)

The best looking girls will tell you that drinking lots of water boosts the look of your skin and keeps you looking healthy and fresh all day long.

## 14. Keep Polish Looking Great

![face, facial expression, hair, eyebrow, nose,](https://img.allw.mn/content/s3/wo/ondsuz6c586ca802119f2177353126_356x200.gif)

Nothing is less glamorous than chipped nail polish. So be sure to keep your manicure in top shape all the time.

## 15. Exfoliate Regularly

![hair, face, blond, person, beauty,](https://img.allw.mn/content/rn/mm/butf4fr7586ca8024d29a634388956_400x225.gif)

Exfoliation keeps your entire body radiant and young so be sure you're doing it at least once or twice a week.

## 16. Use a Scented Body Lotion

![clothing, active undergarment, barechestedness, muscle, arm,](https://img.allw.mn/content/jz/ln/metu8iss586ca80261320153142407_409x480.gif)

A feminine scented body lotion gives you a subtle scent that makes you as glamorous as possible.

## 17. Keep Your Eyebrows Groomed

![hair, hairstyle, brown hair, screenshot, glasses,](https://img.allw.mn/content/mx/ub/m3k4vpj6586ca801f06b0264722032_500x260.gif)

Unruly brows never look glamorous so be sure you keep yours plucked and filled in for the perfect look.
