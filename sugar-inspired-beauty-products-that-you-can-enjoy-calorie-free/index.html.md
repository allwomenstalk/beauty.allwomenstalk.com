# 9 Sugar Inspired Beauty Products That You Can Enjoy Calorie\-free ...

If you’re resolving to cut back on sweets, try some of these delectable, sugar inspired beauty products to curb your sweet tooth! Okay, so it’s not the same as indulging in a creamy cupcake, but I think you’ll find these beauty products to be pretty darn close without all of the extra sugar and calories we’re trying to avoid. If you love sugary scented products and want to enjoy the sweet smell of candy without the cavities, give these sugar inspired beauty products a go!

## 1. Aquolina Pink Sugar Eau De Toilette

![Aquolina Pink Sugar Eau De Toilette](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/12/26175645_4150_499x480.jpg)

One of my favorite sugar inspired beauty products is Aquolina’s Pink Sugar perfume. If you haven’t experienced this fragrance before, it smells just like cotton candy. It’s sweet, girlie and perfect to wear when you need a little pick-me-up. Pink Sugar allows us to indulge our senses with vanilla and caramel whenever we want. Available at Sephora for $30.

## 2. Soap & Glory Sugar Crush Body Scrub

![Soap & Glory Sugar Crush Body Scrub](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/12/26175644_8089_249x199.jpg)

Another sweet beauty product that gives you the rush of sugar without the crash is Sugar Crush Body Scrub. This brown sugar scrub is a must-have to exfoliate dry winter skin and reveal fresh, new skin. If the delicate vanilla scent doesn’t win you over, I think the moisturizing almond oil will. If one of your New Year’s resolutions is to take better care of your skin, start with this product! Available at Sephora for $20.

## 3. Body Fantasies Cotton Candy Fantasy Body Spray

![Body Fantasies Cotton Candy Fantasy Body Spray](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/12/26175644_6685_600x600.jpg)

This is a budget-friendly alternative to Pink Sugar perfume and a great one at that. I have a friend who wore this daily and the scent always reminds me of the fun times we had together. This body spray perfectly captured cotton candy in a bottle and it’s a fun and flirty fragrance to wear on a date or for hanging out with friends. Available at FragranceX.com for $6.59.

## 4. NIVEA Lip Butter Tin Caramel Cream Kiss

![NIVEA Lip Butter Tin Caramel Cream Kiss](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/12/26175645_1760_409x241.jpg)

Give your lips a caramel cream kiss with Nivea’s lip butter. This hydrating and soothing lip butter will calm chapped lips while leaving a delicious fragrance behind. Perfect to use under lipstick or alone, this lip butter is like a sugary smooth topping for your lips! Available at Target for $9 for a pack of 3.

## 5. FRESH Sugar Lemon Shower Gel

![FRESH Sugar Lemon Shower Gel](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/12/26175645_0018_459x433.jpg)

Take your love of sugar inspired beauty products with you to the shower with Fresh's Sugar Lemon Shower Gel. This foamy body wash smells of sweet citrus with lots of moisturizing ingredients like shea butter and comfrey extract to keep your skin hydrated and restored. You can also find perfume, body lotion and sugarbath in the same sweet fragrance. Available at Sephora for $18.50.

## 6. Lavanila Laboratories the Healthy Deodorant in Vanilla Coconut

![Lavanila Laboratories the Healthy Deodorant in Vanilla Coconut](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/12/26175645_4278_599x725.jpg)

I’m not sure I want my underarms to smell like sugar, but I’m a sucker for most things that smell like coconut. Lavanila’s The Healthy Deodorant in Vanilla Coconut not only comes in a tempting fragrance, but it’s also 100% natural and highly effective. If you’re looking for a safer alternative to traditional drugstore deodorants, give this guy a try! Available at Sephora for $8.

## 7. Revlon Parfumerie Scented Nail Enamel in Spun Sugar

![Revlon Parfumerie Scented Nail Enamel in Spun Sugar](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/12/26175648_4619_400x400.jpg)

Let’s not forget about beauty products for our fingernails and toes. Revlon’s scented nail polish in Spun Sugar gives your nails the look and scent of sweet, spun sugar in a bottle. This sparkly silver needs only two coats to achieve opacity and is lightly scented like warm brown sugar that lasts all day. What a fun way to do your nails! Available at Ulta for $5.99.

## 8. Philosophy Vanilla Birthday Cake Flavored Lip Shine

![Philosophy Vanilla Birthday Cake Flavored Lip Shine](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/12/26175648_0541_299x281.jpg)

Now you can have your cake and wear it, too! Philosophy is known for concocting some of the most tempting dessert and sugar inspired beauty products and this lip shine really takes the cake. If you’re looking for a gloss to wear alone, over your favorite lipstick or you just want a lippie that smells and tastes delish, your search stops here. Available at Ulta for $10.

## 9. Carol’s Daughter Chocolat Smooth Blow Drying Cream

![Carol’s Daughter Chocolat Smooth Blow Drying Cream](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/12/26175648_5577_374x480.jpg)

The last sugar inspired beauty product I wanted to introduce you to isn’t necessarily sweet, but it does contain cocoa extract to smooth the frizzies while hydrating and improving the condition of your hair. Use this product in conjunction with other products from the Chocolat Smoothing line for manageable, lustrous hair that you won’t be able to keep your hands off of! Available at Macy’s for $22.

With all of these wonderful sugar inspired beauty products you can enjoy all the sweetness of sugar anytime you please! If you have an unrelenting sweet tooth like I do, feel free to indulge in some of these beauty products so you can get your sugar fix without any guilt. Do you currently use any candy or sugar inspired beauty products?
