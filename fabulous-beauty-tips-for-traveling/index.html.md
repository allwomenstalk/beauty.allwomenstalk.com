# 8 Fabulous Beauty Tips for Traveling ...

Beauty tips for traveling can cover everything from skincare to your makeup routine. Long haul flights can be especially draining and your beauty routine for such occasions almost requires as much planning as the actual trip! A good beauty tip for traveling can help you relax for the duration of your trip or simply have you looking fresh on arrival. Whether you’re traveling in the air, on the ground or on the high seas, hopefully these travel beauty tips will have you covered.

## 1. Prepare Yourself

When it comes to beauty tips for traveling, start with a little bit of pre-travel preparation. Treat your skin to a deep cleanse and exfoliate to remove any dead skin cells. Long haul travel can cause dullness and dryness in skin, so a bit of TLC beforehand is a great way to combat this.

## 2. Downsize

When traveling, you’re often faced with baggage restrictions and limitations. Be smart with your beauty products and choose smaller, travel sized products. Decant your favourite shampoo, conditioner, body lotions, and gels into travel sized empty bottles. For those products that can’t be transferred, buy mini versions instead.

## 3. Make It Multipurpose

When looking at beauty tips for traveling, maximise your luggage space by investing in items that have multiple purposes. Instead of bringing a moisturiser and a foundation, why not try a tinted moisturiser? Better yet, try a tinted moisturiser with SPF protection! Multipurpose balms are also great as they can be used on lips as well as the body.

## 4. Pick a Palette

Keep your makeup routine streamline by investing in a travel-friendly palette. Keep an eye out for palettes that contain a range of products like lip colours, eye shadows, blushes, and highlighters. All your must-haves will be in the one handy place, making it easier to perform touch ups on the go.

## 5. Keep It Simple

When it comes to beauty tips for traveling, it’s all about keeping things simple. Stick to natural colour palettes as these tend to be more versatile than bright and bold colours. On your nails, wear a barely there polish (or none at all) as they are less likely to show chips.

## 6. Stay Hydrated

Flying at high altitudes or traveling in air conditioned compartments can cause skin to dry out, and we all know that dry skin is a big no-no. Stay refreshed for the duration of your trip by keeping your fluids up. Drink plenty of water and even apply a hydrating mist every now and then.

## 7. Remove Makeup

For long haul travel, remove all traces of makeup on your trip (failing that, wear as little makeup as possible). You’ve all heard the ‘don’t sleep with your makeup on’ tip and this beauty trip for traveling is along those same guidelines.

## 8. Stay Sanitised

If you’re touching up your makeup in transit, keep some sanitising gel handy. Prevent possible breakouts and the transfer of germs and dirt to your face by applying sanitising gel to your hands before applying makeup with them.

From packing to in-transit tips, these are just a few beauty tips for traveling that will see you through your next trip. Hopefully they’ll make the travel experience much more enjoyable! What’s your best beauty tip for traveling?
