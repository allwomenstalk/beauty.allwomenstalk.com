DIY Recipes 🥄 That'll Give You Plumper Lips ...

Simple Sugar Lip Scrub • Salt and Cinnamon Scrub • Peppermint Oil Plumper • Cayenne Lip Plumper • Minty Beeswax Plumper • More ...

By Eliza

There’s something about a full plump set of lips that is just so attractive, don’t you think? There are lots of things you can do to take great care of your kisser, including regularly exfoliating and wearing SPF lip balm to protect against the damaging effects of the sun’s rays. Another thing you can do is to whip up one of these easy recipes to give your lips a complete makeover that you will love seeing in the mirror every day.

1. Simple Sugar Lip Scrub

This is probably my favorite recipe because it’s easy to mix up and is super effective for smoothing lips and making them plump and perfect. You probably have everything you need right in your own kitchen.

Ingredients:

Sugar
Olive oil

Directions:

Combine equal amounts sugar and olive oil to make a paste. Use a toothbrush or your finger to gently massage the mixture into your lips.

Source: myawesomebeauty.com

2. Salt and Cinnamon Scrub

This is a great product for use right before you go to bed at night. The cinnamon plumps your lips and the salt helps smooth and soften them. Not only do you get those benefits, but the mixture also smells pretty great too.

Ingredients:

½ teaspoon cinnamon
½ teaspoon salt
1 tablespoon petroleum jelly

Directions:

Combine the ingredients and mix well. Smooth the mixture all over your lips and allow it to sit for five minutes. Rinse well.

Source: diyhealthremedy.com

3. Peppermint Oil Plumper

This is probably the easiest lip plumping recipe you’ll ever find. It feels great when you put it on and will give you the full lips you’ve always craved.

Ingredients:

Your favorite lip gloss
1 to 3 drops peppermint oil

Directions:

Place the peppermint oil into your lip gloss tube and use a cotton swab to mix well. Each time you use the gloss, the peppermint oil will plump your lips.

Source: enhancemyself.com

4. Cayenne Lip Plumper

Cayenne pepper is spicy and you’re probably well acquainted with how tingly your lips get when you eat something that has cayenne in it. That’s why this plumper is such a great option. You are going to love it!

Ingredients:

Olive oil
Pinch cayenne pepper

Directions:

Crush the cayenne pepper very finely, then stir it into the olive oil. Smear it on your lips in small doses.

Source: myawesomebeauty.com

5. Minty Beeswax Plumper

Not only will this recipe work to plump your lips, but it also hydrates them at the same time. This is super easy to make and you’ll want to carry it with you wherever you go.

Ingredients:

Beeswax
Wintergreen essential oil
Menthol essential oil
Cinnamon essential oil

Combine the beeswax with the essential oils and apply it to your lips anytime you need to plump them up.

Source: diyhealthremedy.com

6. Anise Lip Plumper

Here’s another DIY lip plumper that is super easy to make, but one that will have mega benefits for making your lips look plump and beautiful. Chances are you can find all the ingredients at your local health foods store.

Ingredients:

2 teaspoons beeswax
2 teaspoons coconut oil
2 tablespoons sweet almond oil
2 drops anise oil

Directions:

Combine the beeswax and coconut oil in a small bowl and microwave for 20 seconds. Add the almond and anise oil and mix well. Cool the mixture and apply to your lips.

Source: myawesomebeauty.com

7. Simple Cinnamon Lip Plumper

You’ve seen cinnamon in a couple of the other plumpers on this list and that’s because it’s great for giving you lovely full lips. Since you probably already have some in your cabinet, there’s no reason not to try this recipe today.

Ingredients:

Cinnamon
Vaseline

Directions:

Mix a pinch or two of cinnamon into a small container of Vaseline and apply it to your lips anytime you want to.

Source: diyhealthremedy.com

8. For Soft Lips

When you lips are well hydrated and exfoliated, they are much plumper and healthier looking. If dryness is an issue for you, this is the DIY recipe you need today.

Ingredients:

1 teaspoon raw honey
1/2 teaspoon fresh pomegranate juice

Directions:

Combine the two ingredients and apply liberally to your lips. Allow it sit for several minutes before rinsing with warm water.

Source: diynatural.com

9. Cocoa Peppermint Plumper

Peppermint is ideal for plumping your lips and making them look bigger than they are. This DIY recipe tastes as good as it smells.

Ingredients:

Raw cocoa butter
4 to 6 drops peppermint essential oil

Directions:

Combine the two ingredients and apply to your lips. Try not to eat the lip plumper all day long.

Source: bustle.com

10. Cayenne Pepper Plumper

If you can do wih a bit of discomfort, this recipe is the one for you. The components of cayenne will have you plumped up in no time at all.

Ingredients:

Olive oil
Cayenne pepper

Directions:

Combine the oil and pepper to form a paste. Apply to your lips and let sit for 5 minutes. Rinse with warm water.

Source: bustle.com
