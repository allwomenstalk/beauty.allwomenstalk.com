The Afterglow Lip Oil lives up to its promises, delivering a high-shine finish that’s equally nourishing. Let’s be real, lip gloss is all about that glossy finish, but NARS takes it a step further. ## Why We Love It

Aside from its beautiful array of shades, the NARS Afterglow Lip Oil stands out because of its hydrating formula. It’s infused with raspberry seed oil and pomegranate extract, allowing it to nourish your lips while still ensuring that high-gloss finish. Perfect for those dry winter months or sun-kissed summer days.

There’s an understated magic in a product that can transport someone-in this case, to a beachside escape, while fitting seamlessly into anyone’s beauty routine. The NARS Afterglow Lip Oil has captured this essence perfectly, providing not just color and gloss, but an experience.

## How to Wear It

- **Daytime Glow**: Pair it with minimal eye makeup and light blush for a fresh, effortless look.
