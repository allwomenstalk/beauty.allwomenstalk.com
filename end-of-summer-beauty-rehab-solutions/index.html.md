# 7 End of Summer Beauty Rehab Solutions ...

End of summer beauty rehab is essential to prepare for the cold winter days ahead. Did the sun, sand, and chlorine ruin your good skin and hair? No worries, with these few tips, you'll be back on your feet before you know it! Here are 7 end of summer beauty rehab solutions.

## 1. Anti-Freeze Serum

The first end of summer beauty rehab solution you want to use should be for your hair. It's usually the first thing people notice when they look at you. After overloading your hair with chlorine, heating tools, and too much sun, your poor hair will be begging for mercy. Using an anti freeze serum such as Dove's Style + Care Nourishing Dry Ends Serum will not only de-frizz your hair, it will also make it look as good as new. Just put a few drops on the ends before styling or blow drying and there, you're good to go!

## 2. Melting Making up?

Summertime and hot temperatures can cause concealer and foundation to be cakey. Ditch that and use a BB cream instead, like Maybelline New York Fresh BB Cream. Its special formula not only doesn't melt and cake, but it also gives the right amount of coverage plus evens out skin tones.

## 3. Dry Face Issues?

Being in the sun can dry out your skin, but you shouldn't use just any kind of moisturizer for your face for an end of summer rehab. It can break you out and in some cases make you even drier. Using a specific kind, such as Garnier Moisture Rescue Refreshing Gel-Cream, keeps your face glowing with Vitamin B and apple water extract.

## 4. All over Skin Coverage

Being out in the sun all day, every day, for a whole summer has left your skin dry and sunburned. So instead of applying layers of sunblock, aloe vera and lotion why not just apply one layer and get it over with. L'Oreal Paris Sublime Sun Advanced Sunscreen not only protects your skin from the sun, but it moisturizes and prevents fine lines from forming with Vitamin E and white grape seed.

## 5. Extra Dry Skin? No Problem... We've Got You Covered!

Sometimes lotion won't solve all your dry skin problems! When you need something extra, use something like Moroccan Oil Body Buff. It's an exfoliant that buffs away all the rough spots on your body, leaving your skin feeling refreshed and oh so soft. The secret to it? Argan oil and orange peels!

## 6. Feet Issues

Wearing flip flops and sandals all summer has left your feet callused and dry, no doubt. Skip the salon and do some DIY pampering. Use a scrub such as Manicure/Pedicure by OPI Chamomile Mint Mask. Leave it on your heels for five minutes and wash it off in warm water. Voila! Soft feet in just minutes. It's the perfect end of summer rehab solution.

## 7. Dry Nails

Your nails take a beating, especially in the summer! Nails can get dry, brittle, cracked... and then there's hang nails! All that leaves your nails looking yucky! Using a nail cream like Burt's bees Lemon Butter [cuticle cream](https://nails.allwomenstalk.com/best-cuticle-creams-for-nail-biters/), will cure all your nail issues! By using natural ingredients you'll see a difference in no time!

There you go, a whole nice list of end of summer beauty rehab solutions to get you looking and feeling like the beautiful person you are! Have you tried any of these products before? If so, what are your favorites?
