# 7 Tricks to Make Your Eyelashes Grow Gorgeous in Only a Month ...

If you're need **ways to make your eyelashes grow** so they look and feel fabulous, you have come to the right place. Every girl wants long, lush lashes because they are beautiful. That doesn't mean it's easy to get them though. Fortunately, these **tips for longer lashes** can help. By adding them to your beauty routine, you can get the long eyelashes you want without too much work.

## 1. BRUSHING

Why it works: Brushing enhances blood circulation and stimulates hair growth, making it the top answer to **how to grow eyelashes** that are long and full.

How to do: Use a special brush for eyelashes or an old clean brush from mascara. Apply several drops of vitamin E to the brush and carefully comb it through the lashes from roots to the ends. Do this two times a day for 5 minutes.

## 2. OILS

Why it works: Castor, coconut, and olive oils contain fatty acids and vitamins that nourish hair follicles.

How to use: Before going to bed, apply a drop of oil on a clean brush or finger and apply the oil on the eyelashes. For a better effect, makeup artists advise mixing several oils together.

## 3. VASELINE

Why it works: Cosmetic Vaseline perfectly hydrates and strengthens eyelashes.

How to use: Very carefully apply Vaseline with a brush on the eyelashes and leave it for the night. Make sure it doesn’t get into your eyes. In the morning just remove the Vaseline with warm water.

## 4. GREEN TEA

Why it works: Green tea contains antioxidants and vitamins and is very frequently used in lots of cosmetic products that fight hair loss.

How to use: Brew 1 teaspoon of green tea and let it cool. Then apply on your eyelashes with a sponge 1-2 times a day.

## 5. ALOE VERA

Why it works: The juice and pulp of the plant have lots of elements that enhance hair growth.

How to use: Take a fresh leaf of aloe, squeeze a few drops out of it and apply on your eyelashes. Leave the juice there for the night and wash it off with clean water in the morning.

## 6. MASSAGE

Why it works: Massage helps eyelashes get the nutrition they need quickly and as a result enhances their growth.

How to use: Wash your hands thoroughly and take a few drops of olive or coconut oil on the fingers. Massage your eyelids and eyelashes for about five minutes. Repeat several times a week.

## 7. EGG MASK

Why it works: Eggs help strengthen hair and eyelashes as well as prevent their falling out.

How to use: Mix one egg with 1 tablespoon of glycerin and Vaseline and put this mixture on the eyelashes. Repeat the mask three times a week for several months.
